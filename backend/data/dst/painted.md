The **Painted Biome** is a [biome](/wiki/Biomes "Biomes") exclusive to the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC. It is covered in [Painted Sand Turf](/wiki/Painted_Sand_Turf "Painted Sand Turf") which can be dug up with a [Pitchfork](/wiki/Pitchfork "Pitchfork").

The most common resources found in this biome are [Iron Ore](/wiki/Iron_Ore "Iron Ore"), [Tuber Trees](/wiki/Tree/Tuber_Tree "Tree/Tuber Tree") and [Gnat Mounds](/wiki/Gnat_Mound "Gnat Mound"). One may also find [Rocks](/wiki/Rocks "Rocks"), [Flint](/wiki/Flint "Flint"), [Nitre](/wiki/Nitre "Nitre") and [Gold Nuggets](/wiki/Gold_Nugget "Gold Nugget") lying on the ground. [Sparkling Pools](/wiki/Sparkling_Pool "Sparkling Pool") can also be found here, but [Pangoldens](/wiki/Pangolden "Pangolden") generally drink them before the player can spot them.

The [Mobs](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs") commonly found in this biome are [Thunderbirds](/wiki/Thunderbird "Thunderbird"), but Pangoldens and [Gnat Swarms](/wiki/Gnat_Swarm "Gnat Swarm") are also found here.

[Crows](/wiki/Crow "Crow") and [Kingfishers](/wiki/Kingfisher "Kingfisher") are only birds that land in this biome.
