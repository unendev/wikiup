### Developer

**Cult of the Lamb x Don't Starve Together** is an update of *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*.[[1]](#cite_note-1) It is a crossover update with some new [Items](/wiki/Items "Items"), a new [Structure](/wiki/Structure "Structure") and some free skins.

## Features[]

* [Brick Flooring](/wiki/Brick_Flooring "Brick Flooring")
* [Gold Flooring](/wiki/Gold_Flooring "Gold Flooring")
* [Humble Lamb Idol](/wiki/Humble_Lamb_Idol "Humble Lamb Idol")
* [Red Crown](/wiki/Red_Crown "Red Crown")
