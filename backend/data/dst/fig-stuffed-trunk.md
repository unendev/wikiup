**Fig-Stuffed Trunk** is a [Meat](/wiki/Meat "Meat") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in the *[Waterlogged](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together/Version_History#Waterlogged_Beta_.28since_July_26.2C_2021.29 "Don't Starve Together/Version History")* update. It is cooked in the [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") by combining 1 [Fig](/wiki/Fig "Fig") and 1 [Koalefant Trunk](/wiki/Koalefant_Trunk "Koalefant Trunk"). Fillers can be [Twigs](/wiki/Twigs "Twigs"). It takes 40 seconds to cook and 15 [Days](/wiki/Day-Night_Cycle "Day-Night Cycle") to [Spoil](/wiki/Spoil "Spoil").

When consumed, it restores 60 Health, 150 Hunger, and 15 Sanity.
