The **Ice Floe** is a unique area exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in the *[From Beyond](/wiki/From_Beyond "From Beyond")* updates. It is a remote island located far out into the [Ocean](/wiki/Ocean "Ocean") and accessible only by [Boat](/wiki/Boat_Kit "Boat Kit") or using [Woodie's Weregoose form](/wiki/Cursed_Woodie#Don.27t_Starve_Together "Cursed Woodie") or soul hopping as [Wortox](/wiki/Wortox "Wortox").

The Ice Floe is covered with some solid ice tiles with an [Ice Fishing Hole](/wiki/Ice_Fishing_Hole "Ice Fishing Hole") in the center from where the [Frostjaw](/wiki/Frostjaw "Frostjaw") can be spawned. The area around the Ice Floe is covered with [Mini Glaciers](/wiki/Mini_Glacier "Mini Glacier") during late [autumn](/wiki/Autumn "Autumn"), [winter](/wiki/Winter "Winter"), and [spring](/wiki/Spring "Spring"), which will melt until [Summer](/wiki/Summer "Summer").

When it is getting colder around winter, the Ice Floe grows and receives more ice tiles. These tiles will start breaking away when it is getting warmer around spring, resulting into small floating boat-like platforms which will break after a few seconds. Players can enter and leave these platforms before they break.
