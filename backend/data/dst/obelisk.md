**Obelisks** are [indestructible](/wiki/Category:Indestructible_Object "Category:Indestructible Object") [Structures](/wiki/Structures "Structures") usually found surrounding the [Pig King](/wiki/Pig_King "Pig King") in [Survival Mode](/wiki/Survival_Mode "Survival Mode"), but also act as an indicator of the [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity") level of a player. When the Sanity level is 15% and below, the Obelisks around the Pig King change their states: the lowered ones raise, and the raised ones become lowered. The [Shadow Creature](/wiki/Shadow_Creature "Shadow Creature") [Mr. Skitts](/wiki/Shadow_Creature#Mr._Skitts "Shadow Creature") briefly appears to raise and lower the Obelisks.

In [Adventure Mode](/wiki/Adventure_Mode "Adventure Mode"), Obelisks act as road barriers, and can be great for many defensive ideas. However, it will be hard to deal with, as one might have to eat a load of raw [Green Caps](/wiki/Green_Mushroom "Green Mushroom") to lower their Sanity and [Cooked Green Caps](/wiki/Cap/Green "Cap/Green") to raise their Sanity, and it would take a while to defend against something, like [Hounds](/wiki/Hounds "Hounds").

Obelisks may be found in one of two states, depending on the Sanity level of the player: *raised* and *lowered*. Obelisks in the *lowered* state will not act as obstacles in the player's path. Obelisks in the *raised* state will act as obstacles and the player will need to adjust their Sanity level accordingly in order to lower them again. The player cannot use the [Nightmare Amulet's](/wiki/Nightmare_Amulet "Nightmare Amulet") insanity effect to raise or lower the Obelisk.

In Adventure Mode, the player may encounter Obelisks that will impede their progress if their Sanity is not in the correct range. In Sandbox Mode, they serve as a decoration, which surrounds the Pig King.

## Don't Starve Together Don't Starve Together[]

If two players are nearby an Obelisk with varying sanity, the Obelisk will prioritize the player with unfit conditions. For example, if there lay a barrier of Insanity Obelisks and there's a player with 0% Sanity and another with >15% Sanity, the Obelisk will continue to block both players until the player with >15% Sanity has either lowered their Sanity or left the vicinity.

In *[A New Reign](/wiki/A_New_Reign "A New Reign")*, the [Atrium](/wiki/Atrium "Atrium") [Biome](/wiki/Biomes "Biomes") was added to the [Ruins](/wiki/Ruins "Ruins"). Insanity Obelisks block the main paths that lead from the [Tentapillar](/wiki/Tentacle#Big_Tentacle "Tentacle") all the way to the [Ancient Gateway](/wiki/Ancient_Gateway "Ancient Gateway"). There also lay [Ornate Chests](/wiki/Ornate_Chests "Ornate Chests"), all of which are blocked behind Sanity Obelisks - barriers that open only for the sane. Players must be careful around the previously mentioned Obelisks since they can act as impenetrable barriers if they do not have items to raise their Sanity.
