The **Rocky Beach** is a [Biome](/wiki/Biomes "Biomes") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")*. It is completely covered in [Rocky Beach Turf](/wiki/Rocky_Beach_Turf "Rocky Beach Turf") and can only be found as part of the [Lunar Island](/wiki/Lunar_Island "Lunar Island") area amidst the [Ocean](/wiki/Ocean "Ocean").

Rocky Beach contains multiple [Driftwoods](/wiki/Driftwood "Driftwood") and [Sea Bones](/wiki/Sea_Bones "Sea Bones") present in all different forms. There are some [Reeds](/wiki/Reeds "Reeds") and [Boulder](/wiki/Boulder "Boulder"). [Bull Kelp](/wiki/Bull_Kelp "Bull Kelp") are present planted on the ground.

This area can be dangerous due to the multiple [Anenemies](/wiki/Anenemy "Anenemy") and [Shattered Spider Holes](/wiki/Shattered_Spider_Hole "Shattered Spider Hole") present. However, it can provide temporary respite from [Enlightenment](/wiki/Enlightenment "Enlightenment"), as there are no [Celestial Fissures](/wiki/Celestial_Fissure "Celestial Fissure") present in this biome.

## Placeholder Trivia[]
