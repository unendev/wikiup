*Exclusive to:* Don't Starve Together

**Ink Blights** are a special type of shadow creature that appear when players approach a [Nightmare Fissure](/wiki/Nightmare_Light "Nightmare Light") with a [Dreadstone Outcrop](/wiki/Dreadstone_Outcrop "Dreadstone Outcrop"). They appear in triplicate: always spawning one Shriek, one Rasp, and one Jitters. If all three Ink Blights are killed, the Dreadstone Outcrop will disappear.

All Ink Blights have [planar entity protection](/wiki/Planar_Entity_Protection "Planar Entity Protection"), and thus general damage hit are less effective against them, in contrast, the planar damage hit and others damage source can effectively damage it. For example, a [Shadow Reaper](/wiki/Shadow_Reaper "Shadow Reaper") will deal 32.99 general damage and 18 planar damage to them instead of 38 and 18 respectively.

The Ink Blights will each take turns attacking the player in a predictable cycle, with the attack frequency of the remaining Ink Blights increasing as they are killed off.

**Shriek** is a hostile creature exclusive to *Don't Starve Together*. It has 800 health and deals 25 normal damage and 30 planar damage. It does not attack at close range, but fires six projectiles towards the character, covering a pentagonal area. If the character is not in the attack radius, then it fires six projectiles in an arc.

When killed, it drops 2 [Dark Tatters](/wiki/Dark_Tatters "Dark Tatters"), 2 [Pure Horror](/wiki/Pure_Horror "Pure Horror"), and 1 [Nightmare Fuel](/wiki/Nightmare_Fuel "Nightmare Fuel"), as well as an additional [Dark Tatter](/wiki/Dark_Tatters "Dark Tatters"), [Pure Horror](/wiki/Pure_Horror "Pure Horror"), and [Nightmare Fuel](/wiki/Nightmare_Fuel "Nightmare Fuel") with a 67%, 33%, 67% chance, respectively.

**Rasp** is a hostile creature exclusive to *Don't Starve Together*. It has 1200 health and deals 20 general damage and 20 planar damage.

It has two types of attack: jump and attack with tentacles. If the target is standing next to the Rasp, it will deliver five quick tentacle strikes, dealing 20 normal + 20 planar damage each time. If the target is at a distance, the Rasp will jump on the target, trying to swallow it. If the Rasp managed to swallow a player, then it will chew the character six times, dealing a total of 120 normal + 120 planar damage. If it fails to swallow a target, then it will deal damage in an area as it falls, dealing 20 normal + 20 planar damage and cause a large [Knockback](/wiki/Knockback "Knockback"). During the fall, it destroys structures under it. This attack also dismounts players that are riding on a Beefalo.

When killed, it drops 3 [Dark Tatters](/wiki/Dark_Tatters "Dark Tatters"), 1 [Pure Horror](/wiki/Pure_Horror "Pure Horror"), and 2 [Nightmare Fuel](/wiki/Nightmare_Fuel "Nightmare Fuel"), as well as an additional [Dark Tatters](/wiki/Dark_Tatters "Dark Tatters"), [Pure Horror](/wiki/Pure_Horror "Pure Horror"), and [Nightmare Fuel](/wiki/Nightmare_Fuel "Nightmare Fuel") at 33%, 50%, 67% chance respectively.

**Rictus** is a hostile creature added to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*. It has 800 health and deals 35 damage and 20 planar damage with its bite attack, and 15 damage and 10 damage with its leap attack.

When walking close to the [Dreadstone Outcrop](/wiki/Dreadstone_Outcrop "Dreadstone Outcrop"), the Rictus will be in it's stealth form. Only it's sharp mouth can be seen, trying to bite the player. In this state, Rictus cannot be hit or directly targeted by the player. It can be revealed by using an area-of-effect (AOE) attack or an attack that doesn't require selecting an target, for example:

* [Tooth Trap](/wiki/Tooth_Trap "Tooth Trap")/[Bramble Trap](/wiki/Bramble_Trap "Bramble Trap")
* [Gunpowder](/wiki/Gunpowder "Gunpowder")/[Slurtle Slime](/wiki/Slurtle_Slime "Slurtle Slime")
* [Brightshade Bomb](/wiki/Brightshade_Bomb "Brightshade Bomb")/[Brightshade Staff](/wiki/Brightshade_Staff "Brightshade Staff")
* [Brightshade Armor](/wiki/Brightshade_Armor "Brightshade Armor")/[Brambleshade Armor](/wiki/Brambleshade_Armor "Brambleshade Armor")/[Bramble Husk](/wiki/Bramble_Husk "Bramble Husk")
* Thorn Damage of [Fugu Hutch](/wiki/Hutch "Hutch")
* [Dumbbells](/wiki/Dumbbells "Dumbbells")
* [Winona's Catapult](/wiki/Winona%27s_Catapult "Winona's Catapult")
* [Electrification Circuit](/wiki/Circuits#Electrification_Circuit "Circuits")
* [Balloon](/wiki/Pile_o%27_Balloons#Balloon "Pile o' Balloons")
* [Shadow Prison](/wiki/Shadow_Traps#Shadow_Prison "Shadow Traps") (Will turn back invisible when not receiving damage)
* [Earthquake](/wiki/Earthquake "Earthquake")
* Bosses with AOE attacks

When killed, it drops 3-4 [Dark Tatters](/wiki/Dark_Tatters "Dark Tatters"), 1-2 [Pure Horror](/wiki/Pure_Horror "Pure Horror"), and 2-3 [Nightmare Fuel](/wiki/Nightmare_Fuel "Nightmare Fuel").

## Strategy[]

The Ink Blights can be quite challenging without some good preparation.

It is recommended to use an Walking Cane and Magiluminescence to utilize additional movement speed to dodge incoming projectiles and Rasp jump attack.

If armor is a need, it is also recommended to wear a Night Armor or Thulecite Crown, as anything that protects the player less than such, is dangerous.

Because most melee weapons will deal less damage due to [Planar Entity Protection](/wiki/Planar_Entity_Protection "Planar Entity Protection"), if the Celestial Champion has not been defeated yet, Glass Cutters will prove very useful thanks to their increased durability against Shadow creatures.

Characters such as Wolfgang, WX-78, or Maxwell are great at fighting the Ink Blight's. Other characters that are decent are Woodie, Wigfrid, and Walter.

## Trivia[]

* The Ink Blights were introduced in the *[Terrors Below](/wiki/Terrors_Below "Terrors Below")* update.
  + Rictus was added in the *[Depths of Duplicity](/wiki/Depths_of_Duplicity "Depths of Duplicity")* update.
* Hovering the cursor over the Ink Blights will simply display each of their names as "Ink Blight". However, [Characters](/wiki/Character "Character") with their respective [Shadow Affinity](/wiki/Shadow_Affinity "Shadow Affinity") perks are able to see their real names.
* The Ink Blights having red cloth tied around bodies made entirely of shadow makes them strongly resemble the [Ancient Herald](/wiki/Ancient_Herald "Ancient Herald").
