A **Cactus** is a form of flora from the *[Reign of Giants](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Reign_of_Giants "Don't Starve: Reign of Giants")* DLC found only in the [Desert](/wiki/Desert "Desert") biome. [Cactus Flesh](/wiki/Cactus_Flesh "Cactus Flesh") can be harvested from the Cactus at the cost of 6 [Health](/wiki/Health "Health"). Wearing armor will decrease the amount of damage taken. When harvested it will drop [Cactus Flesh](/wiki/Cactus_Flesh "Cactus Flesh") during any season, but only in [Summer](/wiki/Summer "Summer") will it drop its [Flower](/wiki/Cactus_Flower "Cactus Flower").

Cacti cannot be dug up with a [Shovel](/wiki/Shovel "Shovel"). The player will have to wait for them to regrow in 4 (3 during [Spring](/wiki/Spring "Spring")) days to be able to harvest them again. Unlike most other plants, Cacti can grow back during winter, unaffected by the cold. Therefore, Cactus Flesh can be easily farmed and used in lieu of farmed vegetables during Winter.

## Don't Starve Together Don't Starve Together[]

*[A New Reign](/wiki/A_New_Reign "A New Reign")* for *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")* adds a different type of Cacti to the game. Apart from their appearance, they do not differ from the original version. Both types co-exist in regular worlds: The Oasis Cactus only appears in the Desert containing the [Oasis](/wiki/Set_Piece#Oasis "Set Piece") and the [Antlion](/wiki/Antlion "Antlion"), while the original one can be found in the Desert containing the [Dragonfly](/wiki/Dragonfly "Dragonfly") [Set Piece](/wiki/Set_Piece "Set Piece") and [Hound Mounds](/wiki/Hound_Mound "Hound Mound").

## Prototype Tips[]

* Wearing a [Bramble Husk](/wiki/Bramble_Husk "Bramble Husk") in *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")* and *[Reign of Giants](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Reign_of_Giants "Don't Starve: Reign of Giants")* will allow the player to pick the cacti with no health loss, and also no damage to the armor itself. This makes [Wormwood](/wiki/Wormwood "Wormwood") an effective Cactus farmer.
* The Health lost is mostly negated by using a [Football Helmet](/wiki/Football_Helmet "Football Helmet") or [Log Suit](/wiki/Log_Suit "Log Suit"). With either of these equipped when picking the cactus, one takes only 2 (1.2 in Don't Starve Together) damage and eating the cooked flesh will reduce this to 1 (0.2 in Don't Starve Together).

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* Although there appears to be a dug up version shown in image files, there is currently no known way to dig one up.
* The Cactus is modeled after the typical [Barrel Cactus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Barrel_Cactus "wikipedia:Barrel Cactus").
* The Oasis Cactus variant was introduced in the *[Against the Grain](/wiki/A_New_Reign#February_2nd,_2017_-_Against_the_Grain "A New Reign")* update.
* Since the update *The Curse of Moon Quay*, Cacti are now renewable due to regrowth.
