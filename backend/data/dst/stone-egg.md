The **Stone Egg** is an naturally spawning [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC. It is found at the [BFB](/wiki/BFB "BFB")'s nest and can be grown slowly until hatching into [Ro Bin](/wiki/Ro_Bin "Ro Bin") along with the [Ro Bin Gizzard Stone](/wiki/Ro_Bin_Gizzard_Stone "Ro Bin Gizzard Stone"). It will take 3 days for the egg to hatch, so long as it stays within the required heat conditions.

The Stone Egg has three states, normal, overheated, and freezing. The Stone Egg grows while it is normal, and slowly shrinks when it is overheated or freezing. During the [Night](/wiki/Night "Night"), the Egg will be freezing unless being placed close to a [Fire](/wiki/Fire "Fire"), but during the [Day](/wiki/Day "Day"), it will overheat if too close to a Fire. Unlike [Hatching Tallbird Egg](/wiki/Hatching_Tallbird_Egg "Hatching Tallbird Egg"), it will never freeze or heat to death.

Despite being [Eggs](/wiki/Eggs "Eggs"), The Stone Egg cannot be used in [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") and has no Egg value.

## Mosquito Bugs[]

* [Sneezing](/wiki/Hay_Fever "Hay Fever") and dropping the Stone Egg will reset its progress.
* Picking up the egg right as it is about to hatch will make it disappear from your inventory and make you lose the egg.
