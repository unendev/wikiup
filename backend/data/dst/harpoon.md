The **Harpoon** is a ranged [Weapon](/wiki/Weapon "Weapon") in the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC. It can be obtained from an exploding [White Whale](/wiki/White_Whale "White Whale") Carcass or can be found in [X Marks the Spot](/wiki/X_Marks_the_Spot "X Marks the Spot") treasure chests. When this weapon is used to attack, it will be thrown by the player and deal 200 damage upon hitting the target. The Harpoon will land next to its target and can be picked up.

## Prototype Tips[]

* Character damage modifiers apply to the Harpoon’s damage:
  + [Wolfgang](/wiki/Wolfgang "Wolfgang") deals 250-400 damage in Mighty Form and is able to kill [Wildbores](/wiki/Wildbore "Wildbore") in 1 shot at 225 hunger, [Dragoons](/wiki/Dragoon "Dragoon") and [Crawling/Swimming Horrors](/wiki/Shadow_Creature "Shadow Creature") at 250 hunger, [Werepigs](/wiki/Werepig "Werepig") at 275 hunger, and [Elephant Cacti](/wiki/Elephant_Cactus "Elephant Cactus") and [Terrorbeaks](/wiki/Shadow_Creature "Shadow Creature") at 300 hunger. It takes 2 shots to kill [Water Beefalo](/wiki/Water_Beefalo "Water Beefalo") at 225 hunger, [Blue Whales](/wiki/Blue_Whale "Blue Whale") at 263 hunger and [White Whales](/wiki/White_Whale "White Whale") at 300 hunger.
  + [Wigfrid](/wiki/Wigfrid "Wigfrid") deals 250 damage and can kill Wildbores in 1 hit and Water Beefalo in 2.
  + [Wendy](/wiki/Wendy "Wendy") and [Wes](/wiki/Wes "Wes") only deal 150 damage but it is enough to kill [Crocodogs](/wiki/Crocodog "Crocodog") and [Sea Hounds](/wiki/Sea_Hound "Sea Hound") in 1 shot.
* A Harpoon can be used to destroy [Beehives](/wiki/Beehive "Beehive") in a single shot, which does not give a chance for the [Bees](/wiki/Bees "Bees") to defend their home.
* [Elephant Cacti](/wiki/Elephant_Cactus "Elephant Cactus") take two hits from a Harpoon to destroy. With 10 uses, each Harpoon allows gathering 5 Elephant Cacti. Although there are much cheaper alternatives (such as burning rope next to the cacti or dodging the AOE needles, attacking with melee while wearing a log suit that can be crafted into a cactus armor when durability gets low, etc.) this is a quick and easy way to collecting Elephant Cacti.

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* The Harpoon can be seen stabbed in the [White Whale](/wiki/White_Whale "White Whale")'s head, which may be a reference to the novel *[Moby-Dick](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Moby-Dick "wikipedia:Moby-Dick")*.
