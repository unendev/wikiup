The **Gestalt** is a [Mob](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")* and introduced in *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")*. They are found on the [Lunar Island](/wiki/Lunar_Island "Lunar Island"), [Lunar Grotto](/wiki/Lunar_Grotto "Lunar Grotto"), and are summoned by the [Celestial Champion](/wiki/Celestial_Champion "Celestial Champion") during its first and third stages. They are also found wandering in [Moonstorms](/wiki/Moonstorm "Moonstorm").

They appear when the player has more than 25% [Enlightenment](/wiki/Enlightenment "Enlightenment"). Above 50% Enlightenment, they no longer try to avoid the player and will attack if the player is in range. Above 80% Enlightenment, they will follow and attack the player if they are within less than 1.5 tiles.

When approached, they will ball up and shoot in the player's direction. This can be avoided by moving out of the way. Their attacks deal no damage, and will instead increase [Enlightenment](/wiki/Enlightenment "Enlightenment") by 10 and slow down the player for a few seconds. If the player is hit by too many Gestalt in a row, they will fall [asleep](/wiki/Sleeping#Grogginess "Sleeping") for 6 seconds. Gestalts will run away from [Dark Swords](/wiki/Dark_Sword "Dark Sword") and [Night Armor](/wiki/Night_Armor "Night Armor") lying on the ground, as well as from active [Night Lights](/wiki/Night_Light "Night Light"). Gestalts will not attack players who are wearing the [Enlightened Crown](/wiki/Enlightened_Crown "Enlightened Crown").

During the Celestial Champion boss fight, the first and third phase of the fight will summon a ring of Gestalts to attack the player.

The **Greater Gestalt** are mobs exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*. They appear in the [Lunar Grotto](/wiki/Lunar_Grotto "Lunar Grotto") after the [Archive Switch](/wiki/Archive_Switch "Archive Switch") within the [Ancient Archive](/wiki/Ancient_Archive "Ancient Archive") has been activated, and are summoned to aid the [Celestial Champion](/wiki/Celestial_Champion "Celestial Champion") during its second and third stage.

They will attack [Shadow Creatures](/wiki/Shadow_Creature "Shadow Creature") that invade into the Lunar Grotto and also attack the player if they have shadow gear like the [Dark Sword](/wiki/Dark_Sword "Dark Sword"), [Night Armor](/wiki/Night_Armor "Night Armor") or a fueled [Alarming Clock](/wiki/Alarming_Clock "Alarming Clock") equipped. If a player has a shadow item equipped, but is also wearing an [Enlightened Crown](/wiki/Enlightened_Crown "Enlightened Crown"), the Greater Gestalt will continue to ignore them. Their attacks deal 180 damage per hit, capable of one hit killing most characters without armor. Shadow Creatures killed by Greater Gestalt drop no [Nightmare Fuel](/wiki/Nightmare_Fuel "Nightmare Fuel").

They also attack both variants of the [Reanimated Skeleton](/wiki/Reanimated_Skeleton "Reanimated Skeleton"), [Ancient Fuelweaver](/wiki/Ancient_Fuelweaver "Ancient Fuelweaver"), [Woven Shadows](/wiki/Woven_Shadow "Woven Shadow"), [Maxwell](/wiki/Maxwell/Character "Maxwell/Character")'s [Shadow Puppets](/wiki/Shadow_Puppet "Shadow Puppet"), and [Shadow Splumonkeys](/wiki/Shadow_Splumonkey "Shadow Splumonkey"). They don't attack the [Shadow Pieces](/wiki/Shadow_Pieces "Shadow Pieces") though. Lit [Night Lights](/wiki/Night_Light "Night Light") will also be extinguished.

The Celestial Champion will launch waves of Greater Gestalts as projectile to attack players during phase 2 and 3, dealing 135 damage in phase 2 and 180 damage in phase 3. The [Enlightened Crown](/wiki/Enlightened_Crown "Enlightened Crown") will spawn small Greater Gestalts when the players attack a target and the player's enlightenment is above 85%.

## PlaceholderTrivia[]

* Gestalts were introduced in the *[Turn of Tides](/wiki/Return_of_Them#Turn_of_Tides "Return of Them")* update.
* The Greater Gestalt was introduced in the *[Forgotten Knowledge](/wiki/Return_of_Them#Forgotten_Knowledge "Return of Them")* update.
* Gestalt is a German and Swedish word for "figure" or "form". It's also a psychological term used to describe perceiving order in a chaotic world, usually by grouping objects together or connecting them.
* [Wigfrid](/wiki/Wigfrid "Wigfrid"), [Wortox](/wiki/Wortox "Wortox"), [Wolfgang](/wiki/Wolfgang "Wolfgang"), [WX-78](/wiki/WX-78 "WX-78") (with the [Super-Hardy Circuit](/wiki/Super-Hardy_Circuit "Super-Hardy Circuit") plugged in), and [Wurt](/wiki/Wurt "Wurt") with the [King of the Merms](/wiki/King_of_the_Merms "King of the Merms") active are the only characters who can survive an unarmored hit from a Greater Gestalt.
