**Bile-Covered Slop** is a [Meat](/wiki/Meats "Meats") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") found in the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC. The [Tiger Shark](/wiki/Tiger_Shark "Tiger Shark") will spit some every now and then near its [Den](/wiki/Sharkitten_Den "Sharkitten Den") to feed [Sharkittens](/wiki/Sharkitten "Sharkitten").

It can be given to [Volcano Altar of Snackrifice](/wiki/Volcano_Altar_of_Snackrifice "Volcano Altar of Snackrifice") to incur its wrath, therefore making [eruptions](/wiki/Volcano_(object) "Volcano (object)") happen sooner during [Dry Season](/wiki/Dry_Season "Dry Season"). There are many easier options for this purpose, however.
