**Lune Tree Blossoms** are [Items](/wiki/Items "Items") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")*. They are obtained by chopping [Lune Trees](/wiki/Lune_Tree "Lune Tree"). Additionally, they can be found naturally on the ground in [Lunar Forest](/wiki/Lunar_Forest "Lunar Forest").

Lune Tree Blossoms can be eaten to restore 1 Health and are required to craft the [Bath Bomb](/wiki/Bath_Bomb "Bath Bomb").
