The **Sharkitten** is a passive [Mob](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs") found in the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC. It spawns from the [Sharkitten Den](/wiki/Sharkitten_Den "Sharkitten Den") during [Monsoon Season](/wiki/Seasons/Monsoon "Seasons/Monsoon"). If the Sharkitten Den is approached by the player, the [Tiger Shark](/wiki/Tiger_Shark "Tiger Shark") will return to its location to defend its Sharkittens. A Sharkitten drops 2-3 [Raw Fish](/wiki/Raw_Fish "Raw Fish"), one [Shark Fin](/wiki/Shark_Fin "Shark Fin") and 0-2 [Shark Gills](/wiki/Shark_Gills "Shark Gills") when killed. Killing one will increase the player's [naughtiness](/wiki/Naughtiness "Naughtiness") by 4%.

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* Sharkittens share the same base model as [Hounds](/wiki/Hounds "Hounds").
* Sharkittens use higher pitch versions of the [Tiger Shark](/wiki/Tiger_Shark "Tiger Shark")'s sounds.
* While it's implied, in-game Sharkittens do not actually grow into adult Tiger Sharks.
* Since the *Home Sea Home* update, Sharkittens now drop [Shark Fins](/wiki/Shark_Fin "Shark Fin") upon death. This was implemented to compensate for [Sea Hounds](/wiki/Sea_Hound "Sea Hound") becoming rarer.
* Sharkittens despawn when entering the Sharkitten Den. If [Walls](/wiki/Wall "Wall") are placed around the Den, Sharkittens cannot return to it and therefore will not despawn. They can also be seperated from their Den using the [Telelocator Staff](/wiki/Telelocator_Staff "Telelocator Staff"). If unable to reach their Den, they will remain year-round.
* The Sharkitten's name is a portmanteau of the words "shark" and "kitten".

## Mosquito Bugs[]

* [Abigail](/wiki/Abigail "Abigail") will target and attack the Sharkittens on her own, without Wendy attacking them first.
