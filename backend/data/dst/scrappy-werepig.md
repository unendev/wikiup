The **Scrappy Werepig** is a neutral [Boss Monster](/wiki/Boss_Monster "Boss Monster") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in the *[From Beyond](/wiki/From_Beyond "From Beyond")* updates. After defeating its [nightmare form](/wiki/Nightmare_Werepig "Nightmare Werepig"), the Werepig will appear in 10 days inside of the [Teetering Junk Piles](/wiki/Teetering_Junk_Pile "Teetering Junk Pile") in the [Junk Yard](/wiki/Junk_Yard "Junk Yard"). Rummaging these three times will free the Scrappy Werepig.

The Scrappy Werepig has 20% protection from the armor it is wearing.

## Behavior[]

After it is freed, the Scrappy Werepig is neutral and will wander around the Junk Yard. But as soon as it gets attacked (even from another [Creature](/wiki/Creatures "Creatures")), or the player rummages in the tiny junk piles around the set piece 4 times, it will immediately become aggressive and start fighting. It will run to the Teetering Junk Pile and rummage through it until it finds a weapon. It will launch 3 attacks before the weapon breaks, and will run back to the Teetering Junk Pile to get a new weapon. When the player is running out of the Junk Yard, the Scrappy Werepig will start throwing balls of junk, which deal 30 damage.

## Tips[]

* When one of the Scrappy Werepig's weapons break, you should attack the Werepig when he's looking for another weapon to get easy damage.
* Try to not hit the Werepig too many times when he has the Laser Cannon, since it deals some whopping 240 damage, easily breaking a fourth or even a third part of some armors, instead, get a few hits and run away, so the damage is not that punishing (the cannon only deals 60 damage in long distances).
* In post-game, you should use Brightshade or Void kits against the Scrappy Werepig, to deal extra planar damage and pierce through his armor.
* Stational bosses can be really good to deal damage to the Werepig, leaving the Werepig really weak, tremendously reducing the risk of dying and the time the fight takes.
* If you are trying to get the W.A.R.B.I.S kit, the Scrappy Werepig is a really good way to get them, since he is guaranteed to drop 1 blueprint of the [W.A.R.B.I.S. Head Gear](/wiki/W.A.R.B.I.S._Head_Gear "W.A.R.B.I.S. Head Gear") or the [W.A.R.B.I.S. Armor](/wiki/W.A.R.B.I.S._Armor "W.A.R.B.I.S. Armor")

## Trivia[]

* The Scrappy Werepig was introduced in the *[Scrappy Scavengers](/wiki/Scrappy_Scavengers "Scrappy Scavengers")* update.
* The Scrappy Werepig spawn time is not countered in days, rather in real time, so if one tries to force spawn him by skipping days via mods or the console, it will not spawn the Werepig.
* The laser cannon that the Scrappy Werepig uses in his fight can only appear if the Celestial Champion has been defeated
* Some character quotes insinuate that the Werepig has a really bad smell.
* The Scrappy Werepig can be used to farm junk piles, this is because the Scrappy Chapauldron he drops blocks the damage of the Junk Piles he throws in the fight, easily giving you free loot
