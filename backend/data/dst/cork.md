**Cork** is a resource exclusive to the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC. It can be gathered by chopping down [Claw Palm Trees](/wiki/Tree/Claw_Palm_Tree "Tree/Claw Palm Tree") and is required for several [Crafting](/wiki/Crafting "Crafting") recipes.

Cork can also be used to repair [Wood Walls](/wiki/Wood_Wall "Wood Wall") and to refuel [Cork Candle Hats](/wiki/Cork_Candle_Hat "Cork Candle Hat").
