**Sandy Turf** is one of the [Turf](/wiki/Turf "Turf") [Items](/wiki/Items "Items") that appears in the *[Reign of Giants](/wiki/Reign_of_Giants "Reign of Giants")* DLC, acquired by digging [Desert](/wiki/Desert "Desert") tiles with a [Pitchfork](/wiki/Pitchfork "Pitchfork"). Like other Turfs, it can be placed on any bare ground tile or used as [fuel](/wiki/Fire#Fuel "Fire").

[Lureplants](/wiki/Lureplants "Lureplants") can be planted on these turfs but its [Eyeplants](/wiki/Lureplant#Eyeplant "Lureplant") will be prevented from spawning.

When placed on the [Surface World](/wiki/Surface_World "Surface World") and the area is large enough, Crows are the only [Birds](/wiki/Birds "Birds") that will spawn and land on these turfs.

[Farm Plots](/wiki/Farm_Plots "Farm Plots") can be placed on Sandy Turf, so one does not need to worry about not being able to build farms in a desert.

In SW, [Floods](/wiki/Flooding "Flooding") can spawn on this turf.

## Don't Starve Together icon Don't Starve Together[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, Sandy Turf can be crafted and prototyped with 1 [Rocks](/wiki/Rocks "Rocks") and 1 [Bone Shards](/wiki/Bone_Shards "Bone Shards") in the [Decorations Filter](/wiki/Decorations_Filter "Decorations Filter") at [Terra Firma Tamper](/wiki/Terra_Firma_Tamper "Terra Firma Tamper").
