## Brain Behavior[]

The two walruses walk about during the [daytime](/wiki/Day-Night_Cycle "Day-Night Cycle") and return to their igloo at dusk. They will awake at night and run out ready to fight if the hounds bark. If either walrus is alive, the camp emits a small amount of light at [Night](/wiki/Night "Night"), but is generally too dangerous to serve as protection from [Charlie](/wiki/Charlie "Charlie").

The walruses are hostile towards almost any [Mob](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs"). When MacTusk, becomes hostile, he makes a slashing gesture, he then shoots and pursues the target, while Wee MacTusk commands the Hounds to attack. Both walruses will flee when approached.

Should MacTusk and Wee MacTusk die, the Hounds will attack freely until they respawn. If the Hounds are killed but MacTusk survives, he will come out the next day. Dead members respawn in 2.5 days.

**MacTusk** is an aggressive [Mob](/wiki/Mob "Mob") that spawns from Walrus Camps during Winter. Upon death, he drops 1 [Meat](/wiki/Meat "Meat"), 1 [Blow Dart](/wiki/Blow_Dart "Blow Dart"), 1 [Walrus Tusk](/wiki/Walrus_Tusk "Walrus Tusk") (50% chance), & 1 [Tam o' Shanter](/wiki/Tam_o%27_Shanter "Tam o' Shanter") (25% chance).

He attacks with a Blow Dart (which has a longer range but deals less damage), while trying to keep his distance. One can dodge his darts with correct timing; using a [Walking Cane](/wiki/Walking_Cane "Walking Cane") or [Roads](/wiki/Road "Road") will raise their chances.

### Spear Hunting[]

If MacTusk ever gets more than about 4 tiles away from the player, he will shoot the player with his [Blow Dart](/wiki/Dart "Dart") for 33 damage every couple seconds. MacTusk will run about 4 tiles from the nearest player and begin firing. If he gets about 2-3 screens away from his igloo, he sort of gives up and waddles back to his igloo, slowly and peacefully.

The most straight-forward method to kill [MacTusk](/wiki/MacTusk "MacTusk") is to run between him and his igloo (ignoring the [Blue Hounds](/wiki/Blue_Hounds "Blue Hounds")), then chase him until he gets far enough away and goes passive. Then attack MacTusk with a decent melee weapon until dead. The hounds can be kited normally afterwards. [Wee MacTusk](/wiki/Wee_MacTusk "Wee MacTusk") has no attack and can be ignored, or shot with the blow dart after danger has passed.

Most players will need a hot [Thermal Stone](/wiki/Thermal_Stone "Thermal Stone") for warmth, a [Tentacle Spike](/wiki/Tentacle_Spike "Tentacle Spike") or [Ham Bat](/wiki/Ham_Bat "Ham Bat") for damage, and a [Football Helmet](/wiki/Football_Helmet "Football Helmet") and/or [Log Suit](/wiki/Log_Suit "Log Suit") for protection. One might build a [Campfire](/wiki/Campfire "Campfire") a couple screens away, or simply light a tree on fire and drop a thermal stone at its base before a fight.

### Farming[]

An easy and cheap way to farm McTusk hunting party is placing [Fences](/wiki/Wood_Fence "Wood Fence") around the igloo with a [Gate](/wiki/Wood_Gate "Wood Gate") allowing entrance. The cage should be as small as possible, but big enough for the player to move around the igloo. Once the cage is built, if the player is inside - McTusk will never attack the player because he will never reach the distance sufficient for his blow dart to fire. After killing the ice hounds killing the rest of the party is just coming to them and killing them. as they are walking in place.

A ring of [Tooth Traps](/wiki/Tooth_Trap "Tooth Trap") around the igloo makes killing MacTusk relatively quick and painless, but only when the area is loaded (has a player within about 2 screens). Remember: MacTusk only spawns the first time in winter when the area is unloaded, so the player must wait for winter to start, then move into range. Once the party is killed, reset the traps.

[Spiders](/wiki/Spider "Spider") or [Pengulls](/wiki/Pengull "Pengull") will fight MacTusk and the walruses and eat the meat. [Webber](/wiki/Webber "Webber") can safely get the drops from spiders. [Pigs](/wiki/Pigs "Pigs") will fight the hounds, but not the walruses (they will also eat the meat). Tentacles attack everything. Bee mines can work too.

Any of these methods that don't work completely may distract MacTusk allowing the player to kill him easily as he won't be running away from the player when fighting.

Chester can be used as bait, by placing him between MacTusk and the player.

The party can also be chased or led into other Mobs (e.g. [Merms](/wiki/Merm "Merm"), [Pengulls](/wiki/Pengull "Pengull"), [Tentacles](/wiki/Tentacle "Tentacle") ect.), or into [Tooth Traps](/wiki/Tooth_Traps "Tooth Traps").

**Wee MacTusk** (also known as **WeeTusk**) is an aggressive Mob (with no attack) that spawns from Walrus Camps during Winter. Upon death, he drops 1 Meat (Don't Starve Together). He looks like he's controlling the Blue Hounds, but they behave the same without him.

If MacTusk and the hounds are killed, Wee MacTusk will run back to the Walrus Camp and not emerge until MacTusk respawns.

## Prototype Tips[]

* In the [*Reign of Giants (RoG)*](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Reign_of_Giants "Don't Starve: Reign of Giants") [DLC](/wiki/DLC "DLC"), dispatching two Blue Hounds in quick succession (like with the Chester method above) will freeze the attacker, this can be avoided by waiting out the pale hue before killing the second one.
* One can use a combo of Ice Staff and a weapon on MacTusk.
* Should one come across a dormant Walrus Camp, it would be prudent to set up defenses, such as Tooth Traps.
* It is possible to construct a MacTusk drop farm using at least 4 [Pig Houses](/wiki/Pig_House "Pig House"). [Stone Walls](/wiki/Wall#Stone_Wall "Wall") can be used to keep them closer to each other, allowing easy access to the non-food drops. It also makes basing near the Walrus Camp more beneficial.

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* MacTusk N' Son was introduced in *[A Winter's Tale](/wiki/Version_History#February_26,_2013_-_A_Winter.27s_Tale "Version History")* update.
* MacTusk deals 33 damage with his Blow Dart, whereas the player deals 100 damage with the same weapon. This is because MacTusk uses an identical weapon in the code with 0 damage, which takes his damage output. Without it, he would hurt the player instantly.
* [Wolfgang](/wiki/Wolfgang "Wolfgang") refers to MacTusk as "Sea Cow man!". The [Sea Cow](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/West_Indian_manatee) is a marine mammal unrelated to the [Walrus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Walrus).
* As seen by his quotes, [Maxwell](/wiki/Maxwell "Maxwell") knows MacTusk and perhaps created him.
* Both walruses share the same hunchback model as Pigs, Merms, and [Bunnymen](/wiki/Bunnyman "Bunnyman").
* In the [trailer](https://youtu.be/C61XNqoeFdY?t=67) for *A Winter's Tale*, Wee MacTusk also attacks the player.

## Mosquito Bugs[]

* MacTusk will become passive and return to his camp if chased outside of his wandering range, ignoring enemies.
* In *[Don't Starve](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve "Don't Starve")*, WeeTusk won't drop Meat when killed because the wrong set of brackets is used in his loot table.
* If the Walrus Camp is close to the shore, MacTusk may respawn and walk on the ocean, unable to get back on land. This bug also occurs to other mobs (such as Pigs and [Wildbores](/wiki/Wildbore "Wildbore")) if their housing structure is too close to the ocean.
* When the season changes, the group may be locked out of their camp, and therefore, be active outside of Winter.
