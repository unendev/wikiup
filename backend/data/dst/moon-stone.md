The **Moon Stone** is a naturally spawning object in *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[A New Reign](/wiki/A_New_Reign "A New Reign")*. It can usually be found in its own [Set Piece](/wiki/Set_Piece "Set Piece") in a dense [Evergreen](/wiki/Tree "Tree") forest surrounded by several [Suspicious Moonrocks](/wiki/Suspicious_Moonrock "Suspicious Moonrock"), Moon Rubble, and [Fireflies](/wiki/Fireflies "Fireflies"). It can be damaged, but not completely destroyed, and is not flammable.

After the Moon Stone has been repaired with a [Moon Rock](/wiki/Moon_Rock "Moon Rock"), it can be used to turn a [Star Caller's Staff](/wiki/Star_Caller%27s_Staff "Star Caller's Staff") into a [Moon Caller's Staff](/wiki/Moon_Caller%27s_Staff "Moon Caller's Staff"). At [Full Moon](/wiki/Moon_Cycle "Moon Cycle"), a beam of light shoots out from the Staff and begins the transformation. [Hounds](/wiki/Hounds "Hounds") and [Werepigs](/wiki/Pig/Werepig "Pig/Werepig") that are within 30 units of the Moon Stone will attack the Moon Stone, interrupting it if they land six attacks on the platform. Additional [Hounds](/wiki/Hounds "Hounds") and [Werepigs](/wiki/Pig/Werepig "Pig/Werepig") will spawn, and they will also attack the Moon Stone, damaging it. When the process ends, either by the Moon Stone becoming fully damaged, successfully transforming the Staff or a player removing the staff mid-transformation, all remaining monsters will turn into Suspicious Moonrocks. A successful transformation will take 50 seconds (2 day segments, with a 10 second initial delay) to complete, making it possible to transform several to no staves, depending on the night's length.

**Moon Rubble** appears scattered around the Moon Stone set piece and is formed out of petrified [Flowers](/wiki/Flower "Flower"), [Birds](/wiki/Birds "Birds"), [Spiders](/wiki/Spider "Spider") and [Rabbits](/wiki/Rabbit "Rabbit"), all in different states of crumbling. It can be mined with a [Pickaxe](/wiki/Pickaxe "Pickaxe"), destroying it immediately and yielding nothing.

## Tips[]

* A Moon Stone with a Moon Caller's Staff (either transformed or later given by a player) will emit light and drain heat around itself, therefore acting as a permanent stationary Polar Light. This does not consume the staff's durability, making the Moon Stone a good location for a summer base.
* The transformation can be carried out without combat if access to the stone is blocked with walls thick enough to withstand the onslaught, and/or densely place-able objects (e.g. [Mushroom Lights](/wiki/Mushroom_Lights "Mushroom Lights") or [End Tables](/wiki/Stagehand/End_Table "Stagehand/End Table")), carry-able obstacles ([Antlion](/wiki/Antlion "Antlion")'s cave-in boulders, [Suspicious Marbles](/wiki/Suspicious_Marble "Suspicious Marble"), or [Sculptures](/wiki/Chess_Pieces "Chess Pieces")), or a combination of cheap structures (eg. a [Sign](/wiki/Sign "Sign")) and [Walls](/wiki/Wall "Wall").
  + With a high number of mobs walking into the obstacles, one or more may eventually be pushed through.
  + Walls, statues, etc., only block mobs while a player is watching. This event will not complete on its own, no matter what fortifications are built.
* Removing the Star Caller's Staff before the transformation completes allows farming Moon Rocks without consuming the staff. Since the moon event draws in (and then petrifies) any nearby pig or hound, players may increase yield by trapping [Vargs](/wiki/Varg "Varg") or building a high number of pig houses near the Moon Stone.
