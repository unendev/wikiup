The **Royal Tapestry** is a [Structure](/wiki/Structure "Structure") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*. It is obtained by upgrading a DIY Royalty Kit with 20 [Kelp Fronds](/wiki/Kelp_Fronds "Kelp Fronds"), 10 [Pig Skins](/wiki/Pig_Skin "Pig Skin") and 15 [Beefalo Wools](/wiki/Beefalo_Wool "Beefalo Wool").

A normal [Merm](/wiki/Merm "Merm") passing close to the Royal Tapestry will sit on it. The player has to feed the [Merm](/wiki/Merm "Merm") with a total of 50 Hunger to obtain a [King of the Merms](/wiki/King_of_the_Merms "King of the Merms").

The **DIY Royalty Kit** is a [Structure](/wiki/Structure "Structure") exclusive to [Wurt](/wiki/Wurt "Wurt") in *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*. The structure can be upgraded to a Royal Tapestry.
