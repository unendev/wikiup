The **Peg Leg** is a [Weapon](/wiki/Weapon "Weapon") exclusive to the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC. Like the [Spear](/wiki/Spear "Spear"), it deals 34 damage, but has only 50 uses. Peg Legs can be found in [X Marks the Spot](/wiki/X_Marks_the_Spot "X Marks the Spot") chests.

## Tips[]

* In the early days and if playing as Woodlegs while looting the first treasure chests, the player can use the Peg Leg as a temporary weapon until they find or craft a better alternative.
