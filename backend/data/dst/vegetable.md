**“**What seeds shall I sow?**”**

–[Wendy](/wiki/Wendy "Wendy")

There are 12 basic **Vegetable** [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") items in-game — eight with a [cooked](/wiki/Cooked "Cooked") version, and then [Petals](/wiki/Petals "Petals"), [Foliage](/wiki/Foliage "Foliage"), [Lichen](/wiki/Lichen "Lichen") and [Glow Berries](/wiki/Glow_Berry "Glow Berry"), which are also counted as vegetables. There are two additional Vegetables in the *[Reign of Giants](/wiki/Reign_of_Giants "Reign of Giants")* DLC, two additional Vegetables in the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Shipwrecked "Shipwrecked")* DLC, three additional Vegetables in the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Hamlet "Hamlet")* DLC, and ten additional Vegetables in *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*.

There are 11 [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") recipes that require Vegetables. There are three additional recipes in *Reign of Giants*, three additional recipes in *Shipwrecked*, four additional recipes in *Hamlet*, and four additional recipes in *Don't Starve Together*. [Warly's](/wiki/Warly "Warly") [Portable Crock Pot](/wiki/Portable_Crock_Pot "Portable Crock Pot") also has nine recipes that require Vegetables: three in *Shipwrecked* and six in *Don't Starve Together*. Crock Pot recipes differentiate ingredients between [Fruits](/wiki/Fruits "Fruits") and Vegetables. Petals, Foliage, [Succulents](/wiki/Petals#Succulent "Petals"), [Cooked Mandrakes](/wiki/Cooked_Mandrake "Cooked Mandrake"), Glow Berries cannot be used in a Crock Pot. The cooked version of a vegetable almost always [rots](/wiki/Food#Perish_Times "Food") faster than the non-cooked version, except for [Mushrooms](/wiki/Mushrooms "Mushrooms"), [Corn](/wiki/Corn "Corn"), and [Seaweed](/wiki/Seaweed "Seaweed"). All farmable vegetables last the same amount of time when raw.

[Corn](/wiki/Corn "Corn"), [Pumpkin](/wiki/Pumpkin "Pumpkin"), [Eggplant](/wiki/Eggplant "Eggplant"), [Toma Roots](/wiki/Toma_Root "Toma Root"), [Potatoes](/wiki/Potato "Potato"), [Peppers](/wiki/Pepper "Pepper"), [Onions](/wiki/Onion "Onion"), and [Garlic](/wiki/Garlic "Garlic") can only be found through [Farming](/wiki/Farming "Farming") by planting [Seeds](/wiki/Seeds "Seeds"), while the rest of the Vegetables can be found throughout the world. [Carrots](/wiki/Carrot "Carrot"), [Sweet Potatoes](/wiki/Sweet_Potato "Sweet Potato"), [Aloe](/wiki/Aloe "Aloe"), [Asparagus](/wiki/Asparagus "Asparagus"), and [Radishes](/wiki/Radish "Radish") can also be farmed.
