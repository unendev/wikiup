**Sammy, the Lucid Bog Merm** is a [Mob](/wiki/Mob "Mob") exclusive to the [The Gorge](/wiki/The_Gorge "The Gorge") event in *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*. He can be found near the starting area, selling various seeds.

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* According to [Billy](/wiki/Billy "Billy"), Sammy used to be a goat too before he was affected by the plague. The effects of the plague can also be seen by the scales on the [Swamp Pig Elder's](/wiki/Swamp_Pig_Elder "Swamp Pig Elder") hand and [Mumsy's](/wiki/Mumsy "Mumsy") bottom half.
* Sammy states he's been craving fish, but when [Wurt](/wiki/Wurt "Wurt") was introduced into the game [merms](/wiki/Merm "Merm") were established to be herbivores that keep fish as pets
