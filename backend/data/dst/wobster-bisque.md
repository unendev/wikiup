**Wobster Bisque** is a [Meat](/wiki/Meats "Meats") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") introduced in the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC. It is cooked in the [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") by combining a [Wobster](/wiki/Wobster "Wobster") and [Ice](/wiki/Ice "Ice"). Since the Crock Pot will not accept [Dead / Delicious Wobster](/wiki/Wobster#Dead_Wobster_/_Delicious_Wobster "Wobster"), a living Wobster must be used. It takes 10 seconds to cook and 10 [Days](/wiki/Day-Night_Cycle "Day-Night Cycle") to [Spoil](/wiki/Spoil "Spoil").

When consumed, it restores 60 Health, 25 Hunger and 10 Sanity. The [Yaarctopus](/wiki/Yaarctopus "Yaarctopus") will award the player with a [Pirate Hat](/wiki/Pirate_Hat "Pirate Hat") and 1 [Dubloons](/wiki/Dubloons "Dubloons") in exchange for Wobster Bisque.

### Don't Starve Together Don't Starve Together[]

Wobster Bisque was introduced to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")* in the *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")* updates.

The dish is the same as the *Shipwrecked* version, except for it being renamed to **Lobster Bisque** and having a different appearance. Woodie's quote for the dish is changed as his original quote referred to the setting of *Shipwrecked.*

## Trivia[]

* Bisque is a smooth and creamy soup that is French in origin, and is typically made from lobster, crab, shrimp, or crayfish. Bisque is also highly seasoned.
* Wobster Bisque was introduced to *Don't Starve Together* as Lobster Bisque in the *[She Sells Sea Shells](/wiki/Return_of_Them#She_Sells_Sea_Shells "Return of Them")* update.
* Despite Winona's examine text for Lobster Bisque, it isn't a Warly-exclusive food.
