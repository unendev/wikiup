The **Moonrock Pengull** is a hostile [Pengull](/wiki/Pengull "Pengull") variant exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in the *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")* updates. It is found on the [Lunar Island](/wiki/Lunar_Island "Lunar Island") during [Winter](/wiki/Winter "Winter") when a player walks near the [Ocean](/wiki/Ocean "Ocean").

Being especially grotesque, these moon mutated Pengulls will drain [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity") when near and attack other [Mobs](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs") similarly to [Hounds](/wiki/Hound "Hound"). This excludes other Pengulls or Moonrock Pengulls.

Just like normal Pengulls, they have a hunger bar that lasts for 12 days. They will take damage from starvation and will eventually die. The Moonrock Pengull drops [Ice](/wiki/Ice "Ice") and [Monster Meat](/wiki/Monster_Meat "Monster Meat") upon being killed.

## Trivia[]

* Moonrock Pengulls were introduced in the *[Turn of Tides](/wiki/Return_of_Them#Turn_of_Tides "Return of Them")* update.
* Due to its appearance and its drops, it can be inferred that its flesh has been replaced with ice.
