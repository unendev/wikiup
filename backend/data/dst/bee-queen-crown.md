The **Bee Queen Crown** is a head slot [Armor](/wiki/Armor "Armor") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[A New Reign](/wiki/A_New_Reign "A New Reign")*. It is obtained by killing the [Bee Queen](/wiki/Bee_Queen "Bee Queen") or from [Gifts](/wiki/Winter%27s_Feast "Winter's Feast") dropped by the [Loot Stash](/wiki/Loot_Stash "Loot Stash"). The Bee Queen Crown has 945 durability and absorbs 70% of physical damage. It also provides 20% [Wetness](/wiki/Wetness "Wetness") resistance.

While worn, it converts any [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity") drain auras into half as much sanity gain (for example if player is affected by -400/min sanity aura, then wearing the crown will convert it to +200/min aura). It should be noted that it *only* grants sanity from insanity *auras*. This means that no sanity is gained from items with a passive sanity drain, sanity reducing food sources, and [Charlie](/wiki/Charlie "Charlie"). Additionally, the Bee Queen Crown will trigger the Bow Emote on all nearby players when idle.

As of the October QoL update, it can be deconstructed using a [Deconstruction Staff](/wiki/Deconstruction_Staff "Deconstruction Staff") for 4 [Honeycombs](/wiki/Honeycomb "Honeycomb"), 2 [Royal Jelly](/wiki/Royal_Jelly "Royal Jelly"), and 3 [Honey](/wiki/Honey "Honey"). It will also spawn 4 [Bees](/wiki/Bee "Bee").

## Prototype Tips[]

* Certain creatures and structures have insanity auras and are or can be passive to the player. Easy and safe ways to perpetually gain sanity include standing near pacified [Tree Guards](/wiki/Tree_Guard "Tree Guard"), on top of [Evil Flowers](/wiki/Evil_Flowers "Evil Flowers"), near a [Houndius Shootius](/wiki/Houndius_Shootius "Houndius Shootius"), or near passive [Beardlords](/wiki/Beardlords "Beardlords") or [Bearger](/wiki/Bearger "Bearger").
