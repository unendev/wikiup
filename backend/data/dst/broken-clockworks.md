**Broken Clockworks** are [naturally spawning](/wiki/Category:Naturally_Spawning_Objects "Category:Naturally Spawning Objects") [Structures](/wiki/Structures "Structures") that appear in the [Ruins](/wiki/Ruins "Ruins"). The player can use 3 [Gears](/wiki/Gears "Gears") to repair the Clockworks and spawn an allied chess piece, or destroy it with a [Hammer](/wiki/Hammer "Hammer").

In either case, a pile will drop the following loot:

* [Frazzled Wires](/wiki/Frazzled_Wires "Frazzled Wires") (1 + 55% chance of 2nd one)
* [Gears](/wiki/Gears "Gears") (25% + 10% of 2nd if the pile was repaired)
* [Melty Marbles](/wiki/Melty_Marbles "Melty Marbles") (25%)
* [Gems](/wiki/Gems "Gems") (5% for each of [Red](/wiki/Red_Gem "Red Gem"), [Purple](/wiki/Purple_Gem "Purple Gem"), [Yellow](/wiki/Yellow_Gem "Yellow Gem"), [Orange](/wiki/Orange_Gem "Orange Gem") and [Green](/wiki/Green_Gem "Green Gem"). Rolled separately)
* [Thulecite](/wiki/Thulecite "Thulecite") (1% + 5% of 2nd if repaired)

Repairing will cause two rolls of the loot table, doubling the loot gained overall in exchange for the gears spent.

Taking into account the gear drops, the cost to repair each clockwork pile is 2.65 gears.

When hammered, there also exists a 10% chance that [Lightning](/wiki/Lightning "Lightning") will strike and spawn a hostile [Damaged Knight](/wiki/Damaged_Knight "Damaged Knight"), [Damaged Rook](/wiki/Damaged_Rook "Damaged Rook"), or [Damaged Bishop](/wiki/Damaged_Bishop "Damaged Bishop").

There are three different types of Broken Clockworks which spawn different types of clockworks with different probabilities, as indicated by the table below.

| Type | Damaged Knight | Damaged Bishop | Damaged Rook |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Type 1 | 50% | 50% | 0% |
| Type 2 | 70% | 0% | 30% |
| Type 3 | 0% | 70% | 30% |

## Don't Starve Together Don't Starve Together[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, damaged clockworks have slightly modified loot drops:

* [Frazzled Wires](/wiki/Frazzled_Wires "Frazzled Wires") (1 + 55% chance of 2nd one)
* [Gears](/wiki/Gears "Gears") (25% + 10% of 2nd if the pile was repaired)
* [Melty Marbles](/wiki/Melty_Marbles "Melty Marbles") (25%)
* [Gems](/wiki/Gems "Gems") (**25%** [Red](/wiki/Red_Gem "Red Gem"), 5% for each of [Purple](/wiki/Purple_Gem "Purple Gem"), [Yellow](/wiki/Yellow_Gem "Yellow Gem"), [Orange](/wiki/Orange_Gem "Orange Gem") and [Green](/wiki/Green_Gem "Green Gem"). Rolled separately)
* [Thulecite](/wiki/Thulecite "Thulecite") (**1%**)

Allied clockworks will follow player to cave exit, but become hostile after some time .

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* Repaired Clockwork Monsters will be allied, although they will turn hostile if struck with [Lightning](/wiki/Lightning "Lightning").
* Each type of Broken Clockworks is made up of jumbled-up parts of the chess pieces that type can spawn:
  + Type 1 contains bishop's robe and head, and knight's head and bottom half
  + Type 2 contains rook's lower jaw and horn, and knight's head.
  + Type 3 contains bishop's robe and head, and rook's horn and leg.
* In the singleplayer version, game code actually conditions the [Thulecite](/wiki/Thulecite "Thulecite") loot on location - it's only dropped in [Ruins](/wiki/Ruins "Ruins").

## Mosquito Bugs[]

* Allied chess pieces spawned from Broken Clockworks are hostile towards [Chester](/wiki/Chester "Chester").
* In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together"),* the pieces are hostile towards [Hutch](/wiki/Hutch "Hutch") and other players.
