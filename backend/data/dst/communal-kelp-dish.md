The **Communal Kelp Dish** is a character-specific [structure](/wiki/Structure "Structure") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in the *[Skill Spotlight Updates](/wiki/Skill_Spotlight_Update "Skill Spotlight Update")*. It can only be crafted by [Wurt](/wiki/Wurt "Wurt") with 2 [Boards](/wiki/Boards "Boards") and 2 [Cut Reeds](/wiki/Cut_Reeds "Cut Reeds") after mastering the [Community Feeder I](/wiki/Community_Feeder_I "Community Feeder I") skill. The Communal Kelp Dish can only be build on [Marsh Turf](/wiki/Marsh_Turf "Marsh Turf").

### Usage[]

A Communal Kelp Dish increases the respawn rate of Merms for all [Craftsmerm Houses](/wiki/Craftsmerm_House "Craftsmerm House") and [Merm Flort-ifications](/wiki/Merm_Flort-ifications "Merm Flort-ifications") within a 7[m](/wiki/M "M") radius depending on the amount of [Kelp Fronds](/wiki/Kelp_Fronds "Kelp Fronds"). Up to 4 Kelp Fronds can be placed in it and each of them will make Merms respawn faster by 8% up to a maximum of 32%. Wurt can interact with the Communal Kelp Dish to gather all nearby Merms. Each Merm will eat 1 Kelp Fronds from it and start following Wurt.

The Communal Kelp Dish can only be filled by [Wurt](/wiki/Wurt "Wurt") or players wearing a [Clever Disguise](/wiki/Clever_Disguise "Clever Disguise").

The **Superior Communal Kelp Dish** is a character-specific [structure](/wiki/Structure "Structure") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in the *[Skill Spotlight Updates](/wiki/Skill_Spotlight_Update "Skill Spotlight Update")*. It can only be crafted by [Wurt](/wiki/Wurt "Wurt") with 3 [Boards](/wiki/Boards "Boards"), 3 [Cut Reeds](/wiki/Cut_Reeds "Cut Reeds"), and 1 [Tentacle Spot](/wiki/Tentacle_Spot "Tentacle Spot") after mastering the [Community Feeder II](/wiki/Community_Feeder_II "Community Feeder II") skill. The Superior Communal Kelp Dish can only be build on [Marsh Turf](/wiki/Marsh_Turf "Marsh Turf").

### Usage[]

A Superior Communal Kelp Dish increases the respawn rate of Merms for all [Craftsmerm Houses](/wiki/Craftsmerm_House "Craftsmerm House") and [Merm Flort-ifications](/wiki/Merm_Flort-ifications "Merm Flort-ifications") within a 7[m](/wiki/M "M") radius depending on the amount of [Kelp Fronds](/wiki/Kelp_Fronds "Kelp Fronds"). Up to 6 Kelp Fronds can be placed in it and each of them will make Merms respawn faster by 8% up to a maximum of 48%. Wurt can interact with the Superior Communal Kelp Dish to gather all nearby Merms. Each Merm will eat 1 Kelp Fronds from it and start following Wurt.

The Superior Communal Kelp Dish can only be filled by [Wurt](/wiki/Wurt "Wurt") or players wearing a [Clever Disguise](/wiki/Clever_Disguise "Clever Disguise").
