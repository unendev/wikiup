**Fishermerms** are passive [Mobs](/wiki/Mob "Mob") found in [Marsh](/wiki/Marsh "Marsh") biomes in the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC. They spawn from [Fishermerm's Huts](/wiki/Fishermerm%27s_Hut "Fishermerm's Hut") in Merm Villages. Unlike [Merms](/wiki/Merm "Merm"), they are passive creatures and will not attack the player even if provoked. They periodically fish out [Tropical Fishes](/wiki/Tropical_Fish "Tropical Fish") at [Tidal Pools](/wiki/Tidal_Pool "Tidal Pool"), though they do not eat what they catch and simply leave it on the ground. When spotting a player they will run away in random directions in the opposite direction of the player.

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* Fishermerm is a portmanteau of "Merm" and "Fisherman".
* The trident they use to fish looks similar to the [Trident](/wiki/Trident "Trident") weapon that can be obtained by trawling with a [Trawl Net](/wiki/Trawl_Net "Trawl Net") in the [Deep Ocean](/wiki/Ocean "Ocean").
* Fishermerm are the only type of Mobs with names and quotes that cannot be befriended.
