**Fishsticks** is a [Meat](/wiki/Meats "Meats") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") cooked in the [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") by combining 1 [Fishes](/wiki/Fishes "Fishes") and exactly 1 [Twig](/wiki/Twig "Twig"). It takes 40 seconds to cook and 10 [Days](/wiki/Day-Night_Cycle "Day-Night Cycle") to [Spoil](/wiki/Spoil "Spoil").

When consumed, it restores 40 Health, 37.5 Hunger and 5 Sanity

Adding more than 1 Twig in will result in [Wet Goop](/wiki/Wet_Goop "Wet Goop"). Adding [Corn](/wiki/Corn "Corn") may result in [Fish Tacos](/wiki/Fish_Tacos "Fish Tacos"). Using 2 [Monster Meat](/wiki/Monster_Meat "Monster Meat") is *acceptable* since [Monster Lasagna](/wiki/Monster_Lasagna "Monster Lasagna") recipe excludes Twigs. In the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Shipwrecked "Shipwrecked")* DLC, adding more than 2.0 units of Fishes may result in [Seafood Gumbo](/wiki/Seafood_Gumbo "Seafood Gumbo") instead.

Fishsticks are particularly good for restoring large chunks of [Health](/wiki/Health "Health") without using any expensive resources.
