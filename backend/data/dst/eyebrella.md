The **Eyebrella** is a [Dress](/wiki/Dress "Dress") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") from the *[Reign of Giants](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Reign_of_Giants "Don't Starve: Reign of Giants")* DLC that equips to the head slot. It requires 1 [Deerclops Eyeball](/wiki/Deerclops_Eyeball "Deerclops Eyeball"), 15 [Twigs](/wiki/Twigs "Twigs"), and 4 [Bone Shards](/wiki/Bone_Shards "Bone Shards") to craft and an [Alchemy Engine](/wiki/Alchemy_Engine "Alchemy Engine") to prototype. A [Sewing Kit](/wiki/Sewing_Kit "Sewing Kit") repairs 55.5% of its durability.

It provides 240 points of protection from [Overheating](/wiki/Overheating "Overheating") and absolute protection from [Rain](/wiki/Rain "Rain") and [Lightning](/wiki/Lightning "Lightning").

The Eyebrella is considered to be an incredibly valuable dress item for both [Spring](/wiki/Spring "Spring") and [Summer](/wiki/Summer "Summer"), having the highest protective stats in game for the effects of both seasons. With careful use, the Eyebrella can be kept in good condition throughout Spring with only a single use of a [Sewing Kit](/wiki/Sewing_Kit "Sewing Kit").

The Eyebrella is the only item aside from the [Rain Coat](/wiki/Rain_Coat "Rain Coat") to provide 100% protection from wetness, which means that a player can protect against the frequent [Rain](/wiki/Rain "Rain") of spring while keeping both the hands and chest slot free.

While playing as [WX-78](/wiki/WX-78 "WX-78"), the Eyebrella will completely negate all rain damage with only one equipment slot required. It is worth noting, however, that the player will be unable to utilize a SYSTEM OVERLOAD while the item is equipped due to the protection from lightning. It is also wise for WX-78 to use the Eyebrella during Summer, as it will provide maximum overheating protection in the head slot without adding wetness and causing damage as some other dress items will.

## Gift Icon Downloadable Content[]

In the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Hamlet "Hamlet")* DLC, the Eyebrella crafting recipe is unavailable, however, a [Blueprint](/wiki/Blueprint "Blueprint") for it can be bought with 300 [Oincs](/wiki/Oinc "Oinc") from [The Tinkerer's Tower](/wiki/The_Tinkerer%27s_Tower "The Tinkerer's Tower"). The Deerclops Eyeball is available at ['The Sty' Oddities Emporium](/wiki/%27The_Sty%27_Oddities_Emporium "'The Sty' Oddities Emporium").

## Prototype Tips[]

* Players can use the [Hostile Flare](/wiki/Hostile_Flare "Hostile Flare") to fight [Deerclops](/wiki/Deerclops "Deerclops") more than once per [Winter](/wiki/Seasons/Winter "Seasons/Winter") season, with the goal to acquire more than one Deerclops Eyeball.

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* The Eyebrella once temporarily replaced the [Deerclops Eyeball](/wiki/Deerclops_Eyeball "Deerclops Eyeball").
* The Eyebrella was originally a handheld umbrella before becoming a hat.
* The word "Eyebrella" is a portmanteau of the words "Eye" and "Umbrella"
