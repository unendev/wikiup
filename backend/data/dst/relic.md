**Relics** (and Broken Relics) are the remains of the [Ancient Civilization](/wiki/Ancient_Civilization "Ancient Civilization") found in the [Ruins](/wiki/Ruins "Ruins").

When destroyed, via attacking or using explosives, like [Gunpowder](/wiki/Gunpowder "Gunpowder"), they can drop various [items](/wiki/Items "Items"). Broken Relics can be repaired with [Rocks](/wiki/Rocks "Rocks") to restore 20 [sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity").

Tables and chairs can drop [Rocks](/wiki/Rocks "Rocks") (80%), [Cut Stone](/wiki/Cut_Stone "Cut Stone") (10%), and [Frazzled Wires](/wiki/Gravedigger_Items "Gravedigger Items") (5%), with a rare chance to drop [Gears](/wiki/Gears "Gears"), [Green Gems](/wiki/Green_Gem "Green Gem"), [Yellow Gems](/wiki/Yellow_Gem "Yellow Gem"), [Orange Gems](/wiki/Orange_Gem "Orange Gem"), or [Nightmare Fuel](/wiki/Nightmare_Fuel "Nightmare Fuel") (0.2% each).

A vase can drop [Trinkets](/wiki/Gravedigger_Items "Gravedigger Items") (10%), with a rare chance to drop [Silk](/wiki/Silk "Silk"), [Sewing Kits](/wiki/Sewing_Kit "Sewing Kit"), [Thulecite](/wiki/Thulecite "Thulecite") or a second [Trinket](/wiki/Gravedigger_Items "Gravedigger Items") (0.9% each). Breaking a Vase can also spawn a [Cave Spider](/wiki/Cave_Spider "Cave Spider"), [Spitter](/wiki/Spitter "Spitter"), or a [Splumonkey](/wiki/Splumonkey "Splumonkey") (0.45% each).

Plates, Bowls, and Dishes drop nothing.

With *[A New Reign](/wiki/A_New_Reign "A New Reign")* update, players can now craft Replica Relics in the [Decorations Filter](/wiki/Decorations_Filter "Decorations Filter").

To obtain the rare [Blueprints](/wiki/Blueprint "Blueprint") required to prototype them, one has to insert a specific combination of items into the [Ancient Chest](/wiki/Ancient_Chest "Ancient Chest") and close it. The items must be placed into the chest in the precise order as shown, and will be consumed in the process. The valid combinations and crafting recipes for the Replica Relics are listed below:

| Replica Relic | Input in [Ancient Chest](/wiki/Ancient_Chest "Ancient Chest") | Crafting Recipe |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Replica Relic Chair *"A weathered-looking chair."* | Thulecite Medallion, Cut Stone, Nightmare Fuel, Purple Gem, Rabbit, Petals | **Cut Stone ×3** |
| Replica Relic Table *"Don't put your elbows on it."* | Thulecite Medallion, Cut Stone, Nightmare Fuel, Purple Gem, Crow, Rabbit | **Cut Stone ×4** |
| Replica Relic Vase *"A faux-vintage vase."* | Thulecite Medallion, Cut Stone, Nightmare Fuel, Red Gem, Butterfly, Petals | **Cut Stone ×2** |
| Replica Relic Plate *"Craft a fancy broken plate."* | Thulecite Medallion, Cut Stone, Nightmare Fuel, Petals, Berries, Carrot | **Cut Stone ×1** |
| Replica Relic Bowl *"A distressed bowl."* | Thulecite Medallion, Cut Stone, Nightmare Fuel, Rabbit, Carrot, Petals | **Cut Stone ×2** |
| Replica Relic Dish *"Don't double dip."* | Thulecite Medallion, Cut Stone, Nightmare Fuel, Carrot, Berries, Petals | **Cut Stone ×1** |

The rare Blueprint for the Replica Relic Chair can also be obtained by killing a [Resting Horror](/wiki/Resting_Horror "Resting Horror") found sitting on a Relic Chair, when the Sanity is below a certain amount.

With *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")* update, Relics can now also be found in [Ancient Archive](/wiki/Ancient_Archive "Ancient Archive") biome.

To obtain all of the recipes, the player needs:

* 6x Cut Stone, Thulecite Medallion, Nightmare Fuel
* 5x Petals
* 3x Carrot, Rabbit
* 2x Purple Gem, Berries,
* 1x Red Gem, Crow
