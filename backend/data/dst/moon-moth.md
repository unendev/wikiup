The **Moon Moth** is a passive [Mob](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in the *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")* updates. They spawn after a [Lune Tree](/wiki/Tree/Lune "Tree/Lune") is chopped down, and behave much like [Butterflies](/wiki/Butterfly "Butterfly").

Upon death they drop one [Moon Moth Wings](/wiki/Moon_Moth_Wings "Moon Moth Wings"). They can also be caught with a [Bug Net](/wiki/Bug_Net "Bug Net") and then planted to produce a Lune Tree sapling, and are the only method of replanting Lune Trees. When flying, they emit enough light to prevent [Charlie](/wiki/Charlie "Charlie") from attacking.

Unlike Butterflies, Moon Moths will starve outside of the inventory after approximately 4 minutes. Moon Moth inside the player's inventory will starve in 2 days unless fed. They can be fed with any [Vegetable](/wiki/Vegetable "Vegetable") that does not decrease health. Feeding a Moon Moth in the inventory with a vegetable that decreases health will add Moon Moth Wings to the player's inventory and will yield the same results of a player selecting "Murder" on the Moon Moth.

## Trivia[]

* Moon Moths were introduced in the *[Turn of Tides](/wiki/Return_of_Them#Turn_of_Tides "Return of Them")* update.
* Is possibly based on various green species of moths in the genus [*Actias*](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Actias) also known as Moon or Luna Moths.
* Strangely, despite supposedly being a Moon Moth’s juvenile form, the [Mothling](/wiki/Mothling "Mothling") is a lot bigger than the Moon Moths.
