**Caviar** is a [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC. It is cooked in the [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") by adding 1 [Vegetable](/wiki/Vegetable "Vegetable") and 1 [Roe](/wiki/Roe "Roe"). Alternatively, 3 [Cooked Roe](/wiki/Roe "Roe") can be used. It takes 40 seconds to cook and 10 [Days](/wiki/Day-Night_Cycle "Day-Night Cycle") to [spoil](/wiki/Spoil "Spoil").

When consumed, it restores 3 Health, 12.5 Hunger, and 33 Sanity.

Caviar cannot be used to trade with the [Yaarctopus](/wiki/Yaarctopus "Yaarctopus").
