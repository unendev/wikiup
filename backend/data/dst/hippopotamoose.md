The **Hippopotamoose** is a neutral [Mob](/wiki/Mob "Mob") exclusive to the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC. It resides in or around the [Lily Pond](/wiki/Lily_Pond "Lily Pond") biome, entering and exiting the water at its leisure. It remains neutral toward the player until attacked.

If one Hippopotamoose is attacked, nearby Hippopotamoose will become hostile and approach the player. They have a close range bite attack, as well as a long-range jumping slam attack which can knock down [Trees](/wiki/Tree "Tree") and uproot [Tall Grass](/wiki/Tall_Grass "Tall Grass"), [Saplings](/wiki/Sapling "Sapling"), bushes and other plants, and destroy walls.

When moving through the water, they cause small [Waves](/wiki/Waves "Waves") to appear, which can make the player [wet](/wiki/Wetness "Wetness") and damage their [Boat](/wiki/Boats "Boats").

Hippopotamoose roam in "pairs" and reproduce periodically (1.5-2 days) as long as at least one remains.

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* Like the [Ancient Guardian](/wiki/Ancient_Guardian "Ancient Guardian"), the Hippopotamoose shares the same animation model as [Clockwork Rook](/wiki/Clockwork_Rook "Clockwork Rook"). Unlike them, the Hippopotamoose does not attack by charging.
* The Hippopotamoose is a combination between a hippopotamus and a moose.
