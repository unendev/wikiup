**Forest Turf** is one of the [Turf](/wiki/Turf "Turf") [items](/wiki/Items "Items"), acquired by digging [Forest](/wiki/Forest "Forest") tiles with a [Pitchfork](/wiki/Pitchfork "Pitchfork"). Like other Turfs, it can be placed on any bare ground tile or used as [fuel](/wiki/Fire#Fuel "Fire").

Plants like [Berry Bushes](/wiki/Berry_Bush "Berry Bush"), [Grass Tufts](/wiki/Grass_Tuft "Grass Tuft"), [Saplings](/wiki/Saplings "Saplings"), [Spiky Bushes](/wiki/Spiky_Bush "Spiky Bush"), [Lureplants](/wiki/Lureplant "Lureplant"), [Pine Cones](/wiki/Pine_Cone "Pine Cone"), and [Birchnuts](/wiki/Birchnut "Birchnut") can be planted on these Turfs. It will also not stop the Lureplants' [Eyeplants](/wiki/Eyeplant "Eyeplant") from spawning.

When placed on the [Surface World](/wiki/Surface_World "Surface World") in a large enough area, [Birds](/wiki/Birds "Birds") like Crows and Redbirds will start to spawn and land on these turfs. During [Winter](/wiki/Winter "Winter"), Redbirds are replaced by Snowbirds.

## Don't Starve Together icon Don't Starve Together[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, Forest Turf can be crafted and prototyped with 1 [Twigs](/wiki/Twigs "Twigs") and 1 [Pine Cone](/wiki/Pine_Cone "Pine Cone") at [Terra Firma Tamper](/wiki/Terra_Firma_Tamper "Terra Firma Tamper") in the [Decorations Filter](/wiki/Decorations_Filter "Decorations Filter").

## Prototype Tips[]

* Players with very low [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity") may not be able to see [Shadow Creatures](/wiki/Shadow_Creature "Shadow Creature") on Forest Turf at dusk or night. This is especially true if one has been digging up many [Graves](/wiki/Grave "Grave"), as they are typically found in Forests.
