The **Cave Cleft** is a naturally spawning [Structure](/wiki/Structure "Structure") exclusive to *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC.

## https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/dont-starve-game/images/8/85/Door %28Ancient Pig Ruins Exit%29 Map Icon.png/revision/latest?cb=20190203175609 Interior[]

Within, only a single room is able to be explored unlike [Ancient Pig Ruins](/wiki/Ancient_Pig_Ruins "Ancient Pig Ruins") and [Mant Hills](/wiki/Mant_Hill "Mant Hill"). The room is dark with the exception of some light near the exit during [Day](/wiki/Day "Day") and [Dusk](/wiki/Dusk "Dusk").

Upon entering a Cave Cleft, the player will find some structures such as [Stalagmites](/wiki/Stalagmite "Stalagmite") and [Unimportant Pillars](/wiki/Unimportant_Pillar "Unimportant Pillar") which can be mined with a [Pickaxe](/wiki/Pickaxe "Pickaxe") or [Opulent Pickaxe](/wiki/Opulent_Pickaxe "Opulent Pickaxe"). One can also find some [Fern](/wiki/Fern "Fern"), [Mushrooms](/wiki/Mushrooms "Mushrooms") on the ground, and sometimes [Flint](/wiki/Flint "Flint"). At the center of the room, a [Spooky Hole](/wiki/Spooky_Hole "Spooky Hole") will be found.

Only one Cave Cleft per world will lead to another area. After the player is flown to the [fourth island](/wiki/Pinacle "Pinacle") by the [BFB](/wiki/BFB "BFB"), the cave entrance from that island will lead to an exit blocked by a [Stone Plug](/wiki/Stone_Plug "Stone Plug"). Mining this Plug will unlock the exit, which leads to a random Cave Cleft on any of the other 4 islands. This entrance does not have to be re-mined in order to travel through it again.

It is possible to find one or more Vampire Bat(s) sleeping inside. They will remain asleep unless intentionally woken up either by attacking it or from being hit with debris from a cave-in.

While inside a Cave Cleft, the character loses no [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity") because of the darkness.

## Mosquito Bugs[]

* Dying in a different world (any of the three DLC realms, as well as [caves](/wiki/Caves "Caves"), [ruins](/wiki/Ruins "Ruins"), and the [volcano](/wiki/Volcano "Volcano")) and respawning from a [Meat Effigy](/wiki/Meat_Effigy "Meat Effigy") or [Magic Flower](/wiki/Magic_Flower "Magic Flower") that was placed inside any [Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet") DLC interior ([Slanty Shanty](/wiki/Slanty_Shanty "Slanty Shanty") etc.) will cause the player to be respawned out in the void of the world holding the respawn structure. It is recommended to use the command c\_warp("*prefab*") to teleport the character back into the world, as walking back in can be problematic for world types such as [Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked").

## PrototypeTips[]

* It is advised to visit these caves in the very early stages of the game to obtain [Gold Nuggets](/wiki/Gold_Nugget "Gold Nugget") to build a Science Machine or [Alchemy Engine](/wiki/Alchemy_Engine "Alchemy Engine").
* Killing sleeping [Vampire Bats](/wiki/Vampire_Bat "Vampire Bat") will reduce the size of upcoming [Vampire Bat](/wiki/Vampire_Bat "Vampire Bat") attack waves.
