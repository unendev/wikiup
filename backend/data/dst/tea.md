**Tea** is a [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") dish introduced in the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Hamlet "Hamlet")* DLC. It requires at least two [Orange Pikos](/wiki/Orange_Piko "Orange Piko") and 1 [Sweetener](/wiki/Sweetener "Sweetener"), and excluding [Meats](/wiki/Meats "Meats"), [Vegetables](/wiki/Vegetable "Vegetable"), [Twigs](/wiki/Twigs "Twigs"), and [Ice](/wiki/Ice "Ice"). Adding ice will instead result in [Iced Tea](/wiki/Iced_Tea "Iced Tea").

Tea restores 3 Health, 12.5 Hunger and 33 Sanity when consumed. It also increases the player's temperature by 15 and their walking [speed](/wiki/Player_Speed "Player Speed") by 2.5 (half as much as [Coffee](/wiki/Coffee "Coffee")) for 2 minutes.

Tea spoils into [Iced Tea](/wiki/Iced_Tea "Iced Tea") instead of [Rot](/wiki/Rot "Rot") after one day.

## Placeholder Trivia[]

https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/dont-starve-game/images/c/c9/Tea %28Pig Fiesta%29.png/revision/latest?cb=20190602194009

* During the [Pig Fiesta](/wiki/Pig_Fiesta "Pig Fiesta") event, Tea takes on a more festive appearance.
* Ironically, putting Tea in an [Ice Box](/wiki/Ice_Box "Ice Box") will slow down its "spoilage", making it take twice as long to turn into Iced Tea.
* The recipe for Tea includes Orange Pikos as a play on words for [orange pekoe](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tea_leaf_grading#Orange_pekoe "wikipedia:Tea leaf grading"), a specific grade of black tea.
* Tea and Iced tea overrides the effect of coffee and each other respectively.
