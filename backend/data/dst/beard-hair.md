**Beard Hair** is a resource mainly acquired by shaving [Wilson](/wiki/Wilson "Wilson")'s [Beard](/wiki/Beard "Beard") with a [Razor](/wiki/Razor "Razor"). It can also be acquired by killing Beardlings and Beardlords, creatures that replace [Rabbits](/wiki/Rabbit "Rabbit") and [Bunnymen](/wiki/Bunnymen "Bunnymen"), respectively, when [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity") drops below 40% (48 for [Willow](/wiki/Willow "Willow"), 40 to 120 for [WX-78](/wiki/WX-78 "WX-78"), 100 for [Wickerbottom](/wiki/Wickerbottom "Wickerbottom"), 60 for [Wes](/wiki/Wes "Wes"), 40 for [Webber](/wiki/Webber "Webber"), 60 for [Wortox](/wiki/Wortox "Wortox"), and 80 for all other characters) and [Shadow Splumonkeys](/wiki/Shadow_Splumonkey "Shadow Splumonkey"), which replace Splumonkeys during the [Nightmare Phase](/wiki/Nightmare_Cycle "Nightmare Cycle").

Beard Hair is used for the construction of [Meat Effigies](/wiki/Meat_Effigy "Meat Effigy") or simply as [Campfire](/wiki/Campfire "Campfire") fuel.

## Gathering[]

When shaving Wilson's beard, the amount of Beard Hair received depends on the beard's length. Shaving will produce 1 Beard Hair after 4 days, 3 after 8, and 9 after 16. Wilson's beard doesn't grow any more after 16 days.

Gathering Beard Hair from Beardlings and Beardlords requires Sanity to drop below 40%. At this point, Beardlings appear and can be caught in [Traps](/wiki/Trap "Trap") like normal Rabbits. Beardlings only drop Beard Hair 20% of the time, requiring that *on average* 20 or so be killed in order to acquire enough for a [Meat Effigy](/wiki/Meat_Effigy "Meat Effigy"). Beardlords always drop 2 Beard Hair upon being killed.

For gathering Beard Hair from [Beardlings](/wiki/Beardlings "Beardlings") ([Rabbits](/wiki/Rabbits "Rabbits")), there are few methods more advisable - and enjoyable - than the combined use of a [Boomerang](/wiki/Boomerang "Boomerang") and a [Nightmare Amulet](/wiki/Nightmare_Amulet "Nightmare Amulet"). By equipping the amulet just before the boomerang deals the fatal blow, and unequipping it immediately after, the loot of a Beardling is guaranteed, and the Nightmare Amulet will take very little durability loss.

Additionally, while in the [Ruins](/wiki/Ruins "Ruins"), the player can wait for the Nightmare Phase around a [Splumonkey Pod](/wiki/Splumonkey_Pod "Splumonkey Pod") to fight the [Shadow Splumonkeys](/wiki/Splumonkeys "Splumonkeys") that spawn. Caution should be taken as they are very dangerous in large numbers and can overwhelm the player. Each Shadow Splumonkey drops 1 Beard Hair upon death.

As Wilson is the only character with a beard that can be shaved to obtain Beard Hair, playing as any other character will require the player to lose sanity and hunt Beardlings and Beardlords to build a Meat Effigy, find the odd [Set Piece](/wiki/Set_Piece "Set Piece") that might have one or two of them, or hunt down [Shadow Splumonkeys](/wiki/Splumonkeys "Splumonkeys").

## Don't Starve Together Don't Starve Together[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")* Beard Hair can be crafted by Wilson if the player has mastered [Transmute Icky II](/wiki/Wilson#Don't_Starve_Together "Wilson").

## Gift Icon Downloadable Content[]

In the *[Reign of Giants](/wiki/Reign_of_Giants "Reign of Giants")* DLC, Beard Hair has a chance to be obtained when harvesting [Tumbleweeds](/wiki/Tumbleweed "Tumbleweed").

In the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Shipwrecked "Shipwrecked")* DLC, Beard Hair is obtained by killing Beardling [Crabbits](/wiki/Crabbit "Crabbit") (20% chance).

In the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC, Beard Hair is required to craft [Beard Hair Rugs](/wiki/Beard_Hair_Rug "Beard Hair Rug"). Webber is also able to craft the [Shamlet Mask](/wiki/Shamlet_Mask "Shamlet Mask") using Beard Hair. It is obtained by killing [Spider Monkeys](/wiki/Spider_Monkey "Spider Monkey") or transforming back from [Werepig](/wiki/Werepig "Werepig") form as [Wilba](/wiki/Wilba "Wilba").
