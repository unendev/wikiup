A **Lesser Glow Berry** is a [Fruit](/wiki/Fruit "Fruit") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") found in *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*. It is obtained by picking a [Mysterious Plant](/wiki/Mysterious_Plant "Mysterious Plant") in the [Caves](/wiki/Caves "Caves"). It takes 4 days for a Lesser Glow Berry to regrow. It restores 3 Health and 12.5 Hunger at the cost of 10 Sanity. The Lesser Glow Berry can be considered to be a weaker variant of the [Glow Berry](/wiki/Glow_Berry "Glow Berry").

When used as fuel for [Moggles](/wiki/Moggles "Moggles"), it will only restore 6.25% durability. A Lesser Glow Berry will also cause any creature that consumes it, including [Bunnymen](/wiki/Bunnymen "Bunnymen"), [Pigs](/wiki/Pig "Pig"), [Splumonkeys](/wiki/Splumonkey "Splumonkey"), [Gobblers](/wiki/Gobbler "Gobbler"), and [Critters](/wiki/Critter "Critter"), to glow. The intensity of the glowing light decreases over time and will extinguish 60 seconds after consumption. If dropped on the ground, it will glow, similar to a [Light Bulb](/wiki/Light_Bulb "Light Bulb"). It emits enough light to prevent an attack from [Charlie](/wiki/Charlie "Charlie").

[Warly](/wiki/Warly "Warly") can use two Lesser Glow Berries to make [Glow Berry Mousse](/wiki/Glow_Berry_Mousse "Glow Berry Mousse") in the [Portable Crock Pot](/wiki/Portable_Crock_Pot "Portable Crock Pot").

## Prototype Tips[]

* A [Rabbit](/wiki/Rabbit "Rabbit") is the only eater of Glow Berries and Lesser Glow Berries which can be put in the player's inventory. Since the glow remains after being put in inventory, a rabbit in inventory allows even [Wigfrid](/wiki/Wigfrid "Wigfrid") players to utilize these berries.

## PlaceholderTrivia[]

* Character quotes for the Lesser Glow Berry are found in the game files of *Don't Starve*, implying that it may have been a planned feature for the original game.
* The spawn code for the Mysterious Plant is `"wormlight_plant"`.
