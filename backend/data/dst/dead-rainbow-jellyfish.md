**Dead Rainbow Jellyfish** is a [Fish](/wiki/Fishes "Fishes") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") introduced in the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC. It can be obtained by murdering [Rainbow Jellyfish](/wiki/Rainbow_Jellyfish "Rainbow Jellyfish"). It can be dried on a [Drying Rack](/wiki/Drying_Rack "Drying Rack") to obtain [Dried Jellyfish](/wiki/Dried_Jellyfish "Dried Jellyfish"). Eating a Dead Rainbow Jellyfish will make the character glow and give off colored light similar to that of a Rainbow Jellyfish. This glowing effect becomes visibly weaker as time goes on, and only lasts around 2 minutes.

## Prototype Tips[]

* During mid [Dry Season](/wiki/Seasons/Dry "Seasons/Dry"), one can eat Dead Rainbow Jellyfish to stay safe from [Charlie](/wiki/Charlie "Charlie") during the whole night, as nights are shorter during that Season.
* Some [Mobs](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs"), such as [Wildbores](/wiki/Wildbore "Wildbore") and [Spiders](/wiki/Spider "Spider"), will also glow after eating a Dead Rainbow Jellyfish.
* With or without the glowing effect, killing and eating Rainbow Jellyfish in the early game is very useful as Rainbow Jellyfish will not sting the player when attacked like normal [Jellyfish](/wiki/Jellyfish "Jellyfish").

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* Rainbow Jellyfish were added to the *Shipwrecked* DLC more than a year after its official completion in the *[Home Sea Home](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked#November_21,_2017_-_Home_Sea_Home_Update "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* update, after being tested in a beta branch for several months.
