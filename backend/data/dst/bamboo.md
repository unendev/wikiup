**Bamboo** is a [renewable](/wiki/Renewable_resources "Renewable resources") resource that can be gathered by hacking down a [Bamboo Patch](/wiki/Bamboo_Patch "Bamboo Patch") with a [Machete](/wiki/Machete "Machete") in the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC. It can also be obtained by hammering a [Wildbore House](/wiki/Wildbore_House "Wildbore House") or a [Wildbore Head](/wiki/Wildbore_Head "Wildbore Head").

It is used in many [Nautical](/wiki/Nautical "Nautical") recipes and can also be refined into [Cloth](/wiki/Cloth "Cloth").

## Hamlet icon Hamlet[]

Bamboo is also included in the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC where it can be bought for 3 [Oincs](/wiki/Oinc "Oinc") at ['The Sty' Oddities Emporium](/wiki/%27The_Sty%27_Oddities_Emporium "'The Sty' Oddities Emporium") in the [Pig City](/wiki/Pig_City "Pig City"). There is also a small chance(around 7%) for Bamboo to be obtained from hammering the ['A Smashing Pot](/wiki/A_Smashing_Pot "A Smashing Pot"). Using a [Shears](/wiki/Shears "Shears") on a Bamboo Patch will result in 2 **Bamboo** instead of the usual one.
