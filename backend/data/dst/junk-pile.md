The **Junk Pile** is a naturally spawning object exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in the *[From Beyond](/wiki/From_Beyond "From Beyond")* updates. It can be found in the [Junk Yard](/wiki/Junk_Yard "Junk Yard") and can also spawn from the junk ball attack of the [Scrappy Werepig](/wiki/Scrappy_Werepig "Scrappy Werepig"). Rummaging a Junk Pile can result in finding resources or [Mobs](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs"). Depending on its stage, a Junk Pile can be rummaged up to three times before it disappears.

The **Teetering Junk Pile** is a group of several Junk Piles found in the [Junk Yard](/wiki/Junk_Yard "Junk Yard") and is acting similar to the default Junk Pile, but as one big object. It can always be rummaged as it does not shrink or disappear, but there is only a chance to find an item every 2 minutes. The [Scrappy Werepig](/wiki/Scrappy_Werepig "Scrappy Werepig") can be found in it after defeating it as the [Nightmare Werepig](/wiki/Nightmare_Werepig "Nightmare Werepig") in the [Caves](/wiki/Caves "Caves"). In a fight with the Werepig, it will rummage through the Teetering Junk Piles to find weapons.

The Teetering Junk Pile cannot be destroyed.
