The **Radish** is a [Vegetable](/wiki/Vegetable "Vegetable") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC. It can most often be found growing naturally in the ground, but these won't respawn after being picked. Radish can also be [Farmed](/wiki/Farming "Farming") by planting [Seeds](/wiki/Seeds "Seeds") in a [Farm](/wiki/Farm "Farm"). When eaten raw, Radish restore 12.5 [Hunger](/wiki/Hunger "Hunger") and 1 [Health](/wiki/Health "Health"). They take 15 days to [spoil](/wiki/Spoil "Spoil") and can be used in a [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot"). Radish are required to make [Spicy Vegetable Stinger](/wiki/Spicy_Vegetable_Stinger "Spicy Vegetable Stinger").
