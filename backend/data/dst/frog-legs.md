**Frog Legs** are a [Meat](/wiki/Meats "Meats") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [item](/wiki/Item "Item") that is dropped by [Frogs](/wiki/Frog "Frog") and [Merms](/wiki/Merm "Merm") when they are killed. They can be used to make a [Froggle Bunwich](/wiki/Froggle_Bunwich "Froggle Bunwich") in the [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") or dried on a [Drying Rack](/wiki/Drying_Rack "Drying Rack") for 1 [day](/wiki/Day "Day") to produce [Small Jerky](/wiki/Small_Jerky "Small Jerky"). They have a [Meat value](/wiki/Crock_Pot#Food_values "Crock Pot") of 0.5.

Massive amount of Frog Legs may be obtained in a [Frog Rain](/wiki/Frog_Rain "Frog Rain"). They can be first converted to [Egg](/wiki/Egg "Egg") through [Birdcage](/wiki/Birdcage "Birdcage") and then passed to [Pig King](/wiki/Pig_King "Pig King") for [Gold Nugget](/wiki/Gold_Nugget "Gold Nugget").

**Cooked Frog Legs** are made by [Cooking](/wiki/Cooking "Cooking") Frog Legs on a [Campfire](/wiki/Campfire "Campfire"), [Fire Pit](/wiki/Fire_Pit "Fire Pit"), or [Star](/wiki/Star_Caller%27s_Staff "Star Caller's Staff"). They restore 1 [Health](/wiki/Health "Health") and do not drain [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity") unlike their raw counterpart. It can also be used to make the Froggle Bunwich, which is cooked in the Crock Pot.

These are commonly used as a food source in the Adventure Mode level, [A Cold Reception](/wiki/Adventure_Mode#A_Cold_Reception "Adventure Mode"), due to the abundance of Frogs that spawn from the frequent [Frog Rains](/wiki/Frog_Rain "Frog Rain").

## Don't Starve Together icon Don't Starve Together[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, the [Toadstool](/wiki/Toadstool "Toadstool") will also drop Frog Legs when killed.

## Prototype Tips[]

* Frog Legs could be a good meat resource for [Wigfrid](/wiki/Wigfrid "Wigfrid") early game, due to her ability to knock out Frogs in 2 hits with her [Battle Spear](/wiki/Battle_Spear "Battle Spear"), and she can recover even if she gets hit, since she starts with a [Battle Helm](/wiki/Battle_Helm "Battle Helm") and gains health as she kills opponents.

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* Despite being a meat item, Frog Legs cannot be traded to the [Pig King](/wiki/Pig_King "Pig King") for [Gold Nuggets](/wiki/Gold_Nugget "Gold Nugget").
  + Eggs gotten from a [Birdcage](/wiki/Birdcage "Birdcage") can be traded, making it possible to indirectly obtain Gold Nuggets with Frog Legs.
* In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, if [Wilson](/wiki/Wilson "Wilson") is equipped with his full Survivor [Skin](/wiki/Skins "Skins"), his portrait will show him eating Cooked Frog Legs.
* Multiple characters state that this item is still twitchy or still jumps. In real life, frog legs are known to do just that, especially when salt is added.
* Humorously, the gigantic boss [Toadstool](/wiki/Toadstool "Toadstool") drops a pair of Frog Legs the same size as those of a Frog or Merm, despite it being much bigger than either of them.
