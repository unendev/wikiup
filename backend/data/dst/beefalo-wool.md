**Beefalo Wool** is an [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") obtained by either killing a [Beefalo](/wiki/Beefalo "Beefalo") or a [Baby Beefalo](/wiki/Baby_Beefalo "Baby Beefalo"), or by shaving a [sleeping](/wiki/Sleeping "Sleeping") Beefalo with a [Razor](/wiki/Razor "Razor") (by clicking them with a Razor held by the mouse cursor). It is used to make a [Saddle](/wiki/Saddle "Saddle"), [Winter Hat](/wiki/Winter_Hat "Winter Hat"), [Beefalo Hat](/wiki/Beefalo_Hat "Beefalo Hat"), [Puffy Vest](/wiki/Puffy_Vest "Puffy Vest"), and [Carpeted Flooring](/wiki/Carpeted_Flooring "Carpeted Flooring").

Beefalo Wool also makes an excellent [Fuel](/wiki/Fuel "Fuel") source for [Campfires](/wiki/Campfire "Campfire") and Fire Pits. A small herd of Beefalo can easily keep a Fire Pit lit for days, and as the wool grows back, this is a good short term solution.

## Downloadable Content[]

In the *[Reign of Giants](/wiki/Reign_of_Giants "Reign of Giants")* DLC, there are alternative methods of obtaining Beefalo Wool; harvesting a [Tumbleweed](/wiki/Tumbleweed "Tumbleweed"), or befriending a [Catcoon](/wiki/Catcoon "Catcoon"), as friendly Catcoons may give Beefalo Wool as a gift.

## Don't Starve Together Don't Starve Together[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, [Willow](/wiki/Willow "Willow") needs Beefalo Wool to craft [Bernie](/wiki/Bernie "Bernie"). It is also required to craft [Spinnerbait Lures](/wiki/Lures "Lures") and to complete the construction of the [Royal Tapestry](/wiki/Royal_Tapestry "Royal Tapestry").

Wilson can transmute 2 [Beard Hair](/wiki/Beard_Hair "Beard Hair") into Beefalo Wool after learning the [Transmute Icky Ⅱ](/wiki/Wilson#Don't_Starve_Together "Wilson") skill.
