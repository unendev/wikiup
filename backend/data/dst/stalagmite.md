A **Stalagmite** is the [Underground](/wiki/Underground "Underground") equivalent of a [Boulder](/wiki/Boulder "Boulder"). It can be mined using either a [Pickaxe](/wiki/Pickaxe "Pickaxe"), [Opulent Pickaxe](/wiki/Opulent_Pickaxe "Opulent Pickaxe"), or [Pick/Axe](/wiki/Pick/Axe "Pick/Axe"). Stalagmites drop various [minerals](/wiki/Category:Minerals "Category:Minerals") when mined.

Both regular and Tall Stalagmites can be found in 3 different states: full, medium, and low. Each type (and state) has a different loot table.

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* Even though a Stalagmite will appear to be reduced to a lower state as it is mined, it will retain the loot table of its starting state.
* The fact that tall stalagmites can drop [Logs](/wiki/Log "Log") suggests they may be [Petrified Trees](/wiki/Petrified_Tree "Petrified Tree").
