The **Machete** is a [tool](/wiki/Tools "Tools") introduced in the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC. It is used to hack [Bamboo Patches](/wiki/Bamboo_Patch "Bamboo Patch"), [Viney Bushes](/wiki/Viney_Bush "Viney Bush"), and [Coconuts](/wiki/Coconut "Coconut"). The Machete also can be used as a weapon, dealing 29.92 damage per swing. It has 100 uses regardless of how it is used. It can be crafted right from the start with 1 [Twig](/wiki/Twig "Twig") and 3 [Flint](/wiki/Flint "Flint").

The **Luxury Machete** is a version of Machete that can be crafted using [Gold Nuggets](/wiki/Gold_Nuggets "Gold Nuggets") instead of Flint, making it easily renewable. It requires 4 Twigs and 2 Gold Nuggets to craft, and an [Alchemy Engine](/wiki/Alchemy_Engine "Alchemy Engine") to prototype. It has 400 uses, 4 times as much as the regular Machete. Similar to Machete, Luxury Machete deals 29.92 damage per swing when used as a weapon.

The **Obsidian Machete** is a Machete that has been upgraded with [Obsidian](/wiki/Obsidian "Obsidian"). It requires 1 Machete, 1 [Dragoon Heart](/wiki/Dragoon_Heart "Dragoon Heart") and 3 Obsidian to craft. It can also be found in [X Marks the Spot](/wiki/X_Marks_the_Spot "X Marks the Spot") treasure chests. Like all items in the [Volcanic Tab](/wiki/Volcanic_Tab "Volcanic Tab"), it cannot be prototyped and the player must be wearing a [Brain of Thought](/wiki/Brain_of_Thought "Brain of Thought") or be near an [Obsidian Workbench](/wiki/Obsidian_Workbench "Obsidian Workbench") to craft it again.

The Obsidian Machete has 250 uses, 2.5x as much as the regular Machete, and works 2.5 times more efficiently than its normal counterpart. Initially, the Obsidian Machete deals 29.92 per swing when used as a weapon, similar to Machete and Luxury Machete. As the Obsidian Machete is used (either as a tool or a weapon), its charge level will increase causing it to deal up to 2x damage based on the number of charges. As the charge level increases, the Obsidian Machete will also produce heat and light, which protects the players from [Charlie](/wiki/Charlie "Charlie"), but can rapidly cause [Overheating](/wiki/Overheating "Overheating") if it is unequipped. After gaining 75 charges, it will start [igniting](/wiki/Fire "Fire") any [Mobs](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs") and [Plants](/wiki/Plants "Plants") struck. The charge level decrease over time as long as the machete isn't used. Obsidian Machete will not gain any charges during [Hurricane Season](/wiki/Hurricane_Season "Hurricane Season"). Similar to [Thermal Stone](/wiki/Thermal_Stone "Thermal Stone"), the machete will start glowing as it gains charges.

Players should take caution when using an Obsidian Machete with high levels of charge, as any plants ignited by the Obsidian Machete can potentially ignite other nearby trees, resources or [Structures](/wiki/Structure "Structure"). When using this item as a weapon, players should remember that the Mobs that are killed while on fire will drop cooked variants of the [food](/wiki/Food "Food") items and [Ashes](/wiki/Ashes "Ashes") for flammable resources they would otherwise drop. Some Mobs will also panic when on fire, running in random directions, potentially igniting other mobs, resources or Structures around them.

## Downloadable Content[]

In the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC, Machetes are also frequently used, and one can be found at the [Crashed Balloon](/wiki/Crashed_Balloon "Crashed Balloon") marking the player's spawn point. They can be used to cut down [Tall Grass](/wiki/Tall_Grass "Tall Grass"), [Tuber Trees](/wiki/Tuber_Tree "Tuber Tree") and vines blocking [Ruinous Entrances](/wiki/Ruinous_Entrance "Ruinous Entrance") and doorways in the [Ancient Pig Ruins](/wiki/Ancient_Pig_Ruins "Ancient Pig Ruins"). Regular Machetes are sold at the [Pigg and Pigglet's General Store](/wiki/Pigg_and_Pigglet%27s_General_Store "Pigg and Pigglet's General Store") for 5 [Oincs](/wiki/Oinc "Oinc"). Luxury Machetes can also be prototyped and crafted, although Obsidian Machetes can not be acquired unless the player brings them over from a *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* world.

## Trivia[]

* A charged Obsidian Machete placed inside [Chester](/wiki/Chester "Chester"), [Packim Baggims](/wiki/Packim_Baggims "Packim Baggims"), or [Ro Bin](/wiki/Ro_Bin "Ro Bin") will cause the follower to glow until the charge fades.
