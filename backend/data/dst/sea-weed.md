The **Sea Weed** is a [plant](/wiki/Plants "Plants")-like [Mob](/wiki/Mob "Mob") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")* introduced in *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")*. They appear in groups of 8 to 15 in the [Rough Ocean](/wiki/Ocean#Don't_Starve_Together "Ocean"). There are 3 species of Sea Weed: Pink, Yellow, and White.

Every 2.5 to 3.5 minutes (1 to 2 minutes for Pink Sea Weeds), they release a pollen cloud. The cloud attracts nearby [Ocean Fishes](/wiki/Ocean_Fishes "Ocean Fishes") similarly to [Fish Food](/wiki/Fish_Food "Fish Food"). If no other fishes are present, one [Spittlefish](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Wiki:Ocean_Fish#Spittlefish "Don't Starve Wiki:Ocean Fish") appears. When killed or caught, the Spittlefish regenerates in 4 minutes (2 minutes for White Sea Weeds).

The base of a Sea Weed grows up to 3 [Barnacles](/wiki/Barnacles "Barnacles"). They can be harvested directly or by using a [Razor](/wiki/Razor "Razor"). The Barnacles regrow in 2.5 to 4 [days](/wiki/Day "Day") each. Upon harvesting, the Sea Weed and all nearby Sea Weeds wake up and attack the player, unless the player is [Wormwood](/wiki/Wormwood "Wormwood") or they used [Razor](/wiki/Razor "Razor") when the Sea Weed is [sleeping](/wiki/Sleeping "Sleeping") (like during the [night](/wiki/Night "Night")).

Sea Weeds attack by launching projectiles in the air. They do 68 damage to the player. If these projectiles miss, they will instead make a leak in the boat. All nearby Sea Weeds will wake up and attack the player if one is attacked or if a Boat is rammed into them too fast. Sea Weeds will not wake up at night, so it is safe to attack one without the others waking.

Upon death, they drop 2 or 3 [Seedshells](/wiki/Seedshell "Seedshell") (3 plus two times a 50% chance for Yellow Sea Weed), unharvested Barnacles, one [Sea Sprout Starter](/wiki/Sea_Sprout_Starter "Sea Sprout Starter"), and a [Sea Stack](/wiki/Sea_Stack "Sea Stack") which can be mined for 2 [Rocks](/wiki/Rocks "Rocks").

A **Sea Sprout** is obtained by planting a [Sea Sprout Starter](/wiki/Sea_Sprout_Starter "Sea Sprout Starter") on a [Sea Stack](/wiki/Sea_Stack "Sea Stack").

A bud appears on the Sea Sprout after 2 days and Barnacles start to grow every 2.5 to 4 days.

A Sea Sprout turns into a Sea Weed during a [Full Moon](/wiki/Full_Moon "Full Moon"), only if there are 3 Barnacles on the base.

## Prototype Tips[]

* Though slow and expensive, a way to safely eliminate Sea Weeds without sustaining damage would be through the use of a [Houndius Shootius](/wiki/Houndius_Shootius "Houndius Shootius")
* Attacking a Sea Weed while all the surrounding ones are sleeping, like during night, is a safe way to kill them. The nearby Sea Weeds will not notice the player attacking other Sea Weeds while they are sleeping.
