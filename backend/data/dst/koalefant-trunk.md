A **Koalefant Trunk** is a [Meat](/wiki/Meats "Meats") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") item dropped by [Koalefants](/wiki/Koalefant "Koalefant"). It can be used to craft a [Breezy Vest](/wiki/Breezy_Vest "Breezy Vest").

Unlike most meats, Koalefant Trunks cannot be used in the [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") or dried on the [Drying Rack](/wiki/Drying_Rack "Drying Rack"); however, they can be fed to a bird in a [Birdcage](/wiki/Birdcage "Birdcage") to produce an [Egg](/wiki/Egg "Egg"). Cooking increases the Health and Hunger benefits of the Trunk as well as more than doubling the spoil time.

A **Koalefant Trunk Steak** is made by cooking a Koalefant Trunk or a [Winter Koalefant Trunk](/wiki/Winter_Koalefant_Trunk "Winter Koalefant Trunk") over a [Campfire](/wiki/Campfire "Campfire"), [Fire Pit](/wiki/Fire_Pit "Fire Pit") or [Star](/wiki/Star_Caller%27s_Staff "Star Caller's Staff").

Although the Koalefant Trunk is a hearty food it may be wiser to make one first into a Breezy Vest for the Winter, due to the rarity of Koalefants. However, if it is near spoiling, it can be eaten to prevent it going to waste.

## Don't Starve Together Don't Starve Together[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, as of the *[Waterlogged](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together/Version_History#Waterlogged_Beta_.28since_July_26.2C_2021.29 "Don't Starve Together/Version History")* update, the Koalefant Trunk can be used in the Crock Pot with a [Meat](/wiki/Meat "Meat") value of one. A Koalefant Trunk can also be obtained from a [Koalefant Carcass](/wiki/Koalefant_Carcass "Koalefant Carcass").
