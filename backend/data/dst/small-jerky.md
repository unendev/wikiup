**Small Jerky** is created by using a [Drying Rack](/wiki/Drying_Rack "Drying Rack") to dry an uncooked [Morsel](/wiki/Morsel "Morsel"), [Fish](/wiki/Fish "Fish"), [Eel](/wiki/Eel "Eel"), [Drumstick](/wiki/Drumstick "Drumstick") or [Frog Legs](/wiki/Frog_Legs "Frog Legs") for 1 [day](/wiki/Day "Day"), or a [Batilisk Wing](/wiki/Batilisk_Wing "Batilisk Wing") for 2 days. The Small Jerky has an extremely low rate of spoilage (taking 20 days) and restores 12.5 [Hunger](/wiki/Hunger "Hunger"), 8 [Health](/wiki/Health "Health") and 10 [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity") when eaten. This makes it an excellent item for keeping sanity high, particularly in [winter](/wiki/Winter "Winter"), if one has a stock of [rabbits](/wiki/Rabbit "Rabbit") or other constant supply of meat.

Jerky can also be used in the [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") in place of meat or morsels with no negative side effects. Thus, 2 [Meat](/wiki/Meat "Meat") and 2 Morsels turned into 2 pieces of [Jerky](/wiki/Jerky "Jerky") and 2 pieces of Small Jerky can be used to make [Meaty Stew](/wiki/Meaty_Stew "Meaty Stew").

Unharvested Small Jerky will not start to spoil as long it's left on the Drying Rack, making it possible to keep it fresh for an indefinite amount of time.

This food can be useful for recovering from the effects of Woodie's Werebeaver transformation, though it is significantly weaker than Jerky, thus requiring a lot more Small Jerky for such a recovery.

## Downloadable Content[]

In the *[Reign of Giants](/wiki/Reign_of_Giants "Reign of Giants")* and *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLCs, Small Jerky will begin to spoil immediately upon creation on a Drying Rack, regardless of whether it is harvested or not.

## Tips[]

* As Small Jerky is not technically cooked, [Wagstaff](/wiki/Wagstaff "Wagstaff") will still suffer a 3 Health penalty from consuming it, bringing the total healing down to 5 Health. This makes Small Jerky inefficient for healing Wagstaff, at least compared to other meals such as [Fishsticks](/wiki/Fishsticks "Fishsticks") or [Honey Ham](/wiki/Honey_Ham "Honey Ham").
