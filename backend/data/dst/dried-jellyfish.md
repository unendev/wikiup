**Dried Jellyfish** is the product of drying [Dead Jellyfish](/wiki/Dead_Jellyfish "Dead Jellyfish") or [Dead Rainbow Jellyfish](/wiki/Dead_Rainbow_Jellyfish "Dead Rainbow Jellyfish") on a [Drying Rack](/wiki/Drying_Rack "Drying Rack") for 1 [Day](/wiki/Day "Day"). Other than having double the spoilage time of [Cooked Jellyfish](/wiki/Cooked_Jellyfish "Cooked Jellyfish"), it has extra hunger and sanity advantages from being dried.
