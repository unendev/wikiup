The **Beefalo Grooming Station** is a craftable [Structure](/wiki/Structure "Structure") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*. It is found in the [Decorations Filter](/wiki/Decorations_Filter "Decorations Filter") and [Beefalo Riding Filter](/wiki/Beefalo_Riding_Filter "Beefalo Riding Filter"), requiring two [Beefalo Wool](/wiki/Beefalo_Wool "Beefalo Wool"), four [Boards](/wiki/Boards "Boards"), and an [Alchemy Engine](/wiki/Alchemy_Engine "Alchemy Engine") to prototype. It allows players to dress up their [bonded](/wiki/Beefalo_Bell "Beefalo Bell") Beefalo with various skins. All Beefalo skins are free, aside from the Selenial and Sanguine skins. The Beefalo has to be hitched to the station first.
