A **Carrot** is a common [Vegetable](/wiki/Vegetable "Vegetable"). It can most often be found growing naturally in the ground, but these won't respawn after being picked. **Carrots** can also be [Farmed](/wiki/Farming "Farming") by planting [Seeds](/wiki/Seeds "Seeds") for a 31.6% chance of growing or using [Carrot Seeds](/wiki/Carrot_Seeds "Carrot Seeds") (Oblong Seeds), and they can be [Cooked](/wiki/Cooking "Cooking") on a [fire](/wiki/Firepit "Firepit") for extra health when eaten (see below) or used as an ingredient in several [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") recipes.

Carrots have a few miscellaneous interactions and uses. Carrots can be given to [Bunnymen](/wiki/Bunnymen "Bunnymen") to befriend them, and [underground](/wiki/Caves "Caves") they can also be obtained by deconstructing [Rabbit Hutches](/wiki/Rabbit_Hutch "Rabbit Hutch") with a [Hammer](/wiki/Hammer "Hammer") or [Deconstruction Staff](/wiki/Deconstruction_Staff "Deconstruction Staff"), yielding five Carrots per Rabbit Hutch (Ten with the staff). Feeding a Carrot to a Bird in a [Birdcage](/wiki/Birdcage "Birdcage") will produce 1–2 [Carrot Seeds](/wiki/Carrot_Seeds "Carrot Seeds"), as well as a 50% chance for 1 regular [Seed](/wiki/Seeds "Seeds"). In [Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together"), you will always get 1 [Carrot Seed](/wiki/Crop_Seeds "Crop Seeds"). Carrots will also lure [Rabbits](/wiki/Rabbit "Rabbit") towards them, whether as bait for [Traps](/wiki/Trap "Trap"), or simply dropped. This can be useful for getting Morsels, as the Rabbits will not run away when eating a **Carrot** dropped on the ground. However, if they are not taken care of, they will consume the entire carrot. [Rabbits](/wiki/Rabbit "Rabbit") will not eat Carrots if they are left planted in the ground.

A **Roasted Carrot** is a Vegetable [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") item made by [Cooking](/wiki/Cooking "Cooking") a Carrot on a [Campfire](/wiki/Campfire "Campfire"), [Fire Pit](/wiki/Fire_Pit "Fire Pit"), or [Star](/wiki/Star_Caller%27s_Staff "Star Caller's Staff"). It restores slightly more [Health](/wiki/Health "Health") than its raw counterpart. It can be used as an ingredient in several Crock Pot recipes.

## Don't Starve Together Don't Starve Together[]

Carrots are renewable in this branch of the game, thanks to [World Regrowth](/wiki/World_Regrowth "World Regrowth"). Once a Carrot has been destroyed (likely eaten), after 20 days, another carrot will attempt to spawn within a 5-tile radius of its original location. If too close to an entity, within 5 tiles of a base structure or wall, if a player is within 16 tiles, or if it is [Winter](/wiki/Winter "Winter") or [Night](/wiki/Night "Night"), it will wait another 20 days.

Feeding a Carrot to a Bird in a [Birdcage](/wiki/Birdcage "Birdcage") will produce 1 [Carrot Seeds](/wiki/Carrot_Seeds "Carrot Seeds").

Carrots can be used with Papyrus to craft a [Cookbook](/wiki/Cookbook "Cookbook").

Carrots can be used to befriend [Bunnymen](/wiki/Bunnyman "Bunnyman") and they can also be used to befriend [Merms](/wiki/Merm "Merm") as [Wurt](/wiki/Wurt "Wurt") or while wearing the [Clever Disguise](/wiki/Clever_Disguise "Clever Disguise").

The **Carrot Plant** is an object exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in the *[Reap What You Sow](/wiki/Reap_What_You_Sow "Reap What You Sow")* update. It can be obtained by planting [Oblong Seeds](/wiki/Oblong_Seeds "Oblong Seeds") (also named Carrot Seeds, if researched with the [Gardeneer Hat](/wiki/Gardeneer_Hat "Gardeneer Hat")) into [Farm Soil](/wiki/Farm_Soil "Farm Soil") or on the ground as [Wormwood](/wiki/Wormwood "Wormwood"). There is a chance to grow a Carrot Plant by planting regular [Seeds](/wiki/Seeds "Seeds").

The plant's favourite seasons are [Autumn](/wiki/Autumn "Autumn"), [Winter](/wiki/Winter "Winter") and [Spring](/wiki/Spring "Spring"), so in [Summer](/wiki/Summer "Summer") it will grow slower and accumulate more [Stress](/wiki/Farm_Plant#Stress "Farm Plant") points. It will consume 0.0075 units of water per second (0.45/min or 3.5/day) and 4 units of [Growth Formula](/wiki/Nutrient "Nutrient") for every growth stage in return of 2 units of manure and compost.

When harvesting in the full stage, it will give 1 Carrot and 0-2 Oblong/Carrot Seeds depending on the amount of stress points the plant accumulated. It can also grow to a [Giant Crop](/wiki/Giant_Crops#Giant_Carrots "Giant Crops"), if the plant accumulated only 0-1 stress points. When picked and [hammered](/wiki/Hammer "Hammer"), it will drop 2-3 Carrots and 2-3 Oblong/Carrot Seeds.

The Carrot stays 4 days on the plant before rotting and the Giant Carrot stays 6 [Days](/wiki/Day "Day") on the plant. If it have been planted by Wormwood, it lasts 50% longer before spoiling. Picking the plant in its rotten stage will give 1 [Rot](/wiki/Rot "Rot") (normal) or 3 Rot and 1 Carrot Seed (giant). After 4 to 5 days of being in the rotten stage, a non-giant Carrot Plant grows back, but the new plant cannot become giant.

When picking up a rotten plant, a [Fruit Fly](/wiki/Fruit_Fly "Fruit Fly") has a 5% chance to spawn.

In *[The Gorge](/wiki/The_Gorge "The Gorge")* [event](/wiki/Category:Events "Category:Events") of *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, Carrots are grown from Oblong Seeds which can be bought from [Pipton](/wiki/Pipton "Pipton") for one Old Coin. They are used in a variety of [Cooking Recipes](/wiki/The_Gorge_Recipes "The Gorge Recipes") exclusive to *The Gorge*.

## Tips[]

* Since planted Carrots never spoil, they can be used as an emergency food supply. And since they do not respawn, not picking them until it is necessary improves availability of food during times when food sources are otherwise scarce. However, it is worth considering during the second year that the Bearger can pick them.
* Although Carrots can make good bait for many Rabbits, one must be careful, as Rabbits can overwhelm the traps and waste the Carrot.
* Carrots do respawn in Spring. (DST only)
* Players should collect a decent amount of carrots and leave some spare carrots, as it takes a long time to rot (10 days) as it will help with Hunger early on in the game.
