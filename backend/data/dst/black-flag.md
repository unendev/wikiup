The **Black Flag** is an [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[The Curse of Moon Quay](/wiki/The_Curse_of_Moon_Quay "The Curse of Moon Quay")* update. It can be obtained by destroying a [Moon Quay Pirate Banner](/wiki/Moon_Quay_Pirate_Banner "Moon Quay Pirate Banner"). Its use is to either re-create the Banner or to craft [Polly Roger's Hat](/wiki/Polly_Roger%27s_Hat "Polly Roger's Hat").

Black Flags are renewable as the [Sloops](/wiki/Pirate_Sloop "Pirate Sloop") from [Pirate Raids](/wiki/Pirate_Raid "Pirate Raid") always have 1 banner, and the raids are occuring periodically or can be summoned with the [Hostile Flare](/wiki/Hostile_Flare "Hostile Flare").
