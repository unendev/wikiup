**Palmcone Scales** are a resource exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")* introduced in the *[The Curse of Moon Quay](/wiki/The_Curse_of_Moon_Quay "The Curse of Moon Quay")* update. They are dropped from fully grown [Palmcone Trees](/wiki/Palmcone_Tree "Palmcone Tree"). They are used to build [Dock Kits](/wiki/Dock_Kit "Dock Kit") and [Cannon Kits](/wiki/Cannon_Kit "Cannon Kit").
