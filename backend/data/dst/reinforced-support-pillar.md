The **Reinforced Support Pillar** is a [Structure](/wiki/Structure "Structure") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in the *[From Beyond](/wiki/From_Beyond "From Beyond")* updates. It can be crafted as the **Support Pillar Scaffold** in the [Structures Filter](/wiki/Structures_Filter "Structures Filter") with 1 [Cut Stone](/wiki/Cut_Stone "Cut Stone") and 2 [Boards](/wiki/Boards "Boards") after learning the rare [Blueprint](/wiki/Blueprint "Blueprint") gained by defeating the [Ancient Guardian](/wiki/Ancient_Guardian "Ancient Guardian"). After being placed, it will need to be given 40 Rocks to become a finished pillar.

A complete Reinforced Support Pillar protects an area from falling objects during an [Earthquake](/wiki/Earthquake "Earthquake") within a radius of 10 tiles (or 40 units). Each time it blocks a falling object, it loses 1 durability. When the durability reaches certain amounts, it will change its appearance to a more and more broken Pillar. It can be repaired with Rocks. If the durability reaches 0, it will turn into a Support Pillar Wreckage, not giving protection anymore, but can be repaired with up to 40 rocks.

The Pillar can also be [hammered](/wiki/Hammer "Hammer"), only returning 1 Rocks for each stage and 1 Cut Stone when destroying the wreckage. Hammering the scaffold will drop also 1 [Boards](/wiki/Boards "Boards"). It can't be deconstructed with a [Deconstruction Staff](/wiki/Deconstruction_Staff "Deconstruction Staff").

The **Reinforced Dreadstone Pillar** is a Structure exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in the *[From Beyond](/wiki/From_Beyond "From Beyond")* updates. It can be crafted as the **Dreadstone Pillar Scaffold** in the [Structures Filter](/wiki/Structures_Filter "Structures Filter") with 4 [Dreadstone](/wiki/Dreadstone "Dreadstone") and 2 [Boards](/wiki/Boards "Boards") after learning the rare [Blueprint](/wiki/Blueprint "Blueprint") gained by defeating the [Nightmare Werepig](/wiki/Nightmare_Werepig "Nightmare Werepig"). After placed it will be a Pillar Scaffold that needs to be given 40 Dreadstone to become a complete Pillar.

The Reinforced Dreadstone Pillar protects an area from falling objects during an [Earthquake](/wiki/Earthquake "Earthquake") within a radius of 10 tiles (or 40 units). Each time it blocks a falling object, it loses 1 durability. When the Dreadstone Pillar is damaged, it will slowly regenerate its durability, but also drain the players [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity") within a radius of 8 tiles at a maximum rate of -10 Sanity depending on the distance. When the durability reaches certain amounts, its appearance will change to a more and more broken Pillar. When it turns into its wrecked stage, it will not regenerate durability by itself anymore and will need 40 Dreadstone to be repaired.

Although the Reinforced Dreadstone Pillar can be [hammered](/wiki/Hammer "Hammer"), it doesn't drop anything and doesn't lose durability. After every hit with a Hammer the character will be pushed back, giving the Pillar the time to repair the damage the player was causing (*verification needed*). It can't be deconstructed with a [Deconstruction Staff](/wiki/Deconstruction_Staff "Deconstruction Staff").
