**Goat Milk** is an item found in [The Gorge](/wiki/The_Gorge "The Gorge") event. It is obtained by purchasing it from [Mumsy](/wiki/Mumsy "Mumsy") for 1 Red Mark, which gives 3 Goat Milk.

All dairy-based recipes will require Goat Milk, as it is the only source of dairy in The Gorge.

## Prototype Tips[]

* Since Goat Milk spoils quickly, it is advised to only buy it when it is immediately needed for the current recipe.
