**Amberosia** is an Elemental [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")*. It can be cooked in a [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") with 1 [Collected Dust](/wiki/Collected_Dust "Collected Dust") and any 3 fillers. It has the highest priority of any Crock Pot dish in the game: attempting to use Collected Dust with any recipe will always result in Amberosia.

Amberosia cannot be eaten by the player. [Dust Moths](/wiki/Dust_Moth "Dust Moth") fed Amberosia will ornate their [Tidy Hidey-Holes](/wiki/Tidy_Hidey-Hole "Tidy Hidey-Hole") with [Thulecite](/wiki/Thulecite "Thulecite"), which can then be mined for 3 [Thulecite Fragments](/wiki/Thulecite_Fragments "Thulecite Fragments"). This offers a renewable, albeit slow source of Thulecite without the need to reset the [Ruins](/wiki/Ruins "Ruins").

Since there are no restrictions on what the fillers can be, it is advised to use cheap items, like [Twigs](/wiki/Twigs "Twigs").

## Trivia[]

* Amberosia was introduced in the *[Forgotten Knowledge](/wiki/Return_of_Them#Forgotten_Knowledge "Return of Them")* update.
* The name "Amberosia" is a portmanteau of "Amber" and "Ambrosia".
* Amberosia is designed after a [Meringue](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Meringue "wikipedia:Meringue"), as indicated by its internal name.
