**Naked Nostrils** are Food items exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*. They are dropped by [Naked Mole Bats](/wiki/Naked_Mole_Bat "Naked Mole Bat") upon death.

Raw Naked Nostrils are needed to craft the [Milkmade Hat](/wiki/Milkmade_Hat "Milkmade Hat"), when combined with [Kelp Fronds](/wiki/Kelp_Fronds "Kelp Fronds") and a [dairy](/wiki/Dairy "Dairy") product inside of a [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot").
