The **Swamp Brawler Helmet** is an [item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in the *[Skill Spotlight Updates](/wiki/Skill_Spotlight_Update "Skill Spotlight Update")*. It can only be crafted by [Loyal Merm Guards](/wiki/Loyal_Merm_Guard "Loyal Merm Guard") at an [Armermry](/wiki/Armermry "Armermry") when they do not have any head-slot item equipped. Crafting a helmet will use 1 [Log](/wiki/Log "Log") and 1 [Grass](/wiki/Grass "Grass") from the Armermry as long as it has enough resources. Merm Guards will equip a helmet after crafting or when finding one on the ground.

The Swamp Brawler Helmet has a [Durability](/wiki/Durability "Durability") of 300 and absorbs 30% of all physical [damage](/wiki/Damage "Damage"). However, players cannot equip it.

The Swamp Warrior Helmet has a [Durability](/wiki/Durability "Durability") of 450 and absorbs 50% of all physical [damage](/wiki/Damage "Damage"). However, players cannot equip it.
