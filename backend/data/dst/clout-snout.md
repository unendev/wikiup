The **Clout Snout** is an [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*. They are obtained by playing the [Pig King Wrestling Match](/wiki/Year_of_the_Pig_King#Pig_King_Wrestling_Match "Year of the Pig King").

Clout Snouts can be used to summon a random Elite Pig.

The **Elite Pigs** are [Mobs](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs") exclusive to *Don't Starve Together*. They are summoned by using a Clout Snout. They follow the player for 10 seconds before disappearing. Elite Pigs have improved combat abilities compared to regular Pigs.
