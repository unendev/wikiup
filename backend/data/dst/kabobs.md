**Kabobs** is a [Meat](/wiki/Meats "Meats") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") item cooked in the [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") by adding 1 Meats and exactly 1 [Twig](/wiki/Twigs "Twigs"). It takes 40 seconds to cook and 15 [Days](/wiki/Day-Night_Cycle "Day-Night Cycle") to [Spoil](/wiki/Spoil "Spoil").

When consumed, it restores 3 Health, 37.5 Hunger and 5 Sanity.

Adding more than 1 [Monster Food](/wiki/Monster_Food "Monster Food") or Twig will result in [Wet Goop](/wiki/Wet_Goop "Wet Goop"), while adding any [Fishes](/wiki/Fishes "Fishes") will result in [Fishsticks](/wiki/Fishsticks "Fishsticks").

### Don't Starve Together Don't Starve Together[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, Kabobs increases the player's perceived temperature by 1 degree per second for 15 seconds when consumed.

## Tips[]

* Replacing the Twigs with nearly any other food item will result in [Meatballs](/wiki/Meatballs "Meatballs"), which provides the same [Health](/wiki/Health "Health") and [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity") but 25 more [Hunger](/wiki/Hunger "Hunger"), as well as cooking 25 seconds faster.
* Kabobs is best made when the player has an abundance of meat, time, and perhaps Crock Pots, as well as the need to stretch filler ingredients.
* For example: Two Meatballs can be made from 2 Monster Meat and 6 filler, providing 125 Hunger, 10 Sanity and 6 Health. Three Kabobs can be made from the same ingredients, plus three Twigs and a Monster Meat, restoring 112.5 Hunger, 15 Sanity and 9 Health.
  + The Health and Sanity benefits are marginal and should be weighed against the Twigs and Monster Meat supply (usually in abundance). There should be several pots available to parallelize cooking due to its long cooking time.
