*Exclusive to:* Don't Starve Together

**Giant Crops** are objects obtained by harvesting Giant [Farm Plants](/wiki/Farm_Plant "Farm Plant"). They can be smashed with a [Hammer](/wiki/Hammer "Hammer") to drop

* two pieces of produce of the corresponding type plus a 75% chance for another one (2.75 pieces of produce on average);
* two [Plant Seeds](/wiki/Plant_Seeds "Plant Seeds") of the corresponding type plus a 25% chance for another one (2.25 seeds on average).

They can be [weighed](/wiki/Weight#Vegetables "Weight") with a [Produce Scale](/wiki/Produce_Scale "Produce Scale"). They are moved in a similar manner as other large objects, such as [Suspicious Marble](/wiki/Suspicious_Marble "Suspicious Marble").

Giant Crops spoil 4 times slower than their corresponding regular sized crops. Once rotten, they can be hammered for three [Rot](/wiki/Rot "Rot"), one corresponding plant seed, and two spawned [Fruit Flies](/wiki/Fruit_Fly "Fruit Fly").

Giant Crops can be waxed with a [Beeswax](/wiki/Beeswax "Beeswax") to stop spoilage of its structure. Rotten Giant Crops will stay as a rotten structure without the need for any waxing. Waxed Giant Crops can be hammered for one rot.
