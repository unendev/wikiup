**Winona's Catapult** is a [character](/wiki/Character "Character")-exclusive [structure](/wiki/Structure "Structure")/[item](/wiki/Item "Item") craftable only by [Winona](/wiki/Winona "Winona") in *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*. It is found in the [Weapons Filter](/wiki/Weapons_Filter "Weapons Filter"), [Structures Filter](/wiki/Structures_Filter "Structures Filter") and [Survivor Items Filter](/wiki/Survivor_Items_Filter "Survivor Items Filter") and requires one [Trusty Tape](/wiki/Trusty_Tape "Trusty Tape"), three [Twigs](/wiki/Twigs "Twigs"), and 15 [Rocks](/wiki/Rocks "Rocks") to craft. If Winona has mastered the [Portability](/wiki/Portability "Portability") skill, she can craft the Catapult as an [item](/wiki/Item "Item") and dismantling already build Catapults. With this skill she can also craft a [Handy Remote](/wiki/Handy_Remote "Handy Remote") to control the Catapults.

## Usage[]

In order to work, a Catapult has to be in the range of a powered [Generator](/wiki/Winona%27s_Generator "Winona's Generator") or [G.E.M.erator](/wiki/Winona%27s_G.E.M.erator "Winona's G.E.M.erator").

The Catapult will act much like a [Houndius Shootius](/wiki/Houndius_Shootius "Houndius Shootius"). It will automatically attack Mobs targeting the Character or any Mob that the player attempts to attack, except for [Shadow Creatures](/wiki/Shadow_Creatures "Shadow Creatures"). It cannot deal damage to [Glommer](/wiki/Glommer "Glommer"), [Chester](/wiki/Chester "Chester"), [Friendly Fruit Fly](/wiki/Friendly_Fruit_Fly "Friendly Fruit Fly"), and [Houndius Shootius](/wiki/Houndius_Shootius "Houndius Shootius"). It also do not deal any damage to friendly and [walls](/wiki/Wall "Wall").

Winona's Catapult can be attacked by [Mobs](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs") and has 400 HP. It deals 42.5 AoE damage with a single shot. The Catapult also repairs itself by 8.3 HP every 10 seconds. Redeploying a Catapult will fully restore its health.

If a Catapult stops attacking for too long, it will switch to sleep mode. Non-Winona characters can reactivate a Catapult by interacting with it. Winona with the Portability skill can use her Handy Remote to activate and deactivate all Catapults in a certain range.

## Insight Skill Upgrades[]

Catapults can receive different upgrades depending on the skills Winona has mastered:

| Skill | Effect |
| --- | --- |
| Portability [Portability](/wiki/Portability "Portability") | With this skill, Winona can dismantle all her Catapults to an item and also craft them as an item. She can also craft the [Handy Remote](/wiki/Handy_Remote "Handy Remote") to turn the Catapults on and off and to let them perform several attacks. |
| Energy-Saver [Energy-Saver](/wiki/Energy-Saver "Energy-Saver") | Catapults placed by a Winona with this skill will no longer use energy from generators while they are in sleep mode. |
| Rapid-Fire I [Rapid-Fire I](/wiki/Rapid-Fire_I "Rapid-Fire I") | Catapults placed by a Winona with this skill have an increased firing rate of 5%. |
| Rapid-Fire II [Rapid-Fire II](/wiki/Rapid-Fire_II "Rapid-Fire II") | Catapults placed by a Winona with this skill have an increased firing rate of 10%. |
| Rapid-Fire III [Rapid-Fire III](/wiki/Rapid-Fire_III "Rapid-Fire III") | Catapults placed by a Winona with this skill have an increased firing rate of 20%. |
| Explosive Volley I [Explosive Volley I](/wiki/Explosive_Volley_I "Explosive Volley I") | Catapults placed by a Winona with this skill will deal damage to a 25% larger area. |
| Explosive Volley II [Explosive Volley II](/wiki/Explosive_Volley_II "Explosive Volley II") | Catapults placed by a Winona with this skill will deal damage to a 50% larger area. |
| Explosive Volley III [Explosive Volley III](/wiki/Explosive_Volley_III "Explosive Volley III") | Catapults placed by a Winona with this skill will deal damage to a 100% larger area. |
| Pure Horror Generator [Pure Horror Generator](/wiki/Pure_Horror_Generator "Pure Horror Generator") | Catapults placed by a Winona with this skill and fueled with [Pure Horror](/wiki/Pure_Horror "Pure Horror") deal 30 physical damage and 17 planar damage. They also deal +10% damage to [Lunar Aligned](/wiki/Lunar_Aligned "Lunar Aligned") creatures. |
| Brilliance G.E.M.erator [Brilliance G.E.M.erator](/wiki/Brilliance_G.E.M.erator "Brilliance G.E.M.erator") | Catapults placed by a Winona with this skill and fueled with [Pure Brilliance](/wiki/Pure_Brilliance "Pure Brilliance") deal 30 physical damage and 17 planar damage. They also deal +10% damage to [Shadow Aligned](/wiki/Shadow_Aligned "Shadow Aligned") creatures. |

## Trivia[]

* Winona's Catapult, alongside her other gadgets and the [Engineering Tab](/wiki/Engineering_Tab "Engineering Tab"), was introduced in her [Character Update](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together/Version_History#March_7th,_2019_-_Winona_Character_Update "Don't Starve Together/Version History") as part of the character overhaul for *Don't Starve Together*.
  + With the *[Skill Spotlight: Wurt & Winona](/wiki/Skill_Spotlight_Update#Wurt_and_Winona "Skill Spotlight Update")* update, build Catapults can be dismantled into an item and also be crafted as an item, making them portable (only, if Winona has mastered the Portability skill). Also, some catapult-related skills were added.
* A single Winona's Catapult, provided it can attack uninterrupted, will deal 1020 damage per minute (without skills).
