**Butterfly Wings** are a [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [item](/wiki/Item "Item") dropped by killing [Butterflies](/wiki/Butterfly "Butterfly"). They are a very reliable drop (98%), although there is a small chance of getting [Butter](/wiki/Butter "Butter") instead (2%).

Eating Butterfly Wings will restore 8 [Health](/wiki/Health "Health"), the same as a [Spider Gland](/wiki/Spider_Gland "Spider Gland"), and a little bit of [Hunger](/wiki/Hunger "Hunger"). They can be used to create [Butter Muffins](/wiki/Butter_Muffin "Butter Muffin") in a [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot"). Butterfly Wings can be easily farmed by setting up a [Butterfly Farm](/wiki/Farming#Butterfly_Farms "Farming"), with [Beefalo](/wiki/Beefalo "Beefalo") in heat, [Hounds](/wiki/Hounds "Hounds"), or even [Catcoons](/wiki/Catcoons "Catcoons") in RoG.

## Downloadable Content[]

In the *[Reign of Giants](/wiki/Reign_of_Giants "Reign of Giants")* DLC, Butterfly Wings can be acquired by destroying [Tumbleweeds](/wiki/Tumbleweed "Tumbleweed"). Doing this will not provoke [Krampus](/wiki/Krampus "Krampus"). Butterfly Wings can also be given to [Catcoons](/wiki/Catcoon "Catcoon") to befriend them.

In the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC, Butterfly Wings take on a blue appearance as do the tropical Butterflies they drop from.

## Tips[]

* Butterfly Wings are a good healing item during early game and in a pinch. They provide the same amount of healing as Spider Glands do. However, Butterfly Wings can be obtained easier, and with less risk. On the other hand, unlike Spider Glands, Butterfly Wings spoil over time, and are difficult to obtain during the [Night](/wiki/Night "Night") or [Winter](/wiki/Winter "Winter"). While the Spider Glands can be used to make [Healing Salves](/wiki/Healing_Salve "Healing Salve"), Butterfly Wings can be used to make Butter Muffins to heal same amount of additional hit points, however the latter requires a Crock Pot.
* Even though it is dropped by an animal, it is not considered meat. This means it cannot be eaten by [Wigfrid](/wiki/Wigfrid "Wigfrid"), but can be eaten by [Wurt](/wiki/Wurt "Wurt") (this is probably due to the fact that butterflies are considered to be flying flowers and therefore a plant based food)
