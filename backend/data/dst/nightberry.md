**Nightberries** are [fruit](/wiki/Fruit "Fruit") [food](/wiki/Food "Food") [items](/wiki/Items "Items") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in the *[From Beyond](/wiki/From_Beyond "From Beyond")* updates. They can be harvested from [Gloomthorns](/wiki/Gloomthorn "Gloomthorn") every 12-25 [days](/wiki/Day-Night_Cycle "Day-Night Cycle").

When consumed, raw Nightberries restore 12.5 Hunger at a cost of -8 Health and -20 Sanity, and slow the player for a few seconds and grant night vision for 4 minutes. During this time, the player will lose ~4,4 Sanity/min (total of -17.75 Sanity over the entire duration). They take 6 days to spoil.

**Cooked Nightberries** restore 18.75 Hunger at a cost of -3 Health and -5 Sanity, but do not grant night vision. They take 3 days to spoil.

Both Nightberries and Cooked Nightberries can be used as ingredients in the [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot"), and count as 1 [fruit value](/wiki/Crock_Pot#Fruit_value "Crock Pot").
