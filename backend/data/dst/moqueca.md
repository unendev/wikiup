**Moqueca** is a [Warly](/wiki/Warly "Warly")-specific [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [item](/wiki/Item "Item") cooked in the [Portable Crock Pot](/wiki/Portable_Crock_Pot "Portable Crock Pot"), exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*. It requires at least 0.25 [Fishes](/wiki/Fishes "Fishes"), a [Toma Root](/wiki/Toma_Root "Toma Root") and an [Onion](/wiki/Onion "Onion") to cook. Fillers cannot be [Inedibles](/wiki/Inedibles "Inedibles").

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* Moqueca was added to *Don't Starve Together* in the [Warly Character Update](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together/Version_History#July_25,_2019_-_Warly_Character_Update "Don't Starve Together/Version History").
* Moqueca is a Brazilian food with its origins in the mix of Portuguese, African, and Native ingredients. It is made with tropical, accessible, and sea foods like fishes, clams, crustaceans, chicken, and eggs. This dish is still consumed in Angola, Pará, Espírito Santo, and Bahia.
* Giving more hunger but the same health and sanity, Moqueca could be seen as a better form of [Surf 'n' Turf](/wiki/Surf_%27n%27_Turf "Surf 'n' Turf").
