The **Sea Strider** is a mob exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")* and introduced in the *[Waterlogged](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together/Version_History#Waterlogged_Beta_.28since_July_26.2C_2021.29 "Don't Starve Together/Version History")* beta.

Sea Striders are capable of moving on the surface of the [Ocean](/wiki/Ocean "Ocean"), and can jump to and from [Boats](/wiki/Boats "Boats") or dry land.

At [Dusk](/wiki/Day-Night_Cycle "Day-Night Cycle") Sea Striders will emerge from their [Sea Strider Nests](/wiki/Sea_Strider_Nest "Sea Strider Nest") by descending from the canopy above. Sea Striders will also emerge to investigate when a nearby [Mossy Vine](/wiki/Mossy_Vine "Mossy Vine") is picked or the [Great Tree Trunk](/wiki/Great_Tree_Trunk "Great Tree Trunk") the nest is connected to is rammed by a boat. They will climb back to their nest during daytime if they are not in fight or investigation.

Like every other variety of [Spider](/wiki/Spider "Spider") Sea Striders will seek out and consume any type of [Meat](/wiki/Meat "Meat") left on the ground, including any [Ocean Fish](/wiki/Ocean_Fishes "Ocean Fishes") in the water. They will come out from their nest to eat fishes that are swimming close to the nest.

[Webber](/wiki/Webber "Webber") can use any [Meats](/wiki/Meats "Meats") to befriend with Sea Strider, and use [Strider Switcherdoodle](/wiki/Strider_Switcherdoodle "Strider Switcherdoodle") to turn other type of spiders into Sea Striders.
