**Hanging Vines** are [Plants](/wiki/Plant "Plant") or plant-like [Mob](/wiki/Mob "Mob") exclusive to the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC. They appear in [Deep Rainforest](/wiki/Deep_Rainforest "Deep Rainforest") once coming close to them. There are two variants of Hanging Vines. One of them is just a plant and can be destroyed with a [Shears](/wiki/Shears "Shears") to provide a [Rope](/wiki/Rope "Rope"). The other acts like a hostile creature.

Both kinds of Hanging Vines are renewable, after they are destroyed, a new one has a chance to spawn at the same location after about 2-3 days.[[1]](#cite_note-1)

## Behavior[]

Mob Hanging Vine knocks out items from one's inventory when attacking, like a [Frog](/wiki/Frog "Frog"), [Poison Dartfrog](/wiki/Poison_Dartfrog "Poison Dartfrog") or [Piko](/wiki/Piko "Piko"). It also eats all kinds of [food](/wiki/Food "Food") left on the ground. It is neutral towards [Wormwood](/wiki/Wormwood "Wormwood").

## Fighting Strategy[]

* Mob Hanging Vine cannot be frozen with an [Ice Staff](/wiki/Ice_Staff "Ice Staff") and put to sleep with [Sleep Darts](/wiki/Sleep_Dart "Sleep Dart").

As their damage is low, one can simply place a high-stack item like [Grass](/wiki/Grass "Grass") on the leftmost inventory slot and tank them. Placing a Food item nearby is another option, as Hanging Vines prioritize eating food over attacking, so players can attack them without retaliation. As a bonus, using this method can cause a chain of [Leafy Meat](/wiki/Leafy_Meat "Leafy Meat") dropping and causing others to head for it, allowing an easy clearing of hanging vines.

## Tips[]

* It is difficult to distinguish between plant Hanging Vine and mob Hanging Vine. However, the colour of mob Hanging Vine is brown-green and the colour of plant Hanging Vine is brown. One can also found that mob Hanging Vine has spikes and plant Hanging Vine has leaves.
* One can put some food which reduces [Health](/wiki/Health "Health"), such as [Red Mushroom](/wiki/Red_Mushroom "Red Mushroom") and [Monster Meat](/wiki/Monster_Meat "Monster Meat"), near mob Hanging Vine (it is not considered as [Monster](/wiki/Monster "Monster")), it will eat them and die.
