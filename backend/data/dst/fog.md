**“**It's like flying through the clouds!**”**

–[Wheeler](/wiki/Wheeler "Wheeler")

**Fog** is a mechanic found in the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* (SW) and *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLCs. Fog works quite differently in both DLCs, while [Ocean Fog](/wiki/Ocean_Fog "Ocean Fog") serves as world border, [Heavy Fog](/wiki/Heavy_Fog "Heavy Fog") is a prominent mechanic during the [Humid Season](/wiki/Humid_Season "Humid Season").

It is notable that going through fog in SW will teleport you to the direct opposite side of the map. Heavy Fog will slow your movement if wearing any other item but the [Pith Hat](/wiki/Pith_Hat "Pith Hat").
