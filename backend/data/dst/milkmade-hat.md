The **Milkmade Hat** is an item exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")*. It is obtained by cooking 1 raw [Naked Nostrils](/wiki/Naked_Nostrils "Naked Nostrils"), 1 raw [Kelp Fronds](/wiki/Kelp_Fronds "Kelp Fronds"), 1 [Dairy](/wiki/Dairy "Dairy"), and due to its high priority any filler except [Collected Dust](/wiki/Collected_Dust "Collected Dust"). It takes 40 seconds to cook and will only perish while equipped.

It restores 3.9 Hunger every 5 seconds for a total of 187.5 over 4 minutes at the cost of 1.33 Sanity/minute (Wurt instead receives 1.33 Sanity/minute).

Despite being a crock pot dish, it cannot be put in [Warly](/wiki/Warly "Warly")'s [Portable Seasoning Station](/wiki/Portable_Seasoning_Station "Portable Seasoning Station"). [Wigfrid](/wiki/Wigfrid "Wigfrid") will refuse to equip it. Spore bombs thrown by [Toadstool](/wiki/Toadstool "Toadstool") can reduce its perish time.
