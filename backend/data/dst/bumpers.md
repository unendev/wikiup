The **Kelp Bumper Kit** is a craftable [Item](/wiki/Category:Items "Category:Items") in the Seafaring filter, exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")* introduced in *[The Curse of Moon Quay](/wiki/The_Curse_of_Moon_Quay "The Curse of Moon Quay")* update. It requires 3 [Kelp Fronds](/wiki/Kelp_Fronds "Kelp Fronds") and 3 [Cut Grass](/wiki/Cut_Grass "Cut Grass") to craft. It is used to strengthen a [Boat](/wiki/Boat "Boat") by installing a bumper on the edge of the vessel. It has 20 durability, which provides a small protection for the raft from collision damage. If it is damaged and not destroyed, the player can use Kelp Fronds to repair it.

The **Crab King Bumper Kit** is an [Item](/wiki/Category:Items "Category:Items") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[From Beyond](/wiki/From_Beyond "From Beyond")* updates. It cannot be crafted. The [Crab King](/wiki/Crab_King "Crab King") drops 8 Bumper Kits upon defeat.

The **Crab King Bumper** is used to strengthen a [Boat](/wiki/Boat "Boat") by installing a bumper on the edge of the vessel. It has 150 durability, which provides a huge protection for the raft from collision damage and is immune to damage from bumping into land. If it is damaged and not destroyed, the player can use [Rocks](/wiki/Rocks "Rocks"), [Cut Stone](/wiki/Cut_Stone "Cut Stone"), or [Stone Walls](/wiki/Stone_Wall "Stone Wall") to repair it.
