The **Briar Wolf** is a hostile [Mob](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*. It was added in *[A Little Drama](/wiki/A_Little_Drama_and_QOL "A Little Drama and QOL")* update. Briar Wolves spawn from briars disturbed by the player. They serve as a source of [Costumes](/wiki/Costumes "Costumes") needed for [Stage](/wiki/Stage "Stage") performances, which they drop upon death.

Though they normally only spawn from Briars, Briar Wolves are also created by the stage itself if players try to start the show during a closed curtain or if players initiate a performance without wearing the required costume(s). The stage will summon three wolves, which drop various loot when they die:

**Briars** are [Plants](/wiki/Plants "Plants") in *Don't Starve Together*. They are part of a unique [Set Piece](/wiki/Set_Piece "Set Piece") in which four of them surround a [Marble Statue](/wiki/Marble_Statues "Marble Statues"). When harvested or attacked, the Briar transforms into a Briar Wolf. Briars will respawn after one in-game day.

Briars deal 1 damage when harvested.
