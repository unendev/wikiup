A **Butter Muffin** is a [Vegetable](/wiki/Vegetable "Vegetable") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [item](/wiki/Item "Item") cooked in the [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") by combining 1 [Butterfly Wings](/wiki/Butterfly_Wings "Butterfly Wings") and 1 Vegetable. It takes 40 seconds to cook.

When consumed, it restores 20 Health, 37.5 Hunger and 5 Sanity, and takes 15 [Days](/wiki/Day-Night_Cycle "Day-Night Cycle") to [Spoil](/wiki/Spoil "Spoil").

Adding any [Meats](/wiki/Meats "Meats") will result in [Meatballs](/wiki/Meatballs "Meatballs") and possibly other recipes while adding a [Mandrake](/wiki/Mandrake "Mandrake") will result in [Mandrake Soup](/wiki/Mandrake_Soup "Mandrake Soup"). Adding 1 [Dragon Fruit](/wiki/Dragon_Fruit "Dragon Fruit") may result in [Dragonpie](/wiki/Dragonpie "Dragonpie").

## Don't Starve Together Don't Starve Together[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, the Butterfly Wings can be replaced with [Moon Moth Wings](/wiki/Moon_Moth_Wings "Moon Moth Wings").

It is [WX-78](/wiki/WX-78 "WX-78")'s favorite food and can be consumed for 15 more Hunger.
