**Gems** are [items](/wiki/Items "Items") found in *[Don't Starve](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve "Don't Starve")* and *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*. There are 6 types of gems in Don't Starve, plus a 7th in Don't Starve Together. Gems are the only crafting component that cannot be recovered with the [Deconstruction Staff](/wiki/Deconstruction_Staff "Deconstruction Staff") (the only exception is the Iridescent Gem).

## Common[]

The Purple, Blue, and (less common before the first summer) Red gems are mainly used as crafting components for items in the [Magic Tab](/wiki/Magic_Tab "Magic Tab"). They are typically found in seasonal hound loot, clockwork bishops, graves, and stalagmites. The Purple gem can also be crafted from a Blue and Red Gem at the [Prestihatitator](/wiki/Prestihatitator "Prestihatitator").

## Uncommon[]

The Green, Orange, and Yellow gems are used for crafting items in the [Ancient Tab](/wiki/Ancient_Tab "Ancient Tab"). They are typically found in the [Ruins](/wiki/Ruins "Ruins"), [Sunken Chests](/wiki/Sunken_Chest "Sunken Chest"), and in boss loot.

## Rare[]

The Iridescent Gem is the rarest and only used for activating the [Archive Switch](/wiki/Archive_Switch "Archive Switch") or for crafting the [Lunar Grimoire](/wiki/Books#Lunar_Grimoire "Books"). Two of these gems are already in the archive switch (3 are needed to activate it). Additional ones can be created by deconstructing a [Moon Caller's Staff](/wiki/Moon_Caller%27s_Staff "Moon Caller's Staff") after completing a full-moon ritual at the [Moon Stone](/wiki/Moon_Stone "Moon Stone"). [Wilson](/wiki/Wilson "Wilson")'s level 3 [Transmute Gems](/wiki/Wilson/Don%27t_Starve_Together#Transmute_Gems "Wilson/Don't Starve Together") alchemy can turn a complete set of the six other gems into an Iridescent Gem.
