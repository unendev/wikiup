**Sprouting Stone Fruit** is an item exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*. It can be obtained by mining a [Stone Fruit](/wiki/Stone_Fruit "Stone Fruit"), but the chance of receiving a Sprouting Stone Fruit is fairly low, 1%. Sprouting Stone Fruits can be planted to grow a [Stone Fruit Bush](/wiki/Stone_Fruit_Bush "Stone Fruit Bush"), and this Stone Fruit Bush doesn't need to be fertilized.
