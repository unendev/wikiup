**Grass Turf** is one of the [Turf](/wiki/Turf "Turf") [Items](/wiki/Items "Items"), acquired by digging [Grassland](/wiki/Grassland "Grassland") tiles with a [Pitchfork](/wiki/Pitchfork "Pitchfork"). Like other Turfs, it can be placed on any bare ground tile, or used as [fuel](/wiki/Fire#Fuel "Fire").

Plants like [Berry Bushes](/wiki/Berry_Bush "Berry Bush"), [Grass Tufts](/wiki/Grass_Tuft "Grass Tuft"), [Saplings](/wiki/Saplings "Saplings"), [Spiky Bushes](/wiki/Spiky_Bush "Spiky Bush"), [Lureplants](/wiki/Lureplant "Lureplant"), [Pine Cones](/wiki/Pine_Cone "Pine Cone"), and [Birchnuts](/wiki/Birchnut "Birchnut") can be planted on these Turfs. It will not stop the Lureplants' [Eyeplants](/wiki/Eyeplant "Eyeplant") from spawning.

When placed on the [Surface World](/wiki/Surface_World "Surface World") and the area is large enough, only [Redbirds](/wiki/Redbirds "Redbirds") will spawn and land on these Turfs. During [Winter](/wiki/Winter "Winter") Redbirds are replaced by Snowbirds.

## Shipwrecked Shipwrecked[]

In the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC, Grass Turf behaves like [Jungle Turf](/wiki/Jungle_Turf "Jungle Turf"): Parrots and [Parrot Pirates](/wiki/Parrot_Pirate "Parrot Pirate") are the only birds that spawn. During [Hurricane Season](/wiki/Hurricane_Season "Hurricane Season"), there are no birds that spawn and land on these turfs. Floods can spawn on this turf.

## Don't Starve Together Don't Starve Together[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, Grass Turf can be crafted and prototyped at [Terra Firma Tamper](/wiki/Terra_Firma_Tamper "Terra Firma Tamper") with 1 [Cut Grass](/wiki/Cut_Grass "Cut Grass") and 1 [Petals](/wiki/Petals "Petals") in the [Decorations Filter](/wiki/Decorations_Filter "Decorations Filter").
