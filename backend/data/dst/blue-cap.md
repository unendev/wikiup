**Blue Caps** are [Vegetable](/wiki/Vegetable "Vegetable") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Items](/wiki/Item "Item") that can be harvested on [Blue Mushrooms](/wiki/Blue_Mushroom "Blue Mushroom") or [Blue Mushtrees](/wiki/Mushtree "Mushtree"). Raw Blue Caps have the most positive effects of all the Mushrooms. When eaten raw, they restore 20 Health and 12.5 Hunger at the cost of 15 Sanity. They can also be used as [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") filler with a 0.5 vegetable value.

A **Cooked Blue Cap** is a Vegetable Food Item made by [Cooking](/wiki/Cooking "Cooking") a Blue Cap on a fire ([Campfire](/wiki/Campfire "Campfire"), [Fire Pit](/wiki/Fire_Pit "Fire Pit"), or a [Dwarf Star](/wiki/Star_Caller%27s_Staff "Star Caller's Staff")). Unlike its raw counterpart, the Cooked Blue Cap will take away 3 Health and instead restore 10 Sanity when eaten, not affecting hunger at all.

## Downloadable Content[]

In the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC, Blue Caps can be obtained by using the [Slot Machine](/wiki/Slot_Machine "Slot Machine").

In the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC, Blue Caps can be bought for 3 [Oincs](/wiki/Oinc "Oinc") at the [Curly Tails Mud Spa](/wiki/Curly_Tails_Mud_Spa "Curly Tails Mud Spa") in the [Pig City](/wiki/Pig_City "Pig City").

## Don't Starve Together Don't Starve Together[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, the [Toadstool](/wiki/Toadstool "Toadstool") has a chance to drop Blue Cap upon death. Blue Caps are required to craft [Nightflyer Lures](/wiki/Lures "Lures") and the [Blue Funcap](/wiki/Blue_Funcap "Blue Funcap"). A [Mushroom Planter](/wiki/Mushroom_Planter "Mushroom Planter") can be used to grow Blue Mushrooms by planting a Blue Cap or [Blue Mushroom Spores](/wiki/Mushroom_Spore "Mushroom Spore") in it.

## Tips[]

* Raw and Cooked Blue Caps can be eaten together to increase all three stats. Eating **one** raw Blue Cap and **two** cooked Blue Caps results in +14 Health, +12.5 Hunger, and +5 Sanity.
* Using Blue Caps alongside [Taffy](/wiki/Taffy "Taffy") is a great combo, as Taffy restores the exact amount of sanity lost from eating the Blue Cap.
* A raw Blue Cap and a cooked Green Cap can be eaten together to provide +19 Health, +12.5 Hunger, and +0 Sanity.
* Raw Blue Caps can be found in plentiful amounts around the Blue Mushtree biome in the caves, which can be used for emergency healing and hunger restoration.
