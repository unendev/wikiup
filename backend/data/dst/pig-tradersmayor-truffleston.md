**Mayor Truffleston** is a [Pig Trader](/wiki/Pig_Traders "Pig Traders") [Mob](/wiki/Mob "Mob") exclusive to the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC. He is the Mayor of [Swinesbury](/wiki/Pig_City "Pig City"), and thus he can be found in the [Swinesbury City Hall](/wiki/Swinesbury_City_Hall "Swinesbury City Hall"). Only one spawns per village.

The player can give him a [Gold Nugget](/wiki/Gold_Nuggets "Gold Nuggets") and receive 5 [Oincs](/wiki/Oinc "Oinc") in exchange or buy a [Security Contract](/wiki/Security_Contract "Security Contract") (10 Oincs) or a [Deed of home ownership](/wiki/Deed_of_home_ownership "Deed of home ownership") (50 Oincs) from him. There is no limit to the amount of Gold Nugget trades done in a day, however, as Gold Nuggets are especially rare and hard to obtain in the *Hamlet* DLC and cost 10 Oincs to obtain from [Swinesbury Mineral Exchange](/wiki/Swinesbury_Mineral_Exchange "Swinesbury Mineral Exchange"), selling them to the Mayor is not recommended, buying them instead from the Mineral exchange is the better option.

After obtaining the [Key to the City](/wiki/Key_to_the_City "Key to the City") from the [Pig Queen](/wiki/Pig_Queen "Pig Queen") in the [Palace](/wiki/Palace "Palace"), the player can build their own City Hall and become Mayor themselves. This position grants them the right to collect [Oincs](/wiki/Oinc "Oinc") from the villagers who own houses built by the player every 10 days.
