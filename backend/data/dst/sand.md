**Sand** is a resource found in the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC. Sand can be obtained by digging [Sandy Piles](/wiki/Sandy_Pile "Sandy Pile") found on the [Beach](/wiki/Beach "Beach") [biomes](/wiki/Biome "Biome") with a [Shovel](/wiki/Shovel "Shovel"). Each use of Shovel will yield one pile of Sand, and full size Sandy Piles can be dug twice. Sandy Piles regenerate over time, making Sand renewable. Sand is an important resource early in the game, as it is required for crafting [Chimineas](/wiki/Chiminea "Chiminea"), [Buoyant Chimineas](/wiki/Buoyant_Chiminea "Buoyant Chiminea") and [Sandbags](/wiki/Sandbag "Sandbag"), which are structures that greatly help in surviving [Hurricane Season](/wiki/Hurricane_Season "Hurricane Season") and [Monsoon Season](/wiki/Monsoon_Season "Monsoon Season") respectively. It can also be used to craft a [Sand Castle](/wiki/Sand_Castle "Sand Castle") or a [Sea Lab](/wiki/Sea_Lab "Sea Lab"). Sand can also be refined into [Empty Bottles](/wiki/Empty_Bottle "Empty Bottle").

[Strong Winds](/wiki/Strong_Winds "Strong Winds"), which are frequent during [Hurricane Season](/wiki/Hurricane_Season "Hurricane Season"), will cause piles of Sand left on the ground to dissipate.

## Hamlet icon Hamlet[]

Sand can be acquired by hammering the [Bag of Sand](/wiki/Crashed_Balloon "Crashed Balloon") debris scattered around the player's spawn point.
