**Ancient Murals** are *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")* objects, introduced in *[A New Reign](/wiki/A_New_Reign "A New Reign")*. They are present in the [Atrium](/wiki/Atrium "Atrium") Biome, appearing in bigger quantities near the [Ancient Gateway](/wiki/Ancient_Gateway "Ancient Gateway"). Their only purpose is to be inspected for a set of 5 different quotes for each character, giving away part of the history of the [Ancient Civilization](/wiki/Ancient_Civilization "Ancient Civilization").

Upon reading a mural, the character will say their first quote. Reading the same mural will always give the same quote. In order to get the second one, the player must find another Mural, and so on. After getting the 5th inspection, the next one will cycle back to the first
