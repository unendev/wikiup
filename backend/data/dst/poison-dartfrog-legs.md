**Poison Dartfrog Legs** are a [Meat](/wiki/Meat "Meat") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to *[Don't Starve: Hamlet](/wiki/Hamlet "Hamlet")* DLC. Eating Poison Dartfrog Legs raw or cooked will restore a little bit of hunger, though it will [poison](/wiki/Poison "Poison") the player. It also cannot be used as an ingredient in the [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot").

They can be be given to a [Bird](/wiki/Birds "Birds") in a [Birdcage](/wiki/Birdcage "Birdcage") to produce an [Egg](/wiki/Egg "Egg").

Drying raw Poison Dartfrog Legs can turn them into [Small Jerky](/wiki/Small_Jerky "Small Jerky") and remove the poison.

Another use of this item is to make the [Venom Gland](/wiki/Venom_Gland "Venom Gland"), which can be crafted with 3 raw Poison Dartfrog Legs.
