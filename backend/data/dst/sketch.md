## Gathering[]

To obtain Sketches for [Pawns](/wiki/Pawn_Figure_(Marble) "Pawn Figure (Marble)") and [Queenly Figures](/wiki/Queenly_Figure_(Marble) "Queenly Figure (Marble)"), one needs to mine [Marble Statues](/wiki/Marble_Statues "Marble Statues"). For [Kingly Figure](/wiki/Kingly_Figure_(Marble) "Kingly Figure (Marble)") Sketch, [Maxwell Statue](/wiki/Maxwell_Statue "Maxwell Statue"). For [Rook](/wiki/Rook_Figure_(Marble) "Rook Figure (Marble)"), [Bishop](/wiki/Bishop_Figure_(Marble) "Bishop Figure (Marble)"), and [Knight Figure](/wiki/Knight_Figure_(Marble) "Knight Figure (Marble)") Sketches, [Marble Sculpture](/wiki/Marble_Sculpture "Marble Sculpture") of matching type (Note that Marble Sculptures need [Suspicious Marble](/wiki/Suspicious_Marble "Suspicious Marble") to be repaired and then to be destroyed at [Full Moon](/wiki/Full_Moon "Full Moon") to drop the sketch).

Performing each of the above actions will unlock the respective chess piece in the world. This allows for the matching trinket or sketch to be found in [Tumbleweeds](/wiki/Tumbleweed "Tumbleweed"). Chess piece trinkets may be traded with the [Pig King](/wiki/Pig_King "Pig King") to obtain its corresponding sketches.

The [Boss Monster](/wiki/Category:Boss_Monsters "Category:Boss Monsters") Sketches are dropped by the [Deerclops](/wiki/Deerclops "Deerclops"), [Bearger](/wiki/Bearger "Bearger"), [Moose/Goose](/wiki/Moose/Goose "Moose/Goose"), [Dragonfly](/wiki/Dragonfly "Dragonfly"), [Malbatross](/wiki/Malbatross "Malbatross"), [Crab King](/wiki/Crab_King "Crab King"), [Ancient Fuelweaver](/wiki/Ancient_Fuelweaver "Ancient Fuelweaver"), [Toadstool](/wiki/Toadstool "Toadstool"), [Klaus](/wiki/Klaus "Klaus"), [Bee Queen](/wiki/Bee_Queen "Bee Queen"), [Antlion](/wiki/Antlion "Antlion"), [Ancient Guardian](/wiki/Ancient_Guardian "Ancient Guardian"), [Celestial Champion](/wiki/Celestial_Champion "Celestial Champion"), [Eye of Terror](/wiki/Eye_of_Terror "Eye of Terror"), [Twins of Terror](/wiki/Twins_of_Terror "Twins of Terror"), [Nightmare Werepig](/wiki/Nightmare_Werepig "Nightmare Werepig"), [Crystal Deerclops](/wiki/Crystal_Deerclops "Crystal Deerclops"), [Armored Bearger](/wiki/Armored_Bearger "Armored Bearger"), [Possessed Varg](/wiki/Possessed_Varg "Possessed Varg"), and [Frostjaw](/wiki/Frostjaw "Frostjaw").

## Usage[]

Sketches can be given to a [Potter's Wheel](/wiki/Potter%27s_Wheel "Potter's Wheel") to unlock a specific [Chess Piece](/wiki/Chess_Pieces "Chess Pieces") for crafting. Once the Sketch is given to a Potter's Wheel, the figure becomes available to any player near the Potter's Wheel. The Sketch itself is just about the shape of the piece to be crafted, the color of the crafted pieces will be determined based on the materials used to craft it, where using [Marble](/wiki/Marble "Marble") results in a white piece, using [Cut Stone](/wiki/Cut_Stone "Cut Stone") results in a black piece, and using a [Moon Shard](/wiki/Moon_Shard "Moon Shard") results in a glassy blue piece. Note that destroying a Potter's Wheel which has taken any Sketches will also drop the Sketches.

Sketches can be used at a [Cartographer's Desk](/wiki/Cartographer%27s_Desk "Cartographer's Desk") to get one [Papyrus](/wiki/Papyrus "Papyrus"). All Sketches have a fuel value of 15 seconds.

## Trivia[]

* The actual drawing on many Sketches' icons is of an unmined Bishop Sculpture. The [Clay Varg](/wiki/Clay_Varg "Clay Varg") figure sketch was introduced with a unique icon, and of all sketches introduced since then, only the [Eye of Terror](/wiki/Eye_of_Terror "Eye of Terror") and [Twins of Terror](/wiki/Twins_of_Terror "Twins of Terror") figure sketches have the same generic icon as earlier sketches.
* Sketches were introduced in the *[Arts and Crafts](/wiki/A_New_Reign#October_13th,_2016_-_Arts_and_Crafts "A New Reign")* update.
