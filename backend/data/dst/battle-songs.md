*Exclusive to:* Don't Starve Together

*https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/dont-starve-game/images/c/cb/Glommer.png/revision/latest?cb=20231029201751 See also: [Wigfrid](/wiki/Wigfrid "Wigfrid")*

**Battle Songs** are ten [Wigfrid](/wiki/Wigfrid "Wigfrid")-exclusive [items](/wiki/Items "Items") craftable in the [Survivor Items Filter](/wiki/Survivor_Items_Filter "Survivor Items Filter") in *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*. They can be used to give nearby players boosts to their abilities when her Inspiration meter goes up, and others can be used for an instant effect at the cost of Inspiration. They're not destroyed on use. Attempting to sing a Battle Song without an effect slot free or enough Inspiration to cast the instant effect will result in a special animation being played. The effects are cumulative if several Wigfrid players sing the same song.

## Tips[]

* Clear Minded Cadenza and especially Bel Canto of Courage can be very effective at combating enemies with strong Sanity-draining auras, such as [Deerclops](/wiki/Deerclops "Deerclops"), [Shadow Pieces](/wiki/Shadow_Pieces "Shadow Pieces"), or the [Ancient Fuelweaver](/wiki/Ancient_Fuelweaver "Ancient Fuelweaver").
* Rude Interlude can be useful when fighting [Bee Queen](/wiki/Bee_Queen "Bee Queen"), [Dragonfly](/wiki/Dragonfly "Dragonfly"), and other bosses that summon backup, allowing Wigfrid to aggro their minions and protect other players.
* Fireproof Falsetto can protect the team against fires, an enraged Dragonfly, [Lavae](/wiki/Lavae "Lavae") and [Klaus's](/wiki/Klaus "Klaus") [Red Gem Deer](/wiki/Gem_Deer "Gem Deer") spell.
* Startling Soliloquy can be helpful to scare off [Grumble Bees](/wiki/Grumble_Bee "Grumble Bee") during the Bee Queen fight or to prevent Klaus's Gem Deer from casting magic for a time.
* Heartrending Ballad works well with the [Bat Bat](/wiki/Bat_Bat "Bat Bat") to leech health every time Wigfrid hits a foe.
* With Wigfrid's life steal, the Heartrending Ballad, and powerful armor such as a [Marble Suit](/wiki/Marble_Suit "Marble Suit"), it is possible to tank even raid bosses such as [Dragonfly](/wiki/Dragonfly "Dragonfly") while staying at constant max health.
* Weaponized Warble only works for weapons that lose durability by use. It won't affect tools, [Darts](/wiki/Blow_Dart "Blow Dart"), the [Morning Star](/wiki/Morning_Star "Morning Star"), or the [Ham Bat](/wiki/Ham_Bat "Ham Bat").
