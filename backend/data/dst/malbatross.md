The **Malbatross** is a [Boss](/wiki/Category:Boss_Monsters "Category:Boss Monsters") [Mob](/wiki/Mob "Mob") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")*.

 https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/dont-starve-game/images/f/ff/Deep Bass Shoal Map Icon.png/revision/latest?cb=20190915002941 https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/dont-starve-game/images/f/ff/Deep Bass Shoal Map Icon.png/revision/latest?cb=20190915002941 

Deep Bass Shoal map icon.

 

Only 25% of the [Deep Bass](/wiki/Ocean_Fish#Deep_Bass "Ocean Fish") shoals with a map icon can spawn the Malbatross when visited. After 3 days, the Malbatross despawns and again, 25% of the Deep Bass shoals are randomly selected to spawn the Malbatross. The Malbatross also has a 10% chance to spawn when a player hooks a Deep Bass. The Malbatross respawns 5 to 15 days after dying.

Upon death, the Malbatross drops 7 [Meats](/wiki/Meat "Meat"), 2 [Blue Gems](/wiki/Blue_Gem "Blue Gem") plus 30% to get one extra, 5% of a [Yellow Gem](/wiki/Yellow_Gem "Yellow Gem"), one [Malbatross Bill](/wiki/Malbatross_Bill "Malbatross Bill"), 3 [Malbatross Feathers](/wiki/Malbatross_Feather "Malbatross Feather") plus two times 30% to get one extra plus 24 minus the amount spawned by hitting the Malbatross.

## Behavior[]

The Malbatross is neutral towards players. It does attack [Hostile](/wiki/Category:Hostile_Creatures "Category:Hostile Creatures") mobs that come near it. Fishing deep bass in the shoal when the Malbatross spawns in will cause it to become aggressive.

When not fighting, it moves around and dives to catch [Ocean Fishes](/wiki/Ocean_Fish "Ocean Fish"), creating a circle of [Waves](/wiki/Waves "Waves"). Waves will push away a [Boat](/wiki/Boats "Boats") and apply 9 Wetness to player.

The normal attack of the Malbatross deals 75 damage to players and 150 to Mobs. If the Malbatross is over a boat, the attack also does 5 damage to the boat and pushes it away. If the Malbatross is over the [Ocean](/wiki/Ocean "Ocean") the attack also creates 3 waves. Each hit on the Malbatross can drop up to 4 Malbatross Feathers, each feather having a 5% chance of dropping.

When the Malbatross' health goes below 66%, it will dive and run away from the player, creating a circle of waves. When the Malbatross' health goes below 33%, it can swoop over the player, destroying every [Mast](/wiki/Mast "Mast") in its wake. Each Mast destroyed spawns 3 Malbatross Feathers plus two times 30% to get one extra.

## Tips[]

* If the player wishes to deliberately hunt down Malbatross, using an [Ocean Trawler](/wiki/Ocean_Trawler "Ocean Trawler") with [Monster Meat](/wiki/Monster_Meat "Monster Meat")/other [Meats](/wiki/Meats "Meats") as bait over a Deep Bass Shoal almost instantly spawns it.
* Keeping the boat's anchored is strongly recommended, so that Malbatross has substantially more trouble pushing the vessel around.
* Ensuring Malbatross stays within melee range is the key to winning the fight.
  + Hitting Malbatross 5-6 times before running to the other side of the boat will generally keep it within range, without it damaging the player with its normal attack in the process.
  + In addition to the player's melee weapons, a [Pan Flute](/wiki/Pan_Flute "Pan Flute") or enough [Blow Darts](/wiki/Blow_Dart "Blow Dart") is recommended for hitting the Malbatross at a range, in order to start the fight or incase it attempts to fly away from the player.
* The player will likely need a separate boat from their main boat for fighting Malbatross, incase the fight goes badly and the boat is lost or sunk.
  + If this strategy is used, the boat should have an [Anchor](/wiki/Anchor_Kit "Anchor Kit") (but no [Mast](/wiki/Mast "Mast"), as Malbatross may destroy it), a chest with enough [Boat Patches](/wiki/Boat_Patch "Boat Patch") and wood for oars/repairing the boat, a [Fire Pit](/wiki/Fire_Pit "Fire Pit") for light and warmth, and optionally a [Tin Fishin' Bin](/wiki/Tin_Fishin%27_Bin "Tin Fishin' Bin") for bringing back the collected fish from the Ocean Trawler.

## Trivia[]

* Malbatross was introduced in the *[Salty Dog](/wiki/Return_of_Them#Version_History "Return of Them")* beta update.
* The name Malbatross is likely a portmanteau of "marlin" and "albatross" given the Malbatross' blue color and having both fish and bird characteristics.
  + It could additionally be a portmanteau of "malicious" and "albatross" given its hostile nature.
* Warly's quote also points out that the first syllable of its name sounds like the french word for "bad" or "evil".
* It no longer drops an [Advert](/wiki/Advert "Advert") as of the [March 2022 QoL update](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together/Version_History#March_24,_2022_-_March_2022_QoL_Update "Don't Starve Together/Version History").
