The **Books Tab** is a special tab that is only available to [Wickerbottom](/wiki/Wickerbottom "Wickerbottom"). It allows the player to craft Books, which can be used to activate several events at the cost of [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity"). Each book has multiple uses.

## Craftable Items[]

The following items can be crafted in the Books Tab:

[Birds of the World](/wiki/Birds_of_the_World "Birds of the World")Only craftable by WickerbottomBirds of the World
