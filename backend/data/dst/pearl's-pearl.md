**Pearl's Pearl** is an item exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")*. It can be obtained from the [Crabby Hermit](/wiki/Crabby_Hermit "Crabby Hermit") by following her questline.

Its main purpose is to be socketed into the [Crab King](/wiki/Crab_King "Crab King") for the boss fight. Socketing the pearl buffs the Crab King significantly, but it makes the King drop the [Inactive Celestial Tribute](/wiki/Inactive_Celestial_Tribute "Inactive Celestial Tribute"). When the Crab King is defeated, Pearl's Pearl turns into a **Cracked Pearl**.
