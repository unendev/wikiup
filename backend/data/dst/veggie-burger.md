**Veggie Burger** is a [Meat](/wiki/Meats "Meats") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in the *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")* updates. It is cooked in the [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") by combining 1 [Leafy Meat](/wiki/Leafy_Meat "Leafy Meat"), 1 [Onion](/wiki/Onion "Onion") and [Vegetables](/wiki/Vegetable "Vegetable") with a value of at least 1.0. It takes 40 seconds to cook and 6 [Days](/wiki/Day-Night_Cycle "Day-Night Cycle") to [Spoil](/wiki/Spoil "Spoil").

When consumed, it restores 30 Health, 37.5 Hunger and 33 Sanity.

Despite being a "Veggie Burger", it is actually considered as a meat item, thus, it is passable as food for [Wigfrid](/wiki/Wigfrid "Wigfrid"). However, [Wurt](/wiki/Wurt "Wurt") will still refuse to eat it.

## Tips[]

* If one has a [Lureplant](/wiki/Meat_Bulb_(Lureplant) "Meat Bulb (Lureplant)") farm and a reliable way of obtaining Onions, Veggie Burgers can be a solid balanced food option. However, [Beefy Greens](/wiki/Beefy_Greens "Beefy Greens") might be preferable for health and hunger while [Jelly Salad](/wiki/Jelly_Salad "Jelly Salad") provides more sanity.
