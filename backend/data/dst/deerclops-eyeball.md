**Deerclops Eyeball** is a [food](/wiki/Food "Food") [item](/wiki/Item "Item") dropped by the [Deerclops](/wiki/Deerclops "Deerclops"). It is similar to a [Guardian Horn](/wiki/Guardian%27s_Horn "Guardian's Horn"). Both are dropped by a boss, unable to spoil, and restore health and hunger but decrease sanity.

As a [meat](/wiki/Meats "Meats") item, [Pigs](/wiki/Pig "Pig"), [Spiders](/wiki/Spider "Spider"), or [Pengulls](/wiki/Pengull "Pengull") will eat it if left on the ground, and [Bunnymen](/wiki/Bunnymen "Bunnymen") will attack the player on sight if they are carrying it. The eyeball does not spoil and cannot be [Cooked](/wiki/Cooking "Cooking").

## Usage[]

A Deerclops Eyeball can be used as a crafting material; it is required to make the [Houndius Shootius](/wiki/Houndius_Shootius "Houndius Shootius"). It can also be used as emergency food, granting 60 Health, 75 Hunger, and draining 15 Sanity.

## Downloadable Content[]

In all [DLCs](/wiki/DLC "DLC"), as well as *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, the Deerclops Eyeball can now stack up to 10 in the player's [Inventory](/wiki/Inventory "Inventory").

### Hamlet Hamlet[]

In the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Hamlet "Hamlet")* DLC, Deerclops Eyeballs can be bought for 50 [Oincs](/wiki/Oinc "Oinc") at ['The Sty' Oddities Emporium](/wiki/%27The_Sty%27_Oddities_Emporium "'The Sty' Oddities Emporium").

### Don't Starve Together Don't Starve Together[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, the Deerclops Eyeball can additionally be obtained from the [Loot Stash](/wiki/Loot_Stash "Loot Stash"). It can also be put in an [Ice Crystaleyezer](/wiki/Ice_Crystaleyezer "Ice Crystaleyezer") to activate its functions.

## Tips[]

* The [Eyebrella](/wiki/Eyebrella "Eyebrella") is typically crafted with the first Deerclops Eyeball. It completely protects against rain and lightning, making [Spring](/wiki/Spring "Spring") much more survivable. It is also one of the most effective ways to delay [Overheating](/wiki/Overheating "Overheating") in [Summer](/wiki/Summer "Summer"). If used very sparingly, an Eyebrella may last most of a year. It can last indefinitely if repaired in a timely manner with a [Sewing Kit](/wiki/Sewing_Kit "Sewing Kit") (restores 55.5% durability).
* The [Hostile Flare](/wiki/Hostile_Flare "Hostile Flare") can be used to summon Deerclops more than once per [Winter](/wiki/Winter "Winter"), so that additional eyeballs can be gained that year.

## Trivia[]

* The Deerclops Eyeball was added in *[A Winter's Tale](/wiki/Version_History#A_Winter's_Tale "Version History")* update.
* The Deerclops Eyeball shares the same stats with the Guardians Horn.
