**Cut Stone** is a [Refined](/wiki/Refined "Refined") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") crafted with 3 [Rocks](/wiki/Rocks "Rocks"). A [Science Machine](/wiki/Science_Machine "Science Machine") is required to unlock the recipe. Cut Stone is used to craft various [Structures](/wiki/Structures "Structures").

## Don't Starve Together Don't Starve Together[]

Cut Stone can be used on a [Potter's Wheel](/wiki/Potter%27s_Wheel "Potter's Wheel") to produce [Sculptures](/wiki/Chess_Pieces "Chess Pieces").

Also Cut Stone can be crafted by Wilson with 1 [Marble](/wiki/Marble "Marble") if he learned the [Transmute Ore Ⅲ](/wiki/Wilson/Don%27t_Starve_Together#Transmute_Ore "Wilson/Don't Starve Together") skill.

## Tips[]

* Cut Stone stacks up to 10. A full stack of Cut Stone takes a total of 30 Rocks to craft, thus it is less space-efficient than storing a full stack of Rocks (40 max).

## Trivia[]

* Cut Stone was used to build [Ice Boxes](/wiki/Ice_Box "Ice Box") before being replaced with [Gears](/wiki/Gears "Gears").
* In *[Reign of Giants](/wiki/Reign_of_Giants "Reign of Giants")*, players use Cut Stone instead of [Boards](/wiki/Boards "Boards") in some recipes. This is to keep some structures and items nonflammable.
* In the old research system, the [Research Cost](/wiki/Research_points_list "Research points list") of a Cut Stone was 30.
