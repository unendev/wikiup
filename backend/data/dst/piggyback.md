The **Piggyback** is a type of Backpack which requires 4 [Pig Skins](/wiki/Pig_Skin "Pig Skin"), 6 [Silk](/wiki/Silk "Silk"), and 2 [Ropes](/wiki/Rope "Rope") to craft and an [Alchemy Engine](/wiki/Alchemy_Engine "Alchemy Engine") to prototype. It is a larger Backpack which has 12 [Inventory](/wiki/Inventory "Inventory") slots but reduces walking [speed](/wiki/Player_Speed "Player Speed") by 10% (20% in vanilla Don't Starve) while equipped. It has 4 more slots than a Backpack, but 2 less than the [Krampus Sack](/wiki/Krampus_Sack "Krampus Sack"). It cannot be placed in the inventory or in Chests. Unlike the regular [backpack](/wiki/Backpack "Backpack"), it is fireproof.

## Downloadable Content[]

In all [DLCs](/wiki/DLC "DLC") and *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, the Piggyback and its contents are [waterproof](/wiki/Wetness "Wetness") and will not cause sanity drain while equipped. The speed penalty is also reduced from -20% to -10%.

## Tips[]

* This is a good replacement for the [backpack](/wiki/Backpack "Backpack") until you get the [Krampus Sack](/wiki/Krampus_Sack "Krampus Sack").
* Using a [Walking Cane](/wiki/Walking_Cane "Walking Cane"), roads and other means of speed increase can mitigate or negate the speed penalty.
* In the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Hamlet "Hamlet")* DLC, the speed penalty can be mitigated with the [Stalking Stick](/wiki/Stalking_Stick "Stalking Stick").
* The Piggyback does not give the player any rain resistance, and using it in conjunction with an [Umbrella](/wiki/Umbrella "Umbrella") will not protect the rest of the player's items from wetness.
* In *Don't Starve Together,* [Wolfgang](/wiki/Wolfgang/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Wolfgang/Don't Starve Together"), when in Mighty form, ignores the item's speed penalty, making Piggyback a direct upgrade from the Backpack to him.
* In *Don't Starve Together,* [Wormwood](/wiki/Wormwood/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Wormwood/Don't Starve Together") gains +6/13/20% Movement speed when in stages 1/2/3 of Bloom, reducing or ignoring the -10% from the Piggyback.
* In *Don't Starve Together,* [Woodie](/wiki/Woodie/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Woodie/Don't Starve Together") doesn't suffer from the Piggyback's speed penalty, but only when he is transformed in one of his [Wereforms](/wiki/Cursed_Woodie "Cursed Woodie"). This makes the Piggyback useful to equip for extra storage, when going out to gather lots of items using the Wereforms, such as logs and loot from [Lunar Island.](/wiki/Lunar_Island "Lunar Island")

## Trivia[]

* The Piggyback was added in the *[Doorway to Adventure](/wiki/Version_History#March_26,_2013_-_Doorway_to_Adventure "Version History")* update.
* The Piggyback's name is a pun, on both the fact it is a backpack made from a pig's skin and on the action of giving a piggyback.
