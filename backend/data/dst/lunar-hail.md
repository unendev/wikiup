*Exclusive to:* Don't Starve Together

**Lunar Hail** is a naturally occurring event exclusive to *Don't Starve Together*, introduced in the *[From Beyond](/wiki/From_Beyond "From Beyond")* updates. Like [Rain](/wiki/Rain "Rain"), it will occur periodically after [Lunar Rifts](/wiki/Rift_Cycle "Rift Cycle") have been activated and can last up to 90 seconds. Instead of rain drops, [Moon Shards](/wiki/Moon_Shard "Moon Shard") and [Infused Moon Shards](/wiki/Infused_Moon_Shard "Infused Moon Shard") will fall from the sky, dealing 30 damage when hitting a player. Players can avoid hail damage completely while standing under any [Tree](/wiki/Tree "Tree"), using an [Umbralla](/wiki/Umbralla "Umbralla") or wearing [Bone Armor](/wiki/Bone_Armor "Bone Armor") or a [Hardwood Hat](/wiki/Hardwood_Hat "Hardwood Hat"). Unlike [Earthquakes](/wiki/Earthquake "Earthquake") in the [Caves](/wiki/Caves "Caves"), all [Mobs](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs") are currently immune to hail damage.

The falling Moon Shards and Infused Moon Shards have a chance of 25% to remain on the ground for 5 seconds, making this an alternative to [Moonstorms](/wiki/Moonstorm "Moonstorm") for obtaining Infused Moon Shards.

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* Lunar Hail was introduced in the *[Host of Horrors](/wiki/From_Beyond#Host_of_Horrors "From Beyond")* update.
* In the early beta Lunar Hail also dealt damage to mobs. Klei removed this because too many mobs died during Lunar Hail, but stated it could come back when they find a way to protect mobs from the damage.
