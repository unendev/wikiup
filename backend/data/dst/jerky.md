**Jerky** is a product of drying raw [Meat](/wiki/Meat "Meat") on the [Drying Rack](/wiki/Drying_Rack "Drying Rack") for two game days. Jerky has a low rate of spoilage of 20 days, and restores 25 [Hunger](/wiki/Hunger "Hunger"), 20 [Health](/wiki/Health "Health"), and 15 [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity"). Jerky provides the same amount of Hunger, but more Health and Sanity than its raw or cooked counterparts. Jerky can also be used in a [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") as normal [Meat](/wiki/Meat "Meat"). See [general meat uses](/wiki/Meats#Usage "Meats"). Unharvested Jerky will not start to spoil as long it is left on the Drying Rack, making it possible to keep it fresh for an indefinite amount of time.

Due to the fact that Jerky restores Health, Hunger and Sanity at the same time, this item can be useful for recovering from [Woodie's](/wiki/Woodie "Woodie") Werebeaver transformation. The same effect can be achieved with [Small Jerky](/wiki/Small_Jerky "Small Jerky"), albeit, with weaker restorative properties than normal Jerky.

## Downloadable Content[]

In the *[Reign of Giants](/wiki/Reign_of_Giants "Reign of Giants")* and *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLCs, Jerky will begin to spoil immediately upon creation on a Drying Rack, regardless of whether it is harvested or not. This also applies to [Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together").

Additionally, in the *Shipwrecked* DLC, large [Fish](/wiki/Fishes "Fishes") items such as [Dogfish](/wiki/Dogfish "Dogfish"), [Raw Fish](/wiki/Raw_Fish "Raw Fish") and [Swordfish](/wiki/Swordfish "Swordfish") can be dried to obtain Jerky, allowing them to be used in the [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") as Meat instead.

In the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC, it has a 67% chance to spawn Jerky from [Secret Bandit Camp](/wiki/Secret_Bandit_Camp "Secret Bandit Camp") and have a 4% chance to spawn from [A Smashing Pot](/wiki/A_Smashing_Pot "A Smashing Pot").

## Tips[]

* Without Crock Pots, Jerky is the meat food with the highest Sanity restoration. This makes it great for restoring sanity for [Wickerbottom](/wiki/Wickerbottom "Wickerbottom"), who can't sleep due to her insomnia, and [Wigfrid](/wiki/Wigfrid "Wigfrid"), who can only eat meat-based food.
* As Jerky does not technically count as a cooked item, [Wagstaff](/wiki/Wagstaff "Wagstaff") will still suffer a 3 Health penalty from consuming it, bringing the total healing down to 17 Health.
* Unless one wants to make a [Meat Effigy](/wiki/Meat_Effigy "Meat Effigy") or [Ham Bat](/wiki/Ham_Bat "Ham Bat"), it is a good idea to convert all Meat into Jerky, as Meat in a [Salt Box](/wiki/Salt_Box "Salt Box") will last up to 24 days (12 Days in an [Ice Box](/wiki/Ice_Box "Ice Box")), whereas Jerky in an Ice Box lasts for 40 Days.
