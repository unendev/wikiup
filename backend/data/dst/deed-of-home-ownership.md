The **Deed of home ownership** is a purchasable [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC. It can be purchased at the [Swinesbury City Hall](/wiki/Pig_Shops#Swinesbury_City_Hall "Pig Shops") for 50 [Oincs](/wiki/Oinc "Oinc"), making the [Slanty Shanty](/wiki/Slanty_Shanty "Slanty Shanty") available to access by the player. The Deed of home ownership can be read to reveal the Slanty Shanty's location on the [Map](/wiki/Map "Map").
