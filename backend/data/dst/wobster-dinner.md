**Wobster Dinner** is a [Meat](/wiki/Meats "Meats") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") introduced in the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC. It is cooked in the [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") by combining a [Wobster](/wiki/Wobster "Wobster") and [Butter](/wiki/Butter "Butter"). It takes 20 seconds to cook and 15 [Days](/wiki/Day-Night_Cycle "Day-Night Cycle") to [Spoil](/wiki/Spoil "Spoil").

When consumed, it restores 60 Health, 37.5 Hunger and 50 Sanity.

Adding any Meats to the recipe will produce [Meatballs](/wiki/Meatballs "Meatballs") instead. Adding any additional [Fish](/wiki/Fishes "Fishes") with meat value will result in [Seafood Gumbo](/wiki/Seafood_Gumbo "Seafood Gumbo") instead, unless a [Twig](/wiki/Twigs "Twigs") is also added, which may result in [Fishsticks](/wiki/Fishsticks "Fishsticks"). Adding Ice will result in [Wobster Bisque](/wiki/Lobster_Bisque "Lobster Bisque") instead. Adding [Shark Fin](/wiki/Shark_Fin "Shark Fin") will result in [Shark Fin Soup](/wiki/Shark_Fin_Soup "Shark Fin Soup") instead.

This recipe has a higher [Crock Pot Priority](/wiki/Crock_Pot#Priority "Crock Pot") than Monster Lasagna, therefore it allows using more than one [Durians](/wiki/Durian "Durian") and/or [Jellyfishes](/wiki/Jellyfish "Jellyfish") as fillers. However, Monster Meat cannot be used due to recipe restriction of not allowing meats.

## Don't Starve Together Don't Starve Together[]

**Wobster Dinner** was introduced in *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")* along the *[She Sells Sea Shells](/wiki/She_Sells_Sea_Shells "She Sells Sea Shells")* update*.* The dish is similar to the *Shipwrecked* version, except that adding any Fishes will result in Meatballs, and it looks darker than the *Shipwrecked* counterpart.

[Warly](/wiki/Warly "Warly")'s quote for Wobster Dinner has been changed to *"No place is too remote for some fine dining!"*, likely due to his original quote referencing the setting of *Shipwrecked*.

It is [Maxwell](/wiki/Maxwell "Maxwell")'s [favourite food](/wiki/Food#Favorite_Food "Food") and will restore extra 15 Hunger when consumed.

## Tips[]

* Wobster Dinner is a good food choice if the player has a butterfly farm and a reliable source of Wobsters, thanks to its high [Health](/wiki/Health "Health") and [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity") benefits.
* If Sanity is not an issue, it may be a better idea to instead use the Butter to make [Waffles](/wiki/Waffles "Waffles"). Waffles heal the same amount of Health and Hunger (unless you are playing as Maxwell) and uses ingredients that are much more abundant than Wobsters.
  + [Wigfrid](/wiki/Wigfrid "Wigfrid") cannot eat Waffles, and [Wurt](/wiki/Wurt "Wurt") cannot eat Wobster Dinner, so plan accordingly based on what Characters are currently being used.
