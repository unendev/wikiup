The **Codex Umbra Filter** was a [Filter](/wiki/Crafting#Crafting_Filter "Crafting") in the crafting menu for *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")* that was only available to [Maxwell](/wiki/Maxwell "Maxwell"). Here, the player could craft the [Codex Umbra](/wiki/Codex_Umbra "Codex Umbra"), which itself unlocked the ability to bend [Nightmare Fuel](/wiki/Nightmare_Fuel "Nightmare Fuel") into the 4 different variants of [Shadow Puppets](/wiki/Shadow_Puppet "Shadow Puppet").

## Craftable Recipes[]

The following recipes could be crafted in the Codex Umbra Filter:

[Shadow Logger](/wiki/Shadow_Logger "Shadow Logger")Only craftable by MaxwellShadow Logger

[Shadow Miner](/wiki/Shadow_Miner "Shadow Miner")Only craftable by MaxwellShadow Miner

[Shadow Digger](/wiki/Shadow_Digger "Shadow Digger")Only craftable by MaxwellShadow Digger

[Shadow Duelist](/wiki/Shadow_Duelist "Shadow Duelist")Only craftable by MaxwellShadow Duelist
