In *Hamlet*, Dark Tatters are obtained from the [Ancient Herald](/wiki/Ancient_Herald "Ancient Herald"), spawned in during the [Aporkalypse](/wiki/Aporkalypse "Aporkalypse"). They are used to craft and refuel the [Vortex Cloak](/wiki/Vortex_Cloak "Vortex Cloak"), repairing 100% of its Durability. It may also be used to craft the [Nightmarish Bird Whistle](/wiki/Nightmarish_Bird_Whistle "Nightmarish Bird Whistle").

They are obtained by defeating any of the three [Ink Blight](/wiki/Ink_Blight "Ink Blight") that spawn from [Shadow Rifts](/wiki/Rift "Rift").

## Trivia[]

* In the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC, Dark Tatters can be offered to the [Volcano Altar of Snackrifice](/wiki/Volcano_Altar_of_Snackrifice "Volcano Altar of Snackrifice") during [Dry Season](/wiki/Seasons/Dry "Seasons/Dry") to appease the [Volcano](/wiki/Volcano "Volcano") for 32 day segments, or 2 in-game days. This may be a suitable alternative use.
* Its name may be a reference to [Dark Matters](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dark_Matters "wikipedia:Dark Matters").
* Prior to the *[Terrors Below](/wiki/Terrors_Below "Terrors Below")* update to DST, Dark Tatters were only found in Hamlet, making them one of few items that has been transposed into DST from the DLC.
  + Dark Tatters in *Hamlet* compared to DST have a large cosmetic difference, with the *Hamlet* tatters being more akin to black, bat-shaped pieces of cloth, whereas the tatters found in DST are red, making them appear as the red cowls the Ancient Herald and Inkblights wear
