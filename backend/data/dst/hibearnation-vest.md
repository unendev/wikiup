The **Hibearnation Vest** is a [Dress](/wiki/Dress "Dress") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") in the *[Reign of Giants](/wiki/Reign_of_Giants "Reign of Giants")* DLC. It costs 1 [Thick Fur](/wiki/Thick_Fur "Thick Fur"), 1 [Dapper Vest](/wiki/Dapper_Vest "Dapper Vest"), and 2 [Ropes](/wiki/Rope "Rope") to craft, and requires an [Alchemy Engine](/wiki/Alchemy_Engine "Alchemy Engine") to prototype.

The Hibearnation Vest is *tier 3* warm clothing, with an [insulation factor](/wiki/Freezing#mechanics "Freezing") of 240. It restores 4.4 [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity")/min and reduces the rate [Hunger](/wiki/Hunger "Hunger") is lost by 25% when equipped. Characters that lose Hunger at a normal rate of 75/day will, instead, lose Hunger at a rate of 56.3/day. [Wolfgang](/wiki/Wolfgang "Wolfgang")'s normal form will lose 84.4/day while his mighty form will lose 168.8/day, and [Wes](/wiki/Wes "Wes") will lose 70.3/day (only in DS).

A Hibearnation Vest lasts for 7 days when worn, and a [Sewing Kit](/wiki/Sewing_Kit "Sewing Kit") can be used to repair 71% of its [Durability](/wiki/Durability "Durability") each use.

## Gift Icon Downloadable Content[]

In the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Hamlet "Hamlet")* DLC, the Hibearnation Vest crafting recipe is unavailable, however, a [Blueprint](/wiki/Blueprint "Blueprint") for it can be bought with 50 [Oincs](/wiki/Oinc "Oinc") from [The Tinkerer's Tower](/wiki/The_Tinkerer%27s_Tower "The Tinkerer's Tower"). The Thick Fur is available at ['The Sty' Oddities Emporium](/wiki/%27The_Sty%27_Oddities_Emporium "'The Sty' Oddities Emporium").

## PrototypeTips[]

* The Hibearnation Vest is the second best Sanity restoring item in the game, the best one being the [Tam o' Shanter](/wiki/Tam_o%27_Shanter "Tam o' Shanter") and, in the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Shipwrecked "Shipwrecked")* DLC, the [Shark Tooth Crown](/wiki/Shark_Tooth_Crown "Shark Tooth Crown"). It's also the best sanity restoring item for the chest slot.
* Wearing the Hibearnation Vest will mostly negate the [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity") loss during [Dusk](/wiki/Dusk "Dusk") or [Night](/wiki/Night "Night"), and while in the [Caves](/wiki/Caves "Caves") or [Ruins](/wiki/Ruins "Ruins"). While equipped the sanity loss from darkness is -0.6/min, a value the game considers too small to show a sanity loss indicator. This, combined with the delay of hunger loss and insulation factor, makes the vest highly useful when exploring the Caves and Ruins in [Autumn](/wiki/Autumn "Autumn") or [Winter](/wiki/Winter "Winter").
* It is not advisable to spend one`s Oincs on the blueprint of the Hibearnation Vest though since the recipe for the Dapper Vest which is needed for crafting is also not available in Hamlet, nor can its blueprint be acquired at The Sty.
* The vest is used at it's best during winter because of the protection from freezing and the decreased hunger loss.
* Despite having a durability of only 7 days compared to the the [Puffy Vest](/wiki/Puffy_Vest "Puffy Vest")'s 15, a sewing kit will restore almost the same amount of durability to both items (4.97 and 4.95 days respectively). Provided access to a sewing kit every few days, this makes the Hibearnation Vest the superior choice thanks to its hunger drain reduction and sanity gain.

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* "Hibearnation" is a play on the words "Hibernation" and "Bear".
* [Maxwell's](/wiki/Maxwell "Maxwell") and Walani's examination quote references a *Simpsons* episode where Mr. Burns shows off his collection of clothes made from rare animals.
* The Hibearnation Vest used to be dropped by Bearger directly, without needing to be crafted.
