The **Grim Galette** is a [Warly](/wiki/Warly "Warly")-specific [Vegetable](/wiki/Vegetable "Vegetable") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") cooked in the [Portable Crock Pot](/wiki/Portable_Crock_Pot "Portable Crock Pot") that is exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*. It requires two [Nightmare Fuel](/wiki/Nightmare_Fuel "Nightmare Fuel"), a [Potato](/wiki/Potato "Potato") and an [Onion](/wiki/Onion "Onion") to cook. Upon consuming a Grim Galette, the player's current [Health](/wiki/Health "Health") and [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity") percentage values are swapped.

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* Grim Galette was added to *Don't Starve Together* in Warly's [Character Update](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together/Version_History#July_25,_2019_-_Warly_Character_Update "Don't Starve Together/Version History").
* The Grim Galette and [Mushy Cake](/wiki/Mushy_Cake "Mushy Cake") are the only dishes in Don't Starve Together with only one possible recipe.
* Wearing a [Nightmare Amulet](/wiki/Nightmare_Amulet "Nightmare Amulet") or [Bone Helm](/wiki/Bone_Helm "Bone Helm") and eating a Grim Galette will cause instant death.
* Grim Galette, besides [Jellybeans](/wiki/Jellybeans "Jellybeans"), is the only food item that can be used to restore [Wormwood](/wiki/Wormwood "Wormwood")'s health.
* Grim Galette will not swap sanity and age of [Wanda](/wiki/Wanda "Wanda").
* [Galette](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Galette "wikipedia:Galette") is a term used in French cuisine to designate various types of flat round or freeform crusty cakes.
* Despite the name of this item, its appearance and ingredients used are more similar to [butter pie](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Butter_pie "wikipedia:Butter pie") than true galettes, with the Nightmare Fuel in the place of the butter.
