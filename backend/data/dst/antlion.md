The **Antlion** is a [Mob](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[A New Reign](/wiki/A_New_Reign "A New Reign")*. It appears in the [Desert](/wiki/Desert "Desert") biome during [Summer](/wiki/Seasons/Summer "Seasons/Summer") Season. The players can either appease or kill it, otherwise, it creates sinkholes on the surface and causes cave-ins when a player goes inside the caves. The Antlion can be given a stage 1, 4 or 5 [Thermal Stone](/wiki/Thermal_Stone "Thermal Stone") to start combat.

## Behavior[]

When the Antlion first spawns in Summer, when there is no rain and the ground is dry, players have 20-16 seconds to pay tribute before it disturbs the players. When the attack is complete, the Antlion's disturbance timer will become either at most 480 seconds or 80% of the previous calm duration.

During rain, the Antlion will temporarily burrow itself underground, disabling players from interacting with it.

### Tribute[]

Players can contribute the following items to delay the Antlion's disturbance:

| 1.32 | Beach Toy |
| 2.98 | Thermal Stone Thermal Stone |

For example, if one gives the Antlion an Egg, it would delay its attacks for 158 seconds, or 0.33 day. Giving the Antlion 10 rocks is cheaper than a Thermal Stone because the 10 rocks delay the disturbance timer by 3.3 days, while a Thermal Stone (stages 2 and 3) only delays the disturbance timer by 2.98 days, plus costing 2 [Twigs](/wiki/Twigs "Twigs") and 5 [Flint](/wiki/Flint "Flint") to craft. One should keep in mind that its disturbance timer cannot exceed 6 days, so players may need to contribute a hefty amount of items to it at least twice throughout Summer in order to never be attacked, if players are going the appeasement route.

When the Antlion is given anything other than Rocks, Cut Stone, or Thermal Stone, it will give a [Desert Stone](/wiki/Desert_Stone "Desert Stone"). If it is given a [Beach Toy](/wiki/Beach_Toy "Beach Toy"), it will give a blueprint for [The Lazy Deserter](/wiki/The_Lazy_Deserter "The Lazy Deserter") as well as the [Turf-Raiser Helm](/wiki/Turf-Raiser_Helm "Turf-Raiser Helm") but no Desert Stone.

### Summer Disturbances[]

When the timer is up, there will be 3 warnings to let the players know that Sinkholes or Cave-ins will start to spawn soon. Up to 2 players can be targeted by the Antlion in a single attack with between 2-5 sinkholes if on the surface or 6-10 boulders if in the Caves spawning around each of them.

**Sinkholes** are craters that are created by the Antlion during summer when displeased. When they are created, they damage players and destroy [Structures](/wiki/Structures "Structures") where they form. Once formed, new structures cannot be built on them and they'll slow down all players walking on them. Sinkholes cannot be removed by the player and will only begin to self-repair after 21-22 days since they were formed, taking 9-11 more days to fully repair. Players can prevent them from appearing by contributing items to the Antlion (listed above).

Characters also say different quotes depending on how long before the sinkholes happen.

#### Cave-ins[]

**Cave-ins**, similar to Sinkholes, happen in the [Caves](/wiki/Caves "Caves") when the Antlion is displeased. Big [boulders](/wiki/Cave-in_Boulder "Cave-in Boulder") will drop on locations close to the players inside caves, damaging them if hit, destroying any Structures nearby and potentially blocking the way. Like Sinkholes, they can be prevented if the players occasionally give items to the Antlion. The boulders that drop on Cave-ins can be carried, much like [Suspicious Marble](/wiki/Suspicious_Marble "Suspicious Marble"), and can even be brought outside the caves. They can also be mined for rocks using a [Pickaxe](/wiki/Pickaxe "Pickaxe").

**Sand Castles and Sand spikes**

The Sand Spikes and Sand Castle, in increasing sizes, has 25, 65, 100, 250 HP. They last for 6-7, 7-8, 8-10, 15-16 seconds. Any structures placed/built that are hit by Sand Castles or Sand Spikes will be destroyed. Sand Castles are a way of trapping you, and the Sand Spikes are a way of damaging you, dealing either 50, 75, or 100 damage on hit, increasing with the size of the Sand Spike. Mobs take double damage. The Sand Castles and Sand Spikes can be turned into decorative [Glass Castles](/wiki/Glass_Castle "Glass Castle") and [Glass Spikes](/wiki/Glass_Spike "Glass Spike") when set on fire.

## Combat[]

The Antlion can be given a stage 1, 4 or 5 [Thermal Stone](/wiki/Thermal_Stone "Thermal Stone") to start combat. The Antlion will either freeze or smolder, and then attack the nearest player within 15 metres. The Antlion will then attempt to trap the player within Sand Castles (not to be confused with [Sand Castles](/wiki/Sand_Castle "Sand Castle") featured in the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC), and then repeatedly slam into the ground, causing Sand Spikes to jut out of the ground. The Antlion can heal 200 Health each time it eats rocks. The healing can be interrupted by attacking the Antlion.

The Antlion exits combat if players are outside of 15 metres for 10 seconds.

If killed, the Antlion drops 4 [Meat](/wiki/Meat "Meat"), 6–8 [Desert Stones](/wiki/Desert_Stone "Desert Stone"), [Blueprints](/wiki/Blueprint "Blueprint") for the [Lazy Deserter](/wiki/The_Lazy_Deserter "The Lazy Deserter") and [Turf-Raiser Helm](/wiki/Turf-Raiser_Helm "Turf-Raiser Helm"), 2–4 [Rocks](/wiki/Rocks "Rocks") and varying amounts of [Trinkets](/wiki/Trinkets "Trinkets"). The desert stone that is dropped may be insufficient until the following summer, and hence players may want to obtain a good amount of Desert Stones before killing the Antlion. Killing Antlion will ensure the summer will no longer have any disturbance from Antlion.

There are multiple strategies for killing Antlion. Any of them are best with 2 thermal stones (one for Antlion and one for you) and appropriate goggles (for vision and movement). Build a fridge or endothermic fire several tiles away to cool the stones before combat (too close and antlion will destroy them). Start with full health and sanity. Restorative food such as [Surf 'n' Turf](/wiki/Surf_%27n%27_Turf "Surf 'n' Turf") or [Pierogi](/wiki/Pierogi "Pierogi") and any necessary light should complete your preparations.

### Tanking[]

Ideally, with at least 2 players, with 3000HP worth of armor and corresponding heal, [Ham Bat](/wiki/Ham_Bat "Ham Bat") or equivalent weapons. Simply hit Antlion and do not bother to evade any attack, heal up whenever the players health is low.

Single player can consider using 22 [gunpowders](/wiki/Gunpowder "Gunpowder") after starting the fight, to reduce the resources needed for tanking, while obtaining useful loot from the Antlion.

### Kiting[]

Players are likely to need [Desert Goggles](/wiki/Goggles "Goggles") or [Astroggles](/wiki/Astroggles "Astroggles"), paired with unrestrictive body armor such as a [Log Suit](/wiki/Log_Suit "Log Suit") or [Thulecite Suit](/wiki/Thulecite_Suit "Thulecite Suit"). Players can dodge the upcoming Sand Spike, using the sand circle as indicators for their locations. Players also can move preemptively since antlion would always target at least 1 attacker with sand spike. Players should move around to attack antlion from all angles to avoid a dense packing of spikes that will trap the players.

## Trivia[]

* The Antlion was introduced in the *[Against the Grain](/wiki/A_New_Reign#February_2,_2017_-_Against_the_Grain "A New Reign")* update as a trader, while the ability to fight it was added in the *[Heart of the Ruins](/wiki/A_New_Reign#February_23,_2017_-_Heart_of_the_Ruins_(Part_1) "A New Reign")* update.
* The Antlion's name is a pun on its appearance (Ant + Lion) and the fly-like insect, the [Antlion](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antlion "wikipedia:Antlion").
* Wickerbottom and Maxwell's quote for the Desert Stone suggests that the Antlion might have come into contact with gems before, since its bezoars are impure Orange Gems.
* Maxwell's quote for the Antlion is a pun on the word pride, referencing the fact that a group of lions is called a pride.
* According to Wickerbottom, its scientific name is "*Panthera auropunctata*". This alludes to the combination of the scientific names of the two animals that make the Antlion; the genus of the lion "*[Panthera leo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Panthera_leo "wikipedia:Panthera leo")*" and the specific epithet of the little fire ant "*[Wasmannia auropunctata](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wasmannia_auropunctata "wikipedia:Wasmannia auropunctata")*".
* When the Antlion was first released, several characters referred to it as a female. These examination quotes were later changed to a gender-neutral "it".
  + However, [Wigfrid](/wiki/Wigfrid "Wigfrid"), whose quotes for the Antlion were changed to be gender-neutral, refers to the Antlion as "her" when examining the [Antlion Figure](/wiki/Chess_Pieces#Antlion_Figure "Chess Pieces"), which was added long after the quotes were changed.
    - [Warly](/wiki/Warly "Warly"), who was introduced to *Don't Starve Together* after the quotes were changed, refers to the Antlion as "amie", the French word for a female friend, when paying tribute. However, it appears that Warly uses amie and its masculine counterpart ami interchangeably: [Wolfgang](/wiki/Wolfgang "Wolfgang") and [Wormwood](/wiki/Wormwood "Wormwood") are also referred to as amie by him.
* There's a value in the code that shows that the sinkholes could be repairable, but currently do not have any tool assigned to be able to fix them.
* The Antlion and [Dragonfly](/wiki/Dragonfly "Dragonfly") spawn in different desert biomes. Antlion's desert contains the [Oasis](/wiki/Lake "Lake") and limbed [cacti](/wiki/Cactus "Cactus"), while Dragonfly's desert has [spiky trees](/wiki/Spiky_Tree "Spiky Tree"), [hound mounds](/wiki/Hound_Mound "Hound Mound"), and round [cacti](/wiki/Cactus "Cactus"). Both have [flintless boulders](https://dontstarve.fandom.com/wiki/Boulder?so=search#Boulder_(Flintless)), [spiky bushes](/wiki/Spiky_Bush "Spiky Bush"), and [buzzards](/wiki/Buzzard "Buzzard") however. Hound mounds CAN spawn in Antlion's desert if a special [Set Piece](https://dontstarve.fandom.com/wiki/Set_Piece#Hound_Fortress) generates.
* If Antlion is spawned using the [Console](/wiki/Console "Console") in any [Season](/wiki/Seasons "Seasons") other than [Summer](/wiki/Seasons/Summer "Seasons/Summer"), it will disappear after being spawned.
