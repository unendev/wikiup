The **Table Vase** is a craftable [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced within the *[From Beyond](/wiki/From_Beyond "From Beyond")* updates. It can be crafted in the [Decorations Filter](/wiki/Decorations_Filter "Decorations Filter") with 2 [Logs](/wiki/Log "Log") and 1 [Rope](/wiki/Rope "Rope") and requires a [Sawhorse](/wiki/Sawhorse "Sawhorse") to prototype.

It can be placed on a [Wooden Table](/wiki/Wooden_Table "Wooden Table") and acts like the vase on the [End Table](/wiki/End_Table "End Table"). The player can place any kind of [Flower](/wiki/Flower "Flower") in the Vase. This includes [Lune Tree Blossoms](/wiki/Lune_Tree_Blossom "Lune Tree Blossom"), [Light Bulbs](/wiki/Light_Bulb "Light Bulb"), [Glow Berries](/wiki/Glow_Berry "Glow Berry") and [Forget-Me-Lots](/wiki/Forget-Me-Lots "Forget-Me-Lots"). Placing Flowers, Lune Tree Blossoms or Forget-Me-Lots will grant the player 5 [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity"), but only for the first flower placed or when replacing a withered flower. Placed Light Bulbs and Glow Berries will provide light, but only for 10 days before requiring a replacement. Other flowers won't wilt unless the Table Vase catches fire.

A full Table Vase will always show a rose in the inventory, no matter what kind of flower it contains.

## Tips[]

* Because the Table Vase can also be placed on the ground, it can be used as a cheap way to light up areas, as a placed Light Bulb will provide Light for 10 days and can be refilled with only one Light Bulb after withering.
* To get a wilted flower the Table Vase can be set on fire, which can be then extinguished with a [Watering Can](/wiki/Watering_Can "Watering Can").

## Trivia[]

* The Table Vase was introduced in the *[Host of Horrors](/wiki/From_Beyond#Host_of_Horrors "From Beyond")* update.
* At the beginning all flowers in a Table Vase were wilting after 10 days. This was changed in a hotfix shortly after the release of the *Host of Horrors* update to only affect Light Bulbs and Glow Berries.
