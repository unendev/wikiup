The **Lost Scrapbook Page** is an [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in the *[From Beyond](/wiki/From_Beyond "From Beyond")* update. It has a chance to be dropped from a [Sunken Chest](/wiki/Sunken_Chest "Sunken Chest") or non-player [Skeleton](/wiki/Skeleton "Skeleton"), harvesting a [Tumbleweed](/wiki/Tumbleweed "Tumbleweed") or digging a [Grave](/wiki/Grave "Grave"). Using a Lost Scrapbook Page will add 3-4 new random [Creatures](/wiki/Creatures "Creatures"), [Giants](/wiki/Giants "Giants"), Items, [Food](/wiki/Food "Food"), Things, or Point of Interest to the player's [Scrapbook](/wiki/Scrapbook "Scrapbook"). The added objects will not have any information besides the name, the image and connections with some other objects, so further investigation is needed.

Lost Scrapbook Page can't grant knowledge of [Research Notes](/wiki/Research_Notes "Research Notes").
