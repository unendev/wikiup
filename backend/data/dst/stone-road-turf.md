**Stone Road Turf** is a [Turf](/wiki/Turfs "Turfs") exclusive to the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC. It can be found in the [Pig City](/wiki/Pig_City "Pig City") [Biome](/wiki/Biomes "Biomes"). Stone Road Turf can be also be crafted using [Boards](/wiki/Boards "Boards") and two [Cut Stones](/wiki/Cut_Stone "Cut Stone") in the [City Planning Tab](/wiki/City_Planning_Tab "City Planning Tab") if the player is holding the [Key to the City](/wiki/Key_to_the_City "Key to the City").

When placed on the [Surface World](/wiki/Surface_World "Surface World") covering a large enough area, no [Birds](/wiki/Birds "Birds") will spawn and land on these turfs. This turf prevents [BFB](/wiki/BFB "BFB") from landing.

In the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC, this Turf will not spawn [Puddles](/wiki/Flooding "Flooding"). Expanding Puddles can still flood this turf, however.
