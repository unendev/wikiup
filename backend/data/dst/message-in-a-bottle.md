A **Message in a Bottle** is a naturally spawning [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC. It can be found lying on [Beaches](/wiki/Beach "Beach") or floating in the [Ocean](/wiki/Ocean#Shipwrecked "Ocean"), and appears as a regular bottle with a piece of paper inside. Once the message has been read, it will turn into an [Empty Bottle](/wiki/Empty_Bottle "Empty Bottle").

Upon reading the message, the player's [Map](/wiki/Map "Map") will automatically open and [a red X](/wiki/X_Marks_the_Spot "X Marks the Spot") will be placed on it, possibly in an area the player has already explored. If the player travels to the location, they will find two bones laid out in the formation of an X in a pile of dirt which can be dug up with a [Shovel](/wiki/Shovel "Shovel") to uncover a [Chest](/wiki/Chest "Chest") and two [Bone Shards](/wiki/Bone_Shards "Bone Shards"). This Chest usually contains valuables such as [Dubloons](/wiki/Dubloons "Dubloons") and [Gems](/wiki/Gems "Gems"), but may also occasionally cause [Snakes](/wiki/Snake "Snake") to appear.

Each Message in a Bottle corresponds to a single treasure location determined during world generation, making them a finite resource.

A **Message in a Bottle** is also found in *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")*. They are found naturally in the [Coastal Ocean](/wiki/Ocean#Coastal_Ocean "Ocean"), and can also spawn every 1 to 2 days 40[m](/wiki/Metre "Metre") from the players in the [Ocean](/wiki/Ocean "Ocean"). The spawning bottle will need 5m of clear space in the Ocean, and must be at least 35m from any flotsam. The spawning attempt will happen even if the player is on land, but the attempt will fail and retry every 60 seconds. [Marotters](/wiki/Marotter "Marotter") have a chance to drop one on death.

 https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/dont-starve-game/images/2/2b/Sunken Chest Marker.png/revision/latest?cb=20200419163431 https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/dont-starve-game/images/2/2b/Sunken Chest Marker.png/revision/latest?cb=20200419163431 

Sunken Chest Marker.

 

The first message read by a player reveals the [Crabby Hermit](/wiki/Crabby_Hermit "Crabby Hermit")'s location, unless the player already met the Crabby Hermit. Otherwise, reading a message has a 60% chance to generate and reveal a new [Sunken Chest](/wiki/Sunken_Chest "Sunken Chest") (100% if you give Crabby Hermit Cracked pearl) and a 40% chance to reveal nothing. If 10 or more markers are active at a time, the position of an already existing Sunken Chest is revealed instead.

After reading, the Message in a Bottle turns into an [Empty Bottle](/wiki/Empty_Bottle#Don't_Starve_Together "Empty Bottle").

* Have you found your treasure yet? When you do, will you come home?

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* If the player tries to spawn the Message in a Bottle using the [Console](/wiki/Console "Console") and reads it, the character will say "This message is faded, I can't read it".
* If the player carries a Message in a Bottle and uses the [Teleportato](/wiki/Teleportato "Teleportato") to teleport to a new world, once reading the bottle they'll say "This doesn't look like a map of this place".
* Messages in a Bottle were introduced to *Don't Starve Together* in the *[She Sells Sea Shells](/wiki/Return_of_Them#She_Sells_Sea_Shells "Return of Them")* update.
* In DST, if the player writes in the command line "c\_give("messagebottle\_throwable",1)" then the game will give out a bottle with a message that can be equipped and thrown at objects or on the ground. If you throw this bottle with a message at an object in the sea, it will sink.
