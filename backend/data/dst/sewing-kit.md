The **Sewing Kit** is a [Dress Tab](/wiki/Dress "Dress") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") that allows the player to restore 5 [Days](/wiki/Day "Day") of [Durability](/wiki/Durability "Durability") per use on certain items, up to their maximum, lengthening the duration that they can be used. It requires an [Alchemy Engine](/wiki/Alchemy_Engine "Alchemy Engine") to prototype, and costs 8 [Silk](/wiki/Silk "Silk"), 2 [Hound's Tooth](/wiki/Hound%27s_Tooth "Hound's Tooth"), and 1 [Log](/wiki/Log "Log") to craft. The Sewing Kit has 5 uses.

The Sewing Kit is used by selecting it in the inventory, then right-clicking on the item to be repaired. Items can be repaired while they are equipped, in the inventory, resting on the ground, or in a container. It should be noted that items do not lose durability while they are in an inventory or container, only when equipped.

## https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/dont-starve-game/images/d/d8/Gift Icon.png/revision/latest?cb=20151222151842 Downloadable Content[]

In all [DLCs](/wiki/DLC "DLC"), [Umbrella](/wiki/Umbrella "Umbrella") can be repaired with Sewing Kit.

In the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Shipwrecked "Shipwrecked")* DLC, Sewing Kit can be obtained from [Slot Machine](/wiki/Slot_Machine "Slot Machine") and [Steamer Trunk](/wiki/Steamer_Trunk "Steamer Trunk"). It can also be used to repair any kinds of [Sail](/wiki/Sail "Sail").

In the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC, there is a chance for Sewing Kit to spawn in a [Secret Bandit Camp](/wiki/Secret_Bandit_Camp "Secret Bandit Camp"). Sewing Kit can also be used to repair [Stalking Stick](/wiki/Stalking_Stick "Stalking Stick") which cannot be crafted.

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* The Sewing Kit was added in the *[The End of the Beginning](/wiki/Version_History#The_End_of_the_Beginning "Version History")* update.
* Before the *[Underground](/wiki/Version_History#Underground "Version History")* update, clothing had to be removed to be repaired by the Sewing Kit.
* Wilson's quote is actually a pun, as "darning" is a sewing technique.
* In the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC, [Sand Castles](/wiki/Sand_Castle "Sand Castle") used to be repairable with Sewing Kits. This bug was removed in the *Seas the Day* update.
