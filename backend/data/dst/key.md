The **Key** is an item added in the [The Gorge](/wiki/The_Gorge "The Gorge") event. Used to open [safes](/wiki/Safe "Safe"). Purchased from the [Swamp Pig Elder](/wiki/Swamp_Pig_Elder "Swamp Pig Elder"). A very useful item, you can use it to get silver plates and bowls early in the game.
