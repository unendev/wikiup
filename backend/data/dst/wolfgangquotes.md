## Placeholder Trivia[]

* Wolfgang's examination quote for Flower Salad ("Is not spinach, but maybe still work!") may be a reference to [Popeye the Sailor Man](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Popeye "wikipedia:Popeye"), who is known for his consumption of spinach that gives him great strength.
* His examination quote for Trail Mix ("Berries and nuts, berries and nuts!") may be a reference to [this](http://youtu.be/wYX_zhlTDr8?t=14s) commercial's jingle.
* His examination quote for the Eye Bone ("It is eye bone. Eye bone connect to face bone.") is a reference to the song "[Dem Bones](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dem_Bones "wikipedia:Dem Bones")".
* His quote for WX-78 as an attacker ("Ha! Metal can is want to rock'em and sock'em!") is a reference to [Rock 'Em Sock 'Em Robots](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rock_%27Em_Sock_%27Em_Robots "wikipedia:Rock 'Em Sock 'Em Robots").
* His examination quote for the Splumonkey ("Ugly monkey man!") is similar to a quote said by Pigs when the player is nearby in earlier versions of the game.
* His quote for Portal Paraphernalia ("If Wolfgang build it, they will come.") is a reference to a line from the film *[Field of Dreams](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Field_of_Dreams "wikipedia:Field of Dreams")* ("If you build it, he will come").
* His quote for the [Hanging Vine](/wiki/Hanging_Vine "Hanging Vine") ("Watch out for that tree!") is a reference to the theme song of the *[George of the Jungle](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/George_of_the_Jungle "wikipedia:George of the Jungle")* cartoon.
