:   *This article is about the gameplay affecting items. For the cosmetic clothes in Don't Starve Together, see [Skins](/wiki/Skins "Skins").*

**Clothes** are any in-game [Items](/wiki/Items "Items") that can be worn and provide the [Character](/wiki/Character "Character") a variety of bonuses. Unlike [Armor](/wiki/Armor "Armor"), Clothes do not mitigate damage from attacks and usually their [Durability](/wiki/Durability "Durability") is decreased over time as long as they're equipped. Bonuses from different Clothes that can be equipped at the same time will stack with each other.

All the Clothes, except for those that need specific fuel or spoil over time, can be repaired with a [Sewing Kit](/wiki/Sewing_Kit "Sewing Kit"), which will add 5 more days of usage, until fully repaired.
