A **Doydoy Egg** is a large spotted blue-green egg. They can be found by picking [Doydoy Nests](/wiki/Doydoy_Nests "Doydoy Nests"), which are spawned when [Doydoys](/wiki/Doydoys "Doydoys") mate. Unlike [Tallbirds](/wiki/Tallbirds "Tallbirds"), Doydoys will not chase the player if their egg is stolen. Doydoy Eggs will hatch into Baby Doydoys only when they are in a Doydoy Nest, and unlike [Tallbird Eggs](/wiki/Tallbird_Eggs "Tallbird Eggs") they cannot be made to hatch around [Campfire](/wiki/Campfire "Campfire"). However, placing them in empty Nests allows them to hatch.

When used in [Crock Pots](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot"), Doydoy eggs will have an [Egg value](/wiki/Eggs "Eggs") of 1 (this is the same as 1 [Egg](/wiki/Egg "Egg")). Thus, it is not recommended to use Doydoy Eggs in cooking unless you have at least 2 Doydoy Eggs or living Doydoys.

* Wickerbottom's quote on Fried Doydoy Egg is a reference to children's book by Dr. Seuss "Green Eggs and Ham".

Examination quotes indicate this is a version of the egg that has been broken and can no longer hatch. It can still be cooked into a Fried Doydoy Egg. Eating the Cracked Doydoy Egg restores the same as the Doydoy Egg.
