**Fresh Fruit Crepes** is a [Warly](/wiki/Warly "Warly")-specific [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") cooked in the [Portable Crock Pot](/wiki/Portable_Crock_Pot "Portable Crock Pot") by adding 1.5 units of [Fruits](/wiki/Fruits "Fruits"), 1 [Butter](/wiki/Butter "Butter"), and 1 [Honey](/wiki/Honey "Honey"). It takes 40 seconds to cook and restores 60 Health, 150 Hunger, and 15 Sanity when eaten.

Note that [Durian](/wiki/Durian "Durian") and [Dragon Fruit](/wiki/Dragon_Fruit "Dragon Fruit") can be freely used when making, as this recipe has higher priority than [Monster Lasagna](/wiki/Monster_Lasagna "Monster Lasagna") and [Dragonpie](/wiki/Dragonpie "Dragonpie") (However, adding 2 Durians in Don't Starve Together icon may result in [Monster Tartare](/wiki/Monster_Tartare "Monster Tartare") instead). Adding 2 [Berries](/wiki/Berries "Berries") will result in [Fist Full of Jam](/wiki/Fist_Full_of_Jam "Fist Full of Jam") instead, due to insufficient Fruit units requirement.

## Don't Starve Together icon Don't Starve Together[]

Fresh Fruit Crepes are also included in *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")* as a dish exclusive to the Portable Crock Pot. It is also the favorite food of [Wes](/wiki/Wes "Wes"), able to restore 165 hunger. However, as even the default hunger value already exceeds Wes's [Hunger](/wiki/Hunger "Hunger") capacity, this benefit is useless.
