The **Hermit Island** is a unique area exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")*. It is a remote island located far out into the [Ocean](/wiki/Ocean "Ocean") and accessible only by [Boat](/wiki/Boat_Kit "Boat Kit") or using [Woodie's Weregoose form](/wiki/Cursed_Woodie#Don.27t_Starve_Together "Cursed Woodie") or soul hopping as [Wortox](/wiki/Wortox "Wortox"). It is covered with [Moon Crater Turf](/wiki/Moon_Crater_Turf "Moon Crater Turf") and [Shell Beach Turf](/wiki/Shell_Beach_Turf "Shell Beach Turf").

The [Crabby Hermit](/wiki/Crabby_Hermit "Crabby Hermit") lives on this island with her [Hermit Home](/wiki/Hermit_Home "Hermit Home"), a [Bee Box](/wiki/Bee_Box#Hermit_Bee_Box "Bee Box") and 6 [Drying Racks](/wiki/Drying_Rack#Hermit_Drying_Rack "Drying Rack").

Its vegetation is similar to the [Lunar Island's](/wiki/Lunar_Island "Lunar Island"), with [Lune Trees](/wiki/Lune_Tree "Lune Tree"), [Lunar Saplings](/wiki/Sapling "Sapling"), [Moon Glasses](/wiki/Moon_Glass "Moon Glass"), [Driftwoods](/wiki/Driftwood "Driftwood") and [Bull Kelps](/wiki/Bull_Kelp "Bull Kelp"). [Plugged Fissures](/wiki/Plugged_Fissure "Plugged Fissure") are found in place of [Celestial Fissures](/wiki/Celestial_Fissure "Celestial Fissure"), and [Enlightenment](/wiki/Enlightenment "Enlightenment") does not take place on the Hermit Island.

In addition, [Shell Bells](/wiki/Shell_Bells "Shell Bells") are found on the ground and 9 [Shell Clusters](/wiki/Shell_Cluster "Shell Cluster") are present as [Underwater Salvageables](/wiki/Underwater_Salvageable "Underwater Salvageable") around the Island.

The island's location is revealed on the Map once the player interacts with a [Message in a Bottle](/wiki/Message_in_a_Bottle "Message in a Bottle").
