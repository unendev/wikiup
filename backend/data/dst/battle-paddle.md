The **Battle Paddle** is an [item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[The Curse of Moon Quay](/wiki/The_Curse_of_Moon_Quay "The Curse of Moon Quay")* update. It can be obtained by killing a [Prime Mate](/wiki/Prime_Mate "Prime Mate"). The item can be used as an [Oar](/wiki/Oar "Oar") or as a [Melee weapon](/wiki/Weapon#Melee_weapons "Weapon") and cannot be crafted by the player. The Battle Paddle has up to 100 uses when used as a weapon or up to 500 uses when used as an oar, and the force applied to the [Boat](/wiki/Boat_Kit "Boat Kit") it is used on is ×0.6 units. As a weapon it does 51 damage, matching that of a [Tentacle Spike](/wiki/Tentacle_Spike "Tentacle Spike").

It can be deconstructed using a [Deconstruction Staff](/wiki/Deconstruction_Staff "Deconstruction Staff") for 1 [Log](/wiki/Log "Log") and 1 [Palmcone Scale](/wiki/Palmcone_Scale "Palmcone Scale").
