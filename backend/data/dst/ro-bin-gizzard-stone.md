The **Ro Bin Gizzard Stone** is an [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC that spawns from the hatched [Stone Egg](/wiki/Stone_Egg "Stone Egg") along with [Ro Bin](/wiki/Ro_Bin "Ro Bin"). As long as the Gizzard Stone is in the player's [Inventory](/wiki/Inventory "Inventory"), the Ro Bin will always follow them, similar to the [Eye Bone](/wiki/Eye_Bone "Eye Bone") attracting [Chester](/wiki/Chester "Chester").
