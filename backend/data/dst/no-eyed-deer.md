**No-Eyed Deer** are passive, seasonal [Mobs](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[A New Reign](/wiki/A_New_Reign "A New Reign")*. No-Eyed Deer spawn near the beginning of [Autumn](/wiki/Autumn "Autumn") in [Deciduous Forests](/wiki/Deciduous_Forest "Deciduous Forest") or [Mosaic](/wiki/Mosaic "Mosaic") Biomes and are always found in groups of 6 to 9.

Past the beginning of [Winter](/wiki/Winter "Winter") they will grow one of the 3 variations of [Deer Antlers](/wiki/Deer_Antler "Deer Antler"). They will, however, despawn around the end of the season unless they are near a players' base.

They are considered innocent creatures, and killing one adds 4 points to the player's naughtiness level, which can cause [Krampus](/wiki/Krampus "Krampus") to appear.

## Behavior[]

No-Eyed Deer will spawn 3–5 days into Autumn. They will only spawn in [Deciduous Forests](/wiki/Deciduous_Forest "Deciduous Forest") or [Mosaic Biomes](/wiki/Mosaic "Mosaic") at fixed spawn locations, similarly to the [Loot Stash](/wiki/Loot_Stash "Loot Stash"). They will always spawn as a group away from all players. The herd always stays together and all animals move exactly in the same direction.

As Winter starts the No-Eyed Deer will start migrating, growing their antlers after about 1 [Day](/wiki/Day "Day"). If the No-Eyed Deer collides with an object such as a [Tree](/wiki/Tree "Tree") the antler will break off. Within 2 days before or after the end of Winter, No-Eyed Deer will despawn unless they are within 1.5 screens (7.5 [Pitchfork](/wiki/Pitchfork "Pitchfork") tiles) of at least 10 player [Structures](/wiki/Structure "Structure") or [Walls](/wiki/Wall "Wall").

No-Eyed Deer will not run away from the player if approached, but will be startled. If attacked, they will flee.

Despite being a herd animal like [Beefalo](/wiki/Beefalo "Beefalo") and [Volt Goats](/wiki/Volt_Goat "Volt Goat"), they do not appear to breed.

## Trivia[]

* The name "No-Eyed Deer" can be interpreted as a pun on the phrases "doe-eyed" and "no idea". It could also be a reference to the fable, *The One-Eyed Deer*.
* No-Eyed Deer were added in the *[Herd Mentality](/wiki/A_New_Reign#November_24th,_2016_-_Herd_Mentality "A New Reign")* update. Before the *[Winter's Feast](/wiki/A_New_Reign#December_15th,_2016_-_Winter's_Feast "A New Reign")* update, they used the same sounds as Volt Goats and were neutral, attacking players in groups similar to Beefalo.
* The functional versions of the collared No-Eyed Deer were first shown on *Rhymes with Play* #116 and later released as the [Gem Deer](/wiki/Gem_Deer "Gem Deer").
* The Antlered Deer's aesthetic is a possible reference to the popular 1966 Television special *[How The Grinch Stole Christmas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/How_The_Grinch_Stole_Christmas "wikipedia:How The Grinch Stole Christmas").*
* No-Eyed Deer may be able to breed like [Volt Goats](/wiki/Volt_Goat "Volt Goat") but at a somewhat slower rate.[Verification Needed]
