**Infused Iron** is a resource introduced in the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC. It is exclusively obtained from the [Large Iron Hulk](/wiki/Large_Iron_Hulk "Large Iron Hulk") and used solely to craft the [Living Artifact](/wiki/Living_Artifact "Living Artifact"). Infused Iron spoils and will turn into regular [Iron Ore](/wiki/Iron_Ore "Iron Ore") after two days..

## Prototype Tips[]

* Like [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") items, Infused Iron spoils faster on the ground. Putting it in a valid refrigerating container (like an [Insulated pack](/wiki/Insulated_Pack "Insulated Pack") or a [Sea Sack](/wiki/Sea_Sack "Sea Sack")) halves its spoiling rate.

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* Infused Iron is one of three items that spoil into an item other than [Rot](/wiki/Rot "Rot"), [Spoiled Fish](/wiki/Spoiled_Fish "Spoiled Fish"), or [Rotten Eggs](/wiki/Rotten_Egg "Rotten Egg"), the others being [Tea](/wiki/Tea "Tea") and [Infused Moon Shards](/wiki/Infused_Moon_Shard "Infused Moon Shard") from DST.
