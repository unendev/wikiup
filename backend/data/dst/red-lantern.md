**Red Lanterns** are [Items](/wiki/Items "Items") exclusive to New Year [Events](/wiki/Events "Events") in *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*.
They can be acquired in exchange for 3 [Lucky Gold Nuggets](/wiki/Lucky_Gold_Nugget "Lucky Gold Nugget") at a Shrine and can be equipped in the hand slot.

They do not produce as much light as regular [Lanterns](/wiki/Lantern "Lantern"), but last much longer at 12 [Days](/wiki/Day-Night_Cycle "Day-Night Cycle").

## Trivia[]

* The Red Lantern was introduced during the [Year of the Gobbler](/wiki/Year_of_the_Gobbler "Year of the Gobbler") event.
