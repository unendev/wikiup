**Cut Reeds** are an item obtained by harvesting [Reeds](/wiki/Reeds "Reeds") that grow in [Swamps](/wiki/Swamp "Swamp"). Reeds can be refined into [Papyrus](/wiki/Papyrus "Papyrus") and are a crafting ingredient for a [Blow Dart](/wiki/Blow_Dart "Blow Dart"), [Sleep Dart](/wiki/Sleep_Dart "Sleep Dart"), [Fire Dart](/wiki/Fire_Dart "Fire Dart") and [Pan Flute](/wiki/Pan_Flute "Pan Flute"). Reeds grow back after 3 days from being picked, but they will not grow in [Winter](/wiki/Winter "Winter").

## Downloadable Content[]

In the *[Reign of Giants](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Reign_of_Giants "Don't Starve: Reign of Giants")* DLC, there is an alternative method of obtaining Cut Reeds: by destroying [Tumbleweeds](/wiki/Tumbleweed "Tumbleweed"). There is a 1% chance of it dropping from the 3 possible drops. Also, a friendly [Catcoon](/wiki/Catcoon "Catcoon") can gift the player Cut Reeds.

In the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC, Cut Reeds can only be gathered by using a [Boat](/wiki/Boats "Boats"), as Reeds grow on the water in the [Lily Pond](/wiki/Lily_Pond "Lily Pond") [Biome](/wiki/Biomes "Biomes").

## Don't Starve Together Don't Starve Together[]

Reeds can also be harvested from the [Rocky Beach](/wiki/Rocky_Beach "Rocky Beach") biome on the [Lunar Island](/wiki/Lunar_Island "Lunar Island").

[Monkeytails](/wiki/Monkeytails "Monkeytails") are another source of Cut Reeds that can be transplanted near one's base for easy harvesting.
