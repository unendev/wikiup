**Volt Goat Chaud-Froid** is a [Warly](/wiki/Warly "Warly")-specific [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") cooked in the [Portable Crock Pot](/wiki/Portable_Crock_Pot "Portable Crock Pot"), exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*. It requires a [Volt Goat Horn](/wiki/Volt_Goat_Horn "Volt Goat Horn") and two [Sweeteners](/wiki/Sweetener "Sweetener"), and takes 40 seconds to cook.

Consuming Volt Goat Chaud-Froid turns all damage caused by the player to electrical damage for five minutes. This grants a damage boost of x1.5 (x2.5 when target is [wet](/wiki/Wetness "Wetness")).

## Prototype Tips[]

* Volt Goat Chaud-Froid can be a useful food when fighting off [Depths Worm](/wiki/Depths_Worm "Depths Worm") attacks in the [Caves](/wiki/Caves "Caves"), because of the x2.5 damage multiplier against wet mobs.
* Being a crock pot dish, it can be seasoned with [Chili Flakes](/wiki/Chili_Flakes "Chili Flakes") to deal an additional x1.2 damage multiplier. This comes to a total x3 damage multiplier against wet mobs. (x1.8 damage multiplier for dry mobs)
* Damage dealt while riding a [Beefalo](/wiki/Beefalo "Beefalo") is affected by the damage multiplier from Volt Goat Chaud-Froid.
* Volt Goat Chaud-Froid, while not being a meat item, can still be eaten by Wigfrid.
