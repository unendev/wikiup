**Bacon and Eggs** is a [Meat](/wiki/Meats "Meats") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") cooked in the [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") by adding 2.0 units of [Eggs](/wiki/Eggs "Eggs") and 1.25 units of Meats. It takes 40 seconds to cook.

Adding more than 1 [Monster Meat](/wiki/Monster_Meat "Monster Meat") may result in [Monster Lasagna](/wiki/Monster_Lasagna "Monster Lasagna"), unless 1 [Twig](/wiki/Twigs "Twigs") is also added; in this case, a [Tallbird Egg](/wiki/Tallbird_Egg "Tallbird Egg") must be added, as one normal [Egg](/wiki/Egg "Egg") does not provide enough Egg value to create Bacon and Eggs. Adding any [Vegetable](/wiki/Vegetables "Vegetables") will result in [Pierogi](/wiki/Pierogi "Pierogi").

## Don't Starve Together Don't Starve Together[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, Bacon and Eggs is [Wilson](/wiki/Wilson "Wilson")'s favorite food and can be consumed to get an extra 15 Hunger.

When made with a [Tallbird Egg](/wiki/Tallbird_Egg "Tallbird Egg"), adding any Vegetable will result in [Tall Scotch Eggs](/wiki/Tall_Scotch_Eggs "Tall Scotch Eggs").
