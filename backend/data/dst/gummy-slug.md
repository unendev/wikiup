The **Gummy Slug** is a [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC, which can be found exclusively under [Stone Slabs](/wiki/Stone_Slab "Stone Slab"). Eating one, raw or cooked, will restore [Hunger](/wiki/Hunger "Hunger"), but at the cost of [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity").

A Gummy Slug can be combined with a [sweetener](/wiki/Sweetener "Sweetener") to cook a [Gummy Cake](/wiki/Gummy_Cake "Gummy Cake") in the [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot"). The Gummy Cake will also reduce the Sanity of the player but will restore much more Hunger (the same amount as a [Meaty Stew](/wiki/Meaty_Stew "Meaty Stew")).

[Webber](/wiki/Webber "Webber") and [Wilba](/wiki/Wilba "Wilba") can eat Gummy Slugs without the Sanity Penalty. Wilba will turn into a Werepig after eating two gummy slugs.
