**Ancient Obelisks** are naturally spawning structures exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[A New Reign](/wiki/A_New_Reign "A New Reign")*. They replace [Nightmare Lights](/wiki/Nightmare_Light "Nightmare Light") in the [Atrium](/wiki/Atrium "Atrium") Biome of the Ruins. Instead of activating during specific [Nightmare phases](/wiki/Nightmare_Cycle "Nightmare Cycle"), Ancient Obelisks are activated by inserting the [Ancient Key](/wiki/Ancient_Key "Ancient Key") in the [Gateway](/wiki/Ancient_Gateway "Ancient Gateway").

Unlike the Nightmare Light, the Obelisk's insanity aura is not dependent on it being active. At 800 sanity drained per minute, its effect is similar to that of a fully-open nightmare light, and players should avoid standing close to one, unless they have the means to restore the sanity and get past the [Obelisk](/wiki/Obelisk "Obelisk") blockades.

The **Nightmare Rampart** is a naturally spawning object exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")*. They naturally appear in front of the entrance of the [Lunar Grotto](/wiki/Lunar_Grotto "Lunar Grotto") biome after the [Archive Switch](/wiki/Archive_Switch "Archive Switch") within the [Ancient Archives](/wiki/Ancient_Archive "Ancient Archive") has been activated. They spawn [Nightmare Fissures](/wiki/Nightmare_Light "Nightmare Light") around them, which will in turn spawn [Shadow Creatures](/wiki/Shadow_Creature "Shadow Creature"). This will trigger a battle between Nightmare Creatures and [Greater Gestalts](/wiki/Gestalt "Gestalt") in the Lunar Grotto. They never disappear even if the Archive Switch is turned off.

Nightmare Ramparts also have a massive sanity aura of -800 Sanity per minute.

## Prototype Tips[]

* Wearing a [Bee Queen Crown](/wiki/Bee_Queen_Crown "Bee Queen Crown") can make sanity skyrocket in seconds by being near the Nightmare Rampart.
* The area around Nightmare Ramparts is great for collecting [Nightmare Fuel](/wiki/Nightmare_Fuel "Nightmare Fuel") as it's easy to keep sanity low while fighting the Shadow Creatures that spawn from the nearby Nightmare Fissures. This can be further improved with access to [Blue Caps](/wiki/Blue_Cap "Blue Cap") from [Mushroom Planters](/wiki/Mushroom_Planter "Mushroom Planter") or the nearby Blue [Mushtree Forest](/wiki/Mushtree_Forest "Mushtree Forest") to keep sanity low and health high. Finally when done, the Bee Queen Crown can be used to quickly restore sanity.
