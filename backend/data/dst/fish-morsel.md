It counts as 0.5 [Fishes](/wiki/Fishes "Fishes") value when used in the [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot").

It can also be used in various Crock Pot recipes.

## Hamlet icon Hamlet[]

Fish Morsels are also included in the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC. It can be bought for 1 [Oinc](/wiki/Oinc "Oinc") at [The Sterling Trough Deli](/wiki/Pig_Shops "Pig Shops").

## Don't Starve Together Don't Starve Together[]

A Fish Morsel was introduced to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")* in *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")*. It is dropped by [Fish](/wiki/Fish "Fish"),
[Runty Guppy](/wiki/Runty_Guppy "Runty Guppy"), [Needlenosed Squirt](/wiki/Needlenosed_Squirt "Needlenosed Squirt"), [Bitty Baitfish](/wiki/Bitty_Baitfish "Bitty Baitfish"), [Smolt Fry](/wiki/Smolt_Fry "Smolt Fry"), [Scorching Sunfish](/wiki/Scorching_Sunfish "Scorching Sunfish"), [Spittlefish](/wiki/Spittlefish "Spittlefish") and [Crustashine](/wiki/Crustashine "Crustashine") when killed.

It counts as 0.5 [Fishes](/wiki/Fishes "Fishes") and 0.5 [Meats](/wiki/Meats "Meats") value when used in the [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot").
