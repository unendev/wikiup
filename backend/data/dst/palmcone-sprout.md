The **Palmcone Sprout** is an [item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[The Curse of Moon Quay](/wiki/The_Curse_of_Moon_Quay "The Curse of Moon Quay")* update. It can be obtained by cutting down a fully grown [Palmcone Tree](/wiki/Palmcone_Tree "Palmcone Tree"). It also has a small chance to drop from an [Unnatural Portal](/wiki/Unnatural_Portal "Unnatural Portal"). It can then be planted to grow a Palmcone Tree or used as fuel.
