**Slimy Turf** is one of the [Turf](/wiki/Turf "Turf") items, acquired by digging Slimy tiles with a [Pitchfork](/wiki/Pitchfork "Pitchfork"). Like other Turfs, it can be placed on any bare ground tile, or it can be used as [fuel](/wiki/Fire#Fuel "Fire").

Slimy Turf is only found in [Caves](/wiki/Caves "Caves"). It is one of the two Turfs that connect or surround a [Sunken Forest](/wiki/Sunken_Forest "Sunken Forest"). Sometimes, a player may find [Saplings](/wiki/Sapling "Sapling") in an area covered with Slimy Turf.

[Berry Bushes](/wiki/Berry_Bushes "Berry Bushes"), [Grass Tufts](/wiki/Grass_Tufts "Grass Tufts"), [Saplings](/wiki/Saplings "Saplings"), [Spiky Bushes](/wiki/Spiky_Bush "Spiky Bush"), [Pine Cones](/wiki/Pine_Cone "Pine Cone"), [Birchnuts](/wiki/Birchnut "Birchnut"), and [Lureplants](/wiki/Lureplant "Lureplant") can be placed normally on Slimy Turf, and Eyeplants will spawn normally. Crows are the only [Birds](/wiki/Birds "Birds") that will land on Slimy Turf.

Slimy Turf has the third highest overlap priority of any turf in the game, meaning it will almost always appear "on top" when overlapping adjacent turfs. The only turfs with a higher overlap priority is [Cave Rock Turf](/wiki/Cave_Rock_Turf "Cave Rock Turf") and [Mud Turf](/wiki/Mud_Turf "Mud Turf").

If brought to SW, [Floods](/wiki/Flooding "Flooding") can spawn on this turf.

## Don't Starve Together icon Don't Starve Together[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, Slimy Turf can be crafted and prototyped with 1 [Cut Grass](/wiki/Cut_Grass "Cut Grass") and 1 [Foliage](/wiki/Foliage "Foliage") at [Terra Firma Tamper](/wiki/Terra_Firma_Tamper "Terra Firma Tamper") in the [Decorations Filter](/wiki/Decorations_Filter "Decorations Filter").
