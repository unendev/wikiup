The **Tam o' Shanter** is a [Hat](/wiki/Category:Hats "Category:Hats") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") that is obtained by killing [MacTusk](/wiki/MacTusk "MacTusk"). It restores 6.7 [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity") per minute while worn. The hat alone is enough to cancel out the sanity drop of heavy [Rain](/wiki/Rain "Rain") or [Dusk/Night](/wiki/Day-Night_Cycle "Day-Night Cycle"), even when playing as [Wolfgang](/wiki/Wolfgang "Wolfgang").

It's a *tier 2* warm clothing (120 insulation), which allows the character to delay [freezing](/wiki/Freezing "Freezing") in [Winter](/wiki/Winter "Winter") by a rate of 1 degree every 5 seconds. The hat lasts for 25 days, and a [Sewing Kit](/wiki/Sewing_Kit "Sewing Kit") repairs 20% of its durability.

## Gift Icon Downloadable Content[]

In the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC, the Tam o' Shanter can be bought for 50 [Oincs](/wiki/Oinc "Oinc") at [The Sow's Ear Hat Shop](/wiki/The_Sow%27s_Ear_Hat_Shop "The Sow's Ear Hat Shop") in the [Pig City](/wiki/Pig_City "Pig City").

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* The Tam o' Shanter was added in [*A Winter's Tale*](/wiki/Version_History#A_Winter's_Tale "Version History") update.
* A Tam o' Shanter, often abbreviated T.O.S. or Tam, is a nineteenth-century Scottish traditional bonnet worn by men.
* Because of MacTusk's death animation, when the Shanter drops, the player will actually see two of them drop from the walrus. One is the item that the player can pick up, while the other is part of the animation and will disappear with MacTusk.
* The Tam o' Shanter is the most dapper piece of clothing in *Don't Starve*.
* As the Tam o' Shanter isn't craftable, the only way to use an alternate skin on one is with a [Clean Sweeper](/wiki/Clean_Sweeper "Clean Sweeper").
