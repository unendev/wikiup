**Snurtle Shell Armor** is a wearable [Armor](/wiki/Armor "Armor") that can be dropped by [Snurtles](/wiki/Snurtle "Snurtle").

It has 1050 durability (735 in Don't Starve Together) and absorbs 60% of physical damage when worn normally. When the player is hiding inside the shell (by right-clicking on it in the inventory), it absorbs 100% of physical damage. Hiding inside the shell also absorbs freezing from [Deerclops](/wiki/Deerclops "Deerclops") and [Blue Hounds](/wiki/Blue_Hounds "Blue Hounds"), electric shock from [Volt Goats](/wiki/Volt_Goats "Volt Goats") and knocking items out of the inventory from [Frogs](/wiki/Frogs "Frogs"). Not considering its durability, the snurtle shell armor effectively adds 225 hp to the average survivor.

It works like a [Bush Hat](/wiki/Bush_Hat "Bush Hat") in that aggressive [mobs](/wiki/Mob "Mob") will not attack the player if they hide before they aggro on them. Additionally, after hiding inside the shell for around 5 seconds, all attackers will lose interest in the player and stop their attacks. This means that the shell can be used to avoid [Hound](/wiki/Hound "Hound") attacks and [Shadow Creatures](/wiki/Shadow_Creatures "Shadow Creatures").

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* Unlike other armors which can't block more damage than they have durability left, Snurtle Shell Armor completely negates damage from hits strong enough to destroy it.

## Mosquito Bugs[]

* [Willow](/wiki/Willow "Willow"), [Wendy](/wiki/Wendy "Wendy"), [Wigfrid](/wiki/Wigfrid "Wigfrid"), and [Wilba](/wiki/Wilba "Wilba") will have their hair clip through the armor, while [Webber](/wiki/Webber "Webber") has spider legs clip through it. This is only a visual issue, and doesn't affect gameplay.
* If the player is attacked by [Charlie](/wiki/Charlie_(Night_Monster) "Charlie (Night Monster)") while hiding inside the Shell they will receive no Health damage but the hurt animation will play making the player come out of hiding and still take the Sanity damage.
