**Icker Jar** is an [item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in the *[From Beyond](/wiki/From_Beyond "From Beyond")* updates. It can be obtained by taking a blob from an [Icker](/wiki/Icker "Icker") with an [Empty Bottle](/wiki/Empty_Bottle#Don't_Starve_Together "Empty Bottle").

## Usage[]

The Icker Jar can be thrown, leaving a glob on the ground for 1 day; this can be used to slow down [mobs](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs") and even [bosses](/wiki/Bosses "Bosses"). The glob can be recollected with another Empty Bottle before it disappears.
