**Guardian Pigs** are aggressive Pigs that spawn from [Pig Torches](/wiki/Pig_Torch "Pig Torch"). They have higher [Health](/wiki/Health "Health") than regular Pigs but drop the same resources. They cannot be bribed with [Meat](/wiki/Meats "Meats") or attracted by a [One-man Band](/wiki/One-man_Band "One-man Band"), making them appear to be very loyal to their [Pig King](/wiki/Pig_King "Pig King").

If a Guardian Pig is dead, it will respawn at its Pig Torch after 4 days. In [Sandbox Mode](/wiki/Sandbox_Mode "Sandbox Mode"), they may be located seemingly in the middle of nowhere, usually guarding small patches of [Grass](/wiki/Grass "Grass") and [Berry Bushes](/wiki/Berry_Bush "Berry Bush"). While in [Adventure Mode](/wiki/Adventure_Mode "Adventure Mode"), they stay in the [Pig Fortress](/wiki/Pig_Fortress "Pig Fortress"), which is surrounded by [Wood Walls](/wiki/Wood_Wall "Wood Wall"). In world 4 of Adventure Mode, you can find 4 Pig Torches surrounding the Pig King instead of [Obelisks](/wiki/Obelisks "Obelisks"). Sometimes, the Guardian pigs will be protecting a [Maxwell Statue](/wiki/Maxwell_Statue "Maxwell Statue") instead.

## Behavior[]

Guardian Pigs seem to have a certain degree of discipline, as they do not eat any food on the ground and stay near the Pig Torch to keep it lit. They will chase away normal Pigs if they stray too close to them, as well as kill them if the pig is cornered or unable to get away before the Guardian Pig first attacks.

Guardian Pigs' behavior changes according to the location of their Pig Torch. If the torch is beside a Pig King, the guardians will reluctantly allow the player to stay and trade with their king. However, if the torch guards a fortress or is far from the king, the Guardian Pigs will pursue the player until they've run a certain distance from their torch.

## Top Hat Apparel[]

Pigs can wear [headgear items](/wiki/Category:Hats "Category:Hats"). Clicking on a Pig with a headgear item selected will equip it on the Pig.

If a Pig receives a head armor item, it will be able to receive its damage reduction effects. The durability will still be drained if the Pig gets damaged. Usually, the only way to get hats back from Pigs without killing them is to replace it with another hat, as fighting the Pig will break it before the Pig dies. [Frogs](/wiki/Frogs "Frogs") can also make them drop a given hat, too. The Pigs can also wear the [Miner Hat](/wiki/Miner_Hat "Miner Hat"); while wearing it, they will provide light and not panic during dusk or in a [Cave](/wiki/Cave "Cave"), while also preventing nearby Pigs from panicking.

## Trivia[]

* Guardian Pigs were added in *[A Little Rain Must Fall](/wiki/Version_History#A_Little_Rain_Must_Fall "Version History")* update.
* Guardian Pigs attack phrase "For great justice!" is a reference to the [All your base](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/All_your_base_are_belong_to_us "wikipedia:All your base are belong to us") Internet meme.
* Though they cannot be bribed, the player can still feed meat items to Guardian Pigs.
* If the player is killed by a Guardian Pig, their cause of death in the [Morgue](/wiki/Morgue "Morgue") will be listed as "Guard Pig".
