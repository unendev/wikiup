**Bernie** is an [Item](/wiki/Item "Item")/[Mob](/wiki/Mob "Mob") in *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, which can only be crafted by [Willow](/wiki/Willow "Willow") with 2 [Beard Hair](/wiki/Beard_Hair "Beard Hair"), 2 [Beefalo Wool](/wiki/Beefalo_Wool "Beefalo Wool") and 2 [Silk](/wiki/Silk "Silk"). ## Behavior[]

When Willow is below 15% sanity, a Bernie within 8m will become **BERNIE!**. BERNIE! has 2,000 [Health](/wiki/Health "Health") and deals 50 damage per hit. BERNIE! can taunt non-boss enemies within 16[m](/wiki/Metre "Metre") to draw aggro, and is capable of damaging [Shadow Creatures](/wiki/Shadow_Creature "Shadow Creature"). Shadow Creatures killed by BERNIE! will only drop one [Nightmare Fuel](/wiki/Nightmare_Fuel "Nightmare Fuel") each. Only one BERNIE! will become active for each insane Willow player. BERNIE! cannot be placed in the player's inventory. If Willow is above 15% sanity, and BERNIE! is not attacked for 16 seconds or BERNIE! does not attack for 16 seconds, BERNIE! will revert to Bernie.

If there is already a BERNIE! accounted for each Willow, remaining Bernies within 16m will become active but not grow in size. This smaller form can walk around and taunt [Shadow Creatures](/wiki/Shadow_Creature "Shadow Creature") within 16m, causing shadow creatures to target Bernie. This Bernie has 1,000 Health. When Bernie's health drops to 0%, he will stop attracting Shadow Creatures. His appearance and icon change to reflect that he is "dead", and examining him will provide a different quote. BERNIE! will also become this form when he loses all Health. Using a [Sewing Kit](/wiki/Sewing_Kit "Sewing Kit") on Bernie will restore him to 100% durability. Bernie is also deactivated when there are no longer any insane players within 30m.

If Bernie is left dead on the ground for a total of 3 [Days](/wiki/Day "Day"), he will disintegrate, dropping [Beard Hair](/wiki/Beard_Hair "Beard Hair"), [Beefalo Wool](/wiki/Beefalo_Wool "Beefalo Wool"), and [Silk](/wiki/Silk "Silk"). Picking him up does not reset the decay.

Since the [Lunar Island](/wiki/Lunar_Island "Lunar Island") turns [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity") into [Enlightenment](/wiki/Enlightenment "Enlightenment"), Bernie will not become active or turn into BERNIE!. If he was active before entering the Lunar Island, he will immediately deactivate.

## Skills[]

Willow has skills, which can be unlocked to upgrade Bernie:

### Health regeneration[]

With unlocked [Patch Up I](/wiki/Patch_Up_I "Patch Up I") skill Bernie's health regenerates 1 Health/second, with [Patch Up II](/wiki/Patch_Up_II "Patch Up II") 2 Health/second.

### Sanity Treshold[]

With unlocked [Bearly Sane I](/wiki/Bearly_Sane_I "Bearly Sane I") skill Bernie will turn into BERNIE! when Willow's Sanity falls below 36, with [Bearly Sane II](/wiki/Bearly_Sane_II "Bearly Sane II") below 60.

### Running Speed[]

With unlocked [Accelerant I](/wiki/Accelerant_I "Accelerant I") skill BERNIE! gains a 15% speed boost, with [Accelerant II](/wiki/Accelerant_II "Accelerant II") a 30% speed boost.

### Auto-attack[]

With unlocked [Hot-Headed](/wiki/Hot-Headed "Hot-Headed") skill BERNIE! take it upon himself to taunt and attack any nearby [Lunar Aligned](/wiki/Lunar_Aligned "Lunar Aligned") or [Shadow Aligned](/wiki/Shadow_Aligned "Shadow Aligned") mobs.

### Max Health[]

With unlocked [Tough Stuffing I](/wiki/Tough_Stuffing_I "Tough Stuffing I") skill BERNIE!'s max health is set to 2300, with [Tough Stuffing II](/wiki/Tough_Stuffing_II "Tough Stuffing II") to 2600.

### Burning Bernie[]

With unlocked [Burning Bernie](/wiki/Burning_Bernie "Burning Bernie") BERNIE! can be ignited giving him a firey aura for a short time. Mobs that attack an ignited Bernie will take damage back.

### Lunar Bernie[]

With unlocked [Lunar Bernie](/wiki/Lunar_Bernie "Lunar Bernie") skill BERNIE! deals +5 [Planar Damage](/wiki/Planar_Damage "Planar Damage") and receives 15 [Planar Defense](/wiki/Planar_Defense "Planar Defense") against Shadow Aligned mobs.

In *[The Forge](/wiki/The_Forge "The Forge")* [event](/wiki/Category:Events "Category:Events"), Bernie plays the same role as he does in the main game, drawing aggro from mobs while not returning fire. When Bernie loses all his health, all monsters will stop attacking and wander to the nearest player. Willow is able to revive Bernie in three seconds for him to start drawing aggro again.

In the second season of *The Forge*, when Willow wore the Whispering Grand Armor, Bernie would grow larger and get increased speed and hp. His revive time was also reduced.

## Trivia[]

* Bernie first appeared in Willow's *Don't Starve* character portrait as an unnamed prop. He was officially introduced as an exclusive item for Willow in *Don't Starve Together*.
  + Developers revealed during the preview stream for *[A Little Rain Must Fall](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve/Version_History#March_12th.2C_2013_-_A_Little_Rain_Must_Fall "Don't Starve/Version History")* that Bernie was originally intended to make Willow look "cute and innocent".[[1]](#cite_note-1)
* Bernie's name is a pun of the word "burn."
* Bernie's crafting description may be a reference to the song "Mad World" by [Tears for Fears](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tears_for_Fears "wikipedia:Tears for Fears"), in particular the lyrics "all around me are familiar faces".
* Characters have alternate examination quotes for Bernie in the Forge, but the regular quotes were triggered instead, leaving the Forge quote unimplemented.
* According to developers in *Rhymes With Play* #228, a scrapped idea for Winona revolved around her providing upgrades to the other survivors. One concept of this idea presented in the stream was a mechanized Bernie with defensive capabilities.
* Bernie was updated alongside Willow on May 7th, 2019 in Willow's character refresh.[[2]](#cite_note-2)[[3]](#cite_note-3) Before this update, he did not have a BERNIE! form.
* BERNIE!'s voice is that of a person giggling along with the sound of an animal growling.
* If Bernie is dropped in the water, he sinks.
* Bernie has an alternative skin named "Ashley" which is a pun for the word "ashes"
* Willow and Bernie is likely a reference to Annie and Tibbers from *League of Legends.*
  + Annie and Willow are both associated with fire. Annie is a pyromantic mage and Willow loves fire.
  + Annie and Willow both have stuffed bears that can help in battle.
  + Both will yell "GET 'EM, [name].
