**Vines** are a resource in the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Shipwrecked "Shipwrecked")* DLC. They are dropped from chopping a [Viney Bush](/wiki/Viney_Bush "Viney Bush") with a [Machete](/wiki/Machete "Machete") or by digging one up with a [Shovel](/wiki/Shovel "Shovel"). Vines are used in several crafting recipes.

## Hamlet icon Hamlet[]

Vines are also included in the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC, it can be obtained by killing [Snaptooth Seedlings](/wiki/Snaptooth_Seedling "Snaptooth Seedling"), [Snaptooth Flytraps](/wiki/Snaptooth_Flytrap "Snaptooth Flytrap") and [Bramble Blooms](/wiki/Bramble_Bloom "Bramble Bloom"). Using a [Shears](/wiki/Shears "Shears") on a [Viney Bush](/wiki/Viney_Bush "Viney Bush") will result in 2 **Vines** instead of the usual one.
