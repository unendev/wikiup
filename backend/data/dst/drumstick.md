A **Drumstick** is a [Meat](/wiki/Meats "Meats") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") dropped when a [Gobbler](/wiki/Gobbler "Gobbler") or [Pengull](/wiki/Pengull "Pengull") is killed. Two raw drumsticks can be added to a [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") along with another piece of meat, and 1 [Fruit](/wiki/Fruit "Fruit") or [Vegetable](/wiki/Vegetable "Vegetable") to produce a [Turkey Dinner](/wiki/Turkey_Dinner "Turkey Dinner"). A drumstick can also be used as a meat filler for other recipes, or dried on a [Drying Rack](/wiki/Drying_Rack "Drying Rack") for 1 day to produce a [Small Jerky](/wiki/Small_Jerky "Small Jerky"). Like other meats, a drumstick can be given to a [Pig](/wiki/Pig "Pig") to have them follow the player, or to the [Pig King](/wiki/Pig_King "Pig King") in exchange for a [Gold Nugget](/wiki/Gold_Nugget "Gold Nugget").

A **Fried Drumstick** is a Drumstick that has been [Cooked](/wiki/Cooking "Cooking") on a [Campfire](/wiki/Campfire "Campfire"), [Fire Pit](/wiki/Fire_Pit "Fire Pit") or [Star](/wiki/Star_Caller%27s_Staff "Star Caller's Staff"). When eaten, it replenishes 12.5 [Hunger](/wiki/Hunger "Hunger") and 1 [Health](/wiki/Health "Health"). If a Gobbler dies from being set on fire, it will drop a Fried Drumstick instead. A Fried Drumstick can be given to a Pig to have them follow the player, or to the Pig King in exchange for a Gold Nugget. It may also be used as a meat filler in the Crock Pot, however, the Turkey Dinner still requires two raw drumsticks as a prerequisite.

## https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/dont-starve-game/images/d/d8/Gift Icon.png/revision/latest?cb=20151222151842 Downloadable Content[]

In the *[Reign of Giants](/wiki/Reign_of_Giants "Reign of Giants")* DLC, Drumsticks can be acquired by killing [Buzzards](/wiki/Buzzard "Buzzard"), a [Moose/Goose](/wiki/Moose/Goose "Moose/Goose") or [Moslings](/wiki/Mosling "Mosling").

In the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC, Drumsticks are dropped by [Doydoys](/wiki/Doydoy "Doydoy").

In the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC, Drumsticks are dropped by [Peagawks](/wiki/Peagawk "Peagawk") or [Thunderbirds](/wiki/Thunderbird "Thunderbird") and there is a chance to spawn in a [Secret Bandit Camp](/wiki/Secret_Bandit_Camp "Secret Bandit Camp").
