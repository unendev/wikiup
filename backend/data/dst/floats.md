*Exclusive to:* Don't Starve Together

**Floats** are [Items](/wiki/Items "Items") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")*. They are crafted in the [Fishing Filter](/wiki/Fishing_Filter "Fishing Filter") and can be combined with a [Sea Fishing Rod](/wiki/Sea_Fishing_Rod "Sea Fishing Rod") to improve the casting range and accuracy of the landing. They can be crafted and at [Tackle Receptacle](/wiki/Tackle_Receptacle "Tackle Receptacle"), some can also be obtained by [Crabby Hermit](/wiki/Crabby_Hermit "Crabby Hermit") in exchange with [Empty Bottles](/wiki/Empty_Bottle "Empty Bottle").

The [Hardened Rubber Bung](/wiki/Trinket#Hardened_Rubber_Bung "Trinket") Trinket can also be used as a Float, however, it can't be crafted.

Below is a table of the performance statistics for all Floats.

| Float | Max Range | Range deviation  from casting point | Angle deviation  from casting point |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| None | 5 | 30% | 40° |
| Twigs | 7 | 20% | 30° |
| Wooden Ball Bobber | 9 | 20% | 20° |
| Hardened Slip Bobber | 11 | 20% | 20° |
| Hardened Rubber Bung | 13 | 20% | 30° |
| Jet Quill Float Crimson Quill Float Azure Quill Float Saffron Quill Float | 9 | 15% | 15° |
| Down Quill Float Malbatross Quill Float | 13 | 5% | 5° |

* Floats were introduced in the *[Hook, Line, and Inker](/wiki/Return_of_Them#Hook,_Line,_and_Inker "Return of Them")* update.
* The [Hardened Rubber Bung](/wiki/Trinkets#Hardened_Rubber_Bung "Trinkets") is the only trinket that can also be used as a float.
* Before the 2022 March QOL Update, the crafting recipes for the **Jet**, **Crimson**, **Azure**, **Saffron**, **Down**, and the **Malbatross Quill Float** are unlocked by giving their corresponding [Adverts](/wiki/Advert "Advert") to a Tackle Receptacle.
