The **Shadow Reaper** is a craftable [Melee Weapon](/wiki/Melee_Weapon "Melee Weapon") and tool [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in the *[From Beyond](/wiki/From_Beyond "From Beyond")* updates. It can be crafted at the [Shadowcraft Plinth](/wiki/Shadowcraft_Plinth "Shadowcraft Plinth") with 3 [Pure Horror](/wiki/Pure_Horror "Pure Horror") and 1 [Dark Tatters](/wiki/Dark_Tatters "Dark Tatters").

As a tool, Shadow Reaper can harvest all pickable resources with one swing within a 1-square radius of the turf, with the exception of small things like [Mushrooms](/wiki/Mushroom "Mushroom") and [Flowers](/wiki/Flower "Flower"). There is no limit to how many plants can be harvested at once, as long as they are in range.

As a weapon, the Shadow Reaper deals 38 normal damage and 18 [Planar Damage](/wiki/Planar_Damage "Planar Damage") (total damage 56). These values are increased if the [Void Cowl](/wiki/Void_Cowl "Void Cowl") is equipped: normal damage is increased by 10% to 41.8 points, and planar damage is increased by 9 to 27 (total damage 68.8). Furthermore, the Void Cowl increases the planar damage by 4 everytime you hit an enemy, stackable up to 5 times, for a total of 20 additional damage after 5 consecutive hits. At this point, the damage of the Reaper will be 88.8. The bonus disappears if the [Character](/wiki/Character "Character") removes the hood, does not attack for 3 seconds, or gets hit.

In addition, the Shadow Reaper deals 10% more damage to [Lunar Aligned](/wiki/Lunar_Aligned "Lunar Aligned") [Mobs](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs").

When the Shadow Reaper durability is exhausted, it will not break, but instead can no longer be equipped until repaired. Using a [Void Repair Kit](/wiki/Repair_Kits "Repair Kits") will restore the durability to 100%.

It is a level 3 item for [Maxwell's](/wiki/Maxwell/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Maxwell/Don't Starve Together") [Shadow Puppets](/wiki/Shadow_Puppet "Shadow Puppet").

It counts as a shadow weapon for [Wanda](/wiki/Wanda/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Wanda/Don't Starve Together"), applying her 1.2 or 1.75 damage multipliers for being middle aged or old respectively, only to the weapons regular 38 damage.

## Tips[]

* It is possible to reach 217-246 total damage as [Wolfgang](/wiki/Wolfgang "Wolfgang") when mighty, wearing the Void Cowl and [Void Robe](/wiki/Void_Robe "Void Robe"), when under [Warly's](/wiki/Warly "Warly") 1.2 times damage [Chili Flakes](/wiki/Chili_Flakes "Chili Flakes") effect, and if all 3 shadow affinity and all 5 planar damage increasing skills are active in Wolfgang's [Insight](/wiki/Insight "Insight").
  + This damage however only applies on lunar aligned mobs and makes this weapon the best for fighting the [Celestial Champion](/wiki/Celestial_Champion "Celestial Champion").

## Trivia[]

* The Shadow Reaper was introduced in the *[Terrors Below](/wiki/Terrors_Below "Terrors Below")* update.
* The blade of the Shadow Reaper resembles the head of a [Terrorbeak](/wiki/Shadow_Creature "Shadow Creature").
* The quotes while held indicate the the Shadow Reaper used to be a farmer of some kind before turning into what they are now, potentially referring to the Ancients "shedding their skin" depicted in the [Atrium](/wiki/Atrium "Atrium")'s ancient murals.
* The red cloth and shadowy form of this item and other shadow aligned items in the *Terrors Below* update resemble the [Ancient Herald](/wiki/Ancient_Herald "Ancient Herald").
