**Thulecite Fragments** are broken pieces of Thulecite which can be used to craft [Thulecite](/wiki/Thulecite "Thulecite"), repair a broken [Ancient Pseudoscience Station](/wiki/Ancient_Pseudoscience_Station "Ancient Pseudoscience Station"), or repair damaged [Thulecite Walls](/wiki/Thulecite_Walls "Thulecite Walls"). Six fragments are needed to craft one chunk of Thulecite, and the player must be near an Ancient Pseudoscience Station.

[Slurtles](/wiki/Slurtles "Slurtles") and [Snurtles](/wiki/Snurtles "Snurtles") will attempt to eat dropped Thulecite Fragments, and Slurtles will attack a player holding Thulecite Fragments if there isn't any other mineral around for them to eat.

## Don't Starve Together Don't Starve Together[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, Thulecite Fragments can also be gathered in the [Caves](/wiki/Caves "Caves") from [Cave Holes](/wiki/Cave_Hole "Cave Hole") using [The Lazy Forager](/wiki/The_Lazy_Forager "The Lazy Forager") or by feeding a [Dust Moth](/wiki/Dust_Moth "Dust Moth") an [Amberosia](/wiki/Amberosia "Amberosia"), making the moth slowly ornament their [Tidy Hidey-Hole](/wiki/Tidy_Hidey-Hole "Tidy Hidey-Hole"), creating Thulecite and Thulecite Fragments.

## Tips[]

* If you need to turn Thulecite into fragments, craft walls and hammer them. 1 Thulecite becomes 6 walls which yield 1 fragment each, making this process completely reversible at the minor cost of hammer durability.
