The **Bootleg Getaway** is an [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") in *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*. It can be obtained by trading [Fish](/wiki/Fishes "Fishes") with the [Frostjaw](/wiki/Frostjaw "Frostjaw").

When thrown into the ocean, it creates a **Whirlportal** and the map opens to select the destination for the exit portal. The portals remain for 60 seconds.

When directing a [Boat](/wiki/Boat "Boat") onto it, the Boat teleports to the other Whirlportal and the players on the Boat gain 20 Wetness Meter. The portal can also be used to travel back, as long as this is done within the 60 seconds time period.

## Tips[]

* The Whirlportal can be used to lead a [Boat](/wiki/Boat "Boat") from other players to the current location, as long as the area of the other Boat has already been revealed on the map. For the exact position the other Boat can light a [Flare](/wiki/Flare "Flare") or use a [Moonlens](/wiki/Moonlens "Moonlens").
