The **Extra-Adorable Lavae** is a follower exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*. It has 250 [health](/wiki/Health "Health") and can be hatched from the [Lavae Egg](/wiki/Lavae_Egg "Lavae Egg"). The Lavae will follow the [tooth](/wiki/Lavae_Tooth "Lavae Tooth") that dropped from the same egg as it spawned from. It will provide light, heat and allows food to be cooked on it, like a [Campfire](/wiki/Campfire "Campfire") and [Dwarf Star](/wiki/Star "Star"). The Extra-Adorable Lavae is smaller than the hostile [Lavae](/wiki/Lavae "Lavae"), allowing them to be distinguished a little easier. Extra-Adorable Lavae is immune to fire.

Lavae eat [Ashes](/wiki/Ashes "Ashes") and [Charcoal](/wiki/Charcoal "Charcoal") and will starve if they don't eat enough. If a Lavae gets very hungry it will set things on fire on its own so it can eat, ignoring the player. Like [Smallbirds](/wiki/Smallbird "Smallbird"), Lavae will starve to death (after 2 days, or 16 real minutes) if they can't find anything to eat, so it must be constantly fed. Ashes and Charcoal both restore 20% of Lavae's hunger or 3.2 min of life.

If a Lavae is frozen, it will turn into a rock and die after a while unless it is hit. It takes 3 hits from the [Ice Staff](/wiki/Ice_Staff "Ice Staff") to be frozen. Upon death, Lavae's Tooth will turn to Ashes. The Lavae will then become a [Chilled Lavae](/wiki/Chilled_Lavae "Chilled Lavae").

## Prototype Tips[]

* Packing the [Lavae Tooth](/wiki/Lavae_Tooth "Lavae Tooth") in [Bundling Wrap](/wiki/Bundling_Wrap "Bundling Wrap") will also package the Extra-Adorable Lavae, halting its hunger depletion and loss of warmth, saving it from potential attackers until the bundling wrap is reopened.
* Feeding the Lavae either charcoal or ash will heal it for 20 health each.
* Lavae are very useful in [Winter](/wiki/Winter "Winter") as they are essentially walking campfires, though they produce less light.
* The heat Lavae radiate is enough to set objects on fire, so caution is advised.
* Inspecting Lavae will tell where her hunger currently resides. (Ex: Webber says "It's my friend!" at 100%-76%, "Happy Lavae" at 75%-51%, "Poor hungry Lavae" at 51%-26%, and "It's starving!" under 26%)
* The Lavae is one of the few companions that can follow the player between the [Caves](/wiki/Caves "Caves") and the overworld.
* The [Scaled furnace](/wiki/Scaled_Furnace "Scaled Furnace") does not heat it up.

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* The name Lavae is a portmanteau of lava, and the plural of larva (larvae), a juvenile stage of some insects.
