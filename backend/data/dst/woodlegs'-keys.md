*Exclusive to:* Shipwrecked

**[Woodlegs](/wiki/Woodlegs "Woodlegs")' Keys** are items found in the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC. They are used to free Woodlegs from his cage at the [Volcano](/wiki/Volcano "Volcano") and can each be obtained in different ways.
