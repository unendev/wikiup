The **Armermry** is a character-specific [structure](/wiki/Structure "Structure") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in the *[Skill Spotlight Updates](/wiki/Skill_Spotlight_Update "Skill Spotlight Update")*. It can only be crafted by [Wurt](/wiki/Wurt "Wurt") with 2 [Boards](/wiki/Boards "Boards"), 5 [Logs](/wiki/Log "Log"), and 5 [Cut Grass](/wiki/Cut_Grass "Cut Grass") after mastering the [Hardheaded I](/wiki/Hardheaded_I "Hardheaded I") skill. The Armermry can only be build on [Marsh Turf](/wiki/Marsh_Turf "Marsh Turf").

### Usage[]

When [Loyal Merm Guards](/wiki/Loyal_Merm_Guard "Loyal Merm Guard") without an equipped head-item are near an Armermry, they will craft a [Swamp Brawler Helmet](/wiki/Swamp_Brawler_Helmet "Swamp Brawler Helmet") and equip it. Every crafted helmet will use 1 Log and 1 Cut Grass from the Armermry. After 5 crafted helmets, the door of the Armermry will stay open and the Armermry will need to be refilled with Logs and Cut Grass in order to deliver more helmets. Only Wurt and [characters](/wiki/Characters "Characters") wearing a [Clever Disguise](/wiki/Clever_Disguise "Clever Disguise") can refill the Armermry.

The Superior Armermry can only be build on [Marsh Turf](/wiki/Marsh_Turf "Marsh Turf").

### Usage[]

When [Loyal Merm Guards](/wiki/Loyal_Merm_Guard "Loyal Merm Guard") without an equipped head-item are near an Superior Armermry, they will craft a [Swamp Warrior Helmet](/wiki/Swamp_Warrior_Helmet "Swamp Warrior Helmet") and equip it. Every crafted helmet will use 1 Log and 1 Cut Grass from the Superior Armermry. After 5 crafted helmets, the door of the Superior Armermry will stay open and the Superior Armermry will need to be refilled with Logs and Cut Grass in order to deliver more helmets. Only Wurt and [characters](/wiki/Characters "Characters") wearing a [Clever Disguise](/wiki/Clever_Disguise "Clever Disguise") can refill the Superior Armermry.
