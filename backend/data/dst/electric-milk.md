**Electric Milk** is a [Dairy](/wiki/Dairy_product "Dairy product") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [item](/wiki/Item "Item") from the *[Reign of Giants](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Reign_of_Giants "Don't Starve: Reign of Giants")* DLC. It is dropped by killing a [Volt Goat](/wiki/Volt_Goat "Volt Goat") that has been charged, i.e. struck by [Lightning](/wiki/Lightning "Lightning"), or attacked with a [Morning Star](/wiki/Morning_Star "Morning Star") or an [Electric Dart](/wiki/Electric_Dart "Electric Dart"). Electric Milk can be consumed as it is, or used as an ingredient for recipes in the [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot"), such as [Ice Cream](/wiki/Ice_Cream "Ice Cream").

When consumed, it restores 10 Sanity, making it a mediocre [Sanity-restoring item](/wiki/Sanity_Boost "Sanity Boost"); this is far lower than the Sanity restored from Ice Cream, a food that requires a dairy item such as Electric Milk to create.

### Don't Starve Together Don't Starve Together[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, [WX-78](/wiki/WX-78/Don%27t_Starve_Together "WX-78/Don't Starve Together") can receive one unit of Charge when consuming Electric Milk. He can also use Electric Milk to craft the [Electrification Circuit](/wiki/Electrification_Circuit "Electrification Circuit"). [Wigfrid](/wiki/Wigfrid/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Wigfrid/Don't Starve Together")'s [Elding Spear](/wiki/Elding_Spear "Elding Spear") can also be used to charge a Volt Goat.
