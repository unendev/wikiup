The **Dorsal Fin** is an [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") introduced in the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC. It is dropped by killed [Bottlenose Ballphins](/wiki/Bottlenose_Ballphin "Bottlenose Ballphin") and can be used to craft [Ballphin Palaces](/wiki/Ballphin_Palace "Ballphin Palace"). Unlike [Shark Fins](/wiki/Shark_Fin "Shark Fin"), Dorsal Fins are not edible to the player. They can be used as a filler in the [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot"), falling under the same category as [Twigs](/wiki/Twigs "Twigs").

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* Dorsal Fins were added to the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC more than a year after its official completion in the *[Home Sea Home](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked#November_21,_2017_-_Home_Sea_Home_Update "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* update, after being tested in a beta branch for several months.
* While some players can mistake this filler item for a [meat](/wiki/Meats "Meats") food, in actuality it is not, as [Bunnymen](/wiki/Bunnyman "Bunnyman") will not attack the player if it has at least one Dorsal Fin in its inventory.
  + This also applies to mobs, as creatures such as [Tiger Sharks](/wiki/Tiger_Shark "Tiger Shark") or [Wildbores](/wiki/Wildbore "Wildbore") will not consume Dorsal Fins if they are on the ground.
