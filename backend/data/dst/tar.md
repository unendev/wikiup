**Tar** is a resource [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") introduced in the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC. It can be gathered from [Tar Slicks](/wiki/Tar_Slick "Tar Slick") using a [Tar Extractor](/wiki/Tar_Extractor "Tar Extractor") and is required for a variety of crafting recipes.

Tar can be used as an excellent [Fuel](/wiki/Category:Fuel "Category:Fuel") in [Camp Fires](/wiki/Camp_Fire "Camp Fire"), [Fire Pits](/wiki/Fire_Pit "Fire Pit"), as well as their [endothermic](/wiki/Endothermic_Fire "Endothermic Fire") versions, and in the Tar Trap. It can also be used to refuel the [Tar Extractor](/wiki/Tar_Extractor "Tar Extractor") and the [Sea Yard](/wiki/Sea_Yard "Sea Yard").
