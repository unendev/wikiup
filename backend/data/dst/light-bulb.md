A **Light Bulb** is a [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [item](/wiki/Item "Item") obtained in the [Caves](/wiki/Caves "Caves") by harvesting a [Light Flower](/wiki/Light_Flower "Light Flower") or as a drop from a [Slurper](/wiki/Slurper "Slurper") or [Big Tentacle](/wiki/Big_Tentacle "Big Tentacle") (with a 80% chance).

While technically a Food item (restoring 1 [Health](/wiki/Health "Health") each), Light Bulbs are mainly used for other purposes. They can be used to craft the [Lantern](/wiki/Lantern "Lantern") or as fuel for [Miner Hats](/wiki/Miner_Hat "Miner Hat") and [Lanterns](/wiki/Lantern "Lantern") (extending durability by ~19.2% or 1.5 minutes per Light Bulb).

Light Bulbs can be placed on the ground to be used as a [Light](/wiki/Light "Light") source and will prevent an attack from [Charlie](/wiki/Charlie "Charlie").

Since single Light Flowers regrow every 3 days, Light Bulbs are fully renewable, meaning an unlimited supply of fuel for Lanterns and Miner Hats. Double Light Flowers regrow every 5 days, and triple Light Flowers regrow every 6 days.

### Hamlet Hamlet[]

In the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC, Light Bulbs can be obtained by killing [Glowflies](/wiki/Glowfly "Glowfly") (with a 10% drop chance) and [Rabid Beetles](/wiki/Rabid_Beetle "Rabid Beetle") (with a 8% drop chance).

### Don't Starve Together Don't Starve Together[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, Light Bulbs can be obtained by killing [Skittersquids](/wiki/Skittersquid "Skittersquid"), and [Crustashines](/wiki/Crustashine "Crustashine") (with a 25% drop chance) which are both on the surface. After this addition it is possible to craft [Lanterns](/wiki/Lantern "Lantern") on Cave-less worlds. They also drop from [Bulbous Lightbugs](/wiki/Bulbous_Lightbug "Bulbous Lightbug").

## Tips[]

* Light Bulbs spoil quickly, so it may be wise to place them inside a [Bundling Wrap](/wiki/Bundling_Wrap "Bundling Wrap") to preserve them indefinitely.
  + Alternatively, one can keep multiple lanterns in a chest, and refuel them all at once to have a longer-lasting supply of light.

## Trivia[]

* The name Light Bulb is a play on words, in regards to a "light bulb" being the well-known invention popularized by Thomas Edison and "bulb" being the part of a plant where food is stored.
