The **Rabbit King Cudgel** is a [weapon](/wiki/Weapon "Weapon") [item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in the *[From Beyond](/wiki/From_Beyond "From Beyond")* updates. It can be obtained by killing the [Wrathful Rabbit King](/wiki/Wrathful_Rabbit_King "Wrathful Rabbit King").

## Usage[]

The Rabbit King Cudgel can be equipped as a weapon to fight [mobs](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs"). It has a durability of 125 uses and deals 51 [damage](/wiki/Damage "Damage"). It deals 71.4 damage to [Bunnymen](/wiki/Bunnyman "Bunnyman") and [Royal Rabbit Enforcers](/wiki/Royal_Rabbit_Enforcer "Royal Rabbit Enforcer"), but at the cost of 5 Sanity on each hit.

While the Rabbit King Cudgel is equipped, most [characters](/wiki/Characters "Characters") will lose 10 Sanity/min. and Bunnymen will run away from the characters, even if they have [meat](/wiki/Meats "Meats") items in their inventory or if playing as a monster character like [Webber](/wiki/Webber "Webber") or [Wurt](/wiki/Wurt "Wurt").

When [Maxwell](/wiki/Maxwell "Maxwell") equips the Rabbit King Cudgel, his [Shadow Duelist](/wiki/Shadow_Duelist "Shadow Duelist") will receive the tier 2 buff.

## Trivia[]

* The Rabbit King Cudgel was added in the *[Depths of Duplicity](/wiki/Depths_of_Duplicity "Depths of Duplicity")* update.
  + At the beginning of the beta, it was called *Rabbit King Crook*.
