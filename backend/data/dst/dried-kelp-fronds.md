**Dried Kelp Fronds** are a [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")*. It is produced by drying [Kelp Fronds](/wiki/Kelp_Fronds "Kelp Fronds") on a [Drying Rack](/wiki/Drying_Rack "Drying Rack") for a quarter of a [Day](/wiki/Day "Day"). It is good to quickly restore sanity due to its accessibility and long spoil time. It can also be a good ingredient to be used in the [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") as a vegetable, except it cannot be used to cook dishes which require a high vegetable value.

Dried Kelp Fronds restore 1 Health, 9.375 Hunger, 10 Sanity, and last 20 Days. It has 0.5 vegetable value in the Crock Pot.
