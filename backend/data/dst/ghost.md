**Ghosts** are aggressive [mobs](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs") that can appear with a 10% chance when digging up a [Grave](/wiki/Grave "Grave") with the [Shovel](/wiki/Shovel "Shovel") or [Regal Shovel](/wiki/Regal_Shovel "Regal Shovel"), or when all the graves are dug in [Maxwell's Graveyard](/wiki/Set_Pieces#Maxwell's_Cemetery "Set Pieces"). They make unique whispering noises when attacked or defeated. They do not attack like regular mobs, instead inflicting damage whenever next to the player or mob. This damage comes in the form of small attacks that are dealt very quickly over time. Ghosts don't drop anything upon "[death](/wiki/Death "Death")".

## Brain Behavior[]

Ghosts are fairly slow, and the player can easily outrun them. They have a very large insanity aura, so they can be noticed pursuing the player via the aura while they're not visible on screen. Ghosts will attack anything in their path, often killing small animals in a single blow, so they can be led to mobs if the player wants to lose them.

A Ghost will have a sad appearance, similar to [Abigail](/wiki/Abigail "Abigail"), when at low health.

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, Ghosts are neutral towards [Wendy](/wiki/Wendy "Wendy").

## Prototype Tips[]

* Because Ghosts will disappear after a while and do not drop anything, it is best to just let them be. The easiest way to kill a Ghost is by luring it to a few [Pigs](/wiki/Pig "Pig") or a [Beefalo](/wiki/Beefalo "Beefalo"); this way the Pigs or Beefalo will kill the Ghost.
* A Ghost can also be used as a grinder; let it kill whatever enemy it comes across, then take the drops.
* Ghosts are very slow and can be easily outrun. After the player has run a distance, the pursuing Ghost will likely have found another mob to be aggressive against.
* Despite levitating above the ground, Ghosts are not immune to [Tooth Traps](/wiki/Tooth_Traps "Tooth Traps").

## MosquitoBugs[]

* Ghosts try to kill planted [Mandrakes](/wiki/Mandrake "Mandrake"), despite them being invulnerable while planted; this causes them to simply hover above it.
* In *Don't Starve*, it's possible for a Ghost to damage the player even after being killed.
