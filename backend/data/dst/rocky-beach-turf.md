**Rocky Beach Turf** is a [Turf](/wiki/Turf "Turf") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")*. It can be found in the [Rocky Beach](/wiki/Rocky_Beach "Rocky Beach") and [Lunar Archipelago](/wiki/Lunar_Archipelago "Lunar Archipelago") [Biome](/wiki/Biomes "Biomes") on the [Lunar Island](/wiki/Lunar_Island "Lunar Island").

Plants like [Berry Bushes](/wiki/Berry_Bush "Berry Bush"), [Grass Tufts](/wiki/Grass_Tuft "Grass Tuft"), [Saplings](/wiki/Saplings "Saplings"), [Spiky Bushes](/wiki/Spiky_Bush "Spiky Bush"), [Lureplants](/wiki/Lureplant "Lureplant"), [Pine Cones](/wiki/Pine_Cone "Pine Cone"), and [Birchnuts](/wiki/Birchnut "Birchnut") can be planted on these Turfs. It will stop Lureplants' [Eyeplants](/wiki/Eyeplant "Eyeplant") from spawning.

Rocky Beach Turf can be crafted with 1 [Rocks](/wiki/Rocks "Rocks") and prototyped 1 [Driftwood Piece](/wiki/Driftwood_Piece "Driftwood Piece") at [Terra Firma Tamper](/wiki/Terra_Firma_Tamper "Terra Firma Tamper") in the [Decorations Filter](/wiki/Decorations_Filter "Decorations Filter").
