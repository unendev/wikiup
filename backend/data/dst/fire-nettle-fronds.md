**Fire Nettle Fronds** are a [Vegetable](/wiki/Vegetables "Vegetables") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")* and introduced in *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")*.

They can be harvested from [Fire Nettles](/wiki/Fire_Nettles "Fire Nettles"). Upon consumption, they cost 3 health, 5 sanity and increase the player's perceived temperature by 60° for 1 minute, making them less susceptible to [freezing](/wiki/Freezing "Freezing").
