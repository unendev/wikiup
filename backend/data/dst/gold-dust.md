**Gold Dust** is a [Resource](/wiki/Category:Resources "Category:Resources") introduced in the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC. It is obtained by mining artichoke shaped [Boulders](/wiki/Boulder "Boulder"), using a [Gold Pan](/wiki/Gold_Pan "Gold Pan") at a [Sparkling Pool](/wiki/Sparkling_Pool "Sparkling Pool"), or mining [Ruined Sculptures](/wiki/Ruined_Sculptures "Ruined Sculptures") in the [Ancient Pig Ruins](/wiki/Ancient_Pig_Ruins "Ancient Pig Ruins"). Six Gold Dust can be refined to a [Gold Nugget](/wiki/Gold_Nugget "Gold Nugget"). This recipe can be prototyped with a [Science Machine](/wiki/Science_Machine "Science Machine").

If Gold Dust is dropped on the ground near a [Pangolden](/wiki/Pangolden "Pangolden"), the Pangolden will eat it. After consuming three Gold Dust, the Pangolden will produce a Gold Nugget. This is more efficient than refining Gold Dust into Gold Nuggets, as it requires three instead of six Gold Dust.
