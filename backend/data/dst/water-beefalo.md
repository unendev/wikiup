**Water Beefalo**, also known as **Ox**, are the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* version of [Beefalo](/wiki/Beefalo "Beefalo"). They have the same characteristics as Beefalo, with the only differences being that the Water Beefalo do not drop [Beefalo Wool](/wiki/Beefalo_Wool "Beefalo Wool") if killed, they run faster than regular Beefalo, and they drop [Horns](/wiki/Horn "Horn") instead of [Beefalo Horns](/wiki/Beefalo_Horn "Beefalo Horn"). They are native to the [Mangrove](/wiki/Mangrove "Mangrove") biome and are usually found with a small herd. While they spend most of their time in water, they can also walk on land.

Similar to Beefalo, Water Beefalo will periodically drop [Manure](/wiki/Manure "Manure"). When one of them is attacked, the entire herd will rush to their defense. They will huddle together at [Dusk](/wiki/Dusk "Dusk") and sleep through the [Night](/wiki/Night "Night"). Water Beefalo also go through a mating season. During this time, the Water Beefalo will remain neutral on water but be hostile on land.

## Prototype Tips[]

* Leading the [Sealnado](/wiki/Sealnado "Sealnado") to a large herd of Water Beefalo and fighting on the water is an effective tactic. The vortex AoE does not affect the Beefalo and they can withstand up to 7 melee strikes, allowing plenty of time for them to deal significant damage. Unfortunately, the [Tiger Shark’s](/wiki/Tiger_Shark "Tiger Shark") AoE dive attacks can lead to many Beefalo dying and massive spawns of [Crocodogs](/wiki/Crocodog "Crocodog") and [Sea Hounds](/wiki/Sea_Hound "Sea Hound") so this tactic is less effective.
* An entire herd of Water Beefalo can easily be killed by attacking with a [Boat Cannon](/wiki/Boat_Cannon "Boat Cannon") while they huddle together during the [Night](/wiki/Night "Night"). It should be noted that any meat dropped will be cooked and [Horns](/wiki/Horn "Horn") will be burnt to ash. Using a [Volcano Staff](/wiki/Volcano_Staff "Volcano Staff") instead will not burn them if used in the water.
* Water Beefalo run faster than regular [Beefalo](/wiki/Beefalo "Beefalo") and can reliably hit the player even while poisoned, so caution is advised when trying to kite them. It is especially dangerous to kite them with melee weapons while on a boat even with speed boosts from [Coffee](/wiki/Coffee "Coffee") and an [Iron Wind](/wiki/Sail "Sail").
* Water Beefalo cannot enter the shallow waters outside their Mangrove Biome. By staying out of reach and attacking with ranged weapons, players can reliably farm them from a boat. It is wise to also have [Bottlenose Ballphin](/wiki/Bottlenose_Ballphin "Bottlenose Ballphin") allies to help with the resulting [Crocodogs](/wiki/Crocodog "Crocodog") and [Sea Hounds](/wiki/Sea_Hound "Sea Hound") that are likely to spawn due to all the dropped meat.

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* Water Beefalo cannot be shaved with a [Razor](/wiki/Razor "Razor"), meaning there is no Water Beefalo Wool or similar to obtain. This feature might have been planned at some time during development, as in the game files textures for the "shaved" Water Beefalo can be found.
* The previous name for the Water Beefalo was Ox, they were renamed in the *[Hang Ten](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked#December_17_-_Hang_Ten_update "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* update.
* The *Home Sea Home* update poster has a Baby Water Beefalo. In game, however, this was not added until the *[Don't Starve Mega Update](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Mega_Update "Don't Starve Mega Update")* update.
