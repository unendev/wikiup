The **Cactus Flower** is a [Vegetable](/wiki/Vegetable "Vegetable") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") from the *[Reign of Giants](/wiki/Reign_of_Giants "Reign of Giants")* DLC. It is obtained along with [Cactus Flesh](/wiki/Cactus_Flesh "Cactus Flesh") when harvesting a [Cactus](/wiki/Cactus "Cactus") only during [Summer](/wiki/Summer "Summer"). It can be consumed raw or used to make [Flower Salad](/wiki/Flower_Salad "Flower Salad") in a [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot"). It is also used to craft a [Floral Shirt](/wiki/Floral_Shirt "Floral Shirt").
