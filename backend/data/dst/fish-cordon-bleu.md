**Fish Cordon Bleu** is a [Warly](/wiki/Warly "Warly")-specific [Meat](/wiki/Meat "Meat") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") cooked in the [Portable Crock Pot](/wiki/Portable_Crock_Pot "Portable Crock Pot"), exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*. It requires two [Frog Legs](/wiki/Frog_Legs "Frog Legs") and [Fishes](/wiki/Fishes "Fishes") with a total Fish value of 1.0 or higher, and takes 40 seconds to cook. Fillers cannot be [Inedible](/wiki/Inedible "Inedible").

When consumed, it immediately sets the player's [Wetness](/wiki/Wetness "Wetness") to 0, and grants Wetness immunity for 5 minutes.
