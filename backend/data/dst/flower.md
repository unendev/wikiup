**Flowers** are found in [Grasslands](/wiki/Grasslands "Grasslands"), [Forests](/wiki/Forests "Forests"), or [Savanna](/wiki/Savanna "Savanna") biomes. When picked, they restore 5 [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity") and yield one [Petals](/wiki/Petals "Petals"). When eaten, Petals provide 1 health. There are 10 different kinds of Flowers that all behave the same. Any that behave differently are detailed below.

Flowers near a player on the [Surface World](/wiki/Surface_World "Surface World") periodically spawn [Butterflies](/wiki/Butterfly "Butterfly"), which can be captured in a [Bug Net](/wiki/Bug_Net "Bug Net") and planted to create a new Flower. A [Homeless Bee](/wiki/Bee#Homeless_Bee "Bee") will eventually plant a new flower somewhere after pollinating 6 different nearby flowers (see also Evil Flowers below).

Flowers need the same amount of natural light to grow as [Farm Plants](/wiki/Farm_Plant "Farm Plant"). They can grow on the surface or under [Cave Lights](/wiki/Cave_Light "Cave Light"), but when planted in the dark of the caves, they will wither in less than a day. A withered flower can never recover, but can be picked to yield 1 [Cut Grass](/wiki/Cut_Grass "Cut Grass").

6 flowers are needed near each [Bee Box](/wiki/Bee_Box "Bee Box") to enable [Honey](/wiki/Honey "Honey") production.

**Evil Flowers** are a type of Flower that spawn around [Totally Normal Trees](/wiki/Totally_Normal_Tree "Totally Normal Tree") and several
/[Marble Sculptures](/wiki/Marble_Sculptures "Marble Sculptures") set pieces. In *[Don't Starve](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve "Don't Starve")*, they spawn around the [Set Pieces](/wiki/Set_Piece "Set Piece") for [Things](/wiki/Things "Things"), and [Maxwell's Door](/wiki/Maxwell%27s_Door "Maxwell's Door").

Evil Flowers act much like regular flowers, spawning Butterflies and attracting Bees. Unlike regular flowers they have a -25/minute Sanity aura. Picking one costs 5 sanity (instead of giving 5 sanity) and yields [Dark Petals](/wiki/Dark_Petals "Dark Petals") instead of petals.

Evil Flowers are generated with the world. Once they are all picked, there is no way to obtain more. If any remain, they can be propagated very slowly if up to 6 Evil Flowers are pollinated by a [Homeless Bee](/wiki/Bee#Homeless_Bee "Bee") (the higher percentage of 6 flowers pollinated that are evil, the higher the chance that the resulting flower will be evil too, and the faster more can be made). Since evil flowers are mainly useful for Dark Petals, and [Nightmare Fuel](/wiki/Nightmare_Fuel "Nightmare Fuel") is so easy to obtain from shadow creatures, the only reasons to do this would be decoration, or to mass-produce [Psychosis Experiments](/wiki/Psychosis_Experiment "Psychosis Experiment") during [Hallowed Nights](/wiki/Hallowed_Nights "Hallowed Nights") (presumably also for decoration).

It is possible to farm Dark Petals for [Nightmare Fuel](/wiki/Nightmare_Fuel "Nightmare Fuel") using the [Full Moon](/wiki/Moon_Cycle "Moon Cycle") effect, which temporarily turns Flowers into Evil Flowers for a few days. During the course of the game flower generation can become a fire hazard or simply an annoyance requiring pruning. Waiting for the full moon is an easy way to passively collect Evil Petals for [Nightmare Fuel](/wiki/Nightmare_Fuel "Nightmare Fuel"), rather than normal petals for fuel or [Rot](/wiki/Rot "Rot").

**Ferns** are naturally-spawned objects which are similar to Flowers. They grow randomly in [Caves](/wiki/Caves "Caves") and more abundantly in [Ruins](/wiki/Ruins "Ruins"). Ferns can be picked to obtain 1 [Foliage](/wiki/Foliage "Foliage").

When eaten, the Foliage restores 1 Health.

Some Ferns become more red in color during the Nightmare Phase of the Ruins.

## Cultivation[]

### Flower[]

Flowers can be manually cultivated by planting living Butterflies. Using a Bug Net to capture any number of Butterflies, the player can right click them on the ground to plant them as they would plant [Pine Cones](/wiki/Pine_Cone "Pine Cone") or [Grass Tufts](/wiki/Grass_Tuft "Grass Tuft"). One Butterfly can be planted to create one Flower in a location of the player's choosing. The physical area occupied by the Flower is very small, allowing for densely planted Flower [gardens](/wiki/Garden_Digamajig "Garden Digamajig").

Cultivation of Flowers is of interest because of their Sanity bonus when picked and use for [Crafting](/wiki/Crafting "Crafting") the Garland and the [Pretty Parasol](/wiki/Pretty_Parasol "Pretty Parasol"), however the long term use of cultivation is the creation of efficient Honey farms through the use of Bee Boxes, for which an ample supply of Flowers is necessary.

Because Flowers spawn Butterflies, cultivation of a large Flower bed can usually proceed very quickly, provided the player has sufficient materials to build new Bug Nets.

### Evil Flower[]

Using Evil Flowers is one of the few ways to acquire [Nightmare Fuel](/wiki/Nightmare_Fuel "Nightmare Fuel") without lowering Sanity to low levels sanity or insanity inducing items. For this reason, it may be of interest for players to cultivate Evil Flowers as a means of supplementing their supply of Nightmare Fuel. Unlike normal Flowers, Evil Flowers cannot be manually planted.

The player should begin by crafting a Bug Net and capturing some number of Bees. Once captured, these Bees; become "homeless" and will not return to their hive. After releasing these Bees near some existing Evil Flowers, they will begin to visit them as they would normally. It is also necessary for the player to be near the area the Bees are working, since the bee's AI routine will not run if the player is too far away.

Bees must visit 6 different flowers before they can plant another flower. The flower that is planted will depend upon which type of flowers the 6 were, so as soon as you have 6 evil flowers in the area it is a good idea to eliminate any normal flowers so the evil flower chance becomes 100%. Most often the Bees will plant the flower after the 6th flower is visited, but there is a small chance it will visit another flower or land without planting. Clearing the area of [Trees](/wiki/Tree "Tree"), Grass etc. will also make it easier to spot newly spawned Evil Flowers.

* Evil Flowers resemble stylized flowers that can sometimes be found adorning fabrics or wallpaper, and which are faintly seen on Maxwell's walls in some of the [William Carter Puzzle](/wiki/William_Carter_Puzzles "William Carter Puzzles") images.
* Ferns were originally green.

* If you place a flower in a [Cave](/wiki/Caves "Caves") it will wilt, to stop this from happening you should place them under a [Cave Light](/wiki/Cave_Light "Cave Light").
* The [WX-78](/wiki/WX-78 "WX-78") quote for evil flowers is very likely to be a reference to Bender, from Futurama
