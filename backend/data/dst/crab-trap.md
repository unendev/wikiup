The Crab Trap is an item that first appeared in [the Gorge](/wiki/The_Gorge "The Gorge") event Don't Starve Together. You can get a trap from [Billy](/wiki/Billy "Billy") for 1 [red mark](/wiki/Coin "Coin"). This item is necessary for catching a rather expensive ingredient, a [pebble crab](/wiki/Pebble_Crab "Pebble Crab"), for catching which you need a bait in the form of meat or fish (crabs are not caught on stones). Well, or you can set a trap and wait for the crab to accidentally wander into it.

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* You can catch a crab by placing a trap directly under it when it buries itself.
