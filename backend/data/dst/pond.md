**Ponds** are small bodies of water that usually spawn in [Grasslands](/wiki/Grasslands "Grasslands"), [Forests](/wiki/Forest "Forest"), [Marshes](/wiki/Marsh "Marsh"), and [Ruins](/wiki/Ruins "Ruins").

There are three types of Ponds depending on the [Biome](/wiki/Biomes "Biomes") they spawn in. Green Ponds appear in Grasslands and Forests and spawn [Frogs](/wiki/Frog "Frog") during the day. Purple Ponds appear in Marshes and spawn [Mosquitoes](/wiki/Mosquito "Mosquito") at [dusk and night](/wiki/Day-Night_Cycle "Day-Night Cycle"). Blue Ponds are found in Ruins and contains [Eels](/wiki/Eel "Eel") instead of [Fish](/wiki/Fish "Fish").

Using a [Fishing Rod](/wiki/Fishing_Rod "Fishing Rod"), [Fish](/wiki/Fish "Fish") (in surface Ponds) or [Eels](/wiki/Eel "Eel") (in Ruins Ponds) can be caught at any time of [day](/wiki/Day "Day"). Fishing takes 4 seconds plus 4 seconds for each missing fish in the pond. All variations of Ponds contain up to 10 fishes/eels, and one fish/eel is regenerated every 160 seconds.

Ponds on the surface will freeze in winter once the temperature is low enough, and when frozen, they won't spawn creatures. They also cannot be fished, though they can be walked over.

### Don't Starve Together Don't Starve Together[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")* ponds in the caves can grow [Nitre Formations](/wiki/Nitre_Formation "Nitre Formation") during [Acid Rain](/wiki/Acid_Rain "Acid Rain") and will turn yellow until mined with a [Pickaxe](/wiki/Pickaxe "Pickaxe"). Ponds can also be used to fill up [Watering Cans](/wiki/Watering_Can "Watering Can") or [Mosquito Sacks](/wiki/Mosquito_Sack "Mosquito Sack") to make [Water Balloons](/wiki/Water_Balloon "Water Balloon") as long as they aren't frozen.

[Moose/Goose](/wiki/Moose/Goose "Moose/Goose") nests will often spawn close to frog ponds that have [Berry Bushes](/wiki/Berry_Bush "Berry Bush") and [Birchnut Trees](/wiki/Birchnut_Tree "Birchnut Tree") around them (see [Moose/Goose Nest Set Piece](/wiki/Set_Piece#Moose/Goose_Nest "Set Piece")).

Ponds spawn with 2-4 **Plants** along their banks, which can be examined separately. They cannot be picked, but they can be set on [Fire](/wiki/Fire "Fire") to yield [Ashes](/wiki/Ashes "Ashes"). The plants will grow back every [Summer](/wiki/Summer "Summer"). Ponds found in the Ruins are surrounded by [Cave Lichen](/wiki/Cave_Lichen "Cave Lichen") that resemble [Lichen](/wiki/Lichen "Lichen") instead of the normal plants.

## Tips[]

* When fishing, one should be wary of Frogs and Mosquitoes, as they are aggressive.
* When fishing in a Marsh, it should be noted that the Marsh is also the native habitat of [Tentacles](/wiki/Tentacle "Tentacle") and [Merms](/wiki/Merm "Merm").
* One may fish in Frog Ponds unbothered at dusk when the Frogs return to the water, and in the spring and fall when it is warm enough to thaw the Pond but not warm enough for Frogs to spawn. One may fish in Mosquito Ponds unbothered during the day, since Mosquitoes only come out at dusk/night.
* Frog Ponds will sometimes show up in a small cluster. Pig houses can be built near/inside this cluster (preferably walled off) and the frogs will kill any pigs that spawn during the day but will not eat the meat. With this setup, Pig Skin and Meat can be collected regularly at dusk/night.

## Trivia[]

* Frog Ponds and Mosquito Ponds were added in the *[A Little Rain Must Fall](/wiki/Version_History#A_Little_Rain_Must_Fall "Version History")* update, while the ones found in the Ruins were added in the *[Stuff of Nightmares](/wiki/Version_History#The_Stuff_of_Nightmares "Version History")* update.
* After burning the Plant or Algae, saving and reloading the game will cause them to reappear on the pond.
