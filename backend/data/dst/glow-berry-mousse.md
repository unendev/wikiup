**Glow Berry Mousse** is a [Warly](/wiki/Warly "Warly") specific [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [item](/wiki/Item "Item") cooked in the [Portable Crock Pot](/wiki/Portable_Crock_Pot "Portable Crock Pot") that is exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*.

It requires either two [Lesser Glow Berries](/wiki/Lesser_Glow_Berry "Lesser Glow Berry") or one [Glow Berry](/wiki/Glow_Berry "Glow Berry"), and a total Fruits value of 2.0 or higher. Filler can not be [Meats](/wiki/Meats "Meats") or [Inedible](/wiki/Inedible "Inedible"). Note that the two Lesser Glow Berries or one Glow Berry both count for 1 Fruit value. Adding two or more [Durians](/wiki/Durian "Durian") may result in [Monster Tartare](/wiki/Monster_Tartare "Monster Tartare").

When eaten, it causes the consumer to generate a large amount of light (about as much as a [Torch](/wiki/Torch "Torch")), with the light decaying slowly over the course of 2 days.
