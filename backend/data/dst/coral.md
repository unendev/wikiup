**Coral** is a resource in the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC. It can be obtained by mining [Coral Reefs](/wiki/Coral_Reef "Coral Reef"). It is also frequently obtained when using a [Trawl Net](/wiki/Trawl_Net "Trawl Net"). Coral is used to craft [Particulate Purifiers](/wiki/Particulate_Purifier "Particulate Purifier"), [Anti Venom](/wiki/Anti_Venom "Anti Venom"), and [Mussel Beds](/wiki/Mussel_Bed "Mussel Bed"), and can also be [Refined](/wiki/Refine "Refine") into [Limestone](/wiki/Limestone "Limestone").

## Prototype Tips[]

* [Bottlenose Ballphins](/wiki/Bottlenose_Ballphin "Bottlenose Ballphin") can be **hired** to mine coral. While they only work at daytime, befriending a few of them help efficiently mining coral without wearing out the [Pickaxe](/wiki/Pickaxe "Pickaxe")'s durability.
