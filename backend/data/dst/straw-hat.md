The **Straw Hat** is a [Hat](/wiki/Category:Hats "Category:Hats") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") found in the [Dress Tab](/wiki/Dress_Tab "Dress Tab"). It costs 12 [Cut Grass](/wiki/Cut_Grass "Cut Grass") to craft. It is required to construct the [Miner Hat](/wiki/Miner_Hat "Miner Hat") and the [Bush Hat](/wiki/Bush_Hat "Bush Hat"). In the base game, it has no functionality when worn.

## Gift Icon Downloadable Content[]

In [DLCs](/wiki/DLC "DLC") as well as *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, the Straw Hat does not require a [Science Machine](/wiki/Science_Machine "Science Machine") to prototype. It provides small protection from [Wetness](/wiki/Wetness "Wetness") and 60 points of protection from [Overheating](/wiki/Overheating "Overheating"), and loses [Durability](/wiki/Durability "Durability") when worn. The [Sewing Kit](/wiki/Sewing_Kit "Sewing Kit") repairs 100% of its durability.

With the **Seasonal Starting Items** default preset, the Straw Hat will be given to any players joining a world for the first time, during [Spring](/wiki/Spring "Spring").

The simple combination of using the Straw Hat and the [Umbrella](/wiki/Umbrella "Umbrella") is enough to prevent the player from getting wet during rain, being an easy and accessible way out for the Wetness problem as the Straw Hat has a low cost based solely on Cut Grass.

In the *[Reign of Giants](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Reign_of_Giants "Don't Starve: Reign of Giants")* DLC, the Straw Hat is also one of the ingredients required to craft a [Rain Hat](/wiki/Rain_Hat "Rain Hat").

In the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC, it is one of the ingredients required to craft the [Snakeskin Hat](/wiki/Snakeskin_Hat "Snakeskin Hat"), the [Captain Hat](/wiki/Captain_Hat "Captain Hat") and the [Dumbrella](/wiki/Dumbrella "Dumbrella").

In the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC, Straw Hats can be bought for 3 [Oincs](/wiki/Oinc "Oinc") at [The Sow's Ear Hat Shop](/wiki/The_Sow%27s_Ear_Hat_Shop "The Sow's Ear Hat Shop") in the [Pig City](/wiki/Pig_City "Pig City").

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* The Straw Hat used to cost 30 [Science Points](/wiki/Science "Science") to craft and had a value of 10 Science Points when used on a Science Machine in the research days of *Don't Starve*.
* Before the *[Spoiled Rotten](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve/Version_History#January_15th,_2013_-_Spoiled_Rotten "Don't Starve/Version History")* update, the Straw Hat was used to craft the [Spiderhat](/wiki/Spiderhat "Spiderhat").
* Despite being made of Cut Grass, this hat is non-flammable.
* The Straw Hat's description used to be "*How fashionable!*".
