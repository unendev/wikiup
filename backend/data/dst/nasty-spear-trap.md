**Nasty Spear Traps** are [traps](/wiki/Category:Traps "Category:Traps") exclusive to the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC.

They are found within [Ancient Pig Ruins](/wiki/Ancient_Pig_Ruins "Ancient Pig Ruins") and activate periodically, when a [Mob](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs") or player is in close proximity, or by pressure plates. If the pressure plate spawned with an [item](/wiki/Items "Items") on it, the traps will activate when the item is taken; otherwise, they activate when the pressure plate is stepped on or weighed down.

In addition to damaging anything that is on top of them when they activate, Nasty Spear Traps act as a wall, and prevent the player and most other Mobs from passing through (except [Shadow Creatures](/wiki/Shadow_Creatures "Shadow Creatures")).

Nasty Spear Traps can be destroyed using [Weapons](/wiki/Category:Weapons "Category:Weapons"), or instantaneously using [Shears](/wiki/Shears "Shears"). Doing so will cause them to become **Broken Spear Traps**. They are not currently known to regenerate.

## Prototype Tips[]

* A [Ham Bat](/wiki/Ham_Bat "Ham Bat") can be used to destroy Nasty Spear Traps at no cost of weapon durability.
* A [Torch](/wiki/Torch "Torch") can be used to destroy many activated Nasty Spear Traps at once at minimal cost.
