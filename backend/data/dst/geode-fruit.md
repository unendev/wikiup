The **Geode Fruit** is an [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in the *[From Beyond](/wiki/From_Beyond "From Beyond")* updates. Geode Fruits grow periodically on [Sproutrocks](/wiki/Sproutrock "Sproutrock"), where 3 of them can be picked from at once.

## Usage[]

Geode Fruits can be placed near a [Campfire](/wiki/Campfire "Campfire"), [Fire Pit](/wiki/Fire_Pit "Fire Pit"), [Scaled Furnace](/wiki/Scaled_Furnace "Scaled Furnace"), or any other heat source. When they heat up to 60°, the fruits will break, dropping a random [Gem](/wiki/Gems "Gems").

## Trivia[]

* Geode Fruits were added in the *[Staying Afloat](/wiki/Staying_Afloat "Staying Afloat")* update.
