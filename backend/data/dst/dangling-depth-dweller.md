The **Dangling Depth Dweller** is a [Spider](/wiki/Category:Spiders "Category:Spiders") variant found in the [Ruins](/wiki/Ruins "Ruins") and from webbed mushtrees in blue mushtree forests. They drop from the ceiling when the player steps on their web or chops the webbed trees.

On the ground, they can leap at enemies in the same way as [Spider Warriors](/wiki/Spider_Warrior "Spider Warrior"). Their webs are commonly found in the [Military](/wiki/Military "Military") and [Labyrinth](/wiki/Labyrinth "Labyrinth") [Biomes](/wiki/Biomes "Biomes"), along with [Ornate Chests](/wiki/Ornate_Chest "Ornate Chest"). Their nests are stuck on the ceiling and unreachable, although they can be destroyed by igniting anything flammable in the center of the web.

Dangling Depth Dwellers have the same drops as all other Spiders: [Monster Meat](/wiki/Monster_Meat "Monster Meat"), [Spider Glands](/wiki/Spider_Gland "Spider Gland"), or [Silk](/wiki/Silk "Silk").

## Reign of Giants icon Reign of Giants[]

When playing as [Webber](/wiki/Webber "Webber") in the *[Reign of Giants](/wiki/Reign_of_Giants "Reign of Giants")* DLC, Dangling Depth Dwellers are neutral and can be befriended with [Meats](/wiki/Meats "Meats").

## Don't Starve Together icon Don't Starve Together[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, Dangling Depth Dwellers will retreat to their dens during the day. Their webs are permanent and cannot be destroyed by lighting nearby items on fire. Dangling Depth Dweller dens will convert nearby blue mushtrees to [webbed Mushtrees](/wiki/Mushtree "Mushtree"). Chopping a webbed mushtree will cause spiders to spawn from all nearby dens. When caught in a trap, they become an inventory item rather than being instantly killed.

## Prototype Tips[]

* A resource-friendly way to dispose of a nest involves lighting a single [Log](/wiki/Log "Log") on fire directly underneath the nest. This can be done by dropping a Log at the center of the web. The center can be identified by the player character icon covering the body of the spider icon on the [map](/wiki/Map "Map"). Destroying the spiders that drop down first will make this task easier, but if multiple nests are located close to each other, more than two spiders might drop at the same time. This tactic does not work in *Don't Starve Together*.
* [Shadow Creatures](/wiki/Shadow_Creature "Shadow Creature") that spawn from [Nightmare Lights](/wiki/Nightmare_Light "Nightmare Light") can activate the Dweller's web, which if left unchecked will spawn a large number of Spiders over time.
