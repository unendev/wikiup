The **Stone Plug** is a Naturally spawning [Structure](/wiki/Structure "Structure") exclusive to *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC. It is a unique object which can exclusively be found inside the BFB's Caves; it mainly acts as an obstacle which has to be mined in order to exit these caves.

Once the player has been transported to the [BFB's Nest](/wiki/BFB%27s_Nest "BFB's Nest"), its only way to escape the island is to mine the [Sinkhole](/wiki/Sinkhole "Sinkhole") on its surface, and enter the Caves just below. These Caves contain many rooms, and a Stone Plug will be present in the back wall of one of them, which the player will have to mine with a [Pickaxe](/wiki/Pickaxe "Pickaxe") or [Opulent Pickaxe](/wiki/Opulent_Pickaxe "Opulent Pickaxe") in order to exit them. This will lead the player to one of the [Cave Clefts](/wiki/Cave_Cleft "Cave Cleft") on one of the four other islands, and only then, to the surface.

It takes 6 hits to be mined, and will have a chance to yield [Rocks](/wiki/Rocks "Rocks"), [Flint](/wiki/Flint "Flint"), and [Nitre](/wiki/Nitre "Nitre").
