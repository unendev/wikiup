**Dried Seaweed** is a [Vegetable](/wiki/Vegetable "Vegetable") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") introduced in the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC. It is produced by drying [Seaweed](/wiki/Seaweed "Seaweed") on a [Drying Rack](/wiki/Drying_Rack "Drying Rack") for 1/4 of a [Day](/wiki/Day "Day").

Dried Seaweed restores 3 Health, 12 Hunger, 10 Sanity and lasts 20 days. In comparison, [Cooked Seaweed](/wiki/Cooked_Seaweed "Cooked Seaweed") restores same Health, a bit less Hunger and no Sanity benefit and lasts half as long. Neither Cooked nor Dried Seaweed can be used for [California Rolls](/wiki/California_Roll "California Roll"), so its only use in the [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") is as general [Vegetable](/wiki/Vegetable "Vegetable") filler.
