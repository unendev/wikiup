*Exclusive to:* Don't Starve Together

**Emoticons** are a type of [Curio](/wiki/Curio_Cabinet "Curio Cabinet") in *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*. They can be acquired through daily or randomly timed drops, through event [Chests](/wiki/Treasury "Treasury") (either purchased through the Shop or earned by leveling up) or weaved with [Spools](/wiki/Spools "Spools"). Emoticons can be viewed in the [Curio Cabinet](/wiki/Curio_Cabinet "Curio Cabinet").
