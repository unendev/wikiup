The **Ice Staff** is a [Magic](/wiki/Magic "Magic") [ranged weapon](/wiki/Weapon#Ranged_Weapons "Weapon"). It requires 1 [Spear](/wiki/Spear "Spear") and 1 [Blue Gem](/wiki/Blue_Gem "Blue Gem") to craft and a [Prestihatitator](/wiki/Prestihatitator "Prestihatitator") to prototype. It may also be found in an [Ornate Chest](/wiki/Ornate_Chest "Ornate Chest") or dropped by an [Ancient Pseudoscience Station](/wiki/Ancient_Pseudoscience_Station "Ancient Pseudoscience Station") when struck with a [Hammer](/wiki/Hammer "Hammer").

When used, the Ice Staff temporarily freezes a [Mob](/wiki/Mob "Mob") usually within 1-4 blasts. Small creatures such as [Birds](/wiki/Birds "Birds") and [Rabbits](/wiki/Rabbits "Rabbits") take one blast to be frozen, while big ones such as [Treeguards](/wiki/Treeguard "Treeguard") take four blasts before they are frozen. Each use costs 5% durability, equating to 20 uses.

Each time an Ice Staff is used, the character's [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity") is decreased by 1.

Creatures that periodically drop things (such as [Beefalo](/wiki/Beefalo "Beefalo") producing [Manure](/wiki/Manure "Manure")) will still leave them behind while frozen.

[Lureplants](/wiki/Lureplant "Lureplant"), Eyeplants, [Rock Lobsters](/wiki/Rock_Lobster "Rock Lobster"), [Depths Worms](/wiki/Depths_Worm "Depths Worm"), [Chester](/wiki/Chester "Chester"), [Baby Beefalo](/wiki/Baby_Beefalo "Baby Beefalo"), [Blue Hounds](/wiki/Blue_Hound "Blue Hound"), [Ghosts](/wiki/Ghost "Ghost"), [Shadow Creatures](/wiki/Shadow_Creature "Shadow Creature"), [Packim Baggims](/wiki/Packim_Baggims "Packim Baggims"), and [Tiger Sharks](/wiki/Tiger_Shark "Tiger Shark") are immune to the Ice Staff's freezing effect.

## Gift Icon Downloadable Content[]

In the *[Reign of Giants](/wiki/Reign_of_Giants "Reign of Giants")* DLC, the Ice Staff can be used to extinguish smoldering or burning objects. The amount of blasts needed to freeze a [Deerclops](/wiki/Deerclops "Deerclops") is increased to six.

In the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Hamlet "Hamlet")* DLC, Ice Staves can be bought for 30 [Oincs](/wiki/Oinc "Oinc") at [The Flying Pig Arcane Shop](/wiki/The_Flying_Pig_Arcane_Shop "The Flying Pig Arcane Shop") in the [Pig City](/wiki/Pig_City "Pig City").

## Don't Starve Together icon Don't Starve Together[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, select freezable mobs (mostly bosses) have the *diminishing returns* trait. A mob with this trait will gradually require more and more hits with the ice staff to freeze, up to a maximum of 2.5 \* its natural *resistance*. This extra resistance rises at a rate of 25% of the natural resistance each time the mob is frozen. Additionally, the time it takes for the mob to thaw and unfreeze is also gradually reduced, down to a minimum of just 0.1 second. The extra resistance gained slowly decays.

## Ice Staff Trap[]

 https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/dont-starve-game/images/9/95/Ice staf.png/revision/latest?cb=20130515171010 https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/dont-starve-game/images/9/95/Ice staf.png/revision/latest?cb=20130515171010 



 

Occasionally, an Ice Staff can be found surrounded by sleeping Blue Hounds: this is a [Set Piece](/wiki/Set_Piece "Set Piece") trap. When the Staff is taken, the player will lose 33 [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity"), it will rain, and all the Hounds will wake up and attack - otherwise, the player can walk among them safely. The Blue Hounds can be killed one by one while asleep before picking up the staff, and the others will not wake up. The Ice Staff will have a random amount of durability.
