**Forget-Me-Lots** are a [Vegetable](/wiki/Vegetables "Vegetables") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")* and introduced in *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")*.

They can be harvested from a [Forget-Me-Lots Plant](/wiki/Weeds#Forget-Me-Lots "Weeds"). They are used to prepare [Soothing Tea](/wiki/Soothing_Tea "Soothing Tea"). They spoil very quickly, but can be stored in an [Ice Box](/wiki/Ice_Box "Ice Box"), [Insulated Pack](/wiki/Insulated_Pack "Insulated Pack"), or [Bundling Wrap](/wiki/Bundling_Wrap "Bundling Wrap"). They cannot be stored in a [Salt Box](/wiki/Salt_Box "Salt Box").
