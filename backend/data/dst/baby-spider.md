**Baby Spiders** are allies added to *[The Forge](/wiki/The_Forge "The Forge")* in *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*.
The Little Spiders appears only when [Webber](/wiki/Webber "Webber") is in the game. They are greatly reduced in size from normal spiders.

They can't be attacked and are periodically damaging enemies. If [Webber](/wiki/Webber "Webber") dies, the spiders go to sleep. They always appear in a group of 3.
