**Crocodogs** are [Monsters](/wiki/Monster "Monster") exclusive to the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC. They replace [Hounds](/wiki/Hound "Hound") in *Shipwrecked* worlds, and attack periodically the same way Hounds do. Crocodogs are not restricted to land or water and can traverse freely between them.

Crocodogs have the same stats as regular Hounds. However, unlike Hounds, Crocodogs can actually be stun-locked; both on land and in the water.

[Meats](/wiki/Meats "Meats") dropped into any part of the [Ocean](/wiki/Ocean "Ocean") have a chance to spawn either one Crocodog or 2-4 [Sea Hounds](/wiki/Sea_Hound "Sea Hound") (60% chance of a Crocodog and 40% chance of Sea Hounds). The meat does not have to be dropped by the player themselves. The probability of an attack is based on the hunger value of the item dropped into the sea, and can be calculated by dividing the hunger value of a single item in the stack by 200. This means that the size of the stack does not increase the odds of an attack, but a large number of separate stacks of meat (such as those dropped by puncturing a bloated [Whale](/wiki/Whale "Whale") carcass) can trigger a massive feeding frenzy. [Monster Food](/wiki/Monster_Food "Monster Food"), [Roe](/wiki/Roe "Roe") and [Shark Fins](/wiki/Shark_Fin "Shark Fin") will not trigger an attack.

Regular Crocodogs may spawn during all [Seasons](/wiki/Seasons "Seasons") and drop 1 [Monster Meat](/wiki/Monster_Meat "Monster Meat") when killed, with a 1 in 8 chance to drop up to 2 [Hound's Teeth](/wiki/Hound%27s_Tooth "Hound's Tooth").

**Yellow Crocodogs** [poison](/wiki/Poison "Poison") the player and other creatures when attacking them. They are immune to poison themselves. When killed, they will always drop 1 Monster Meat and 1 Hound's Tooth, with an additional chance for a [Venom Gland](/wiki/Venom_Gland "Venom Gland").

Yellow Crocodogs spawn during [Dry Season](/wiki/Seasons/Dry "Seasons/Dry"). Although they share the same stats with [Red Hounds](/wiki/Hound#Red_Hound "Hound"), they will not burst into flames or drop [Red Gems](/wiki/Red_Gem "Red Gem") when killed.

**Blue Crocodogs** can spawn [Puddles](/wiki/Flooding "Flooding") on land by shaking the water off themselves. When killed, they always drop 1 Monster Meat and 2 Hound's Teeth, with an additional chance to drop 1 [Seaweed](/wiki/Seaweed "Seaweed"), and will rapidly produce puddles with each hit within an area.

Blue Crocodogs spawn during [Monsoon Season](/wiki/Seasons/Monsoon "Seasons/Monsoon") and [Hurricane Season](/wiki/Hurricane_Season "Hurricane Season"). Although they share the same stats with [Blue Hounds](/wiki/Hound#Blue_Hound "Hound"), they will not freeze the player and nearby creatures or drop [Blue Gems](/wiki/Blue_Gem "Blue Gem") upon death.

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* Crocodogs were added to the *Shipwrecked* DLC more than a year after its official completion in the *[Home Sea Home](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked#November_21st,_2017_-_Home_Sea_Home_Update "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* update, after being tested in a beta branch for several months.
* Crocodogs have replaced both Sea Hounds and Hounds in the periodic attacks in *Shipwrecked* DLC in the *[Home Sea Home](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked#November_21st,_2017_-_Home_Sea_Home_Update "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* update. However, both can still be encountered.
* The Crocodog's name is a portmanteau of the words "crocodile" and "dog".
