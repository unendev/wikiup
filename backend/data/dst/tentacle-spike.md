The **Tentacle Spike** is a [Melee Weapon](/wiki/Weapon#Melee_Weapons "Weapon"). It is a purple tentacle with spikes protruding from one end, similar to a spiked mace. It has a 50% chance to drop from [Tentacles](/wiki/Tentacle "Tentacle"), which are found in [Marshes](/wiki/Marsh "Marsh") and [Caves](/wiki/Caves "Caves"), or [Big Tentacles](/wiki/Big_Tentacle "Big Tentacle"), which are found only in Caves. If a Big Tentacle has already dropped one, no other Big Tentacles can drop them for the rest of the [Day](/wiki/Day "Day").

The Tentacle Spike does 51 damage per hit for players with the normal damage multiplier. It is a highly useful melee weapon for most instances, having the fourth highest damage of all weapons, being relatively easy to obtain, and having no drawbacks.

## Gift Icon Downloadable Content[]

In the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Shipwrecked "Shipwrecked")* DLC, the [Quacken](/wiki/Quacken "Quacken") boss spawns a variant of tentacles. Quacken Tentacles have 5% chance to drop a Tentacle Spike when killed.

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* The Tentacle Spike used to have a value of 1-10 [Research Points](/wiki/Research_Points_List "Research Points List") depending on its durability.
* Before the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Hamlet "Hamlet")* DLC, the Tentacle Spike used to sink, and the only way to retrieve it was by using a [Trawl Net](/wiki/Trawl_Net "Trawl Net").
