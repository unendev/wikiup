## Placeholder Trivia[]

* Willow's quote for the Electrical Doodad ("Electrical doo-dah, doo-dah.") is a reference to the widely-familiar song, "[Camptown Races](http://youtu.be/-5b41ZWHFTI)".
* Her quote for a burnt Prestihatitator ("Double, double toil and... oh. It's burned.") is a reference to the "[Song of the Witches](http://www.poetryfoundation.org/poem/171942)" poem in Shakespeare's play, "Macbeth".
* Willow's quote for the Mosquito Sack ("Blood must bubble in currents muggy and thick.") is a reference to a quote from the novel *[Berlin Alexanderplatz](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Berlin_Alexanderplatz "wikipedia:Berlin Alexanderplatz")*, written by Alfred Döblin.
* Her quote for a Thulecite Wall ("And they'll huff and they'll puff!") is a reference to the popular children's story "The Three Little Pigs".
* Her quote for the [Festive Baubles](/wiki/Winter%27s_Feast "Winter's Feast") ("Bibble-ty bauble-ty boo!") may be a reference to the Disney movie *[Cinderella](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cinderella_(1950_film) "wikipedia:Cinderella (1950 film)")*, where a similar phrase is used to activate the Fairy Godmother's wand.
* Her quote for a [Dragoon](/wiki/Dragoon "Dragoon") ("If only I could figure out how to train one.") may be a reference to the movie *[How to Train Your Dragon](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/How_to_Train_Your_Dragon_(film) "wikipedia:How to Train Your Dragon (film)")*.
* Her beta examination quote for characters who revived her ("Who do ghosts call? %s!") may be a reference to the theme song for the movie *[Ghostbusters](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ghostbusters "wikipedia:Ghostbusters")*.
* Her quote for the old Gas Mask ("I like not smelling smelly smells.") may be a reference to a famous Mr. Krabs quote from the cartoon *[Spongebob Squarepants](https://spongebob.fandom.com/wiki/Eugene_H._Krabs "w:c:spongebob:Eugene H. Krabs")*.
* Her quote for the Knight head Suspicious Marble ("Great, now we've irritated some marble crime boss!") may be a reference to the infamous horse head scene from *[The Godfather](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Godfather "wikipedia:The Godfather")*.
* Her quote for the Sail Stick ("I'm the queen of this boooooat!") is a reference to the scene in *[Titanic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Titanic_(1997_film) "wikipedia:Titanic (1997 film)")* in which Jack Dawson (played by Leonardo DiCaprio) stands at the bow of a ship and exclaims "I'm the king of the world!"
* Her quote for the Bee Queen Crown ("That bee's head looks kinda tasty, doesn't it?") may refer to the fact it looks like two croissants glued together.
* Her quote for Barnacle Pita ("Pita pita, barnacle eater.") references the rhyme "[Peter Peter Pumpkin Eater](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Peter_Peter_Pumpkin_Eater "wikipedia:Peter Peter Pumpkin Eater")".
