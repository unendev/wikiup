**Parrot Pirates** are [Birds](/wiki/Bird "Bird") introduced in the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC. They are a version of [Parrots](/wiki/Parrot "Parrot") wearing pirate hats, similar to [Wolly the Parrot](/wiki/Sunken_Boat "Sunken Boat") from the intro when a new world is started.

They appear at random times in [Jungle](/wiki/Jungle "Jungle") biomes on the screen instead of a normal Parrot and instead of leaving [Seeds](/wiki/Seeds "Seeds") behind like normal Birds do, they instead leave behind 1 [Dubloon](/wiki/Dubloons "Dubloons") when they fly away. When around the player, they will taunt the player. Parrot Pirates have a [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity") aura, restoring 25 sanity per minute. Like other [Birds](/wiki/Bird "Bird"), Parrot Pirates are attracted to Seeds, and they can be captured using a [Bird Trap](/wiki/Bird_Trap "Bird Trap") or low damage ranged attacks.

When picked up, they cannot be murdered for a [Morsel](/wiki/Morsel "Morsel") or a [Feather](/wiki/Feather "Feather"), however they can be cooked over fire to obtain a cooked Morsel. They can be imprisoned in a [Bird Cage](/wiki/Bird_Cage "Bird Cage"), and like normal Birds, they produce [Eggs](/wiki/Eggs "Eggs") for giving them [Meats](/wiki/Meats "Meats"), and Seeds for giving them [Fruits](/wiki/Fruits "Fruits") or [Vegetables](/wiki/Vegetables "Vegetables"). While being carried in the inventory, they will comment on player actions, much like [Lucy the Axe](/wiki/Lucy_the_Axe "Lucy the Axe") does. They will not speak when they are in a Bird Cage, however.

They take 1 blast from the [Ice Staff](/wiki/Ice_Staff "Ice Staff") to be frozen and 1 [Sleep Dart](/wiki/Sleep_Dart "Sleep Dart") to be put to sleep.

## Prototype Tips[]

* Like other birds, Parrot Pirates go to sleep when in complete darkness. Dropping a couple of Parrot Pirates together in the [Caves](/wiki/Caves "Caves") or [Ruins](/wiki/Ruins "Ruins") allows for an easy source of sanity.
  + However, it should be noted that [Earthquakes](/wiki/Earthquake "Earthquake") will eventually kill Parrot Pirates, as will any [Depths Worms](/wiki/Depths_Worm "Depths Worm") or [Batilisks](/wiki/Batilisk "Batilisk") that target them. It is recommended to only deploy them when sanity is low, and recapture them again by placing a bird trap directly under them, or walk away when the character signals an earthquake, so that the birds are not loaded.
  + Furthermore, when the player leaves the Caves or Ruins, reloading the world will have the birds load momentarily awake, causing them to fly up and disappear.

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* "Blackbeard" is a reference to the [infamous pirate](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Blackbeard "wikipedia:Blackbeard") in history.
* "Crackers" may be a reference to the phrase uttered by talking parrots, 'Polly wanna a cracker'.
* "Dan Van 3000" seems to be a reference to the Canadian music band [Bran Van 3000](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bran_Van_3000 "wikipedia:Bran Van 3000").
* "Donny Jepp" is a reference to [Johnny Depp](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Johnny_Depp "wikipedia:Johnny Depp"), as an allusion to his character Jack Sparrow from the Pirates of The Carribeans films.
* "Jean Claud Van Dan" is most likely a reference to the Belgian actor [Jean-Claude Van Damme](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jean-Claude_Van_Damme "wikipedia:Jean-Claude Van Damme").
* The name "Pollygon" is a portmanteau of the stereotypical parrot name Polly and polygon.
* "Scurvy" is a [disease](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scurvy "wikipedia:Scurvy") caused by a lack of vitamin C. It was a common ailment among those who made long distance sea travel.
* "Reginald VelJohnson" is a reference to the [actor](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Reginald_VelJohnson "wikipedia:Reginald VelJohnson") of the same name.
* The name "Van Dader" is a possible reference to [Darth Vader](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Darth_Vader "wikipedia:Darth Vader").
* The name "Lightoller" is referencing a survivor of the Titanic, [Charles Lightoller](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Charles_Lightoller "wikipedia:Charles Lightoller").
* "Boxhall" also references a [survivor](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Joseph_Boxhall "wikipedia:Joseph Boxhall") of the Titanic.
* The name "Iago" is a reference to the Shakespearean play, *[Othello](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Othello "wikipedia:Othello")*, or to the character Iago, a parrot in the animated movie *[Aladdin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aladdin_(franchise) "wikipedia:Aladdin (franchise)")*.
* The name "Long John" is a reference to [Long John Silver](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Long_John_Silver "wikipedia:Long John Silver"), the main villain of the adventure novel *[Treasure Island](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Treasure_Island "wikipedia:Treasure Island")*.
