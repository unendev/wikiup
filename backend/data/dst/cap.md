**“**Nothing safer than eating a strange mushroom off the ground.**”**

–[Wheeler](/wiki/Wheeler "Wheeler")

**Caps** are [items](/wiki/Items "Items") found in *[Don't Starve](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve "Don't Starve")* and *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*. They can be harvested from [Mushrooms](/wiki/Mushrooms "Mushrooms") or by cutting [Mushtree](/wiki/Mushtree "Mushtree"). There are three types of caps, plus another one in *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*:
