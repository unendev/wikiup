The **Resting Horror** is a passive [Mob](/wiki/Mob "Mob") and [Shadow Creature](/wiki/Shadow_Creature "Shadow Creature") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced within the [From Beyond](/wiki/From_Beyond "From Beyond") updates. It can be found in the [Ruins](/wiki/Ruins "Ruins") sitting on one of the [Relic Chairs](/wiki/Relic_Chair "Relic Chair") and is only visible, when the [Characters](/wiki/Character "Character") [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity") is low. The Resting Horror doesn't react to the character and is in a semi-dormant state. It has only 10 [Health](/wiki/Health "Health"), so it dies from any attack, leaving behind a rare [Blueprint](/wiki/Blueprint "Blueprint") for the [Replica Relic Chair](/wiki/Replica_Relic_Chair "Replica Relic Chair") and 1 [Nightmare Fuel](/wiki/Nightmare_Fuel "Nightmare Fuel"). If a [Shadow Rift](/wiki/Shadow_Rift "Shadow Rift") is open, it will drop 1 [Pure Horror](/wiki/Pure_Horror "Pure Horror") instead.

Like other [Shadow Creatures](/wiki/Shadow_Creatures "Shadow Creatures"), it can only be attacked while the character is insane. Care should be taken to attack and not accidentally break the chair, which causes it to despawn dropping nothing.

If the character is insane standing close to the Resting Horror drains 40 Sanity per minute. There is no sanity drain while the character is sane.

The Resting Horror has [Planar Entity Protection](/wiki/Planar_Entity_Protection "Planar Entity Protection"), so after a Shadow Rift is open general [Damage](/wiki/Damage "Damage") hits are less effective against it. In contrast, both the planar damage hit and others damage source can effectively damage it. But because it has only 10 health, two or three hits will kill it. It respawns after 2 days on a random Relic Chair.

## Trivia[]

* The Resting Horror was introduced in the *[Terrors Below](/wiki/Terrors_Below "Terrors Below")* update.
* Originally the Resting Horror required a Shadow Rift to be opened to show up. This was removed some months later in the *[Host of Horrors](/wiki/Host_of_Horrors "Host of Horrors")* update to give the players an easier way to obtain the Blueprint for the Replica Relic Chair, as it is needed to get the Blueprint for the [Sawhorse](/wiki/Sawhorse "Sawhorse") to prototype the new furniture and table decorations.
