**Rope** is a [Refined](/wiki/Refined "Refined") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") crafted with 3 [Cut Grass](/wiki/Cut_Grass "Cut Grass"). A [Science Machine](/wiki/Science_Machine "Science Machine") is required to unlock the recipe. Ropes can be used to craft various [Tools](/wiki/Tools "Tools"), [Structures](/wiki/Structures "Structures"), [Survival items](/wiki/Survival "Survival"), and [Weapons](/wiki/Weapons "Weapons"). It can also be used to [refuel](/wiki/Fire#Fuel "Fire") a [Fire Pit](/wiki/Fire_Pit "Fire Pit") by 90 seconds or a [Campfire](/wiki/Campfire "Campfire") by 45 seconds.

Ropes can sometimes be found around Fallen Survivors [Set Pieces](/wiki/Set_Piece "Set Piece").

## Downloadable Content[]

In the *[Reign of Giants](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Reign_of_Giants "Don't Starve: Reign of Giants")* DLC and *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, Ropes can be found in [Tumbleweeds](/wiki/Tumbleweed "Tumbleweed").

In the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC, Rope can also be acquired by [Hammering](/wiki/Hammer "Hammer") [Crates](/wiki/Crate "Crate").

In the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC, Rope can also be acquired by [Hammering](/wiki/Hammer "Hammer") [A Smashing Pot](/wiki/A_Smashing_Pot "A Smashing Pot"). Both kinds of [Hanging Vines](/wiki/Hanging_Vine "Hanging Vine") drop one, too.
