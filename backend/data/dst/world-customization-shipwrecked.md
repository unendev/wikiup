*Exclusive to:* Shipwrecked

The *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked") DLC* replaces most of the options of the original game, as the *Shipwrecked* world is separated from the base game world.
