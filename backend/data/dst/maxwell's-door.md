**Maxwell's Door** is a portal that transfers a player from [Survival Mode](/wiki/Sandbox_Mode "Sandbox Mode") to [Adventure Mode](/wiki/Adventure_Mode "Adventure Mode"). It's surrounded by [Evergreens](/wiki/Evergreen "Evergreen"), [Fireflies](/wiki/Fireflies "Fireflies"), and [Evil Flowers](/wiki/Evil_Flower "Evil Flower"). When the player goes near it, the machine will become active, appearing as [Maxwell](/wiki/Maxwell_(NPC) "Maxwell (NPC)")'s face. It resembles the machine which brought [Wilson](/wiki/Wilson "Wilson") to [Maxwell's dimension](/wiki/The_Constant "The Constant") in the first place, as seen in the animated short *[Forbidden Knowledge](/wiki/Animated_Shorts_and_Trailers#Forbidden_Knowledge "Animated Shorts and Trailers")*. It can be found with the help of a [Divining Rod](/wiki/Divining_Rod "Divining Rod").

 https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/dont-starve-game/images/1/13/Maxwells door message.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20150519121133 https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/dont-starve-game/images/1/13/Maxwells door message.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20150519121133 

Warning message after the activation of Maxwell's Door.

 

Upon activating it, a warning message will appear informing the player about Adventure Mode and asking them if they want to proceed or not. If they do, the player will be grabbed by a [Shadow Hand](/wiki/Shadow_Hand "Shadow Hand") and then appear in the first chapter of Adventure Mode with no inventory (except [special items](/wiki/Category:Character-Specific "Category:Character-Specific") such as [Lucy](/wiki/Lucy "Lucy"), [Willow's Lighter](/wiki/Willow%27s_Lighter "Willow's Lighter"), or [Abigail's Flower](/wiki/Abigail%27s_Flower "Abigail's Flower")) or recipes, so players are required to begin anew in Adventure Mode. [Health](/wiki/Health "Health"), [Hunger](/wiki/Hunger "Hunger"), and [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity") levels will be restored, however. If the player dies in Adventure Mode, they will wake up in front of the Maxwell's Door in Sandbox Mode with their original [Inventory](/wiki/Inventory "Inventory"), Health, Hunger, and Sanity level as if they never activated the Door.

Maxwell's Door does not spawn in *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* nor *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* worlds. It's also absent in *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*.

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* Maxwell's Door was added to the game with the *[Doorway to Adventure](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve/Version_History#Doorway_to_Adventure "Don't Starve/Version History")* update.
* Getting close to the Door, a faint version of the "[Ragtime](/wiki/Soundtrack "Soundtrack")" song can be heard (referring to the epilogue of Adventure Mode, with the [Gramaphone](/wiki/Gramaphone "Gramaphone")).
