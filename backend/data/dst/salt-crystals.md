**Salt Crystals** are an [item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")*. They are dropped by destroyed or mined [Salt Formations](/wiki/Salt_Formation "Salt Formation") found in the [Ocean](/wiki/Ocean "Ocean").

## Usage[]

Salt Crystals are required to craft the [Salt Box](/wiki/Salt_Box "Salt Box"), the [Gourmet Salt Lick](/wiki/Gourmet_Salt_Lick "Gourmet Salt Lick"), and [Collected Dust](/wiki/Collected_Dust "Collected Dust"). Three Salt Crystals can also be ground into two [Seasoning Salt](/wiki/Seasoning_Salt "Seasoning Salt") spices by [Warly](/wiki/Warly "Warly") using the [Portable Grinding Mill](/wiki/Portable_Grinding_Mill "Portable Grinding Mill").

Salt Crystals can be used to add 50% freshness to perishable [Foods](/wiki/Food "Food") (up to a maximum of 100% freshness). If used on a stack of Food, the effect is divided by the size of the stack such that 5 foods of 45% freshness would become 55% freshness (50 / 5 = 10 freshness each). Salt Crystals can only be used on food items that can be put in a [Salt Box](/wiki/Salt_Box "Salt Box").

Salt Crystals can also be used to restore the durability of a Gourmet Salt Lick by 80.

Salt Crystals were initially introduced in *[The Gorge](/wiki/The_Gorge "The Gorge")* [event](/wiki/Category:Events "Category:Events"). In this event, in order to receive Salt Crystals, one must install a [Salt Rack](/wiki/Salt_Rack "Salt Rack") at a [Salt Pond](/wiki/Salt_Pond "Salt Pond") and wait two minutes until it is ready to be harvested. Once the time is up, Salt Crystals will appear out of the pond and can then be harvested, resulting in a Salt Crystal which can be ground up with the [Mealing Stone](/wiki/Mealing_Stone "Mealing Stone"), granting the player three Salt.

**Salt** is very useful as it can be used to extend the freshness of cooked meals while also increasing the value of the food item that is given to the [Gnaw](/wiki/The_Gnaw "The Gnaw"). In addition, three Salt can be traded with the [Swamp Pig Elder](/wiki/Swamp_Pig_Elder "Swamp Pig Elder") for a [Key](/wiki/Key "Key").
