A **Stinger** is an [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") commonly dropped by a [Bee](/wiki/Bee "Bee") or [Killer Bee](/wiki/Killer_Bee "Killer Bee") when killed. It is mainly an ingredient in [Sleep Darts](/wiki/Sleep_Dart "Sleep Dart").

Stingers can be obtained by either killing Bees or Killer Bees with [Weapons](/wiki/Weapons "Weapons"), or with the help of other [Mobs](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs"), or by catching Bees or Killer Bees with a [Bug Net](/wiki/Bug_Net "Bug Net") then murdering them in the inventory. If the player or any mob attacks a Bee (including catching them with the Bug Net) remaining members of the same [Bee Hive](/wiki/Bee_Hive "Bee Hive") (or [Bee Box](/wiki/Bee_Box "Bee Box")) will emerge as Killer Bees and all members of the hive in the vicinity will target the attacker.

As Stingers are a very common drop from Bees and Killer Bees, they can easily become clutter in vanilla *[Don't Starve](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve "Don't Starve")* and *[Reign of Giants](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Reign_of_Giants "Don't Starve: Reign of Giants"),* especially given that it is almost only used for Sleep Darts, with [Jet Feathers](/wiki/Feather "Feather") being tricky to obtain from [Crows](/wiki/Birds "Birds") in the early game and [Cut Reeds](/wiki/Cut_Reeds "Cut Reeds") are dangerous to obtain due to [Tentacles](/wiki/Tentacle "Tentacle") lurking in the [Marsh](/wiki/Marsh "Marsh"). **Stingers** have further uses in *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")* and the DLCs *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Shipwrecked "Shipwrecked")* & *[Hamlet](/wiki/Hamlet "Hamlet")* (see below).

## Downloadable Content[]

In the *[Reign of Giants](/wiki/Reign_of_Giants "Reign of Giants")* DLC, Stingers have a chance to be obtained by harvesting [Tumbleweeds](/wiki/Tumbleweed "Tumbleweed").

In the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Shipwrecked "Shipwrecked")* DLC, Stingers can be used to craft [Boat Repair Kits](/wiki/Boat_Repair_Kit "Boat Repair Kit").

In the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Hamlet "Hamlet")* DLC, Stingers have a chance to be dropped by [Scorpions](/wiki/Scorpion "Scorpion"). They can be used by [Wormwood](/wiki/Wormwood "Wormwood") to craft [Bramble Traps](/wiki/Bramble_Trap "Bramble Trap").

## Don't Starve Together Don't Starve Together[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, Stingers can be used to craft [Booster Shots](/wiki/Booster_Shot "Booster Shot"), [Boat Patches](/wiki/Boat_Patch "Boat Patch"), and [Dock Kits](/wiki/Dock_Kit "Dock Kit").
