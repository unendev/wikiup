The **Flytrap Stalk** is a [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC. It is dropped only by fully grown [Snaptooth Flytraps](/wiki/Snaptooth "Snaptooth") and when consumed, restores 12.5 [Hunger](/wiki/Hunger "Hunger") and depletes the player's [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity") by 10. Flytrap Stalks cannot be cooked or used in the [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot"), but can be dried on a [Drying Rack](/wiki/Drying_Rack "Drying Rack") to turn them into [Stalking Sticks](/wiki/Stalking_Stick "Stalking Stick") (the *Hamlet* equivalent of the [Walking Cane](/wiki/Walking_Cane "Walking Cane")), or used to craft the [Bug B'Gone](/wiki/Bug_B%27Gone "Bug B'Gone") repellent and the [Root Trunk](/wiki/Root_Trunk "Root Trunk").

It is the only [Meat](/wiki/Meat "Meat") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") that can be set on fire.
