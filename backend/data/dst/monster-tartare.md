## Don't Starve Together icon Don't Starve Together[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, Monster Tartare is made with 2 [Monster Meats](/wiki/Monster_Meat "Monster Meat") and 2 edible fillers. It restores 62.5 Hunger like [Meatballs](/wiki/Meatballs "Meatballs"), but takes away -20 Sanity and -20 Health like [Monster Lasagna](/wiki/Monster_Lasagna "Monster Lasagna") ([Webber](/wiki/Webber "Webber") suffers no penalty and [Wormwood](/wiki/Wormwood "Wormwood") only the sanity). It takes 10 seconds to cook.

Feeding this dish to [Woby](/wiki/Woby "Woby") instantly transforms her into big Woby, potentially saving one monster meat.

### Prerequisites[]

* Requires: 2×Monster Meat

## Shipwrecked icon Shipwrecked[]

In *[Don't Starve: Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")*, Monster Tartare is made with any 2 [Monster Foods](/wiki/Monster_Food "Monster Food"), 1 [Vegetables](/wiki/Vegetables "Vegetables"), and 1 [Eggs](/wiki/Eggs "Eggs"). It restores 37.5 Hunger 10 Sanity and 3 Health and takes 40 seconds to cook.

### Prerequisites[]

* Requires: Monster Foods
* Requires: Eggs(single-player only)
* Requires: Vegetables(single-player only)
