**Fossil Fragments** are *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")* exclusive items, introduced in *[A New Reign](/wiki/A_New_Reign "A New Reign")*. They are dropped by [Stalagmites](/wiki/Stalagmite "Stalagmite") at a 10% rate and by [Spilagmites](/wiki/Spilagmite "Spilagmite") at a 100% rate. One of their uses is to repair an odd skeleton. The other use is for blocking mobs, similar to walls except fossils are unbreakable by them.

In order to build the **Odd Skeleton**, players have to place a Fossil Fragment on the ground and then repair it using additional Fossil Fragments in a process similar to the repairing of [Walls](/wiki/Wall "Wall"). The built pile can be [Hammered](/wiki/Hammer "Hammer") down to yield every single Fossil Fragments used on it, regardless of its size.

There are three Odd Skeleton designs: a complete correct form, and two incorrect forms. It takes eight Fossil Fragments to completely assemble an Odd Skeleton, but the form (Correct, Wrong 1 or Wrong 2) will already be decided when the player places the fifth Fossil Fragments on the Odd Skeleton, at which point the player may want to Hammer it down to start building another one right away if the form is incorrect. It has a 60% chance to become the correct form, and a 20% chance to become one of the two wrong variations each. If built near an active [Ancient Gateway](/wiki/Ancient_Gateway "Ancient Gateway") and a [Shadow Atrium](/wiki/Shadow_Atrium "Shadow Atrium"), the correct form is guaranteed. Only the correct Odd Skeleton form can be resurrected into the [Reanimated Skeleton](/wiki/Reanimated_Skeleton "Reanimated Skeleton") or [Ancient Fuelweaver](/wiki/Ancient_Fuelweaver "Ancient Fuelweaver") by using a Shadow Atrium.

## Tips[]

* Fossil Fragments are not renewable and limited by number after all Stalagmites and Spilagmites have been mined. The only renewable source as of now[[1]](#cite_note-1) is by killing the [Great Depths Worm](/wiki/Great_Depths_Worm "Great Depths Worm") with a 0.85% chance.
