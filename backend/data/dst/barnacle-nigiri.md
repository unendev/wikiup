**Barnacle Nigiri** is a [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") recipe exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")* introduced in *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")*. It is made of 1 [Barnacles](/wiki/Barnacles "Barnacles"), 1 [Kelp Fronds](/wiki/Kelp_Fronds "Kelp Fronds") and 1 [Eggs](/wiki/Eggs "Eggs"). It takes 10 seconds to cook and 10 [Days](/wiki/Day-Night_Cycle "Day-Night Cycle") to [Spoil](/wiki/Spoil "Spoil").

When consumed, it restores 40 Health, 37.5 Hunger and 15 Sanity.

Only Raw and Cooked Kelp Fronds are accepted. Using [Dried Kelp Fronds](/wiki/Dried_Kelp_Fronds "Dried Kelp Fronds") will result in [Barnacle Pita](/wiki/Barnacle_Pita "Barnacle Pita") instead.

## Trivia[]

* Barnacle Nigiri was introduced in the *[Troubled Waters](/wiki/Return_of_Them#Troubled_Waters "Return of Them")* update.
* Barnacle Nigiri appears to be Tamagoyaki (aka Tamago) a rolled sweet egg omelette often put on top of sushi or in bento, as referenced by the necessity of egg in the recipe, with the barnacle replacing the sushi rice.
