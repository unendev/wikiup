**Coffee** is a [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC. It can be cooked in the [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") by combining 3 [Roasted Coffee Beans](/wiki/Roasted_Coffee_Beans "Roasted Coffee Beans") and 1 [Dairy](/wiki/Dairy "Dairy"), [Sweetener](/wiki/Sweetener "Sweetener"), or a fourth Roasted Coffee Bean. It takes 10 seconds to cook and 10 [Days](/wiki/Day-Night_Cycle "Day-Night Cycle") to [spoil](/wiki/Spoil "Spoil").

When consumed, it restores 3 Healtha and 9.375 Hunger at a cost of -5 Sanity.

Coffee provides a movement [speed](/wiki/Player_Speed "Player Speed") increase of 5 for half a [Day](/wiki/Day "Day") (4 real minutes). For [characters](/wiki/Characters "Characters") with no other speed buffs the base speed is 6, therefore this increment translates to the character moving about 83% faster. The speed buff also applies while on a [Boat](/wiki/Boats "Boats") or while using a [Walking Cane](/wiki/Walking_Cane "Walking Cane") or a [Sleek Hat](/wiki/Sleek_Hat "Sleek Hat"), stacking their effects while equipped.

## Trivia[]

* Coffee was introduced in the *[Wilbur Vs. the Volcano](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked#February_4th,_2016_-_Wilbur_Vs._The_Volcano_Update "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* update.
* Mobs that consume Coffee (or Roasted Coffee Beans) will also gain the extra movement speed.
* The loss of [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity") when drinking Coffee may be a reference to the collateral effect of tachycardia and general sense of anxiety when people get addicted to it.
