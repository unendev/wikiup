​**Mysterious Plant** is a [Plant](/wiki/Plant "Plant") that can be found in the [Cave](/wiki/Cave "Cave"), commonly in the [Wilds](/wiki/Wilds "Wilds") biome.

Mysterious Plant emits light constantly on a small radius. They can be harvested for 1 [Lesser Glow Berry](/wiki/Lesser_Glow_Berry "Lesser Glow Berry") and regrows in 4 [Days](/wiki/Day "Day").

Idle [Depths Worm](/wiki/Depths_Worm "Depths Worm") transform themself to look alike Mysterious Plant and lure players. They wake up and attack any player who tries to harvest them or stays nearby for too long.

## Prototype Tips[]

* Depths Worm's Mysterous Plant will always be wet, thus, it is possible to distinguish between the two.
  + In the case of rain, the Mysterious Plant is capable of being set on fire, the Depths Worm's however is not.
