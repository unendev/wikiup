**Rocky Turf** is one of the [Turf](/wiki/Turf "Turf") [items](/wiki/Items "Items"), acquired by digging [Rockyland](/wiki/Rockyland "Rockyland") tiles with a [Pitchfork](/wiki/Pitchfork "Pitchfork"). Like other Turfs, it can be placed on any bare ground tile or it can be used as [fuel](/wiki/Fire#Fuel "Fire"). It can also be used to craft [Cobblestones](/wiki/Cobblestones "Cobblestones"), which are placeable [Roads](/wiki/Roads "Roads").

[Berry Bushes](/wiki/Berry_Bush "Berry Bush"), [Saplings](/wiki/Sapling "Sapling"), [Grass Tufts](/wiki/Grass_Tuft "Grass Tuft"), [Spiky Bushes](/wiki/Spiky_Bush "Spiky Bush"), [Pine Cones](/wiki/Pine_Cones "Pine Cones"), and [Birchnuts](/wiki/Birchnut "Birchnut") cannot be planted on Rocky Turf. [Eyeplants](/wiki/Lureplant "Lureplant") cannot spawn on this Turf, so it is great for planting [Lureplant](/wiki/Lureplant "Lureplant") farms while not spending any more resources than those used to craft the Pitchfork.

When placed on the [Surface World](/wiki/Surface_World "Surface World") and the area is large enough, [Crows](/wiki/Crow "Crow") are the only Birds that will spawn and land on these turfs.

In *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")*, floods can start on this turf.

## Don't Starve Together icon Don't Starve Together[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, Rocky Turf can be crafted and prototyped with 1 [Rocks](/wiki/Rocks "Rocks") and 1 [Flint](/wiki/Flint "Flint") at [Terra Firma Tamper](/wiki/Terra_Firma_Tamper "Terra Firma Tamper") in the [Decorations Filter](/wiki/Decorations_Filter "Decorations Filter").
