## Placeholder Trivia[]

* Woodie's examination quote for [Monster Lasagna](/wiki/Monster_Lasagna "Monster Lasagna") is a reference to the comic strip *Garfield*, where the main character is a feline whose favorite food is Lasagna.
* Woodie's quote for Lucy is a reference to the famous sitcom, *[I Love Lucy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/I_Love_Lucy "wikipedia:I Love Lucy")*.
* When examining a Spider Den, Woodie will say, "Holy Mackinaw! Look at that thing!" This is a reference to the catchphrase of Canadian sportscaster [Joe Bowen](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Joe_Bowen "wikipedia:Joe Bowen").
* Woodie's examination quote for Rot is a reference to [John Diefenbaker](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_Diefenbaker "wikipedia:John Diefenbaker"), Canada's 13th Prime Minister.
* Woodie's examination quote for [Bone Shards](/wiki/Bone_Shards "Bone Shards") is a reference to the fairy tale "Jack and the Beanstalk", in which the giant says that he will "Grind [the man's] bones to make my bread."
* Woodie's examination quote for the burnt Science Machine in RoG ("How did it get burned?!") may be a reference to *The Wickerman*, where when examining a burnt doll, the main protagonist yells "HOW'D IT GET BURNED?!"
* Woodie's examination quote for pengulls refers to a song recorded by Rush and [Bob and Doug McKenzie](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bob_and_Doug_McKenzie "wikipedia:Bob and Doug McKenzie"). The lyrics go "Take off/To the Great White North/Take off/It's a beauty way to go". The characters Bob and Doug McKenzie also used catchphrases like "hoser" (or "hosehead") and made frequent references to backbacon and toques, which informs Woodie's quotes.
* His quote for Wendy as a murderer ("Hereeee's Woodie!") is a reference to an iconic quote from *[The Shining](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Shining_(film) "wikipedia:The Shining (film)")* ("Hereeee's Johnny!"), including the fact that Jack also wields an axe.
* Woodie's examination quote for Mushroom Lights is a reference to the song "[Colors of the Wind](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Colors_of_the_Wind "wikipedia:Colors of the Wind")" from the film *[Pocahontas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pocahontas_(1995_film) "wikipedia:Pocahontas (1995 film)")*.
* In his quotes for Rocks and a Magma Pile in *Shipwrecked*, Woodie mentions building an [inukshuk](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Inuksuk "wikipedia:Inuksuk"), which are figures made of stone by the peoples of the Arctic.
* Woodie's examination quote for the [One-man Band](/wiki/One-man_Band "One-man Band") is a reference to the line in the [Canadian National Anthem](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/O_Canada "wikipedia:O Canada"), ("O Canada, we stand on guard for thee!").
