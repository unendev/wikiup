## Placeholder Trivia[]

* Her quote for a Cooked Fish ("Fish are friends, not food!") may be a reference to a line said by the character Bruce from *[Finding Nemo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Finding_Nemo "wikipedia:Finding Nemo")*.
* Wurt's quotes for a Varg ("What big teeth he have!") and a Koalefant ("What big ears you have!") are references to *[Little Red Riding Hood](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Little_Red_Riding_Hood "wikipedia:Little Red Riding Hood")*.
* Her quote for a Rabbit ("Be vewy vewy quiet.") is a reference to a line said by the character Elmer Fudd from [Warner's](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Warner_Bros. "wikipedia:Warner Bros.") *[Looney Tunes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Looney_Tunes "wikipedia:Looney Tunes")/[Merrie Melodies](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Merrie_Melodies "wikipedia:Merrie Melodies")*.
* Her quote for a Saladmander ("It not easy being green.") is a reference to the song "[Bein' Green](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bein%27_Green "wikipedia:Bein' Green")" originally sung by Jim Henson as Kermit the Frog.
* Her quote for Honey ("Hello my honey!") may be a reference to the song "[Hello! Ma Baby](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hello!_Ma_Baby "wikipedia:Hello! Ma Baby")", a song most well known for being featured in the Warner Bros. short *[One Froggy Evening](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/One_Froggy_Evening "wikipedia:One Froggy Evening")*.
