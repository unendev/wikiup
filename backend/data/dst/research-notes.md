The **Research Notes** are an [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in the *[From Beyond](/wiki/From_Beyond "From Beyond")* updates. They will spawn in the [Abandoned Junk](/wiki/Abandoned_Junk "Abandoned Junk") [Set Piece](/wiki/Set_Piece "Set Piece"). When a player picks them up, a [Scrapbook](/wiki/Scrapbooking "Scrapbooking") will be unlocked titled "Day 56 - Lunar Mutations Study" a number of [Mobs](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs") that are vulnerable to lunar possession.

Though the Research Notes can be used as fuel for [Campfires](/wiki/Campfire "Campfire"), they are not flammable.

## Trivia[]
