The **Shelmet** is a wearable [Armor](/wiki/Armor "Armor") item. It has a 10% chance to drop from a [Slurtle](/wiki/Slurtle "Slurtle"). It has 750 (525Don't Starve Together) [durability](/wiki/Durability "Durability") and absorbs 90% of physical damage.

The Shelmet is the second strongest head armor item, with 450 (315Don't Starve Together) less durability than the [Thulecite Crown](/wiki/Thulecite_Crown "Thulecite Crown").

The Shelmet can be given to a [Pig](/wiki/Pig "Pig") or [Bunnyman](/wiki/Bunnyman "Bunnyman") to wear as armor. ​​​​

## Reign of Giants icon Reign of Giants[]

In the *[Reign of Giants](/wiki/Reign_of_Giants "Reign of Giants")* DLC, the Shelmet also provides 20% [water resistance](/wiki/Wetness "Wetness") during [Rain](/wiki/Rain "Rain").

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* The Shelmet was added in the *[Underground](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve/Version_History#Underground "Don't Starve/Version History")* update.
* The name "Shelmet" is a portmanteau of the words "Shell" and "Helmet".
* Wagstaff estimates the shelmet rates a 6 on the Mohs Hardness Scale, the same rating as uranium and titanium.
