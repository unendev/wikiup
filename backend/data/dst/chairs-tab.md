The **Chairs Tab** is a crafting category exclusive to the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC. It is found under the [Renovate Tab](/wiki/Renovate_Tab "Renovate Tab"). All Chairs can be rotated in multiple directions.
