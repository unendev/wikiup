**Mutated Merms** are [mobs](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in the *[Skill Spotlight Updates](/wiki/Skill_Spotlight_Update "Skill Spotlight Update")*. They arise when [Wurt](/wiki/Wurt "Wurt") with the [Lunar Mutated Merm](/wiki/Lunar_Mutated_Merm "Lunar Mutated Merm") skill gives loyal [Merms](/wiki/Merm "Merm") a [Moon Shard](/wiki/Moon_Shard "Moon Shard") or [Infused Moon Shard](/wiki/Infused_Moon_Shard "Infused Moon Shard"), or when a Wurt with the [Terra Mutation](/wiki/Terra_Mutation "Terra Mutation") skill uses the [Brilliant Mudslinger](/wiki/Brilliant_Mudslinger "Brilliant Mudslinger") to create mutated [Marsh Turf](/wiki/Marsh_Turf "Marsh Turf").

When killed, Mutated Merms drop a [Freshwater Fish](/wiki/Freshwater_Fish "Freshwater Fish") and [Frog Legs](/wiki/Frog_Legs "Frog Legs"). There is a 25% chance they also drop [Tentacle Spots](/wiki/Tentacle_Spots "Tentacle Spots").

Mutated Merms are [Lunar Aligned](/wiki/Lunar_Aligned "Lunar Aligned").

### Behavior[]

Like Merms, Mutated Merms deal 30 [damage](/wiki/Damage "Damage"), or 40 damage when there is an alive [King of the Merms](/wiki/King_of_the_Merms "King of the Merms") in the world. Additional to this, Mutated Merms deal 40 AoE damage to nearby enemies when they get attacked.

When a Wurt with the Lunar Mutated Merm skill uses [Pure Brilliance](/wiki/Pure_Brilliance "Pure Brilliance") or the Brilliant Mudslinger, 20 of the physical damage is replaced with 20 [planar damage](/wiki/Planar_damage "Planar damage") for all loyal Mutated Merms in range. This buff is indicated by glowing eyes and lasts 4 minutes.

Mutated Merms do not lose their loyalty over time and do not retreat when Wurt kills a fish. When Wurt dies, Mutated Merms will transform back to their regular form. To dismiss Mutated Merms, Wurt can either enter the [Caves](/wiki/Caves "Caves") (or the [Surface World](/wiki/Surface_World "Surface World"), if she and her Mutated Merms are in the Caves) or leave the server.

When killed, Mutated Merms Guards drop a [Freshwater Fish](/wiki/Freshwater_Fish "Freshwater Fish") and [Frog Legs](/wiki/Frog_Legs "Frog Legs"). There is a 25% chance they also drop [Tentacle Spots](/wiki/Tentacle_Spots "Tentacle Spots").

Mutated Merm Guards are [Lunar Aligned](/wiki/Lunar_Aligned "Lunar Aligned").

### Behavior[]

Like Loyal Merm Guards, Mutated Merm Guards deal 20 [damage](/wiki/Damage "Damage"), or 50 damage when there is an alive [King of the Merms](/wiki/King_of_the_Merms "King of the Merms") in the world. Additional to this, Mutated Merm Guards deal 40 AoE damage to nearby enemies when they get attacked.

When a Wurt with the Lunar Mutated Merm skill uses [Pure Brilliance](/wiki/Pure_Brilliance "Pure Brilliance") or the Brilliant Mudslinger, 20 of the physical damage is replaced with 20 [planar damage](/wiki/Planar_damage "Planar damage") for all loyal Mutated Merm Guards in range. This buff is indicated by glowing eyes and lasts 4 minutes.

Mutated Merm Guards do not lose their loyalty over time and do not retreat when Wurt kills a fish. When Wurt dies, Mutated Merm Guards will transform back to their regular form. To dismiss Mutated Merm Guards, Wurt can either enter the [Caves](/wiki/Caves "Caves") (or the [Surface World](/wiki/Surface_World "Surface World"), if she and her Mutated Merm Guards are in the Caves) or leave the server.

## Insight Skill Upgrades[]

Mutated Merms and Mutated Merm Guards can receive different upgrades depending on the skills Wurt has mastered:

| Skill | Effect |
| --- | --- |
| Sensible Swamp Fighter [Sensible Swamp Fighter](/wiki/Sensible_Swamp_Fighter "Sensible Swamp Fighter") | Loyal Merm Guards will retreat from battle if they have less than 25% of their [health](/wiki/Health "Health") left and will rejoin, if their health is restored. |
| The King's Pauldron Quest [The King's Pauldron Quest](/wiki/The_King%27s_Pauldron_Quest "The King's Pauldron Quest") | When Wurt gives a [Marble Suit](/wiki/Marble_Suit "Marble Suit") to the [King of the Merms](/wiki/King_of_the_Merms "King of the Merms"), all loyal Merms with head armor will receive additional 15% damage protection. |
| The King's Crown Quest [The King's Crown Quest](/wiki/The_King%27s_Crown_Quest "The King's Crown Quest") | When Wurt gives a [Thulecite Crown](/wiki/Thulecite_Crown "Thulecite Crown") to the King of the Merms, all loyal Merms will learn to dodge. The dodge has a 10 seconds cooldown. |
| The King's Trident Quest [The King's Trident Quest](/wiki/The_King%27s_Trident_Quest "The King's Trident Quest") | When Wurt gives a [Strident Trident](/wiki/Strident_Trident "Strident Trident") to the King of the Merms, all loyal Merms will deal 5% more damage. All Loyal Merm Guards also have a 33% chance to perform a triple attack with 26.3 damage for each strike. |
