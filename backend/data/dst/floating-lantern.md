**Floating Lanterns** are [items](/wiki/Items "Items") exclusive to New Year [events](/wiki/Events "Events") in *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*. They can be acquired in exchange for 3 [Lucky Gold Nugget](/wiki/Lucky_Gold_Nugget "Lucky Gold Nugget") at a Shrine.

When placed on the ground, they emit light in a small radius for a duration of 6 Days. They also float around slowly when placed in the [Ocean](/wiki/Ocean "Ocean"). They spontaneously combust upon expiring.
