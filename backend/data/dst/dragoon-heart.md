**Dragoon Hearts** are [Meat](/wiki/Meats "Meats") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Items](/wiki/Item "Item") introduced in the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC. They are obtained by killing [Dragoons](/wiki/Dragoon "Dragoon") or by destroying [Dragoon Dens](/wiki/Dragoon_Den "Dragoon Den"). They can be used to craft Dragoon Dens and [Volcanic Tab](/wiki/Volcanic_Tab "Volcanic Tab") items. When eaten, they will restore 11 [Health](/wiki/Health "Health") and 25 [Hunger](/wiki/Hunger "Hunger") at the cost of 10 [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity"). Eating them causes the consumer to glow for 90 seconds, similar to the effect of the [Glow Berry](/wiki/Glow_Berry "Glow Berry"). This applies both to the player and to [Mobs](/wiki/Mob "Mob") such as [Wildbores](/wiki/Wildbore "Wildbore"). Dragoon Hearts cannot be used for cooking in a [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot"). When on the ground, they will produce enough light to ward off [Charlie](/wiki/Charlie "Charlie").

## Reign of Giants icon Reign of Giants[]

When brought to a connected *[Reign of Giants](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Reign_of_Giants "Don't Starve: Reign of Giants")* world, Dragoon Hearts can be used to refuel [Moggles](/wiki/Moggles "Moggles"), restoring 33.25% of their durability.
