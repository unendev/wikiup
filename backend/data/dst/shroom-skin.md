**Shroom Skin** is an [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") found in *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[A New Reign](/wiki/A_New_Reign "A New Reign")*. It is obtained while fighting the [Toadstool](/wiki/Toadstool "Toadstool"), and every time the player chips off a third of the Toadstool's health, one is dropped. Shroom Skin is a major crafting ingredient of the [Mushlight](/wiki/Mushlight "Mushlight"), the [Glowcap](/wiki/Glowcap "Glowcap") and the [Napsack](/wiki/Napsack "Napsack").

The Shroom Skin also has a chance to be unwrapped from the [Loot Stash](/wiki/Loot_Stash "Loot Stash") after fighting [Klaus](/wiki/Klaus "Klaus").
