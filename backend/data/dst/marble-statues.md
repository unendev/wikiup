**Marble Statues** are naturally spawning objects exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[A New Reign](/wiki/A_New_Reign "A New Reign")*. They are similar to Marble [Pillars](/wiki/Marble_Pillar "Marble Pillar") and [Trees](/wiki/Marble_Trees "Marble Trees") in that they can be mined down to yield 2 [Marble](/wiki/Marble "Marble") or 3 (30%). Comedy or Tragedy Statues can be mined, giving the player a [Queenly Figure](/wiki/Chess_Pieces#Queenly_Figure "Chess Pieces") [Sketch](/wiki/Sketch "Sketch"), while the Pawn Statue can be mined to obtain a [Pawn Figure](/wiki/Chess_Pieces#Pawn_Figure "Chess Pieces") Sketch.

Mining the Pawn and either Tragedy or Comedy statues is a requirement for the Pawn and Queenly Figure Sketches to drop from [Tumbleweeds](/wiki/Tumbleweed "Tumbleweed") in the world.

## Trivia[]

* Marble Statues were added in the *[A Little Fixer Upper](/wiki/A_New_Reign#September_1,_2016_-_A_Little_Fixer_Upper "A New Reign")* update.
* The first two statues are references to the Comedy and Tragedy genres from classic Greece, fitting [Charlie's](/wiki/Charlie "Charlie") theatrical theme.
