A **Glow Berry** (not to be confused with a [Lesser Glow Berry](/wiki/Lesser_Glow_Berry "Lesser Glow Berry")) is the glowing lure of a [Depths Worm](/wiki/Depths_Worm "Depths Worm"), dropped as loot upon its [death](/wiki/Death "Death"). When eaten, it restores 11 Health and 25 Hunger but reduces Sanity by 10.

A Glow Berry will also cause any creature that consumes it, including [Bunnymen](/wiki/Bunnymen "Bunnymen"), [Pigs](/wiki/Pig "Pig"), [Splumonkeys](/wiki/Monkey "Monkey"), [Prime Apes](/wiki/Prime_Ape "Prime Ape") and [Gobblers](/wiki/Gobbler "Gobbler"), to glow. The intensity of the glowing light decreases over time and will extinguish 90 seconds after consumption. If dropped on the ground, it will glow, similar to a [Light Bulb](/wiki/Light_Bulb "Light Bulb"). It emits enough light to prevent an attack from [Charlie](/wiki/Charlie "Charlie").

## Downloadable Content[]

In all [DLCs](/wiki/DLC "DLC"), Glow Berries can be stacked up to 10 in the player's [Inventory](/wiki/Inventory "Inventory").

In the *[Reign of Giants](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Reign_of_Giants "Don't Starve: Reign of Giants")* DLC, Glow Berries can be used to craft [Moggles](/wiki/Moggles "Moggles"). Additionally, they can be used to refuel Moggles, extending their durability by 33%.

In the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC, Glow Berries can be bought for 5 [Oincs](/wiki/Oinc "Oinc") at ['The Sty' Oddities Emporium](/wiki/%27The_Sty%27_Oddities_Emporium "'The Sty' Oddities Emporium").

## Don't Starve Together Don't Starve Together[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, the glow will last longer than in *Don't Starve*: for a total of 240 seconds after consumption. [Warly](/wiki/Warly "Warly") can use Glow Berries to make [Glow Berry Mousse](/wiki/Glow_Berry_Mousse "Glow Berry Mousse") in the [Portable Crock Pot](/wiki/Portable_Crock_Pot "Portable Crock Pot").
