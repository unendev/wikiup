**Boulders** are large rocks commonly found in [Savannas](/wiki/Savanna "Savanna") and [Rockylands](/wiki/Rockyland "Rockyland"), although they may appear anywhere in the [world](/wiki/World "World"). Their main purpose is to provide [Rocks](/wiki/Rocks "Rocks") when mined. Additionally, Boulders may also provide [Flint](/wiki/Flint "Flint"), [Gold Nuggets](/wiki/Gold_Nugget "Gold Nugget"), and [Nitre](/wiki/Nitre "Nitre"). Boulders do not respawn; however, Rocks, Flint, Gold Nuggets, and Nitre are renewable resources because they can be found after [Earthquakes](/wiki/Earthquakes "Earthquakes") while [underground](/wiki/Caves "Caves").

## Icon Tools Mining[]

Most kinds of Boulders can be mined using the [Pickaxe](/wiki/Pickaxe "Pickaxe"), [Opulent Pickaxe](/wiki/Opulent_Pickaxe "Opulent Pickaxe"), or [Pick/Axe](/wiki/Pick/Axe "Pick/Axe"). To do this, click the tool on the Boulder or equip it from the inventory by right-clicking. Once the character has begun to swing the tool at the Boulder, either the LMB or Spacebar must be held or continuously clicked to continue mining.

It takes 6 strikes to mine a Boulder with a Pickaxe and Opulent Pickaxe, and 5 with the Pick/Axe. It depletes a normal Pickaxe's durability by 18%, Opulent Pickaxe's by 4.5%, and Pick/Axe's by 4%.

**Boulders** are large rocks commonly found in [Savannas](/wiki/Savanna "Savanna") and [Rockylands](/wiki/Rockyland "Rockyland"), although they may appear anywhere in the [world](/wiki/World "World"). Their main purpose is to provide [Rocks](/wiki/Rocks "Rocks") when mined, which can be used to build a number of objects/structures. A single Boulder will drop 3 [Rocks](/wiki/Rocks "Rocks"), 1 [Flint](/wiki/Flint "Flint") plus a 60% chance to get 1 extra and 1 [Nitre](/wiki/Nitre "Nitre") plus a 25% to get 1 extra.

The **Obsidian Boulder** is introduced in the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Shipwrecked "Shipwrecked")* DLC. It cannot be mined with a [Pickaxe](/wiki/Pickaxe "Pickaxe"), but only be destroyed with Explosives or [Weather Pain](/wiki/Weather_Pain "Weather Pain"). It takes 6 [Slurtle Slime](/wiki/Slurtle_Slime "Slurtle Slime") (dropped in individual stacks), a single [Gunpowder](/wiki/Gunpowder "Gunpowder"), a single [Coconade](/wiki/Coconade "Coconade")/[Obsidian Coconade](/wiki/Obsidian_Coconade "Obsidian Coconade"), a single [Dragoon Egg](/wiki/Dragoon_Egg "Dragoon Egg") summoned by the [Volcano Staff](/wiki/Volcano_Staff "Volcano Staff") or the [Joy of Volcanology](/wiki/Joy_of_Volcanology "Joy of Volcanology") to break an Obsidian Boulder. Obsidian Boulders provide [Obsidian](/wiki/Obsidian "Obsidian") when destroyed. Obsidian Boulders are renewable; a new one has a chance to spawn at the same location during [Dry Season](/wiki/Dry_Season "Dry Season").

They are guaranteed to provide 2 [Obsidian](/wiki/Obsidian "Obsidian") and have a 50% chance to give one extra, plus two additional 25% chances for another one or two more.

During a meteor shower, a special Smooth Meteor Boulder will have a chance of falling which is differentiated from regular Meteor Boulders by its round shape. Only one of this kind of boulder can be found, as it can be mined to produce the [Celestial Orb](/wiki/Celestial_Orb "Celestial Orb").

It drops the Celestial Orb, 1 [Rocks](/wiki/Rocks "Rocks"), 1 [Flint](/wiki/Flint "Flint") and 3 [Moon Rock](/wiki/Moon_Rock "Moon Rock") plus a 30% chance to get one extra when mined.
