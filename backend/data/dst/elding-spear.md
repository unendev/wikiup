The **Elding Spear** is a [Melee Weapon](/wiki/Melee_Weapon "Melee Weapon") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*. It can only be crafted by [Wigfrid](/wiki/Wigfrid "Wigfrid") after unlocking the [Elding Spear Skill](/wiki/Elding_Spear_Skill "Elding Spear Skill") and needs 2 [Twigs](/wiki/Twigs "Twigs"), 2 [Volt Goat Horns](/wiki/Volt_Goat_Horn "Volt Goat Horn") and 1 [Beefalo Wool](/wiki/Beefalo_Wool "Beefalo Wool") to craft.

The Elding Spear deals 59.5 [Damage](/wiki/Damage "Damage") to dry targets and 89.25 Damage to wet targets.

---

### Insight Skill Upgrades[]

When [Elding Spear Enhancement I](/wiki/Elding_Spear_Enhancement_I "Elding Spear Enhancement I") is unlocked, Wigfrid can perform a special attack similar to the one with the [Pith Pike](/wiki/Pith_Pike "Pith Pike") in [The Forge](/wiki/The_Forge "The Forge"), rushing forward and hitting every enemy in the way, dealing 68 damage. This attack has a cooldown of 3 seconds. Also, when charged it repairs itself by 2% on every hit or 4% when hitting multiple targets. For this the following Skill has to be unlocked too and the Spear upgraded.

When [Elding Spear Enhancement II](/wiki/Elding_Spear_Enhancement_II "Elding Spear Enhancement II") is unlocked, the Elding Spear can be upgraded with a [Restrained Static](/wiki/Restrained_Static "Restrained Static") changing it to the Charged Elding Spear and adding 20 [Planar Damage](/wiki/Planar_Damage "Planar Damage") to it. This also gives Wigfrid a 20% speed bonus when equipped and activates the self-repair perk from the previous upgrade. Also the cooldown of the special attack is reduced to 1.5 seconds.
