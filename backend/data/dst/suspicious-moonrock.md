**Suspicious Moonrocks** are naturally spawning objects exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[A New Reign](/wiki/A_New_Reign "A New Reign")*. They are [Moon Rock](/wiki/Moon_Rock "Moon Rock")-made sculptures shaped like [Werepigs](/wiki/Werepig "Werepig") and [Hounds](/wiki/Hound "Hound") and can be found scattered around the [Moon Stone](/wiki/Moon_Stone "Moon Stone") set piece. Mining them with a [Pickaxe](/wiki/Pickaxe "Pickaxe") will yield one Moon Rock plus 50% to get one extra.

Additionally, after placing a [Star Caller's Staff](/wiki/Star_Caller%27s_Staff "Star Caller's Staff") onto the Moon Stone during a [Full Moon](/wiki/Full_Moon "Full Moon"), several Werepigs and Hounds will spawn and attack the Staff. After breaking the base, all of them will turn into Suspicious Moonrock sculptures. They will turn once again into the corresponding [Mobs](/wiki/Mob "Mob") if the base is fixed and the staff is put back on it.

There are three different appearances for the Werepig, corresponding to its attack-, death and howl-animations, and two different appearances for the Hound, corresponding to its attack- and death-animations.

## Prototype Tips[]

* One should mine down all Suspicious Moonrocks before attempting to use the Moon Stone again, as it will keep spawning more enemies along with the ones from previous attempts.

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* Suspicious Moonrocks were introduced in the *[A Little Fixer Upper](/wiki/A_New_Reign#September_1,_2016_-_A_Little_Fixer_Upper "A New Reign")* update.
