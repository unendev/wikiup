**Trail Mix** is a [Fruit](/wiki/Fruit "Fruit") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") introduced in the *[Reign of Giants](/wiki/Reign_of_Giants "Reign of Giants")* DLC. It is cooked in a [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") by combining 1 [Roasted Birchnut](/wiki/Birchnut "Birchnut"), 1 [Berry](/wiki/Berries "Berries"), and 1 [Fruit](/wiki/Fruit "Fruit"). It takes 10 seconds to cook and 15 [Days](/wiki/Day-Night_Cycle "Day-Night Cycle") to [Spoil](/wiki/Spoil "Spoil").

When consumed, it restores 30 Health, 12.5 Hunger and 5 Sanity.

### Don't Starve Together Don't Starve Together[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, Trail Mix can be made with raw Birchnuts.

It is also the [Favorite Food](/wiki/Food#Favorite_Food "Food") of [Walter](/wiki/Walter "Walter"), for whom it restores 27.5 Hunger instead of 12.5.

## Tips[]

* Trail Mix is one of the cheapest healing dishes that can be made, restoring more [Health](/wiki/Health "Health") than its components, making it a useful early game healing item. However, it restores less total [Hunger](/wiki/Hunger "Hunger") than its individual ingredients, even for [Walter](/wiki/Walter "Walter").
* Trail Mix is one of the better healing items for [Beefalo](/wiki/Beefalo "Beefalo"), for whom it restores 120 health (due to their 4x food health multiplier). It can be made earlier in the game than [Dragonpie](/wiki/Dragonpie "Dragonpie") and lasts longer than [Beefalo Treats](/wiki/Beefalo_Treats "Beefalo Treats") (15 days vs. 10).
