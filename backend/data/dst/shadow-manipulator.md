The **Shadow Manipulator** is the tier 2 [Magic](/wiki/Magic "Magic") station which unlocks all of the Magic Tab recipes. It requires a [Prestihatitator](/wiki/Prestihatitator "Prestihatitator") to prototype and costs 3 [Living Logs](/wiki/Living_Log "Living Log"), 1 [Purple Gem](/wiki/Purple_Gem "Purple Gem"), and 7 [Nightmare Fuel](/wiki/Nightmare_Fuel "Nightmare Fuel") to craft. Prototyping an [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") using the Shadow Manipulator will increase [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity") by 15, like any other station.

As the second tier magic structure, all craftables offered by Prestihatitator can also be accessed and prototyped from a Shadow Manipulator. ## Downloadable Content[]

In the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Shipwrecked "Shipwrecked")* DLC, the Shadow Manipulator can be [flooded](/wiki/Flooding "Flooding").

## Tips[]

* Chopping down a [Totally Normal Tree](/wiki/Totally_Normal_Tree "Totally Normal Tree") and digging up its stump yields enough [Living Logs](/wiki/Living_Logs "Living Logs") to craft a Shadow Manipulator without the need to kill a [Treeguard](/wiki/Treeguard "Treeguard"). However, making other items such as [Dark Swords](/wiki/Dark_Sword "Dark Sword") would require Living Logs.

## Bugs[]

* In [Winter](/wiki/Winter "Winter"), snow on top of the device disappears with the rising and falling animations (when the player is near).

## Trivia[]

* The Shadow Manipulator was added in *[A Little Rain Must Fall](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve/Version_History#A_Little_Rain_Must_Fall "Don't Starve/Version History")* update.
* A Shadow Manipulator can be found in the [Miner's Camp](/wiki/Set_Piece#Miner's_Camp "Set Piece") set piece.
* The Shadow Manipulator was the tier 3 [Science](/wiki/Science "Science") research lab before Science and [Magic](/wiki/Magic "Magic") research were separated into different categories.
* In *Don't Starve Together*, the [Clean Sweeper](/wiki/Clean_Sweeper "Clean Sweeper") also used to require a Shadow Manipulator to be prototyped until March 2022 Quality of Life update where it was moved to Science Tier 2 and its recipe was changed from 6 Dark Petals and 3 Living Logs to 4 Petals and 1 Twig.

## Don't Starve Together Skins[]

*https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/dont-starve-game/images/c/cb/Glommer.png/revision/latest?cb=20231029201751 See also: [Belongings#Shadow\_Manipulator](/wiki/Belongings#Shadow_Manipulator "Belongings")*

Shadow Manipulator comes with multiple different skins it can be crafted with or later changed using a [Clean Sweeper](/wiki/Clean_Sweeper "Clean Sweeper") in Don't Starve Together. Ancient Sigil belongs in the Builder's Belongings Chest within the [Year of the Carrat](/wiki/Year_of_the_Carrat "Year of the Carrat") collection while Reclaimed Relic was released in the Adventurer's Belongings Chest, included with the Spring Cleaning Bundle. Both skins are of **Woven - Elegant** rarity and can be woven with 1350 spools, unraveled into 450 spools or obtained from Weekly Drops. Enchanted Crystal was a part of the crystalline collection wave of Twitch Stream drops and can be obtained from [Klei Rewards](https://accounts.klei.com/login?goto=https://accounts.klei.com/account/rewards). Monstrous Horror was a part of the Hollowed Nights Collection as the Twitch Stream drop of Holloween 2021.
