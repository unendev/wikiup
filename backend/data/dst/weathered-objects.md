**Weathered Objects** are objects introduced in the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC. They can exclusively be found in the [BFB's Nest](/wiki/BFB%27s_Nest "BFB's Nest") [Biome](/wiki/Biome "Biome"), either in the heart of the nest itself, or scattered around the island.

These objects are made of basic materials, so they are likely here to allow the player to build tools (especially a [Pickaxe](/wiki/Pickaxe "Pickaxe")) to escape the BFB's island if they haven't got one. However, all of these objects are non-renewable: when they are destroyed, it will be permanent.
