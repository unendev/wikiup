**Mushy Cake** is a Goodies [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") recipe exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")*. It is cooked in the [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") by adding 1 [Moon Shroom](/wiki/Moon_Shroom "Moon Shroom"), 1 [Red Cap](/wiki/Red_Cap "Red Cap"), 1 [Green Cap](/wiki/Green_Cap "Green Cap") and 1 [Blue Cap](/wiki/Blue_Cap "Blue Cap"). Any recipe without the four different mushrooms will result in [Ratatouille](/wiki/Ratatouille "Ratatouille"). It takes 20 seconds to cook and 15 [Days](/wiki/Day-Night_Cycle "Day-Night Cycle") to [Spoil](/wiki/Spoil "Spoil").

When consumed, it restores 25 Hunger and 10 Sanity and increases the [Sleep Resistance](/wiki/Grogginess "Grogginess") from 10 to 33.3 seconds for 1 [Day](/wiki/Day "Day").

## Trivia[]

* The Mushy Cake was introduced in the *[Forgotten Knowledge](/wiki/Return_of_Them#Forgotten_Knowledge "Return of Them")* update.
* The Mushy Cake and [Grim Galette](/wiki/Grim_Galette "Grim Galette") are the only dishes in Don't Starve Together that have only one possible recipe.
* Wendy's quote is likely a reference to the Super Mushroom and Mini Mushroom from the *Super Mario* series, the former making Mario grow in size and the latter, shrinking him. It could also be in part a reference to *Alice in Wonderland*'s 'Eat Me' cake and 'Drink Me' potion.
