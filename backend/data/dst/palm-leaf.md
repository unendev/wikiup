**Palm Leaves** are a resource in [*Shipwrecked*](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked") DLC. They can be obtained by chopping down [Palm Trees](/wiki/Palm_Tree "Palm Tree").

They can be used for several crafting recipes, including [Thatch Packs](/wiki/Backpack#Thatch_Pack "Backpack"), [Thatch Sails](/wiki/Thatch_Sail "Thatch Sail"), [Sea Traps](/wiki/Sea_Trap "Sea Trap"), [Tropical Parasols](/wiki/Tropical_Parasol "Tropical Parasol"), [Palm Leaf Huts](/wiki/Palm_Leaf_Hut "Palm Leaf Hut"), [Wildbore Houses](/wiki/Wildbore_House "Wildbore House"), [Sand Castles](/wiki/Sand_Castle "Sand Castle"), [Blubber Suits](/wiki/Blubber_Suit "Blubber Suit") and [Tar Suits](/wiki/Tar_Suit "Tar Suit").

Palm Leaves are also a rather inefficient source of [Fuel](/wiki/Fuel "Fuel").
