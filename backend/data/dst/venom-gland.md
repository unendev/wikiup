A **Venom Gland** is a healing [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") introduced in the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC. It is used for crafting items such as the [Anti Venom](/wiki/Anti_Venom "Anti Venom"), as well as poisoned [Weapons](/wiki/Weapons "Weapons"). Venom Glands can also be directly consumed to cure the [Poison](/wiki/Poison "Poison") effect. Consuming a Venom Gland to cure poison deals up to 75 poison damage, however this damage will not kill the player character. If the player character has less than 80 [Health](/wiki/Health "Health") left, Venom Gland will only deal enough damage, if at all, to reduce the remaining health to a minimum of 5. Consuming the Venom Gland while having less than 5 health will not increase the health.

## Hamlet icon Hamlet[]

Venom Glands are also included in the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC where they can be crafted in the [Refine Tab](/wiki/Refine_Tab "Refine Tab") by using 3 [Poison Dartfrog Legs](/wiki/Poison_Dartfrog_Legs "Poison Dartfrog Legs"). They also have a chance to drop from [Scorpions](/wiki/Scorpion "Scorpion") and [Poison Dartfrogs](/wiki/Poison_Dartfrog "Poison Dartfrog"). [Wormwood](/wiki/Wormwood "Wormwood") requires Venom Glands to craft [Poison Balm](/wiki/Poison_Balm "Poison Balm").

## Prototype Tips[]

* In *Shipwrecked*, one of the safest and most efficient ways to farm Venom Glands is by luring [Sea Hounds](/wiki/Sea_Hound "Sea Hound") or [Crocodogs](/wiki/Crocodog "Crocodog") into a group of [Stink Rays](/wiki/Stink_Ray "Stink Ray") and then leaving. Most, if not all, of the Stink Rays will be killed, and when the player returns, they will find multiple Venom Glands floating in the ocean. This method removes the chance of being poisoned while fighting venomous creatures, and gives the player multiple glands that can be kept on hand for crafting [Anti Venom](/wiki/Anti_Venom "Anti Venom").
* Due to [Maxwell](/wiki/Maxwell "Maxwell")'s low health, it is advised to craft an Anti Venom, instead of ingesting a Venom Gland.
