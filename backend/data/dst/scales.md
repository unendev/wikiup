**Scales** are a rare item in *[Reign of Giants](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Reign_of_Giants "Don't Starve: Reign of Giants")*. They are dropped by the [Summer](/wiki/Summer "Summer") Giant, [Dragonfly](/wiki/Dragonfly "Dragonfly"). Scales are used to craft fire-resistant items: the [Scalemail](/wiki/Scalemail "Scalemail"), which grants wearer immunity to fire damage, and the fire-proof container, the [Scaled Chest](/wiki/Scaled_Chest "Scaled Chest").

## Hamlet icon Hamlet[]

Scales are also included in the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC, however, the Dragonfly is not present. Instead, Scales can be bought for 30 [Oincs](/wiki/Oinc "Oinc") at ['The Sty' Oddities Emporium](/wiki/%27The_Sty%27_Oddities_Emporium "'The Sty' Oddities Emporium") in the [Pig City](/wiki/Pig_City "Pig City").

## Don't Starve Together icon Don't Starve Together[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, Scales can be used to craft [Scaled Flooring](/wiki/Scaled_Flooring "Scaled Flooring") and the [Scaled Furnace](/wiki/Scaled_Furnace "Scaled Furnace"). The flooring limits the rate that fire spreads to objects placed on it, while the furnace is an infinite light and heat source similar to [Magma](/wiki/Magma "Magma").
