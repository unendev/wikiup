**Dragoons** are hostile [Mobs](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs") introduced in the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Shipwrecked "Shipwrecked")* DLC. They spawn from [Dragoon Dens](/wiki/Dragoon_Den "Dragoon Den"). They will also hatch from intact [Dragoon Eggs](/wiki/Dragoon_Egg "Dragoon Egg"). When killed, they will drop 2 [Monster Meat](/wiki/Monster_Meat "Monster Meat"), and have a 25% chance to drop a [Dragoon Heart](/wiki/Dragoon_Heart "Dragoon Heart").

## Brain Behavior[]

Dragoons are active during the [day](/wiki/Day "Day"), and will sleep through the [night](/wiki/Night "Night"). If they belong to a [Dragoon Den](/wiki/Dragoon_Den "Dragoon Den"), they will return to their den at [dusk](/wiki/Dusk "Dusk"). While idle they will periodically spit lava on the ground, which can ignite nearby [Structures](/wiki/Structures "Structures") or resources.

They are hostile to the player character and most other Mobs, and will attack them on sight.

When entering combat, they will charge their target and throw a punch. While charging, they leave small flames in their wake, which can ignite items on the ground. Dragoons do not help each other in combat.

## Spear Strategy[]

Dragoons are relatively easy to kill using melee [weapons](/wiki/Weapons "Weapons"). Players can allow the Dragoon to charge towards them, and then dodge the punch. Striking the Dragoon after dodging its punch stun-locks it for a short period, allowing for the player to land 7-8 weapon hits. However, if they are hit while striking a target, they will not be stun-locked and be free to attack back.

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* Dragoons were introduced in the *[Wilbur Vs. the Volcano](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked#February_4th,_2016_-_Wilbur_Vs._The_Volcano_Update "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* update.

* Dragoons have a similar animation to the Catcoon.
* Willow's quote may be a reference to *How To Train Your Dragon*.
