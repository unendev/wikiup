**Alloy** is a resource exclusive to the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC. It is obtained by refining [Iron Ore](/wiki/Iron_Ore "Iron Ore") in a [Smelter](/wiki/Smelter "Smelter") and is required for several [Crafting](/wiki/Crafting "Crafting") recipes. There is a 16.67% chance to spawn a single Alloy in the [Secret Bandit Camp](/wiki/Secret_Bandit_Camp "Secret Bandit Camp").
