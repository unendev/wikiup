The **Landscaping Tab** is a *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")* exclusive tab that lets the player [craft](/wiki/Crafting "Crafting") various [Turfs](/wiki/Turfs "Turfs"). It is accessed by standing next to a placed [Terra Firma Tamper](/wiki/Terra_Firma_Tamper "Terra Firma Tamper").
