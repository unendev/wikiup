## Summary[]

One of the simplest, and first farms a player can generally make is for [Berry Bushes](/wiki/Berry_Bushes "Berry Bushes"). In essence, simply digging up and moving Berry Bushes from across the world to their base. Once [fertilized](/wiki/Fertilizers "Fertilizers"), this acts as a great farm for fillers in many [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_pot "Crock pot") recipes; the classic [Meatballs](/wiki/Meatballs "Meatballs") being made with nothing but a [Monster Meat](/wiki/Monster_Meat "Monster Meat") and three [Berries](/wiki/Berries "Berries").

But we can do better than this. There are a few additions to this simple farm setup, the first of which is a [Lightning Rod](/wiki/Lightning_Rod "Lightning Rod"). This is vital for the farm, as Berry Bushes are incredibly flammable, and when packed tightly together, one unlucky lightning cast could be disastrous.

Using an [Ice Flingomatic](/wiki/Ice_Flingomatic "Ice Flingomatic") isn't necessarily essential, as it depends on if the player is using a 'Summer Base', as either the [Oasis](/wiki/Oasis "Oasis") or in the [Caves](/wiki/Caves "Caves"). The *[Constant](/wiki/The_Constant "The Constant")* [wildfires](/wiki/Fire#Downloadable_Content "Fire") during [Summer](/wiki/Seasons/Summer "Seasons/Summer") are dreadful, so if you are planning on staying home during the season, ensure you have an [Ice Flingomatic](/wiki/Ice_Flingomatic "Ice Flingomatic") placed down too.

However, what makes the *Incredible Inedible* so special is the dual-functionality of the farm. While [Gobblers](/wiki/Gobblers "Gobblers") appear to be an unlucky spawn to new players, as the turkey runs around eating all of their Berries, these creatures can be easily tricked and killed. Once spawned, if the Gobbler spots something edible on the floor nearby, it will instantly walk towards it in the hopes of a snack. This works in our favour, as we can place some simple [Fences](/wiki/Wood_Fence "Wood Fence") in-between it and the food on the floor.

 https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/dont-starve-game/images/8/8c/Simple incredible inedible.png/revision/latest?cb=20201011231411 



 

While the designs can vary, in its most basic form, the *Incredible Inedible* is having Berry Bushes surrounding a food item on the floor with [Fences](/wiki/Wood_Fence "Wood Fence") around it. Creating a pen with a [Lightning Rod](/wiki/Lightning_rod "Lightning rod") and [Powdercake](/wiki/Powdercake "Powdercake") with Berry Bushes around it is the most efficient design, as the picture (minus the Lightning Rod) shows. Ensure that you mainly harvest the Berries during the [day](/wiki/Day "Day"), as [Gobblers](/wiki/Gobblers "Gobblers") will instantly flee to another bush during [dusk](/wiki/Dusk "Dusk") or [night](/wiki/Night "Night"). The [Gobblers](/wiki/Gobblers "Gobblers") will spawn, track the [Powdercake](/wiki/Powdercake "Powdercake"), and walk into the fences. Free Berries and [Drumsticks](/wiki/Drumsticks "Drumsticks")! You'll have virtually everything you need for a [Turkey Dinner](/wiki/Turkey_Dinner "Turkey Dinner").
