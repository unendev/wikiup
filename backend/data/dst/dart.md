**Darts** are craftable [Ranged Weapons](/wiki/Weapon#Ranged_weapons "Weapon") that cost 2 [Cut Reeds](/wiki/Cut_Reeds "Cut Reeds"), a type of [Feather](/wiki/Feather "Feather"), & another item to prototype at a [Science Machine](/wiki/Science_Machine "Science Machine"). Blow Dart, Fire Dart, and Sleep Dart are present in the base game; Poison Dart is available in the [*Shipwrecked*](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked") [DLC](/wiki/DLC "DLC"); and Electric Dart is exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*. Each dart has only one use.

**Blow Dart** costs 2 Cut Reeds, 1 [Hound's Tooth](/wiki/Hound%27s_Tooth "Hound's Tooth"), & 1 [Azure](/wiki/Feather "Feather") Feather to craft. It is also dropped by [MacTusk](/wiki/MacTusk_N%27_Son "MacTusk N' Son"). On use, it deals 100 damage to its target.

1 shot will turn neutral Mobs, such as [Koalefants](/wiki/Koalefant "Koalefant"), hostile. 2 shots destroy a [Beehive](/wiki/Beehive "Beehive"). 1-5 shots can kill normal [Mobs](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs") (e.g. [Guardian Pigs](/wiki/Guardian_Pig "Guardian Pig"), MacTusk, [Merms](/wiki/Merm "Merm")).

### Downloadable Content[]

In *Shipwrecked*, the [Slot Machine](/wiki/Slot_Machine "Slot Machine") may drop Blow Darts.

In *[Hamlet](/wiki/Hamlet "Hamlet")*, Blow Darts drop when using [Disarming Tools](/wiki/Disarming_Tools "Disarming Tools") on [Striking Carvings](/wiki/Striking_Carving "Striking Carving") or [Striking Statues](/wiki/Striking_Statue "Striking Statue") in the ruins. They can also be bought for 10 [Oincs](/wiki/Oinc "Oinc") at [The Boar's Tusk Weapon Shop](/wiki/The_Boar%27s_Tusk_Weapon_Shop "The Boar's Tusk Weapon Shop").

**Fire Dart** costs 2 [Cut Reeds](/wiki/Cut_Reeds "Cut Reeds"), 1 [Charcoal](/wiki/Charcoal "Charcoal"), & 1 [Crimson](/wiki/Feather "Feather") Feather to craft. On use, it deals 0 direct damage but up to 120 fire damage.

Mobs killed by fire drop food as cooked (e.g. a [Rabbit](/wiki/Rabbit "Rabbit") would drop a cooked [Morsel](/wiki/Morsel "Morsel")); most other drops turn to [Ashes](/wiki/Ashes "Ashes"). Firing at a [Spider Den](/wiki/Spider_Den "Spider Den") will destroy it (without dropping any loot) and force [Spiders](/wiki/Spider "Spider") out. Fire Darts can also set [Hay](/wiki/Hay_Wall "Hay Wall") and [Wood](/wiki/Wood_Wall "Wood Wall") walls on fire.

[Abigail](/wiki/Abigail "Abigail"), [Baby Tentacles](/wiki/Tentacle#Baby_Tentacle "Tentacle"), [Big Tentacles](/wiki/Tentacle#Big_Tentacle "Tentacle"), [Depths Worms](/wiki/Depths_Worm "Depths Worm"), [Frogs](/wiki/Frog "Frog"), [Ghosts](/wiki/Ghost "Ghost"), [Houndius Shootius](/wiki/Houndius_Shootius "Houndius Shootius"), [Shadow Creatures](/wiki/Shadow_Creature "Shadow Creature"), and [Tentacles](/wiki/Tentacle "Tentacle") are immune to catching fire but not to fire damage. [Red Hounds](/wiki/Hound#Red_Hound "Hound") are immune to fire.

### Downloadable Content[]

In all DLCs, Mobs take 5 points of direct damage from Fire Darts.

In *[Reign of Giants (RoG)](/wiki/Reign_of_Giants "Reign of Giants"),* [Dragonfly](/wiki/Dragonfly "Dragonfly") is immune to fire.

In *Shipwrecked,* [Dragoons](/wiki/Dragoon "Dragoon") are immune to fire. [Elephant Cacti](/wiki/Elephant_Cactus "Elephant Cactus"), [Tiger Shark](/wiki/Tiger_Shark "Tiger Shark"), and aquatic Mobs do not catch fire but take fire damage. The Slot Machine may drop Fire Darts.

In *Hamlet,* [Gnat Swarms](/wiki/Gnat_Swarm "Gnat Swarm") and [Large Iron Hulk](/wiki/Large_Iron_Hulk "Large Iron Hulk") are immune to fire. [Ancient Herald](/wiki/Ancient_Herald "Ancient Herald"), [Ancient Spirits](/wiki/Ancient_Spirit "Ancient Spirit"), [Hanging Vines](/wiki/Hanging_Vine "Hanging Vine"), [Pugalisk](/wiki/Pugalisk "Pugalisk"), and [Queen Womant](/wiki/Queen_Womant "Queen Womant") do not catch fire but take fire damage. Mobs that live in water are not immune to fire. Frogs in *Hamlet*-compatible worlds can catch fire.

**Sleep Dart** costs 2 Cut Reeds, 1 [Stinger](/wiki/Stinger "Stinger"), & 1 [Jet](/wiki/Feather "Feather") Feather to craft. On use, it puts the target to [sleep](/wiki/Sleeping "Sleeping"); however, the creature will wake up immediately if attacked. Some Mobs are sleep-resistant, requiring up to five darts.

[Butterflies](/wiki/Butterfly "Butterfly"), Depths Worms, Ghosts, [Mandrakes](/wiki/Mandrake "Mandrake"), Shadow Creatures, and Tentacles are immune to Sleep Darts.

### Hamlet Hamlet[]

In the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Hamlet "Hamlet")* DLC, [Ancient Herald](/wiki/Ancient_Herald "Ancient Herald"), [Large Iron Hulk](/wiki/Iron_Hulk#Large_Iron_Hulk "Iron Hulk"), [Pugalisk](/wiki/Pugalisk "Pugalisk"), [BFB](/wiki/BFB "BFB"), [Ancient Spirit](/wiki/Ancient_Spirit "Ancient Spirit"), [Dung Beetle](/wiki/Dung_Beetle "Dung Beetle"), [Gnat Swarm](/wiki/Gnat_Swarm "Gnat Swarm"), [Hanging Vine](/wiki/Hanging_Vine "Hanging Vine"), [Snaptooth Flytrap](/wiki/Snaptooth#Snaptooth_Flytrap "Snaptooth"), and [Weevole](/wiki/Weevole "Weevole") can't be put to sleep.

### Don't Starve Together Don't Starve Together[]

In *DST*, selected Mobs (mostly bosses) have the *diminishing returns* trait (**bolded** in the table below). Their sleep time will be gradually reduced each time they are put to sleep, down to a minimum of 0.2 seconds; otherwise, it will return to normal duration.

**Electric Dart** was introduced to *DST* in the *[A New Reign](/wiki/A_New_Reign "A New Reign")* update. It requires 2 Cut Reeds, 1 [Gold Nugget](/wiki/Gold_Nugget "Gold Nugget"), & 1 [Saffron](/wiki/Feather#Don't_Starve_Together "Feather") Feather to craft. The base damage is 60, but being electrical gives it a 1.5x modifier, raising damage to 90; moreover, a [wet](/wiki/Wetness "Wetness") target provides an additional 1x modifier for a total of 150 damage (for characters with normal damage modifiers). Like the [Morning Star](/wiki/Morning_Star "Morning Star"), it can electrify [Volt Goats](/wiki/Volt_Goat "Volt Goat") for acquiring [Electric Milk](/wiki/Electric_Milk "Electric Milk").

The Blow Dart was added in *[A Winter's Tale](/wiki/Version_History#A_Winter's_Tale "Version History")*, and the Electric Dart was added in *[Warts and All](/wiki/A_New_Reign#September_23rd,_2016_-_Warts_and_All "A New Reign")*.
* MacTusk deals only 33 damage with the Blow Dart.
* Striking Carving and Striking Statue deals only 34 damage with the Blow Dart.
* In the old [Research](/wiki/Research_Points_List "Research Points List") system, the research cost for every dart was 100.
* Should one die to a Volt Goat electrified by Electric Dart, their cause of death will be listed as "Shenanigans".

## Bugs[]

* Shooting a dart with certain headgears equipped will cause Wilson's hair to briefly glitch through them.
* When shooting wet darts in *RoG*, one dart may slip out of grasp and the rest will disappear.
* Sometimes, especially when attacking stationary targets, the dart will not disappear after being shot but instead hover erratically near the target.
* A bird shot with a Fire Dart while on fire will behave normally, despite showing flames and taking fire damage; it may even fly off, with a cooked Morsel or Ashes falling from the sky shortly.
