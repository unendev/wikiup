The **Archaic Boat Kit** is an [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in the *[From Beyond](/wiki/From_Beyond "From Beyond")* updates. It cannot be crafted, but has a 15% chance to drop from a [Sunken Chest](/wiki/Sunken_Chest "Sunken Chest"). It can be placed on the [Ocean](/wiki/Ocean "Ocean") to create an Archaic Boat.

The **Archaic Boat** can be used like a normal [Boat](/wiki/Boat "Boat"), but has more health (400 instead of 200), receives less damage (40 maximum damage instead of 70) and less fire damage (60% compared to a normal Boat). As a special feature it has a built-in container with 16 slots, which [Powder Monkeys](/wiki/Powder_Monkey "Powder Monkey") cannot steal from.
