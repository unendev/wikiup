A **Spider Gland** is a healing [item](/wiki/Item "Item") dropped by [Spiders](/wiki/Category:Spiders "Category:Spiders") when killed or a [Spilagmite](/wiki/Spilagmite "Spilagmite") when mined. It restores 8 [Health](/wiki/Health "Health") when used by the players or when used on [Mobs](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs") (including hostile enemies). Spider Glands can be used to craft [Healing Salve](/wiki/Healing_Salve "Healing Salve").

Spider Glands are flammable and turn to [Ashes](/wiki/Ashes "Ashes") when burnt by [Fire](/wiki/Fire "Fire").

## https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/dont-starve-game/images/f/f8/Reign of Giants icon.png/revision/latest?cb=20140408110712 Reign of Giants[]

In *[Reign of Giants](/wiki/Reign_of_Giants "Reign of Giants")*, [Webber](/wiki/Webber "Webber") can use 6 Spider Glands, along with 12 [Silk](/wiki/Silk "Silk") and 6 [Papyrus](/wiki/Papyrus "Papyrus"), to make [Spider Eggs](/wiki/Spider_Eggs "Spider Eggs").

## Don't Starve Together Don't Starve Together[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, Spider Glands are used to craft [Telltale Hearts](/wiki/Telltale_Heart "Telltale Heart"), that revive [Ghost Characters](/wiki/Ghost_Characters "Ghost Characters"). Spiders haunted by Ghost Characters always drop a Spider Gland when killed.

## Trivia[]

* Spider Glands were added in the *[Doorway to Adventure](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve/Version_History#Doorway_to_Adventure "Don't Starve/Version History")* update.
* Prior to the *[Moderately Friendly Update](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve/Version_History#A_Moderately_Friendly_Update "Don't Starve/Version History")*, the player could use Spider Glands to repair [Walls](/wiki/Walls "Walls").
* Looking at [WX-78](/wiki/WX-78 "WX-78")'s quote on the [Healing Salve](/wiki/Healing_Salve "Healing Salve") and [Woodie](/wiki/Woodie "Woodie")'s quote on the gland itself, it is inferred that the player heals by using the spider's poison to kill harmful bacteria on their wounds.
