The **Prestihatitator** is the tier 1 [Magic](/wiki/Magic "Magic") station used to prototype basic Magic and [Refine](/wiki/Refine "Refine") recipes. The Prestihatitator requires a [Science Machine](/wiki/Science_Machine "Science Machine") to prototype and costs 4 [Rabbits](/wiki/Rabbits "Rabbits"), 4 [Boards](/wiki/Boards "Boards"), and 1 [Top Hat](/wiki/Top_Hat "Top Hat") to craft. Prototyping an item using the Prestihatitator will increase [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity") by 15.

Presihatitator and its DLC variations can be found under the Magic Tab in Singleplayer *[Don't Starve](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve "Don't Starve")* and under the Prototypers & Stations filter in *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*.

The Prestihatitator has a 10% chance of spawning a Rabbit when used to prototype a new recipe.

## Downloadable Content[]

In *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Shipwrecked "Shipwrecked")* DLC, Prestihatitator is replaced with [Piratihatitator](/wiki/Piratihatitator "Piratihatitator"), which is crafted with a [Parrot](/wiki/Parrot "Parrot") as the live animal ingredient instead of Rabbits, which are replaced with [crabbits](/wiki/Crabbits "Crabbits") in *Shipwrecked* worlds.

In the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC, the Prestihatitator is replaced with the [Hogus Porkusator](/wiki/Hogus_Porkusator "Hogus Porkusator") due to the lack of Rabbits.

## Tips[]

* As with any other recipe that requires limited use items, any durability Top Hat can be used when crafting a Prestihatitator and will drop a full durability Top Hat when the Prestihatitator is [Hammered](/wiki/Hammer "Hammer") down.
* [Beardlings](/wiki/Beardling "Beardling") can be used instead of Rabbits to craft a Prestihatitator.
* Rabbits produced from this machine are homeless and sleep outside at night, making them easy food.
* After a [Shadow Manipulator](/wiki/Shadow_Manipulator "Shadow Manipulator") is built, the prestihatitator can be safely destroyed as all its crafts are also covered by the Shadow Manipulator.

## Don't Starve Together Skins[]

*https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/dont-starve-game/images/c/cb/Glommer.png/revision/latest?cb=20231029201751 See also: [Belongings#Prestihatitator](/wiki/Belongings#Prestihatitator "Belongings")*

Prestihatitator is one of the few craftables that are considered as bi-products of other craftables, in this case the Top Hat. Therefore [Top Hat skins](/wiki/Belongings#Top_Hat "Belongings") correspond to prestihatitator skins. Currently all Top Hat skins have a corresponding Prestihatitator skins and there are no exclusive Prestihatitator skins that are independent from their top hat counterpart. As the Prestihatitator has no Belongings entries, all Top Hat skins that are obtained from drops, bought from in-game shop or steam marketplace, redeemed from merchandise or woven using spools will automatically grant a Prestihatitator skin too.

Jiggly Prestitator cannot be obtained but is made available to everyone when [Winter's Feast](/wiki/Winter%27s_Feast "Winter's Feast") is enabled.

## Trivia[]

* The name "Prestihatitator" is a portmanteau of "prestidigitator" and "hat".
* The Prestihatitator spawning [Rabbits](/wiki/Rabbit "Rabbit") is a reference to the magic trick of pulling a rabbit from a hat.
* The Prestihatitator used to have a different recipe: 1 [Living Log](/wiki/Living_Log "Living Log"), 4 [Gold Nuggets](/wiki/Gold_Nugget "Gold Nugget"), and a [Top Hat](/wiki/Top_Hat "Top Hat").
* Winona's quote ("Why do we even have that lever?!") is a reference to *The Emperor's New Groove*.
