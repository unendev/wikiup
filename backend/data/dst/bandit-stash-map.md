The **Bandit Stash Map** is an [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC that is dropped by the [Masked Pig](/wiki/Masked_Pig "Masked Pig") upon death. If the player reads it, a red **X** appears on their [Map](/wiki/Map "Map") indicating the location of a [Secret Bandit Camp](/wiki/Secret_Bandit_Camp "Secret Bandit Camp").

Upon reaching the Bandit Camp, it must be dug up using a [Shovel](/wiki/Shovel "Shovel"). This will produce a [Peagawk Plume](/wiki/Peagawk_Plume "Peagawk Plume") and a [Chest](/wiki/Chest "Chest") which will contain rare/valuable items, such as [Food](/wiki/Food "Food"), [Gold](/wiki/Gold_Nugget "Gold Nugget"), [Armor](/wiki/Armor "Armor"), or [Oincs](/wiki/Oinc "Oinc").

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* The Bandit Stash Map acts similarly to a [Message in a Bottle](/wiki/Message_in_a_Bottle "Message in a Bottle") from the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC.
* It is impossible to read a Bandit Stash Map inside a house. If the player tries to do it, the Map will open, but no X mark will be displayed. However, when the player leaves the house, the X will be on the map.
* If the player uses [Console](/wiki/Console "Console") [Commands](/wiki/Console/Commands "Console/Commands") to spawn a Bandit Stash Map, it will not be readable by the Character, and will vanish from the [Inventory](/wiki/Inventory "Inventory") upon trying.
* The chests can be damaged/discovered if smaller parts of the [Iron Hulk](/wiki/Iron_Hulk "Iron Hulk") perform a leap attack and their AoE hits an area where the chest is hidden, even if you haven't discovered it with a Bandit Stash Map. Though, this is obviously an unreliable method of finding these chests.
