**Rainforest Turf** is a [Turf](/wiki/Turf "Turf") item exclusive to the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC, acquired by digging [Rainforest](/wiki/Rainforest "Rainforest") tiles with a [Pitchfork](/wiki/Pitchfork "Pitchfork"). Like other Turfs, it can be placed on any bare ground tile or used as fuel. Additionally, it can be used to craft [Cultivated Turf](/wiki/Cultivated_Turf "Cultivated Turf").

It spawns naturally in the [Rainforest](/wiki/Rainforest "Rainforest") [Biome](/wiki/Biome "Biome").

When placed on the [Surface World](/wiki/Surface_World "Surface World") covering a large enough area, [Toucans](/wiki/Toucan "Toucan"), [Parrots](/wiki/Parrot "Parrot") and [Kingfishers](/wiki/Kingfisher "Kingfisher") are the only Birds that will spawn and land on these turfs.

In SW, [Puddles](/wiki/Flooding "Flooding") can spawn on this turf.
