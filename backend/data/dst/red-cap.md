The **Red Cap** is a [Vegetable](/wiki/Vegetable "Vegetable") food item that can be harvested from [Red Mushrooms](/wiki/Red_Mushroom "Red Mushroom") or [Red Mushtrees](/wiki/Mushtree "Mushtree"). Eating it raw will restore 12.5 Hunger at the cost of 20 Health, making it unviable as a food source for every character except for [Wormwood](/wiki/Wormwood "Wormwood"). However, it can be used as a [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") filler without any health penalty. Red Caps can be dropped on the ground to indirectly damage [Mobs](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs") if they eat it.

**Cooked Red Cap** is a [Vegetable](/wiki/Category:Vegetables "Category:Vegetables") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") item made by [Cooking](/wiki/Cooking "Cooking") a Red Cap on a fire ([Campfire](/wiki/Campfire "Campfire"), [Fire Pit](/wiki/Fire_Pit "Fire Pit"), or a [Dwarf Star](/wiki/Star_Caller%27s_Staff "Star Caller's Staff")). Unlike its raw counterpart, the Cooked Red Cap will not deduct any health points, instead restoring 1 Health. However, it will now take away 10 Sanity instead, while restoring no hunger. The Cooked Red Cap can also be used as fuel.

## Downloadable Content[]

In the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC, Red Caps can be obtained by using the [Slot Machine](/wiki/Slot_Machine "Slot Machine").

## Don't Starve Together Don't Starve Together[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, the [Toadstool](/wiki/Toadstool "Toadstool") has a chance to drop Red Caps upon death. Red Caps are required to craft [Sunrise Lures](/wiki/Lures "Lures") and the [Red Funcap](/wiki/Red_Funcap "Red Funcap"). A [Mushroom Planter](/wiki/Mushroom_Planter "Mushroom Planter") can be used to grow Red Mushrooms by planting a Red Cap or [Red Mushroom Spores](/wiki/Mushroom_Spore "Mushroom Spore") in it.

## Tips[]

* Red Caps damaging Mobs that eat it is especially useful when dealing with [Gobblers](/wiki/Gobbler "Gobbler") as 3 Red Caps in a stack can kill one Gobbler, providing [Drumsticks](/wiki/Drumstick "Drumstick"). 13 Red Caps fed to [Beefalo](/wiki/Beefalo "Beefalo") in a row will kill it without attracting attention of the herd.
* [Wormwood](/wiki/Wormwood "Wormwood") can eat Red Caps to gain easy [Hunger](/wiki/Hunger "Hunger"), as he doesn't lose [Health](/wiki/Health "Health") when eating them.

## Trivia[]

* The Red Cap's appearance and toxicity is likely based on the [Amanita muscaria](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Amanita "wikipedia:Amanita") which contain a toxin that causes hallucinations and intoxication.
