The **Misshapen Bird** is a hostile mob exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, and introduced in the *[Eye of the Storm](/wiki/Return_of_Them#Eye_of_the_Storm "Return of Them")* beta.

Misshapen Birds will attack any player that gets close by lobbing a projectile in an arc. This projectile deals damage in a small area around where it lands. This can lead to a Misshapen Bird damaging itself and dying as a result.

They will land in flocks alongside [Moonblind Crows](/wiki/Moonblind_Crow "Moonblind Crow") inside of [Moonstorms](/wiki/Moonstorm "Moonstorm") while [Wagstaff](/wiki/Grainy_Transmission "Grainy Transmission") is containing the storm. They will slowly move toward the [Restrained Static](/wiki/Restrained_Static "Restrained Static") container and attempt to attack it if within range. Regardless of if the containment succeeds or not, all Misshapen Birds that spawned will remain and wander the area until they are killed.

Misshapen Birds can be caught using a [Trap](/wiki/Trap "Trap") or [Bird Trap](/wiki/Bird_Trap "Bird Trap"). They can then be placed into a [Birdcage](/wiki/Birdcage "Birdcage") or murdered for one [Rot](/wiki/Rot "Rot").

If placed in a Birdcage, the Misshapen Bird will drop Rot instead of [Crop Seeds](/wiki/Crop_Seeds "Crop Seeds") from being fed a raw [Fruit](/wiki/Fruit "Fruit") or [Vegetable](/wiki/Vegetable "Vegetable"), and will drop a [Rotten Egg](/wiki/Rotten_Egg "Rotten Egg") instead of an [Egg](/wiki/Egg "Egg") if fed [Meats](/wiki/Meats "Meats"). A Misshapen Bird inside a Birdcage does not sleep at night during a [Full Moon](/wiki/Moon_Cycle "Moon Cycle").
