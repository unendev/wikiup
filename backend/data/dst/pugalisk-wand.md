The **Pugalisk Wand**  is an item exclusive to the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")*  DLC. It can be crafted in the [Magic Tab](/wiki/Magic_Tab "Magic Tab") using a [Pugalisk Skull](/wiki/Pugalisk_Skull "Pugalisk Skull"), [Bone Shards](/wiki/Bone_Shards "Bone Shards"), and 2 [Nightmare Fuel](/wiki/Nightmare_Fuel "Nightmare Fuel"). When used, the Pugalisk Wand generates hypnotic rays that freeze all [Mobs](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs") hit by it, similar to one of the [Pugalisk](/wiki/Pugalisk "Pugalisk")'s attacks. The range and direction of the four projectiles can be directed using the mouse. The freezing effect of Pugalisk Wand is 5 times that of an [Ice Staff](/wiki/Ice_Staff "Ice Staff").

Unlike most held items, the Pugalisk Wand has no durability and instead rots after four minutes in a way similar to the [Ham Bat](/wiki/Ham_Bat "Ham Bat"). There is no difference in its effectiveness depending on its duration remaining, and it can be used an infinite number of times for the duration. Once it rots fully, it will return one Bone Shards.

## Prototype Tips[]

* One can bundle it in a [bundling wrap](/wiki/Bundling_Wrap "Bundling Wrap") to make it stop rotting.
