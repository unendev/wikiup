The **Ice Crystaleyezer Kit** is a craftable [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in the *[From Beyond](/wiki/From_Beyond "From Beyond")* updates. It requires 1 full [Spark Ark](/wiki/Spark_Ark "Spark Ark"), 8 [Moon Shards](/wiki/Moon_Shard "Moon Shard"), 5 [Pure Brilliance](/wiki/Pure_Brilliance "Pure Brilliance") and 3 [Moon Rock](/wiki/Moon_Rock "Moon Rock") to craft at the Brightsmithy. The kit can be deployed on the ground to create the Crystaleyezer itself.

When placing a [Deerclops Eyeball](/wiki/Deerclops_Eyeball "Deerclops Eyeball") into the empty Ice Crystaleyezer, said Ice Crystaleyezer will become active and create a small area of ice around it similar to the ice attack of [Klaus](/wiki/Klaus "Klaus")' blue [Gem Deer](/wiki/Gem_Deer "Gem Deer"). This will cause players standing within the area to [freeze](/wiki/Freezing "Freezing") immediately and to walk slowly when stepping on it. [Mobs](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs") stepping on the ice area will get cold too, making freezing them via an [Ice Staff](/wiki/Ice_Staff "Ice Staff") or [Freeze Rounds](/wiki/Slingshot_Ammo "Slingshot Ammo") easier.

An active Ice Crystaleyezer will also cool the area around it in a radius of 35[m](/wiki/Distance "Distance") (8.75 [Pitchfork](/wiki/Pitchfork "Pitchfork") tiles), having the same effect on players as [Winter](/wiki/Seasons/Winter "Seasons/Winter")'s low [temperature](/wiki/Freezing "Freezing") and decreased [spoilage](/wiki/Food "Food") rate. It will also prevent [Wildfires](/wiki/Wildfires "Wildfires") and plants from withering and will create up to 6 [Mini Glaciers](/wiki/Mini_Glacier "Mini Glacier"). [Thermal Stones](/wiki/Thermal_Stone "Thermal Stone") will be cooled by it, but at a very slow rate unless placed right next to the Crystaleyezer itself. Its icon is displayed on the map and has the same effects as an [Ocuvigil](/wiki/Ocuvigil "Ocuvigil").

The Ice Crystaleyezer can be deactivated by taking the Eyeball out of it. When hammered, it will drop an empty Spark Ark. Using a [Deconstruction Staff](/wiki/Deconstruction_Staff "Deconstruction Staff") on it will drop the full Spark Ark that was used for crafting.

## Trivia[]

* The Ice Crystaleyezer was introduced in the *[Host of Horrors](/wiki/From_Beyond#Host_of_Horrors "From Beyond")* update.
* In an early Beta version the Ice Crystaleyezer did not drop a Spark Ark on destruction.
* Its name is a portmanteau of the words 'Crystalliser' and 'Eye'.
