**Steel Wool** is an [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") obtained by killing a [Ewecus](/wiki/Ewecus "Ewecus"). It is a major crafting ingredient of the [War Saddle](/wiki/Saddle "Saddle") and the [Brush](/wiki/Brush "Brush"). It can also be burned, providing the same burn time as a [Log](/wiki/Log "Log"). In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, Steel Wool is required to adopt an [Ewelet](/wiki/Critters#Ewelet "Critters") at the [Rock Den](/wiki/Rock_Den "Rock Den").

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* Steel Wool was introduced to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")* in the *[Stuck in the Middle with Ewe](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together/Version_History#December_2nd,_2014_-_Stuck_in_the_Middle_with_Ewe "Don't Starve Together/Version History")* update. It was later added to *[Don't Starve](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve "Don't Starve")* in the *[Quality of Life](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve/Version_History "Don't Starve/Version History")* update.
* Steel Wool is considered to be part of *[A New Reign](/wiki/A_New_Reign "A New Reign")*, even though it was introduced before the official start of the beta branch.
