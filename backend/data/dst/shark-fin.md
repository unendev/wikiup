**Shark Fins** are a [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") source introduced in the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC. They are dropped by [Sea Hounds](/wiki/Sea_Hound "Sea Hound") and [Sharkittens](/wiki/Sharkitten "Sharkitten"), and also have a rare chance to be traded by the [Yaarctopus](/wiki/Yaarctopus "Yaarctopus"). Shark Fins cannot be cooked but can be used in [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") recipes, and have a food value of 0.5 [Meat](/wiki/Meats "Meats") and 1 [Fish](/wiki/Fishes "Fishes"). They will not be eaten by [Mobs](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs") that consume edible items on the ground and cannot be fed to [Wildbores](/wiki/Wildbore "Wildbore"). They also cannot be given to the Yaarctopus.
