**Puffed Potato Soufflé** is a [Warly](/wiki/Warly "Warly")-specific [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") cooked in the [Portable Crock Pot](/wiki/Portable_Crock_Pot "Portable Crock Pot"), that is exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*. It requires two [Potatoes](/wiki/Potato "Potato") and an [Egg](/wiki/Eggs "Eggs"), and takes 40 seconds to cook. The filler can not be [Inedible](/wiki/Inedible "Inedible") or [Meats](/wiki/Meats "Meats").

## Prototype Tips[]

* When comparing the minimum ingredients required to cook it to the output, this is the least efficient dish in the game, losing even more stat points over its ingredients than the joke dish [Powdercake](/wiki/Powdercake "Powdercake"). As such, even Warly players should opt for other uses for their potatoes; such as [Fancy Spiralled Tubers](/wiki/Fancy_Spiralled_Tubers "Fancy Spiralled Tubers") for similar hunger to its ingredients and some sanity, [Creamy Potato Purée](/wiki/Creamy_Potato_Pur%C3%A9e "Creamy Potato Purée") for benefits similar to a cooked potato and 33 sanity (Keep in mind Warly cannot eat cooked potatoes himself), or simply hand them to other players in a multiplayer setting as potatoes cooked over a fire have great stats.
