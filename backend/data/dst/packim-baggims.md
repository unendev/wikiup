**Packim Baggims** is a following companion in the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC. He functions similarly to [Chester](/wiki/Chester "Chester") and has almost identical stats as him, including regenerating 22.5 Health every 3 seconds. He will eat any [fish food](/wiki/Fishes "Fishes") placed inside him, so caution is advised. He is summoned with the [Fishbone](/wiki/Fishbone "Fishbone"), which may be found in any land [Biome](/wiki/Biome "Biome") on any island, except the starting island.

Packim Baggims can eat most basic fish food items, has max [hunger](/wiki/Hunger "Hunger") of 150. He loses 0.1 Hunger/second or 48 Hunger/day. This is affected by [Poison](/wiki/Poison "Poison"), and like player characters he will lose 20% less hunger, total of 38.4 Hunger/day, if he is poisoned. He will not lose health while starving. He will eat the following items if put inside him:

|  |  |  |  |  |  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Fish | Cooked Fish | Tropical Fish | Limpets | Cooked Limpets | Wobster | Dead Wobster | Cooked Wobster |
| Mussel | Cooked Mussel | Fish Morsel | Cooked Fish Morsel | Raw Fish | Fish Steak | Dead Dogfish | Dead Swordfish |

If he eats [Dogfish](/wiki/Dogfish "Dogfish") or [Swordfish](/wiki/Swordfish "Swordfish") he will spit out [Spoiled Fish](/wiki/Spoiled_Fish "Spoiled Fish"). He cannot be frozen with the [Ice Staff](/wiki/Ice_Staff "Ice Staff") and takes 3 [Sleep Darts](/wiki/Sleep_Dart "Sleep Dart") to be put to sleep. When Packim Baggims gets [poisoned](/wiki/Poison "Poison"), food inside it will spoil 200% faster.

**Fat Packim Baggims** is a special version of Packim Baggims. If Packim Baggims is fed enough fish to bring his hunger above 120 points, he will turn into the Fat version and gain 3 additional slots, similar to [Shadow Chester](/wiki/Chester#Shadow_Chester "Chester"), except one doesn't have to wait for a [Full Moon](/wiki/Full_Moon "Full Moon") for him to transform. He will revert to normal Packim Baggims if his hunger reaches 0, therefore he must be constantly fed to keep him fat. When reverting to normal version, he will drop any items stored in the three extra slots.

**Fire Packim Baggims** is a special version of Packim Baggims. By placing at least one [Obsidian](/wiki/Obsidian "Obsidian") in each slot, Packim Baggims will transform into Fire Packim Baggims, just like the transformation with Chester, except one doesn't have to wait for a Full Moon for him to transform. In this version, he will be immune to [Fire](/wiki/Fire "Fire") and will automatically attack any monsters or hostile mobs, excluding [Webber](/wiki/Webber "Webber"), with a ranged fire attack and possibly spread fire. He will also cook any placed food inside him, including fish food, which he will not eat anymore. Also any [flammable](/wiki/Category:Flammable_Objects "Category:Flammable Objects") items put inside of Packim Baggims will be turned into [Ash](/wiki/Ash "Ash") upon closing and opening it again. Fire Packim Baggims can transform from Fat Packim Baggims, and will require having Obsidian in the extra 3 slots as well.

## Prototype Tips[]

* Raw food placed inside Fire Packim Baggims will be instantly replaced by the cooked version at full freshness when his inventory is closed. Not only does this save time when cooking large batches of food, it effectively extends the spoilage time for all foods that can be cooked and negates the main disadvantage of fighting with poisoned weapons.

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* Packim seems to be inspired by the [pelican](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pelican "wikipedia:Pelican"), which have large stretchable throat pouches to catch fish and are iconic to tropical landscapes.
* Packim's name is a play on "packing bags", which is a common phrase which is said when people take a vacation.
* If multiple Fishbones exist in the world via console commands or bugs, or a Fishbone and an [Eye Bone](/wiki/Eye_Bone "Eye Bone") coexist for the same reasons, Packim will follow whichever one is in the player's inventory.
  + In the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC, Packim does not follow the [Ro Bin Gizzard Stone](/wiki/Ro_Bin_Gizzard_Stone "Ro Bin Gizzard Stone").

## Mosquito Bugs[]

* If the player travels through the [Fog](/wiki/Fog "Fog"), Packim Baggims may lose the ability to track the character and will remain where they left him. One has to go back to the spot to get him to follow the character again; dropping the Fishbone and picking it back up won't fix this.
* Sailing in a swift boat (ie. [Armored Boat](/wiki/Armored_Boat "Armored Boat") with [Sail](/wiki/Sail "Sail")) or riding on waves to accelerate travel can cause Packim Baggims to lose track of the player and they will have to retrieve him. His icon is visible on the map where he is stuck.
* When he is fed Dogfish and Swordfish, he will spit out one Spoiled Fish per stack, instead of one per item.
* If he is hit during the fire transformation, it will appear as if the transformation failed, reloading the game will solve the bug
* If you click the 'Enter Volcano' button twice, upon entering Packim Baggins will be despawned, and another one will replace him, effectively erasing Packim Baggim's inventory completely.
