A **Rotten Egg** is a [food](/wiki/Food "Food") [item](/wiki/Item "Item") produced by allowing an uncooked [Egg](/wiki/Egg "Egg") to spoil. Unlike most foods, which turn to [Rot](/wiki/Rot "Rot") when they spoil, Eggs become Rotten Eggs.

## Gathering[]

Eggs come from [Birds](/wiki/Birds "Birds") imprisoned in [Birdcages](/wiki/Birdcage "Birdcage"). Feeding the Bird any [Meat](/wiki/Meats "Meats") (besides raw [Monster Meat](/wiki/Monster_Meat "Monster Meat") except in DST) will produce an Egg. Once the Egg is allowed to spoil, it will become a Rotten Egg.

Rotten Eggs can also be found lying on the ground in [Pengull](/wiki/Pengull "Pengull") nesting areas at the end of [Winter](/wiki/Winter "Winter"), a quick and safe way to obtain many Rotten Eggs.

### Don't Starve Together Don't Starve Together[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, imprisoning a [Misshapen Bird](/wiki/Misshapen_Bird "Misshapen Bird") or a [Moonblind Crow](/wiki/Moonblind_Crow "Moonblind Crow") in a Birdcage and feeding it any Meat food will result in a Rotten Egg instead of a fresh Egg.

## Usage[]

Rotten Eggs can be used to fertilize crops or as fuel in a [Campfire](/wiki/Campfire "Campfire") or [Fire Pit](/wiki/Fire_Pit "Fire Pit"). Rotten Eggs can also be used to craft [Gunpowder](/wiki/Gunpowder "Gunpowder"); an explosive which deals 200 damage and can destroy most [structures](/wiki/Structures "Structures").

### Don't Starve Together Don't Starve Together[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, [Wormwood](/wiki/Wormwood "Wormwood") can use a Rotten Egg to restore 1 Health/sec over 6 seconds for a total of 6 Health. It can also be used on [Farm Soil Turf](/wiki/Farm_Soil_Turf "Farm Soil Turf") to add [Nutrients](/wiki/Nutrient "Nutrient") to it (+16 Compost).
