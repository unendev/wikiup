**Ocean Fish** are passive [Mobs](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs") in *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")*. A new school of fish appears every 15 to 60 seconds in the vicinity of players in the [Ocean](/wiki/Ocean "Ocean"). There are seventeen species of fish in the ocean, each with its specific location, [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") value, and [Weight](/wiki/Weight "Weight").

Fish are typically caught with an [Sea Fishing Rod](/wiki/Sea_Fishing_Rod "Sea Fishing Rod") using [Lures](/wiki/Lures "Lures") and [Floats](/wiki/Floats "Floats"), or with an [Ocean Trawler](/wiki/Ocean_Trawler_Kit "Ocean Trawler Kit"), or even a [Strident Trident](/wiki/Strident_Trident "Strident Trident").

## Fishes Food Item[]

**Runty Guppy**, **Needlenosed Squirt**, **Bitty Baitfish**, **Smolt Fry** and **Spittlefish** can be murdered to obtain one [Fish Morsel](/wiki/Fish_Morsel "Fish Morsel"), which can be cooked for one [Cooked Fish Morsel](/wiki/Cooked_Fish_Morsel "Cooked Fish Morsel"). The murder of a **Scorching Sunfish** results in an already Cooked Fish Morsel. They can be used as a [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") ingredient, counting as 0.5 [Meats](/wiki/Meats "Meats") and 0.5 [Fishes](/wiki/Fishes "Fishes"). They spoil after one day in the inventory, leaving one Fish Morsel. The only exception is the Scorching Sunfish, which only drops Cooked Fish Morsel when killed.

**Mudfish**, **Deep Bass**, **Dandy Lionfish**, **Black Catfish**, **Dappled Koi**, and **Golden Koi** can be murdered to obtain one [Raw Fish](/wiki/Raw_Fish "Raw Fish"), which can be cooked for one [Fish Steak](/wiki/Fish_Steak "Fish Steak"). They can be used as a Crock Pot ingredient and count for one Meats and one Fishes. They spoil after one day in the inventory, leaving one [Fish Meat](/wiki/Fish_Meat "Fish Meat").

**Ice Bream** can be murdered to obtain one Raw Fish, and can be cooked for one Fish Steak. It can be used as a Crock Pot ingredient and count for one Meats, one Fishes and one Ice. It spoils after one day in the inventory, leaving one Fish Meat.

**Popperfish** can be murdered or cooked to obtain [Popcorn](/wiki/Popcorn "Popcorn"). It can be used as a Crock Pot ingredient and count for one Popcorn. It spoils after one day in the inventory, leaving one Popcorn.

**Corn Cod** can be murdered to obtain one [Corn](/wiki/Corn "Corn"), and can be cooked for one Popcorn. It can be used as a Crock Pot ingredient and count for one Corn. It spoils after one day in the inventory, leaving one Corn.

The **Fallounder** and **Bloomfin Tuna** can be murdered to obtain one [Leafy Meat](/wiki/Leafy_Meat "Leafy Meat"), which can be cooked for one [Cooked Leafy Meat](/wiki/Cooked_Leafy_Meat "Cooked Leafy Meat"). They can be used as a [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") ingredient, counting as 0.5 [Meats](/wiki/Meats "Meats") and 0.5 [Fishes](/wiki/Fishes "Fishes"). They spoil after one day in the inventory, leaving one [Rot](/wiki/Rot "Rot").

All species can be placed in a [Tin Fishin' Bin](/wiki/Tin_Fishin%27_Bin "Tin Fishin' Bin") to stop spoiling.

## Sea Fishing Rod Gathering[]

Ocean Fish gather in Shoals, moving around in the Ocean. They can be fished by using a [Sea Fishing Rod](/wiki/Sea_Fishing_Rod "Sea Fishing Rod"), gathered with an [Ocean Trawler](/wiki/Ocean_Trawler_Kit "Ocean Trawler Kit"), or ejected out of water with a [Strident Trident](/wiki/Strident_Trident "Strident Trident"). Each species of Fish have different fishing behaviors and are attracted by different types of [Lures](/wiki/Lures "Lures") / Bait.

The "Heavy" column shows 70% of the max weight needed to qualify for a friendship point from the [Crabby Hermit](/wiki/Crabby_Hermit "Crabby Hermit"): min + ((max - min)\*0.7). Spawn Likelihoods are taken from the code where 4 = Very common, 2 = Common, 1 = uncommon, 0.5 = Rare, 0.25 = Very Rare.

The **Sweetish Fish** is a fish introduced in the *[Waterlogged](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together/Version_History#Waterlogged_Beta_.28since_July_26.2C_2021.29 "Don't Starve Together/Version History")* beta. they are large fish found in the Waterlogged biome. They eat berries and figs[Verification Needed]. Its name seems to be a pun on Swedish Fish, a popular candy.
