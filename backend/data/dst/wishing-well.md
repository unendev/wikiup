The **Wishing Well** is a naturally-spawning structure exclusive to the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC that can be found in some rooms of the [Ancient Pig Ruins](/wiki/Ancient_Pig_Ruins "Ancient Pig Ruins").

Players can give treasures to the Wishing Well, in exchange for a chance for getting healed and curing [Poison](/wiki/Poison "Poison"). The amount of health healed is equal to 125% of its percentage chance for successfully triggering the healing. For example, a [Gold Nugget](/wiki/Gold_Nugget "Gold Nugget") has an 80% chance to trigger healing, and will heal 100 Health when the effect occurs.

The **End's Well** can be found in a room of the Ancient Pig Ruins containing the [Aporkalypse Calendar](/wiki/Aporkalypse_Calendar "Aporkalypse Calendar") on the 5th island. While in its room, a deep, ambient humming sound can be heard. It appears to contain a whirlpool of [Nightmare Fuel](/wiki/Nightmare_Fuel "Nightmare Fuel").

The End's Well accepts a larger variety of items than a regular Wishing Well does[[1]](#cite_note-1). When a non-gem item is given to the End's Well, either a [Crawling Nightmare](/wiki/Shadow_Creature#Crawling_Horror "Shadow Creature") or a [Nightmarebeak](/wiki/Shadow_Creature#Terrorbeak "Shadow Creature") will appear and begin to attack the player. But if a non-purple gem is given to the End's Well, it has a chance to throw out one [Purple Gem](/wiki/Purple_Gem "Purple Gem"). The chance for Purple Gems is based on the item given, shown on the table.

| Item | Loots |
| --- | --- |
| Red GemBlue GemOrange GemYellow GemGreen Gem | Crawling Horror **(30%),** Terrorbeak **(20%),** Purple Gem**×1 (50%)** |
| Nightmare Fuel | Purple Gem**×1 (50%),** Purple Gem**×3 (50%)** |
| Other Tradable Items | Crawling Horror **(60%),** Terrorbeak **(40%)** |

## Prototype Tips[]

* Rather than throwing in [Tenpiece Oincs](/wiki/Oinc#Tenpiece_Oinc "Oinc"), it's much more efficient to throw in [Gold](/wiki/Gold "Gold") if not in emergency, since Gold can be purchased with Tenpiece Oincs at [Swinesbury Mineral Exchange](/wiki/Swinesbury_Mineral_Exchange "Swinesbury Mineral Exchange").
* Players can turn basic items into Purple Gems at the End's Well by giving them to the well, killing the Shadow Creatures that spawn, and then giving the Nightmare Fuel received back to the Well.
