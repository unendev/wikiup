**Abigail's Flower** is a character-specific item used by [Wendy](/wiki/Wendy "Wendy") to summon the [Ghost](/wiki/Ghost "Ghost") of her twin sister, [Abigail](/wiki/Abigail "Abigail").

In *Don't Starve*, Abigail is summoned by placing Abigail's Flower on the ground.

A mob death near the flower is necessary for Abigail to spawn. However, if Abigail "dies" and despawns into a flower and the mobs die (or Wendy kills something) around the flower after the cooldown period, Abigail will not spawn unless the flower is picked up first and *then* placed down near a death occurring.

Summoning Abigail drains [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity") by 50 points. When she "dies", she will drop the flower so Wendy can spawn her again after the cooldown period has expired. Note that one hit from the player will kill her and she can often be targeted unexpectedly by the player during battle.

The flower has three stages of blooming. Initially, it will start as a pale, closed flower. Once charged, it becomes a more reddish pink color and is in bloom. In the third stage, when ready for the blood sacrifice, it will levitate slightly above the ground. Abigail's Flower does not need to be in the player's inventory to bloom, so it may be left at the player's base in lieu of inventory space while it (re-)charges. Abigail's Flower is also visible from the map screen, making it easy to relocate should the player accidentally lose track of it. If the flower somehow falls into the ocean, it will disappear like a normal item and will re-spawn near the player.

In situations where Abigail somehow ends up following a player character other than Wendy (e.g. by completing an Adventure Mode run), Abigail will continue to follow the new character around in [Sandbox Mode](/wiki/Sandbox_Mode "Sandbox Mode") but will not drop the flower upon dying.

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, [Abigail](/wiki/Abigail "Abigail") can be summoned by using Abigail's Flower in the inventory without any other condition, and Wendy gains 15 Sanity. Wendy can also use Abigail's Flower on Abigail to desummon her and to lose 15 Sanity.

Abigail's Flower can be crafted from 1 [Mourning Glory](/wiki/Mourning_Glory "Mourning Glory") and 1 [Nightmare Fuel](/wiki/Nightmare_Fuel "Nightmare Fuel") in the [Shadow Magic Filter](/wiki/Shadow_Magic_Filter "Shadow Magic Filter") and [Survivor Items Filter](/wiki/Survivor_Items_Filter "Survivor Items Filter").

Wendy can ask Abigail to stay close to her or roam more free via Abigail's flower. In the "Sooth" state, Abigail follows Wendy and will attack Mobs that either attack Wendy or that Wendy herself attacks. In the "Rile Up" state, Abigail attacks every Mob close to Wendy, be it enemy or neutral.

If there are multiple Abigail's Flowers at the same time, they all behave the same. The flower has 4 blooming stages. The first stage is obtained when Wendy is far from Abigail's Flower, on the ground, or in other player inventory. The second, third, and fourth stages are linked to the maximum health of Abigail.

Wendy can also apply [Ghostly Elixir](/wiki/Ghostly_Elixir "Ghostly Elixir") directly to Abigail's Flower to affect Abigail's stats and powers.

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* There is a [Forge](/wiki/The_Forge "The Forge") exclusive [profile icon](/wiki/Profile_Icons#The_Forge_Profile_Icons "Profile Icons") depicting Abigail's Flower. According to the icon's flavor text, this flower is Wendy's only remaining keepsake of Abigail.
* In the animated short *Next of Kin*, a picture of Abigail's Flower can be seen pinned on a board that connects various people, places, and events to Charlie's disappearance.
* Prior to Wendy's character refresh, Abigail's Flower could be crafted for 6 [Petals](/wiki/Petals "Petals") and 1 [Nightmare Fuel](/wiki/Nightmare_Fuel "Nightmare Fuel") in *Don't Starve Together*.
* Prior to Wendy's character refresh, Abigail's Flower could turn into a normal flower or petal (depending on turf/tiles) if left on the ground for 1440 seconds (3 days).
* In the singleplayer game, Abigail's Flower appears in your inventory when you enter the caves if it isn't already present.
* Abigail's Flower can be found in *Terraria* as [a weapon](https://terraria.fandom.com/wiki/Abigail%27s_Flower) added as part of the *Terraria* x *Don't Starve Together* crossover update.
* Abigail's Flower will not reach further stages while Abigail is present, if a second flower is acquired through glitches.
