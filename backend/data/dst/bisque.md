**Bisque** is a [Fish](/wiki/Fishes "Fishes") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to the the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC. It can be cooked in a [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") by combining 3 raw [Limpets](/wiki/Limpets "Limpets") and 1 [Ice](/wiki/Ice "Ice"). It takes 20 seconds to cook and 10 [Days](/wiki/Day-Night_Cycle "Day-Night Cycle") to [spoil](/wiki/Spoil "Spoil").

When consumed, it restores 60 Health, 18.75 Hunger and 5 Sanity.

## Trivia[]

* Bisque is a smooth and creamy soup that is French in origin, and is typically made from lobster, crab, shrimp, or crayfish. Bisque is also highly seasoned.
* In single-player [Don't Starve](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve "Don't Starve"), Bisque is the only crock pot dish that requires 4 exact ingredients in its recipe (no substitution ingredients are allowed).
