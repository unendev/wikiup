**Beefalo Treats** is a [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") item exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")* that can only be fed to [Beefalos](/wiki/Beefalo "Beefalo"). It is prepared in a [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") from [Twigs](/wiki/Twigs "Twigs"), [Birchnut](/wiki/Birchnut "Birchnut") seed and [Forget-Me-Lots](/wiki/Forget-Me-Lots "Forget-Me-Lots"). It takes 40 seconds to cook and 10 [Days](/wiki/Day-Night_Cycle "Day-Night Cycle") to [Spoil](/wiki/Spoil "Spoil").

Beefalo receive 4 times the listed healing value of any food so they restore 300 health, making them the best beefalo healing item in the game. Additional notes on Beefalo healing and foods are listed under [Beefalo - Hunger (and Health)](/wiki/Beefalo#Hunger_(and_Health) "Beefalo").
