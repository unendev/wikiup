The **Saladmander** is a neutral [Mob](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")*. It is found on the [Lunar Island](/wiki/Lunar_Island "Lunar Island"), specifically the [Lunar Baths](/wiki/Lunar_Baths "Lunar Baths") biome.

## Behavior[]

Saladmanders will claim a [Hot Spring](/wiki/Hot_Spring "Hot Spring") as their own, and will occasionally fall asleep near it. It will regenerate 5 Health per second while sleeping. If another Saladmander tries to approach a claimed Hot Spring, its owner will run towards them and the two will attempt to intimidate the other into submission by hissing at each other for some time. Shortly after, the "loser" will run away while the "winner" claims the spring. If a Hot Spring has had a Bath Bomb thrown into it, a Saladmander near it will become hot enough to turn ripe shortly.

The player can speed up the ripening process by setting any heat source near the Saladmander, for example, a [Campfire](/wiki/Campfire "Campfire").

If the player has a [Thermal Stone](/wiki/Thermal_Stone "Thermal Stone") that is at least somewhat hot within their inventory, Saladmanders will hiss at each other until one leaves, and from then they will begin to follow the player until they fall asleep or the thermal stone loses its heat.

## Fighting[]

Saladmanders both Ripe and Unripe have both high health and high damage. However they are slow and have a short range. They can not be stunlocked, but can be kited with 2-3 strikes. Their propensity for sleep can be used to land a few hits. They don't assist each other, so one can attack a Saladmander even if there are other Saladmanders around without having to fear a group attack. Upon death it drops one [Leafy Meat](/wiki/Leafy_Meat "Leafy Meat") in normal form and one [Dragon Fruit](/wiki/Dragon_Fruit "Dragon Fruit") in Ripe form. Ripe Saladmanders have a flame attack that can set things within 3.5 range around them on fire, and dealing 25 fire damages to them which can't be mitigated by [Armor](/wiki/Armor "Armor"). This flame attack has cooldown of 6 seconds.

## Wormwood Filter Wormwood's Saladmander[]

If [Wormwood](/wiki/Wormwood "Wormwood") has learned the [Lunar Cultivator III](/wiki/Wormwood/Don%27t_Starve_Together#Lunar_Cultivator_III "Wormwood/Don't Starve Together") skill, he can transform a Dragon Fruit into a Saladmander at the cost of 25 HP. Wormwood can have a maximum of 2 Saladmanders at a time.

Unlike regular Saladmanders:

* They are slightly smaller.
* They have 600 Health, and regenerate 5 Health every 2.5 seconds.
* They deal 40 damage.
* They don't sleep.
* They don't turn into a ripe form.
* They cannot drop [Souls](/wiki/Soul "Soul").

The Saladmanders wander around near Wormwood and will run towards him, if they got 16m (4 [Tiles](/wiki/Tile "Tile")) away from him. They will fight if Wormwood gets attacked, is fighting a mob, or if they get attacked themselves. After 16 minutes (2 in-game days), or if killed, they transform back into a Dragon Fruit. Wormwood can transform them back with just one hit, even without a weapon.

## Trivia[]

* Saladmanders were introduced in the *[Turn of Tides](/wiki/Return_of_Them#Turn_of_Tides "Return of Them")* update.
* Saladmander is a portmanteau of "salad" and "salamander".
* As of the *Turn of Tides* update, they use [Merm](/wiki/Merm "Merm") attack sounds and [Grass Gekko](/wiki/Grass_Gekko "Grass Gekko") hurt and death sounds.
* Saladmanders can be created again via [Steeped Lunar Essence](/wiki/Lunar_Experiment "Lunar Experiment") from the [Mad Scientist Lab](https://dontstarve.fandom.com/wiki/Hallowed_Nights#Mad_Scientist_Lab). Making them renewable even if all of them were killed in the lunar island. They can be relocated and fenced in base. Putting a [Scaled Furnace](/wiki/Scaled_Furnace "Scaled Furnace") will caused them to ripen.
* When spawning Wormwood's Saladmander via [Console](/wiki/Console/Don%27t_Starve_Together_Commands "Console/Don't Starve Together Commands"), they will not be followers.
  + Trying to get a Saladmander as another Character with activated c\_freecrafting() will not work; instead the character will say the same quote as when trying to get a second pet at the [Rock Den](/wiki/Rock_Den "Rock Den").
