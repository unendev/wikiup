The **Birchnutter** is a small, round [monster](/wiki/Monster "Monster") that is spawned in groups by [Poison Birchnut Trees](/wiki/Poison_Birchnut_Tree "Poison Birchnut Tree"). They attack their target by leaping at them, dealing 5 damage on each hit. They are spawned periodically to defend the Poison Birchnut Tree. The large amount of Birchnutters, along with the Poison Birchnut Tree's dangerous roots, make it fairly challenging to cut the tree down. Their attack is avoidable however, and Birchnutters are easy to kill individually. Birchnutters drop [Twigs](/wiki/Twigs "Twigs") or [Birchnuts](/wiki/Birchnut "Birchnut"), meaning they are a good way to farm Birchnuts.

In *[Don't Starve](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve "Don't Starve")*, wearing any kind of head and body [Armor](/wiki/Armor "Armor") at the same time will prevent the player from taking damage and being stunlocked by Birchnutters.

Birchnutters, unlike [Eyeplants](/wiki/Eyeplant "Eyeplant"), can spawn from any kind of turf. They take one hit from an [Ice Staff](/wiki/Ice_Staff "Ice Staff") to be frozen, but can't be put to sleep. Instead, they go back underground when a [Pan Flute](/wiki/Pan_Flute "Pan Flute") or other similar [items](/wiki/Items "Items") are used against them.
