**Jelly Salad** is a [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Items "Items") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together"),* introduced in *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")*. It is cooked by combining 2 [Leafy Meat](/wiki/Leafy_Meat "Leafy Meat") and a [Sweetener](/wiki/Sweetener "Sweetener") value of at least 2.0. It takes 40 seconds to cook and 6 [Days](/wiki/Day-Night_Cycle "Day-Night Cycle") to [Spoil](/wiki/Spoil "Spoil").

When consumed, it restores 37.5 Hunger and 50 Sanity.

It counts as a [Meat](/wiki/Meat "Meat") item so [Wigfrid](/wiki/Wigfrid "Wigfrid") will eat it, but [Wurt](/wiki/Wurt "Wurt") will not.

## Tips[]

* This is a great food for [Wigfrid](/wiki/Wigfrid "Wigfrid") players, since it restores a large amount of [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity"), and Wigfrid cannot eat [Cooked Green Caps](/wiki/Cooked_Green_Cap "Cooked Green Cap").
* If the player has developed a Leafy Meat farm through the use of [Meat Bulb (Lureplant)](/wiki/Meat_Bulb_(Lureplant) "Meat Bulb (Lureplant)") on a boat and has access to Bee farms, the player can consistently restore their sanity easily.
* The player can use [Mushroom Planters](/wiki/Mushroom_Planter "Mushroom Planter") to farm [Green Caps](/wiki/Green_Cap "Green Cap"), which when eaten raw will drop the players sanity by 50 points at once. Combined with the 50 sanity gain from eating Jelly Salad, this allows the player to quickly manipulate their sanity at will.
