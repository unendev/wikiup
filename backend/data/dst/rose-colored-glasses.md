## Usage[]

Only Winona can wear the Rose-Colored Glasses. They can be used to "Closely Examine" objects for certain actions:

### Vine Bridge[]

Winona can use the Rose-Colored Glasses to create a temporary Vine Bridge over short gaps between coasts. When there is an opposite coast with a gap of 1-3 [tiles](/wiki/Distance "Distance"), the option "Closely Examine" will appear when hovering the mouse over the coast. Doing so will spawn a "Dark Vestige", that can be activated to spawn the bridge. The bridge will decay after 18 sec, indicated by a shaking close to the end. Standing on the bridge while it disappears will make the character [drown](/wiki/Drowning "Drowning"); before the bridge decays, the character will give a warning.

### Looting[]

Winona can also use the Rose-Colored Glasses to closely examine Charlie- or shadow-related objects to spawn a Dark Vestige. Activating the Dark Vestige will drop a [Nightmare Fuel](/wiki/Nightmare_Fuel "Nightmare Fuel") from this object. There is a cooldown of 4 minutes to activate the Dark Vestige again.

After learning the [Shadow Seeker II](/wiki/Shadow_Seeker_II "Shadow Seeker II") skill, there is a chance for [Pure Horror](/wiki/Pure_Horror "Pure Horror") to drop instead of Nightmare Fuel with a chance of 5% or 25% depending on the object:

### Wormhole and Big Slimy Pit Traveling[]

After learning the [Shadow Seeker II](/wiki/Shadow_Seeker_II "Shadow Seeker II") skill, Winona can use the Rose-Colored Glasses to closely examine [Worm Holes](/wiki/Worm_Hole "Worm Hole") or [Big Slimy Pits](/wiki/Big_Slimy_Pit "Big Slimy Pit") (holding "Alt" on the keyboard is required to force the examine option), activate the Dark Vestige and travel to any already explored Worm Hole or Big Slimy Pit on the [Map](/wiki/Map "Map").

### Fragile Rose[]

## Fragile Rose

Fragile Rose

Perk

|  |
| --- |
| Revives Winona when in her inventory. |

[Code](/wiki/Console "Console")

|  |
| --- |
| `"charlierose"` |

After learning the [Shadow Seeker II](/wiki/Shadow_Seeker_II "Shadow Seeker II") skill, Winona can use the Rose-Colored Glasses to closely examine [Flowers](/wiki/Flowers "Flowers") (holding "Alt" on the keyboard is required to force the examine option), activate Dark Vestige to turn it into a Fragile Rose and pick it up. When Winona dies with a Fragile Rose in her inventory, she will be revived next to a [Night Light](/wiki/Night_Light "Night Light") or next to the [Florid Postern](/wiki/Florid_Postern "Florid Postern") if no Night Light exists.

When the Fragile Rose is dropped on the ground, it will disappear.
