The **Silver Plate** is an item added in [The Gorge](/wiki/The_Gorge "The Gorge") event. You can put a [dish](/wiki/The_Gorge_Recipes "The Gorge Recipes") on a plate, then its cost will increase. All [dishes](/wiki/The_Gorge_Recipes "The Gorge Recipes") can be put except for dishes labeled "Soup". Used later in the game to [red mark](/wiki/Coin "Coin") and [gnaw's favor](/wiki/Coin "Coin").
