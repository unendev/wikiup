**Dogfish** are passive [Mobs](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs") found in the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC. They can be seen across the [Ocean](/wiki/Ocean "Ocean") on their own, or in abundance in [Coral Reefs](/wiki/Coral_Reef "Coral Reef"). When the player's boat approaches a Dogfish's shadow, it will surface and attempt to swim away, before retreating to the water once more. Dogfishes are only spawned at world creation time, and they do not respawn once killed, making them a limited resource. They are considered innocent creatures, and killing one adds 2 points to the player's [Naughtiness](/wiki/Krampus#Naughtiness "Krampus") level, which can cause [Krampus](/wiki/Krampus "Krampus") to appear.

When a Dogfish is killed, it becomes a **Dead Dogfish**. It can be eaten to restore 1 Health and 25 Hunger. Dead Dogfish can be [cooked](/wiki/Cooking "Cooking") in a [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") and count as 0.5 units of [Meat](/wiki/Meats "Meats") and 1 unit of [Fish](/wiki/Fishes "Fishes"). Cooking a Dead Dogfish at a [Campfire](/wiki/Campfire "Campfire"), [Fire Pit](/wiki/Fire_Pit "Fire Pit"), [Chiminea](/wiki/Chiminea "Chiminea"), or [Dwarf Star](/wiki/Star_Caller%27s_Staff "Star Caller's Staff") will yield [Fish Steak](/wiki/Fish_Steak "Fish Steak"). It can also be [dried](/wiki/Drying_Rack "Drying Rack") to get [Jerky](/wiki/Jerky "Jerky") and can be traded to [Yaarctopus](/wiki/Yaarctopus "Yaarctopus") for [Dubloons](/wiki/Dubloons "Dubloons"). When a Dead Dogfish is left to spoil, it will turn into a [Spoiled Fish](/wiki/Spoiled_Fish "Spoiled Fish"). Placing a Dead Dogfish inside normal or fat [Packim Baggims](/wiki/Packim_Baggims "Packim Baggims") will cause him to eat it and spit out a Spoiled Fish.

## Spear Hunting[]

Dogfish swim fairly fast and will avoid the player. They can be killed easily using a [Spear Gun](/wiki/Spear_Gun "Spear Gun"), a normal [Spear](/wiki/Spear "Spear") thrown by the Spear Gun deals enough damage to kill the Dogfish in a single shot for characters without reduced damage modifiers. Alternatively, they can be chased towards a shore and cornered there, allowing them to be killed with melee [Weapons](/wiki/Weapons "Weapons").

## Prototype Tips[]

* [Abigail](/wiki/Abigail "Abigail") can outrun a Dogfish, and given enough time can kill it. To reduce the time needed, this is best attempted at dusk or night.
* [Woodlegs](/wiki/Woodlegs "Woodlegs") can use [The 'Sea Legs's](/wiki/The_%27Sea_Legs%27 "The 'Sea Legs'") unlimited cannon to kill Dogfishes easily. Players should keep in mind that the cannon shot will create big [Waves](/wiki/Waves "Waves") that will cause [Wetness](/wiki/Wetness "Wetness") and damage The 'Sea Legs' if they aren't dodged.
* By using a [Trawl Net](/wiki/Trawl_Net "Trawl Net"), a player can get a renewable source of Dead Dogfish. This renders the resource renewable.

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* According to Wickerbottom's quote ("Canis Fishus"), the Dogfish is a dog-like creature (like wolves, foxes, etc) that looks like a fish and not a fish that looks like a dog.
  + It should be noted that the cartilaginous fish family [Squalidae](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Squalidae "wikipedia:Squalidae") are commonly called **dogfish**, though are not related to the ones seen in the game.
* Webber's quote for the Dead Dogfish is a reference to the movie *All Dogs Go to Heaven*.
