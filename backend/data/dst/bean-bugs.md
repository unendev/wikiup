**Bean Bugs** are a [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Items "Items") exclusive to the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC. They can be found under [Stone Slabs](/wiki/Stone_Slab "Stone Slab"). Eating them raw or cooked will restore [Hunger](/wiki/Hunger "Hunger"), but at the cost of [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity") loss.

Cooking three Bean Bugs and any [Meat](/wiki/Meats "Meats") in the [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") makes [Feijoada](/wiki/Feijoada "Feijoada").

[Webber](/wiki/Webber "Webber") and [Wilba](/wiki/Wilba "Wilba") can eat Bean Bugs without the Sanity Penalty. Wilba will turn into a [Werepig](/wiki/Werepig "Werepig") after eating two Bean Bugs.

**Cooked Bean Bugs** are a Food item made by [Cooking](/wiki/Cooking "Cooking") Bean Bugs on a [Campfire](/wiki/Campfire "Campfire"), [Fire Pit](/wiki/Fire_Pit "Fire Pit"), or other viable source of fire. Much like its counterpart the [Gummy Slug](/wiki/Gummy_Slug "Gummy Slug"), it is not a practical food item to cook and eat as it takes a significant amount of its superior spoil time away, still damages sanity and has inferior hunger restoration compared to the crock pot item that is made from Bean Bugs.
