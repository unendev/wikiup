**Marble Sculptures** are naturally spawning structures in *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[A New Reign](/wiki/A_New_Reign "A New Reign")*. They spawn in some [Set Pieces](/wiki/Set_Piece "Set Piece") that can be found anywhere in the world, alongside several [Marble Statues](/wiki/Marble_Statues "Marble Statues").

The player can mine the statues with a [Pickaxe](/wiki/Pickaxe "Pickaxe"), causing them to drop [marble](/wiki/Marble "Marble") and become deformed (Missing pieces, broken, etc.) and begin to look like [Clockwork Monsters](/wiki/Category:Clockwork_Monsters "Category:Clockwork Monsters"). [Suspicious Marbles](/wiki/Suspicious_Marble "Suspicious Marble") can be found anywhere in the world and be placed on the sculptures, repairing them.

When a [Full Moon](/wiki/Moon_Cycle "Moon Cycle") occurs, each repaired Sculpture can be mined with a [Pickaxe](/wiki/Pickaxe "Pickaxe") to drop a [Sketch](/wiki/Sketch "Sketch"), [Marble](/wiki/Marble "Marble"), and spawn its corresponding [Clockwork Bishop](/wiki/Clockwork_Bishop "Clockwork Bishop"), [Clockwork Knight](/wiki/Clockwork_Knight "Clockwork Knight"), or [Clockwork Rook](/wiki/Clockwork_Rook "Clockwork Rook"). If Full Moon ends, mining the sculpture will simply return the Suspicious Marble.

Alternatively, when a [New Moon](/wiki/Moon_Cycle "Moon Cycle") occurs, each repaired sculpture can be mined with a Pickaxe to drop a Sketch, Marble and spawn its corresponding [Shadow Piece](/wiki/Shadow_Pieces "Shadow Pieces"). Since the Sculptures are not renewable, players must turn to [Chess Pieces](/wiki/Chess_Pieces "Chess Pieces") to spawn more Shadow Pieces.

Reanimating the sculpture during either moon phase will unlock the respective [Trinkets](/wiki/Trinkets "Trinkets") to drop in [Tumbleweeds](/wiki/Tumbleweed "Tumbleweed").
