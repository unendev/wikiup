**Ice** is a [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [item](/wiki/Item "Item") in the *[Reign of Giants](/wiki/Reign_of_Giants "Reign of Giants")* DLC, obtained by mining [Mini Glaciers](/wiki/Mini_Glacier "Mini Glacier"). It can be used to craft various temperature-reducing items, added to an [Ice Cube](/wiki/Ice_Cube "Ice Cube") to repair it, eaten to reduce the player's temperature by 40 degrees for 7.5 seconds, or used to extinguish [Fire](/wiki/Fire "Fire") and smoldering objects. Ice is also used in a few [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") recipes, or can be used as a filler, much like [Twigs](/wiki/Twigs "Twigs").

Ice [melts](/wiki/Durability "Durability") in three days. It will never melt when put in an [Ice Box](/wiki/Ice_Box "Ice Box") or when the world temperature is at or below 0 degrees (typically only in [Winter](/wiki/Seasons/Winter "Seasons/Winter")) and will melt much faster when being near a fire (at a rate of 30). If the player is still holding a chunk of Ice while it melts, [Wetness](/wiki/Wetness "Wetness") will be increased by 2 points per unit of ice.

## Gift Icon Downloadable Content[]

Ice is also included in the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Shipwrecked "Shipwrecked")* DLC, as well as the craftables it is used for. Mini Glaciers are not found here; however, Ice can be obtained by [refining](/wiki/Refine "Refine") [Hail](/wiki/Hail "Hail") that can be collected in [Hurricane Season](/wiki/Hurricane_Season "Hurricane Season"). The DLC also introduces the [Ice Maker 3000](/wiki/Ice_Maker_3000 "Ice Maker 3000"), which can produce Ice. It is also used in several more Crock Pot recipes, including [Bisque](/wiki/Bisque "Bisque") and [Ceviche](/wiki/Ceviche "Ceviche").

In the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC, Ice can be bought for 1 [Oinc](/wiki/Oinc "Oinc") at [Swinesbury Fine Grocer's](/wiki/Swinesbury_Fine_Grocer%27s "Swinesbury Fine Grocer's") and is required to craft [Sprinklers](/wiki/Sprinkler "Sprinkler").

## Don't Starve Together icon Don't Starve Together[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, Ice can be used to craft [Water Balloons](/wiki/Water_Balloon "Water Balloon"), [Fish Scale-O-Matics](/wiki/Fish_Scale-O-Matic "Fish Scale-O-Matic") and [Moon Dials](/wiki/Moon_Dial "Moon Dial"). Additionally, [Snow Chester](/wiki/Snow_Chester "Snow Chester") will completely prevent Ice from melting.

## Prototype Tips[]

* Ice can be used as an edible filler ingredient in most [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") recipes, greatly increasing the bonus points of those recipes. Because Ice never spoils in an [Ice Box](/wiki/Ice_Box "Ice Box"), it is recommended to keep several stacks of Ice in an [Ice Box](/wiki/Ice_Box "Ice Box") next to Crock Pots.
* It's recommended to eat some Ice if the player needs to lower their temperature during [Summer](/wiki/Seasons/Summer "Seasons/Summer") or [Dry Season](/wiki/Seasons/Dry "Seasons/Dry").

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* When Ice melts, it gains the prefixes of "Melting" and "Melted" instead of "Stale" and "Spoiled", like other perishable foods.
