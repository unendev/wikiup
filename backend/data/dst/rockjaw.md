**Rockjaw** is a shark-like [Mob](/wiki/Mob "Mob") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")* introduced in *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")*.

Every time a fish school emerges in Rough or Swell [Ocean](/wiki/Ocean#Don't_Starve_Together "Ocean") near player, there is a 7.5% chance that a Rockjaw also appears. When killed, the Rockjaw drops 3-5 [Raw Fish](/wiki/Raw_Fish "Raw Fish"), 3-5 [Rocks](/wiki/Rocks "Rocks"), 2-3 [Flint](/wiki/Flint "Flint"), 1-3 [Barnacles](/wiki/Barnacles "Barnacles"), and occasionally a [Deep Bass](/wiki/Deep_Bass "Deep Bass") with a chance of 15%.

## Behavior[]

The Rockjaw is aggressive towards anything that isn't another Rockjaw, even flying [Birds](/wiki/Birds "Birds"). When fighting [Creatures](/wiki/Creatures "Creatures") on water, it simply swims up to it and bites it, dealing 30 damage, before kiting it by swimming away for about 6 seconds and attacking again.

When fighting creatures located on a [Boat](/wiki/Boat "Boat"), they jump on the Boat and perform a triple AoE attack before going back into the water. If a Rockjaw jumps on the boat three times without hitting a player with its attacks, it will lose interest and begin roaming again. Just like [Beefalos](/wiki/Beefalos "Beefalos") and [Bunnymen](/wiki/Bunnymen "Bunnymen"), a Rockjaw regenerates lost health over time, taking 3 days to regenerate its full health.

If a Rockjaw is attacked, Rockjaws nearby will also join the fight. It will despawn when unloaded from the player's vicinity.

## Trivia[]

* Rockjaws were introduced in the *[Troubled Waters](/wiki/Return_of_Them#Troubled_Waters "Return of Them")* update.
* Rockjaws are considered [Monsters](/wiki/Monster "Monster") and are auto-attacked by not only [Pigs](/wiki/Pigs "Pigs") or Bunnymen, but also [Gnarwails](/wiki/Gnarwail "Gnarwail").
* When Rockjaws are near the player, boat horn-like sounds can be heard.
* Before the *[Scrappy Scavengers](/wiki/Scrappy_Scavengers "Scrappy Scavengers")* update, the Rockjaw was only dropping Raw Fish. Due to the name and look of the Rockjaw, Rocks, Flint and Barnacles were added to its loot.
