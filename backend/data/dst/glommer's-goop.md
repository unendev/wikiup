**Glommer's Goop** is an [item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to the *[Reign of Giants](/wiki/Reign_of_Giants "Reign of Giants")* DLC and *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*. It is dropped by [Glommer](/wiki/Glommer "Glommer") every 2-4 [Days](/wiki/Day "Day") (or on death).

## Usage[]

Glommer's Goop can be eaten for 9.4 Hunger -50 Sanity 40 Health. Like other generic foods, Wigfrid won't eat it.

It can be used as [Fuel](/wiki/Fire#Fuel "Fire"), burning for 360 seconds in a [Fire Pit](/wiki/Fire_Pit "Fire Pit"), the same as [Boards](/wiki/Boards "Boards") or 4 [Logs](/wiki/Log "Log").

Glommer's Goop can used as a [Fertilizer](/wiki/Fertilizer "Fertilizer") for growing crops or healing [Wormwood](/wiki/Wormwood "Wormwood"). It speeds [Farm Plant](/wiki/Farm_Plant "Farm Plant") growth, just like [Manure](/wiki/Manure "Manure").

### Don't Starve Together Don't Starve Together[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, it provides 8 units of each nutrient: Compost, Growth Formula, and Manure.

## Tips[]

* [Wormwood](/wiki/Wormwood "Wormwood") uses it to fertilize himself instead of eating it, which heals him without lowering sanity or satisfying hunger.
* The sanity penalty is helpful to
  + pass an [Insanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity") [Obelisk](/wiki/Obelisk "Obelisk")
  + [Gather Nightmare Fuel](/wiki/Nightmare_Fuel#Gathering "Nightmare Fuel"), though there are many cheaper and more renewable ways to lower sanity.
  + prevent [Enlightenment](/wiki/Enlightenment "Enlightenment") in *DST* (especially while fighting [Celestial Champion](/wiki/Celestial_Champion "Celestial Champion") since it heals as much as a [Pierogi](/wiki/Pierogi "Pierogi")).
* Because a world can never gain more than one goop every 3 days (on average) and Glommer is only available on nights with a full moon, many players make a point of picking [Glommer's Flower](/wiki/Glommer%27s_Flower "Glommer's Flower") on day 11 in Don't Starve Together, bringing Glommer to base, and protecting Glommer from [Hounds](/wiki/Hound "Hound") and other threats.

## Trivia[]
