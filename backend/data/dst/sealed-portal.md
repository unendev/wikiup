The **Sealed Portal** is naturally spawning object exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")*. It can be found in the [Ancient Archive](/wiki/Ancient_Archive "Ancient Archive").

It appears to be the only device in the Ancient Archive that will not be activated once the [Archive Switch](/wiki/Archive_Switch "Archive Switch") is turned on.

## Trivia[]

* The Sealed Portal was introduced in the *[Forgotten Knowledge](/wiki/Return_of_Them#Forgotten_Knowledge "Return of Them")* update.
* As of yet, the Sealed Portal has no known function.
* Some character examines seem to imply that the Sealed Portal may be a way out of the constant.
