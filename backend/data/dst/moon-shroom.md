Moon Shrooms can be harvested from [Lunar Mushtrees](/wiki/Lunar_Mushtree "Lunar Mushtree") or [Naked Mole Bat Burrows](/wiki/Naked_Mole_Bat_Burrow "Naked Mole Bat Burrow"), both inside the [Lunar Grotto](/wiki/Lunar_Grotto "Lunar Grotto"). Eating it raw will restore 12.5 Hunger and 10 Sanity. It also applies 6.6 seconds of [Grogginess](/wiki/Grogginess "Grogginess"), slowing down the player (except [Wormwood](/wiki/Wormwood "Wormwood"), if he have learned the [Moon Shroom Cloud](/wiki/Moon_Shroom_Cloud "Moon Shroom Cloud") skill).

Mixing uncooked Moon Shrooms along with uncooked [Red](/wiki/Red_Cap "Red Cap"), [Green](/wiki/Green_Cap "Green Cap") and [Blue Caps](/wiki/Blue_Cap "Blue Cap") inside a [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") will make the [Mushy Cake](/wiki/Mushy_Cake "Mushy Cake"), which increases [sleep resistance](/wiki/Grogginess "Grogginess"). They are also used to make [Stuffed Night Cap](/wiki/Stuffed_Night_Cap "Stuffed Night Cap"), which puts enemy to sleep when they eat it.

Moon Shrooms can be used to craft the [Lunar Funcap](/wiki/Lunar_Funcap "Lunar Funcap") and [Mutated Fungal Turf](/wiki/Mutated_Fungal_Turf "Mutated Fungal Turf").
