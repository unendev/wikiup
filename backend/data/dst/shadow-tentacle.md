The **Shadow Tentacle** is a mob occasionally spawned by the [Thulecite Club](/wiki/Thulecite_Club "Thulecite Club") on hit, as well as the [Ancient Guardian](/wiki/Ancient_Guardian "Ancient Guardian") in its second phase.

When attacking with a Thulecite Club, there is aÂ 20% chance that a Shadow Tentacle will spawn from the ground, which lasts for 9 seconds. Additionally, the Ancient Guardian occasionally releases Shadow Tentacles when stunned and below half health. The Shadow Tentacle attacks any enemies within its range. Shadow Tentacles have the same stats as normal [Tentacles](/wiki/Tentacles "Tentacles"), except for a more limited attack range. Shadow Tentacles lower [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity") by 40 Sanity per minute when close just like normal ones. These Shadow Tentacles cannot be targeted.

## Don't Starve Together Don't Starve Together[]

In [Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together"), Shadow Tentacles are also spawned when [Walter](/wiki/Walter/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Walter/Don't Starve Together") hits an enemy with [Cursed Rounds](/wiki/Cursed_Rounds "Cursed Rounds") by using his [Trusty Slingshot](/wiki/Trusty_Slingshot "Trusty Slingshot"). Also, when a [Wurt](/wiki/Wurt/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Wurt/Don't Starve Together") with the [Terra Corruption](/wiki/Terra_Corruption "Terra Corruption") skill creates a mutated area with the [Dreaded Mudslinger](/wiki/Dreaded_Mudslinger "Dreaded Mudslinger"), Shadow Tentacles have a 50% chance to spawn when hitting an enemy on this area.
