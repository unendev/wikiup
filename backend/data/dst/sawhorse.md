The **Sawhorse** is a craftable [Structure](/wiki/Structure "Structure") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in the *[From Beyond](/wiki/From_Beyond "From Beyond")* updates. It can be crafted in the [Prototypers & Stations Filter](/wiki/Prototypers_%26_Stations_Filter "Prototypers & Stations Filter") with 4 [Boards](/wiki/Boards "Boards") and 4 [Flint](/wiki/Flint "Flint").

The recipe requires learning a rare [Blueprint](/wiki/Blueprint "Blueprint"), which can be obtained from [Pearl](/wiki/Crabby_Hermit "Crabby Hermit") after placing a [Replica Relic Chair](/wiki/Replica_Relic_Chair "Replica Relic Chair") on the [Hermit Island](/wiki/Hermit_Island "Hermit Island").

## Properties

### Stacks up to

N/A

The Sawhorse is a Prototyper. When standing close to it, the players are able to prototype more Structures and [Items](/wiki/Items "Items") like the [Wooden Table](/wiki/Wooden_Table "Wooden Table"), [Wooden Chair](/wiki/Wooden_Chair "Wooden Chair") and some other decorative items. It can also be used to refine 20 [Logs](/wiki/Log "Log") into 5 Boards at once.

## Properties

### Stacks up to

N/A

The Sawhorse can be upgraded with a [Moon Glass Saw Blade](/wiki/Moon_Glass_Saw_Blade "Moon Glass Saw Blade") to craft stone furniture like the [Stone Chair](/wiki/Stone_Chair "Stone Chair") and [Stone Table](/wiki/Stone_Table "Stone Table"). It can also be used to refine 15 [Rocks](/wiki/Rocks "Rocks") into 5 [Cut Stone](/wiki/Cut_Stone "Cut Stone") at once.

## Tips[]

* The Sawhorse makes a loud noise when a player is nearby, and as such it is advised to not have it in the main area of your base.

## Trivia[]

* The Sawhorse was introduced in the *[Host of Horrors](/wiki/From_Beyond#Host_of_Horrors "From Beyond")* update.
* With the *[Scrappy Scavengers](/wiki/Scrappy_Scavengers "Scrappy Scavengers")* update, the Sawhorse received the ability to turn Logs into Boards.
* In the *[Depths of Duplicity](/wiki/Depths_of_Duplicity "Depths of Duplicity")* update, the Sawhorse received the ability to turn Rocks into Cut Stone and to craft stone furniture.
