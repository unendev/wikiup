The **Powder Monkey** is a [Mob](/wiki/Mob "Mob") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")* introduced in *[The Curse of Moon Quay](/wiki/The_Curse_of_Moon_Quay "The Curse of Moon Quay")* update.

They can be found living on the [Moon Quay](/wiki/Moon_Quay "Moon Quay") where they appear during the [day](/wiki/Day-Night_Cycle#Day "Day-Night Cycle") and in [dusk](/wiki/Day-Night_Cycle#dusk "Day-Night Cycle"), spawning from [Monkey Huts](/wiki/Monkey_Hut "Monkey Hut") as well as occasionally appearing from the [Unnatural Portal](/wiki/Unnatural_Portal "Unnatural Portal"). Powder Monkeys are also encountered as part of [Pirate Raids](/wiki/Pirate_Raid "Pirate Raid").

Upon spawning, they have a 30% chance of receiving a [Pirate's Bandana](/wiki/Pirate%27s_Bandana "Pirate's Bandana"). Powder Monkeys always appear from [Monkey Huts](/wiki/Monkey_Hut "Monkey Hut") with a [Cutless](/wiki/Cutless "Cutless"). On death, a new Powder Monkey will reappear from its Hut after three in-game days.

## Brain Behavior[]

Powder Monkeys populate the area around the [Queen of Moon Quay](/wiki/Queen_of_Moon_Quay "Queen of Moon Quay") and on the [Docks](/wiki/Docks "Docks").

Powder Monkeys, if not attacked, will not harass players who have the [Right of Passage](/wiki/Queen_of_Moon_Quay#Right_of_Passage "Queen of Moon Quay") or [Wonkey](/wiki/Wonkey "Wonkey").

Powder Monkeys will use any nearby [Cannons](/wiki/Cannon "Cannon") if it is facing a boat, launching [Cannonballs](/wiki/Cannonball "Cannonball") at the player.

They have an inventory of 20 slots used for items they pick/steal. They can only pick/steal item if they have available inventory slot. Powder Monkeys considers items, including items inside players inventory, within 15[m](/wiki/Distance "Distance"). They priortise picking/stealing banana, otherwise they pick/steal the nearest items. They will knock items out of the players' inventory on sight, similarily to Frogs, but without doing any damage unless a player strikes back, causing other Powder Monkeys in a large radius to join the fight. If the item is a [Banana](/wiki/Banana "Banana"), the Monkeys will take it as a gift to the [Queen of Moon Quay](/wiki/Queen_of_Moon_Quay "Queen of Moon Quay"). Stolen items can be retrieved by killing the Powder Monkey who stole it or, after it escaped into a [Monkey Hut](/wiki/Monkey_Hut "Monkey Hut"), finding a [Pirate Stash](/wiki/Pirate_Map#Pirate_Stash "Pirate Map").

Powder Monkeys are able to empty [Chests](/wiki/Chest "Chest") within 10m, knocking the items on the floor to steal them, similarily to [Krampus](/wiki/Krampus "Krampus"). Dropped [Backpacks](/wiki/Category:Backpacks "Category:Backpacks") and [Ice Boxes](/wiki/Ice_Box "Ice Box") are not targeted.

Powder Monkey harvest banana from bush within 15m.

They wander by running 0.2-1.0 seconds, stopping for 1-5 seconds before running again.

## Prototype Tips[]

* The player should keep stacks of common items like [Cut Grass](/wiki/Cut_Grass "Cut Grass") or [Twigs](/wiki/Twigs "Twigs") in their leftmost inventory slots to prevent valuable items from getting stolen.
* Homeless Powder Monkey can be used to pick up items like logs.

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* "[Powder monkey](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Powder_monkey "wikipedia:Powder monkey")" was a term used historically for sea men (often children) that would transport gunpowder for the ship's cannons.
* At the start of The Curse of Moon Quay's Rhymes With Play stream, artist and animator May Wa jumped out from behind a poster to parody a Powder Monkey. He held a banana and a plunger in his hand, and a small piece of paper was pasted over one eye instead of a pirate eyepatch.
