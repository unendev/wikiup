**Ruined Sculptures** are naturally spawning objects exclusive to the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC. They can be found occasionally in the [Rainforest](/wiki/Rainforest "Rainforest") and [Deep Rainforest](/wiki/Deep_Rainforest "Deep Rainforest") [biomes](/wiki/Biomes "Biomes"), as well as the [Ancient Pig Ruins](/wiki/Ancient_Pig_Ruins "Ancient Pig Ruins").
There are seven different Ruined Sculptures that have different loot, including the **Eroding Totem**, the **Tempting Idol**, three types of **Crumbling Visages**, **The Blue Sow**, and **The Jeweled Truffle**.

To get the [Gold Nuggets](/wiki/Gold_Nugget "Gold Nugget") or [Lost Relics](/wiki/Lost_Relics "Lost Relics"), the sculptures have to be chiseled using a [Ball Pein Hammer](/wiki/Ball_Pein_Hammer "Ball Pein Hammer"). After a sculpture has been chiseled, it is labeled **Chiseled Stone**. At that point they can be furthered mined with a [Pickaxe](/wiki/Pickaxe "Pickaxe") for 2 [Rocks](/wiki/Rocks "Rocks") and a chance for 1 [Flint](/wiki/Flint "Flint"), 1 [Nitre](/wiki/Nitre "Nitre") or 1 [Gold Dust](/wiki/Gold_Dust "Gold Dust"). An [Ancient Spirit](/wiki/Ancient_Spirit "Ancient Spirit") can spawn when mining a Crumbling Visage. Mining a sculpture before chiseling it, will destroy the special loot.

Mining or chiseling the Ruined Sculptures found within Ancient Pig Ruins may trigger nearby [Striking Carvings](/wiki/Striking_Carving "Striking Carving").

The Blue Sow and The Jewelled Truffle are unique in that they can only be found in special rooms of the Ancient Pig Ruins, which are typically hidden behind [Suspicious Cracks](/wiki/Ancient_Pig_Ruins#Suspicious_Cracks "Ancient Pig Ruins").

## Trivia[]

* The Crumbling Visage A (smiling pig) has a string named "Crumbling Icon", but it is not used in the game.
* The Crumbling Visage B (ant), the Blue Sow, and the Jeweled Truffle don't have their own quotes. Instead, the quotes from [Boulders](/wiki/Boulder "Boulder") are used.
* After chiseling a Ruined Sculpture, turning into a Chiseled Stone, the [Characters](/wiki/Characters "Characters") will still use the initial quotes, even if many of them don't fit anymore.
