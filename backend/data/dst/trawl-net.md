The **Trawl Net** is a [Boat](/wiki/Boats "Boats") attachment from the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC. It is crafted from 3 [Rope](/wiki/Rope "Rope") and 2 [Bamboo](/wiki/Bamboo "Bamboo") and must be prototyped at the [Science Machine](/wiki/Science_Machine "Science Machine"). It is also a reward from the [Yaarctopus](/wiki/Yaarctopus "Yaarctopus") in exchange for [Bisque](/wiki/Bisque "Bisque"). Trawl Nets cannot be attached to rafts.

The Trawl Net is used for capturing small marine life and items on the water bed. It has 9 storage slots and drops upon being full. It can be detached manually before it is full, but it cannot be attached or retrieved again. The player can click on a detached Trawl Net to open it and retrieve the contents. Detached Trawl Nets cannot be retrieved, and will sink 90 seconds after being detached, dropping any items it contains once it disappears. If the player fails to collect them before the Trawl Net sinks, any living creatures will die and their dead versions will drop instead. Items caught in the Trawl Net will not stack even if they normally would in the inventory.

The Trawl Net is equipped in the same slot on a boat as [Sails](/wiki/Sails "Sails"), forcing the player to paddle the boat. A new item can be caught in the Trawl Net for every 100 units traveled in any direction. Each item caught reduces the player's movement speed by about 2.8%, up to a maximum penalty of about -25% with 9 items caught. Dragging the Trawl Net over a [Mussel](/wiki/Mussel "Mussel") spawn will yield a single mussel and consume the spawn. [Jellyfish](/wiki/Jellyfish "Jellyfish") and [Wobsters](/wiki/Wobster "Wobster") can also be caught with the Trawl Net by sailing close by them and catching them in the net. Trawl Nets are the only way to acquire [Seaweed Stalks](/wiki/Seaweed_Plant "Seaweed Plant") and Sunken Prefabs. [Bioluminescence](/wiki/Bioluminescence "Bioluminescence") can also be collected by the Trawl Net during the [Dusk](/wiki/Dusk "Dusk") and the night time. The [Quacken](/wiki/Quacken "Quacken") has a chance to spawn every time an item is collected in the deep ocean.

Additionally, items that sunk in the Ocean can be retrieved by sailing over them with an attached Trawl Net. These items will periodically generate bubbles to indicate their location. Items can only be retrieved this way if they didn't sink in the [Deep Ocean](/wiki/Ocean#Shipwrecked "Ocean"), and less than 2 days have passed since they sunk. Items that remain sunk in the ocean after 2 days, are lost forever, and cannot be retrieved. Items sunk in Deep Ocean tiles are immediately lost and cannot be retrieved.

## Prototype Tips[]

* When collecting [Seaweed Stalks](/wiki/Seaweed_Plant "Seaweed Plant"), the Seaweed itself should be harvested before sailing over it with the Trawl Net in order to save space. Otherwise, a [Seaweed](/wiki/Seaweed "Seaweed") and a Seaweed Stalk will be collected and take up 2 slots in the net.
* It is recommended to use faster boats when trawling for items. While the Trawl Net is mutually exclusive with [Sail](/wiki/Sail "Sail"), you can still increase the boat's speed with items such as [Walking Cane](/wiki/Walking_Cane "Walking Cane"), [Sleek Hat](/wiki/Sleek_Hat "Sleek Hat") as well as speed boosting consumables such as [Coffee](/wiki/Coffee "Coffee"), [Purple Groupers](/wiki/Purple_Grouper "Purple Grouper"), [Tropical Bouillabaisse](/wiki/Tropical_Bouillabaisse "Tropical Bouillabaisse"), etc.
* If summoning the Quacken quickly is the intent, players can drop common items such as twigs and grass in front of their boat then sail over them in the deep ocean to speed up the process.
* To get [Pan Flutes](/wiki/Pan_Flute "Pan Flute") or the [Trident](/wiki/Trident "Trident"), trawling in deep water during Hurricane Season or Dry Season is optimal, although there isn't too much difference in chances of getting them during Mild and Monsoon season.
* To get [Gold Nuggets](/wiki/Gold_Nugget "Gold Nugget") or [Dubloons](/wiki/Dubloons "Dubloons"), trawling in medium water during Hurricane Season is best, with deep water during Mild and Monsoon seasons close behind, and medium water in Dry Season.
* To get [Obsidian](/wiki/Obsidian "Obsidian"), trawling in shallow water during Dry Season is best.
