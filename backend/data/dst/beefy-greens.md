**Beefy Greens** is a [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Items "Items") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")* introduced in *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")*. It is cooked in a [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") by combining at least 1 [Leafy Meat](/wiki/Leafy_Meat "Leafy Meat") and a [Vegetable](/wiki/Vegetable "Vegetable") value of at least 3.0. It has a Crock Pot Priority of 25 and it takes 40 seconds to cook.

When consumed, it restores 40 Health, 75 Hunger and 5 Sanity, and takes 6 [Days](/wiki/Day-Night_Cycle "Day-Night Cycle") to [Spoil](/wiki/Spoil "Spoil").

Beefy Greens technically count as a [Meat](/wiki/Meat "Meat") item in [DST](/wiki/DST "DST"), making [Wigfrid](/wiki/Wigfrid "Wigfrid") accept it as food while [Wurt](/wiki/Wurt "Wurt") and [Beefalo](/wiki/Beefalo "Beefalo") refuse to eat it.
