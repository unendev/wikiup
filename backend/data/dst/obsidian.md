**Obsidian** is an [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") found in the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC. It is used to craft improved versions of most [Tools](/wiki/Tool "Tool") and some [Weapons](/wiki/Weapons "Weapons"). It can be acquired by destroying [Obsidian Boulders](/wiki/Obsidian_Boulder "Obsidian Boulder") on the [Volcano](/wiki/Volcano "Volcano") using explosives such as [Gunpowder](/wiki/Gunpowder "Gunpowder") or [Coconade](/wiki/Coconade "Coconade") or extinguishing a [Lava Pool](/wiki/Lava_Pool "Lava Pool") with [Ice](/wiki/Ice "Ice"). Obsidian can also be obtained by mining intact [Dragoon Eggs](/wiki/Dragoon_Egg "Dragoon Egg").

Obsidian can also be found surrounding a [Volcano Staff](/wiki/Volcano_Staff "Volcano Staff"), as a random item using a [Trawl Net](/wiki/Trawl_Net "Trawl Net") during the [Dry Season](/wiki/Dry_Season "Dry Season"), specifically in the [Shallow Ocean](/wiki/Ocean#Ocean_(Shallow) "Ocean") for the highest chance, and by rolling three gold nuggets (14.3% chance) or even three carrots with the [Slot Machine](/wiki/Slot_Machine "Slot Machine").

Most of the items that require Obsidian as an ingredient can be crafted using the [Obsidian Workbench](/wiki/Obsidian_Workbench "Obsidian Workbench") found in the Volcano.
