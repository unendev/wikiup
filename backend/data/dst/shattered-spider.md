**Shattered Spiders** are hostile mobs exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")*. They are found exclusively on the [Lunar Island](/wiki/Lunar_Island "Lunar Island") spawning from [Shattered Spider Holes](/wiki/Shattered_Spider_Hole "Shattered Spider Hole").

Just like regular [spiders](/wiki/Spiders "Spiders"), these can be caught in [traps](/wiki/Traps "Traps"), becoming an inventory item which can be "murdered" for the normal drops.

## Brain Behavior[]

They act similarly to [Spiders](/wiki/Spider "Spider"), but have a larger aggro range and an attack where they slam the ground, causing 3-7 spikes to rupture and damaging an area in front of them. They currently have identical drops to a normal Spider, despite their stony appearance.

If their attack hits a normal [Spider](/wiki/Spider "Spider") or [Spider Warrior](/wiki/Spider_Warrior "Spider Warrior"), they aggro to each other.

Just like with other Spiders, they can be befriended by [Webber](/wiki/Webber "Webber") for one [Meat](/wiki/Meat "Meat") item each.

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* Shattered Spiders were introduced in the *[Turn of Tides](/wiki/Return_of_Them#Turn_of_Tides "Return of Them")* update.
* During the [Hallowed Nights](/wiki/Hallowed_Nights "Hallowed Nights") event, Shattered Spiders can be obtained by using the [Lunar Experiment](/wiki/Lunar_Experiment "Lunar Experiment") on other [Spiders](/wiki/Spider "Spider").
