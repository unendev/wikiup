**Crab Guards** are hostile [mobs](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in the *[From Beyond](/wiki/From_Beyond "From Beyond")* updates. They spawn during the [Crab King](/wiki/Crab_King "Crab King") fight after an area of [Ice Floe](/wiki/Ice_Floe "Ice Floe") is created. Between 3 and 8 Crab Guards spawn depending on the amount of socketed [Purple Gems](/wiki/Purple_Gem "Purple Gem") on the Crab King.

Crab Guards have 200 - 840 [health](/wiki/Health "Health"), depending on the amount of socketed Purple Gems. When killed, they drop 1-2 [Meat](/wiki/Meat "Meat"), 1-2 [Rocks](/wiki/Rocks "Rocks"), and 0-2 [Flint](/wiki/Flint "Flint").

### Behavior[]

Crab Guards are hostile towards players. They deal 30 [damage](/wiki/Damage "Damage") and have a [sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity") aura of -25Sanity/min.
Crab Guards can be put to sleep with 2 [Sleep Darts](/wiki/Sleep_Dart "Sleep Dart"); this number increases with every socketed Purple Gem up to 22.

**Crab Knights** are hostile [mobs](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in the *[From Beyond](/wiki/From_Beyond "From Beyond")* updates. They spawn during the [Crab King](/wiki/Crab_King "Crab King") fight after the [health](/wiki/Health "Health") of the Crab King gets below 3000 and an area of [Ice Floe](/wiki/Ice_Floe "Ice Floe") is created.

Crab Knights have 1000 health. When killed, they drop 1-3 [Meat](/wiki/Meat "Meat"), 1-3 [Rocks](/wiki/Rocks "Rocks"), and 1-2 [Flint](/wiki/Flint "Flint").

### Behavior[]

Crab Knights are hostile towards players. They deal 80 [damage](/wiki/Damage "Damage") and have a [sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity") aura of -40Sanity/min. Crab Knights can perform a special attack, spinning quickly in one direction for a short time.

Crab Knights can be put to sleep with 12 [Sleep Darts](/wiki/Sleep_Dart "Sleep Dart"); this number increases with every socketed Purple Gem up to 32.

## Pearl's Pearl Gem Effects[]

The Crab King's fighting abilities depend on the number of gems of each color socketed into him. [Iridescent Gems](/wiki/Iridescent_Gem "Iridescent Gem") count as 1 of each color and the [Pearl's Pearl](/wiki/Pearl%27s_Pearl "Pearl's Pearl") counts as 3 of each color.
