**Stuffed Night Cap** is a [Meat](/wiki/Meats "Meats") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in the *[From Beyond](/wiki/From_Beyond "From Beyond")* updates. It is cooked in the [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") by combining 2 raw [Moon Shrooms](/wiki/Moon_Shroom "Moon Shroom"), 1 raw [Monster Meat](/wiki/Monster_Meat "Monster Meat") and a filler.

The purpose of the Stuffed Night Cap is to put [Meat](/wiki/Meats "Meats") eating [Creatures](/wiki/Creatures "Creatures") to sleep. When a creature eats a Stuffed Night Cap, it will sleep for 20 seconds unless attacked.

When consumed, it restores 18.75 Hunger, but at a cost of 20 Health and 15 Sanity. It also puts the [Character](/wiki/Character "Character") to sleep.

## Trivia[]

* Stuffed Night Cap was introduced in the *[Scrappy Scavengers](/wiki/Scrappy_Scavengers "Scrappy Scavengers")* update.
* Weirdly, or unintentionally enough, the dish does not hurt Wormwood but for Webber. The ingredients required however, can be eaten safely by both characters.
* The Stuffed Night Cap is a similar practice to Euthanasia, a medical practice to intentionally ending life to reduce pain and suffering. However, since it requires multiple dishes to kill a mob, it could also be classified as a sedative.
