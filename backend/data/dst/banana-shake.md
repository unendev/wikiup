**Banana Shake** is a [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") recipe exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")* introduced in the *[The Curse of Moon Quay](/wiki/The_Curse_of_Moon_Quay "The Curse of Moon Quay")* update. It is made from 2 [Banana](/wiki/Banana "Banana"), and 2 filler which can include [Twigs](/wiki/Twigs "Twigs").

Using [Ice](/wiki/Ice "Ice") as filler may result in [Frozen Banana Daiquiri](/wiki/Frozen_Banana_Daiquiri "Frozen Banana Daiquiri"). Using 1 Twig and 1 Ice will result in [Banana Pop](/wiki/Banana_Pop "Banana Pop").

Banana Shakes provide the same amount of [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") as the two Bananas required as ingredients, so it is primarily valuable as a [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity") dish with a decent perish time. This is particularly the case when visiting the [Ruins](/wiki/Ruins "Ruins"), an environment where Bananas are fairly abundant and sanity is drained quickly.
