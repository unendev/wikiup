**Limpet Rocks** are a naturally spawning objects in the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC. They are common in [Beach](/wiki/Beach "Beach") [biomes](/wiki/Biomes "Biomes") and yield one [Limpets](/wiki/Limpets "Limpets") each. They can be also mined for [Rocks](/wiki/Rocks "Rocks") and [Flint](/wiki/Flint "Flint"). If the player does not mine them, they will replenish themselves over 3 days, making them a good food source.

During [Hurricane Season](/wiki/Hurricane_Season "Hurricane Season"), [Seagulls](/wiki/Seagull "Seagull") will visit land and pick Limpet Rocks for food. During [Dry Season](/wiki/Dry_Season "Dry Season"), Limpet Rocks can wither, thus stopping Limpet generation. The player must then apply [Seaweed](/wiki/Seaweed "Seaweed") to the Limpet Rock for it to begin producing Limpets again during Dry Season.
