**Tillweeds** are a [Vegetable](/wiki/Vegetables "Vegetables") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")* and introduced in *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")*.

Tillweeds can be harvested from a [Tillweed](/wiki/Tillweed "Tillweed"). They are used to prepare [Tillweed Salve](/wiki/Tillweed_Salve "Tillweed Salve").
