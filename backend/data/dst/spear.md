The **Spear** is a craftable [Melee Weapon](/wiki/Weapon#Melee_weapons "Weapon"). It requires 1 [Flint](/wiki/Flint "Flint"), 1 [Rope](/wiki/Rope "Rope"), and 2 [Twigs](/wiki/Twigs "Twigs") to craft and a [Science Machine](/wiki/Science_Machine "Science Machine") to prototype. It can also be found near the walled area where the [Box Thing](/wiki/Box_Thing "Box Thing") lies.

The Spear's [Durability](/wiki/Durability "Durability") allows for 150 hits, meaning each hit will reduce it by 0.66%.

## Trivia[]

* The Spear was added in the (unnamed) [October 2nd, 2012](/wiki/New_Features#October_2.2C_2012.5B18.5D "New Features") update.
* In the old [research system](/wiki/Research_points_list "Research points list"), the Spear used to cost 100 Science Points to craft and had a value of 1-10 Science Points depending on its durability when used on a [Science Machine](/wiki/Science_Machine "Science Machine").
* An unused spear attack animation called "spearjab" would have been used for the Spear instead of the normal attack animation, if it were not removed. This animation is used by the [Fencing Sword](/wiki/Fencing_Sword "Fencing Sword") that was added later.
