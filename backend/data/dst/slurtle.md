The **Slurtle** is a neutral [Mob](/wiki/Mob "Mob") that spawns from [Slurtle Mounds](/wiki/Slurtle_Mound "Slurtle Mound") found in [Caves](/wiki/Caves "Caves"). They are small and slow, but they have high [Health](/wiki/Health "Health") and can retreat into their shells after a number of hits, which absorbs 95% of damage, so they are very difficult to kill. When killed, they drop two [Slurtle Slime](/wiki/Slurtle_Slime "Slurtle Slime") and [Broken Shell](/wiki/Broken_Shell "Broken Shell") or [Shelmet](/wiki/Shelmet "Shelmet"). Every once in a while they will drop [slime](/wiki/Slurtle_Slime "Slurtle Slime") while still alive, similar to how [Beefalo](/wiki/Beefalo "Beefalo") drop [Manure](/wiki/Manure "Manure").

They will consume any [Mineral](/wiki/Category:Minerals "Category:Minerals") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item"), except [Marble](/wiki/Marble "Marble"), when left on the ground. When there aren't any of those rocks around, and the character's inventory has them, the Slurtle will attack the character. A successful hit will knock a rock out of the character's inventory onto the ground (Similar to how [Frogs](/wiki/Frog "Frog") attack), which the Slurtle will then attempt to eat. It will also knock rocks out of a [backpack](/wiki/Backpack "Backpack") left on the ground or from a nearby chest. Upon eating a rock-type item, there is a chance that it will drop [slime](/wiki/Slurtle_Slime "Slurtle Slime"). If the player hits a Slurtle while it is chewing its food, the mineral will be dropped back onto the ground.

When consuming a mineral, a Slurtle will regenerate 10 health.

A **Snurtle** is an alternate version of a Slurtle with a different kind of shell, which absorbs 80% of damage. It actively flees from the player and will retreat into its shell instead of attacking back. Upon death, Snurtles drop two [Slurtle Slime](/wiki/Slurtle_Slime "Slurtle Slime") and sometimes a [Snurtle Shell Armor](/wiki/Snurtle_Shell_Armor "Snurtle Shell Armor") or a Broken Shell. They spawn rarely from [Slurtle Mounds](/wiki/Slurtle_Mound "Slurtle Mound").

As with Slurtles, they eat [Minerals](/wiki/Category:Minerals "Category:Minerals") except [Marble](/wiki/Marble "Marble") and can be forced out of a Slurtle Mound by hitting the mound. When [ignited](/wiki/Fire "Fire"), a Slurtle or a Snurtle will explode in a few seconds, dealing 300 damage in a small radius. Killing it this way will make it a useful bomb; however, it will not drop any items.

When consuming a mineral, a Snurtle will regenerate 10 health.

## Spear Hunting[]

Players can kite Slurtles: strike one a few times, then run in the opposite direction before it can snap. Try not to fight more than three at once, since they're slow and group easily. When fighting a single Slurtle, it's possible to hit it 2-3 times before it has a chance to strike.

Snurtles are rarer than Slurtles but have a large chance to drop Snurtle Shell Armor. Due to their hiding ability and moderately large health, they take some time to kill. For this reason, it is a good idea to fight them when no other creatures (which might join the battle) are nearby.

Like [Cave Spiders](/wiki/Cave_Spider "Cave Spider"), one of the easiest ways to kill Snurtles is to simply attack them each time they poke their head out of their shell until their health is worn down. Doing such is also more efficient than any other method - the player will do far less damage whilst they are in the shell, making it more taxing on one's weapons to get a kill. Slurtles will retreat into their shell when the player throws a [Boomerang](/wiki/Boomerang "Boomerang"), mitigating most damage, yet still consuming the 10% per throw durability on the Boomerang. Using the [Tail o' Three Cats](/wiki/Tail_o%27_Three_Cats "Tail o' Three Cats") will allow you to stunlock snurtles. Hit the snurtle as it is going into its shell and the whip crack will let you stunlock them with any other weapon, leading to an easy kill.

If the player is not interested in its loot, the fastest, easiest way to dispose of Slurtles is simply lighting them on fire by hitting them with a [Torch](/wiki/Torch "Torch") (doesn't work in single player) or a fire staff. Once lit, the Slurtles will burn, spreading the fire, until they explode.

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* The name "Slurtle" is a portmanteau of "slug" and "turtle", while "Snurtle" is a portmanteau of "snail" and "turtle."
* [Maxwell](/wiki/Maxwell_(character) "Maxwell (character)") doesn't remember creating either the Snurtle or Slurtles, and [Wickerbottom](/wiki/Wickerbottom "Wickerbottom") has never encountered them in her home world, meaning that the creature's origins are a mystery.
* Like the Slurtle, the Snurtle won't make a breaking noise (the breaking noise is when a Broken Shell is dropped) when the Shell Armor is dropped upon death.
* Woodie's quote for the Slurtles ("He's just misunderstood.") may be a reference to his curse and how he consumes wood; similar to how Slurtles (and Snurtles) consume minerals.
