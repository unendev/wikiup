**Honey Nuggets** is a [Meat](/wiki/Meats "Meats") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [item](/wiki/Item "Item") cooked in the [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") by combining 1 [Honey](/wiki/Honey "Honey") and any Meats. However, adding more than 1.5 unit of Meats will result in [Honey Ham](/wiki/Honey_Ham "Honey Ham") instead. It takes 40 seconds to cook and 15 [Days](/wiki/Day-Night_Cycle "Day-Night Cycle") to [Spoil](/wiki/Spoil "Spoil").

When consumed, it restores 20 Health, 37.5 Hunger and 5 Sanity.

Adding more than 1 [Monster Meat](/wiki/Monster_Meat "Monster Meat") will result in [Monster Lasagna](/wiki/Monster_Lasagna "Monster Lasagna"), adding 1 [Twig](/wiki/Twig "Twig") will result in [Kabobs](/wiki/Kabobs "Kabobs"), but adding any other [Inedibles](/wiki/Inedibles "Inedibles") will result in [Wet Goop](/wiki/Wet_Goop "Wet Goop").

### Don't Starve Together Don't Starve Together[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, Honey Nuggets is [Woodie](/wiki/Woodie "Woodie")'s favorite food thus it restores 15 more Hunger if consumed by him.

## Tips[]

* In most cases, replacing one of the fillers with more meat items, such as adding [Monster Meat](/wiki/Monster_Meat "Monster Meat") to a recipe using only one [Frog Legs](/wiki/Frog_Legs "Frog Legs"), will instead create [Honey Ham](/wiki/Honey_Ham "Honey Ham"), which heals significantly more Health and Hunger. However, in situations where there is a large amount of meat available, such as after a Frog Rain or immediately after a [Hound](/wiki/Hound "Hound") attack, mass-producing Honey Nuggets will allow your meat supply to last longer, at the cost of large amounts of honey.
  + If playing as Woodie, it is almost always better to cook Honey Nuggets en masse as opposed to Honey Ham; as his favorite food, it is a very efficient way for him to heal large amounts of Hunger using ingredients that are relatively easy to come across year-round.

## Trivia[]

* During the [Pig Fiesta](/wiki/Pig_Fiesta "Pig Fiesta") event in the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC, Honey Nuggets take on a more festive appearance.
* Wilson's quote can be a reference to the fact that Honey Nuggets don't have any chicken inside, because chickens don't exist in-game anymore.
