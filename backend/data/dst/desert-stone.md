**Desert Stones** are [Items](/wiki/Items "Items") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[A New Reign](/wiki/A_New_Reign "A New Reign")*. They are given by the [Antlion](/wiki/Antlion "Antlion") in exchange for [Trinkets](/wiki/Trinkets "Trinkets") and [Eggs](/wiki/Egg "Egg") and can be used to craft [The Lazy Deserter](/wiki/The_Lazy_Deserter "The Lazy Deserter").

They can also be used by players to teleport themselves to each other and will be consumed in the process.

In order to teleport using Desert Stones, one must right click on the stone whilst another player is channeling the Lazy Deserter.

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* Desert Stones were added in the *[Against the Grain](/wiki/A_New_Reign#February_2nd,_2017_-_Against_the_Grain "A New Reign")* update.
* According to [Wickerbottom](/wiki/Wickerbottom "Wickerbottom"), Desert Stones are the Antlion's [bezoars](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bezoar "wikipedia:Bezoar"), which are stones found in an animal's stomach and were traditionally believed to be able to cure poison.
* According to [Maxwell](/wiki/Maxwell "Maxwell"), Desert Stones are impure [Orange Gems](/wiki/Orange_Gem "Orange Gem"), which makes sense since [Orange Gems](/wiki/Orange_Gem "Orange Gem") themselves are also used for teleportation.
* Both Maxwell and Wickerbottom's quotes suggest that Antlion could be the source of orange gems in the constant, possibly refining stone, sand and rocks over time into the orange gems, leading to the Antlion expelling them once complete into the caves.
