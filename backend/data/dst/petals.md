**Petals** are a [Vegetable](/wiki/Vegetable "Vegetable") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") obtained by picking [Flowers](/wiki/Flower "Flower"), which are commonly found in [Grassland](/wiki/Grassland "Grassland") [Biomes](/wiki/Biomes "Biomes"). They restore 5 [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity") when picked and 1 [Health](/wiki/Health "Health") point when eaten. Like other vegetables, they can be fed to [Pigs](/wiki/Pig "Pig") to produce [Manure](/wiki/Manure "Manure"). No variant of petals may be used in a [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot").

Petals can be used to craft the [Garland](/wiki/Garland "Garland"), a [Headwear item](/wiki/Category:Hats "Category:Hats") which restores Sanity over time, the [Whirly Fan](/wiki/Whirly_Fan "Whirly Fan") which slows overheating when walking, and the [Pretty Parasol](/wiki/Pretty_Parasol "Pretty Parasol") which offers a little bit of rain protection. Petals are also used to decorate [Wendy](/wiki/Wendy "Wendy")'s [Sisturn](/wiki/Sisturn "Sisturn") to activate its +25 [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity") / min aura, and increase the regeneration rate of Abigail's health pool.

[Evil Flowers](/wiki/Flower#Evil_Flower "Flower") yield **Dark Petals** when picked, for a cost of 5 sanity. A [Tumbleweed](/wiki/Tumbleweed "Tumbleweed") has a 0.2% chance to have Dark Petals as any one of it's 3 items. In Don't Starve and https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/dont-starve-game/images/f/f8/Reign of Giants icon.png/revision/latest?cb=20140408110712, Dark Petals can be obtained by trading trinkets and meats at the [Sunken Boat](/wiki/Sunken_Boat "Sunken Boat"). A [Deconstruction Staff](/wiki/Deconstruction_Staff "Deconstruction Staff") can be used on [Nightmare Fuel](/wiki/Nightmare_Fuel "Nightmare Fuel") (presumably dropped by a shadow creature and not constructed from Dark Petals) to yield Dark Petals, but this costs 1/4 [Green Gem](/wiki/Green_Gem "Green Gem"), 1/2 a Nightmare Fuel, and 20 sanity.

Dark Petals drain 5 Sanity points when eaten (not affected by freshness). 4 can be used to craft one [Nightmare Fuel](/wiki/Nightmare_Fuel "Nightmare Fuel") at a [Prestihatitator](/wiki/Prestihatitator "Prestihatitator"). During the [Hallowed Nights](/wiki/Hallowed_Nights "Hallowed Nights") event in Don't Starve Together, one is required to craft the [Psychosis Experiment](/wiki/Psychosis_Experiment "Psychosis Experiment").

**Foliage** is a Vegetable Food Item obtained by picking a [Fern](/wiki/Fern "Fern"). Similar to Petals, Foliage can be eaten to restore 1 Health or used as fuel in a [Campfire](/wiki/Campfire "Campfire") or [Fire Pit](/wiki/Fire_Pit "Fire Pit"). They can also be crafted into a [Potted Fern](/wiki/Potted_Fern "Potted Fern").

**Succulent** is a Vegetable Food Item exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[A New Reign](/wiki/A_New_Reign "A New Reign")*. They are obtained by picking a [Succulent](/wiki/Flower#Succulent "Flower") at the [Oasis](/wiki/Set_Piece#Oasis "Set Piece") in the [Desert](/wiki/Desert "Desert") Biome and can be used to craft [Potted Succulents](/wiki/Potted_Succulent "Potted Succulent").

## Downloadable Content[]

In the *[Reign of Giants](/wiki/Reign_of_Giants "Reign of Giants")* DLC, [Tumbleweeds](/wiki/Tumbleweed "Tumbleweed") may drop Petals, Dark Petals, or Foliage when harvested.

In the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Shipwrecked "Shipwrecked")* DLC, Petals are used to craft [Tropical Parasols](/wiki/Tropical_Parasol "Tropical Parasol") and [Floral Shirts](/wiki/Floral_Shirt "Floral Shirt"). There also is a small chance to obtain them while spending [Dubloons](/wiki/Dubloons "Dubloons") at the [Slot Machine](/wiki/Slot_Machine "Slot Machine").

In the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC, Petals can be bought for 1 [Oinc](/wiki/Oinc "Oinc") at the [Curly Tails Mud Spa](/wiki/Curly_Tails_Mud_Spa "Curly Tails Mud Spa") in the [Pig City](/wiki/Pig_City "Pig City"). Foliage can be used as a [crock pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") ingredient, and counts as a [Vegetable](/wiki/Vegetable "Vegetable").

## Don't Starve Together Don't Starve Together[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, Petals can also be acquired by picking [Roses](/wiki/Flower#Rose "Flower"). They are required to craft the [Whirly Fan](/wiki/Whirly_Fan "Whirly Fan") and [Willow's Lighter](/wiki/Willow%27s_Lighter "Willow's Lighter"). [Gift Wraps](/wiki/Gift_Wrap "Gift Wrap") are available to craft during the time of the [Winter's Feast](/wiki/Winter%27s_Feast "Winter's Feast") event.

Succulents can be used to craft a [Potted Succulent](/wiki/Potted_Succulent "Potted Succulent").

[Ghost Players](/wiki/Ghost_Players "Ghost Players") have 50% of turning Petals into Evil Petals from haunting them.

## Trivia[]

 https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/dont-starve-game/images/d/d9/Foliage old.png/revision/latest?cb=20140121215810 https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/dont-starve-game/images/d/d9/Foliage old.png/revision/latest?cb=20140121215810 

Old Foliage icon.

 

* Dark Petals were added in *[A Little Rain Must Fall](/wiki/Version_History#A_Little_Rain_Must_Fall "Version History")* update.
* Foliage used to be green when it was implemented in the *[It's not a Rock!](/wiki/Version_History#It's_not_a_rock! "Version History")* update.
  + Wilson's quote for Foliage was not changed in the update and still refers them as "Some leafy greens".
    - Wendy remarks this in DST with her quote for Ferns: "Wilson calls them 'greens'... but they're purple...".
* Petals used to be required to craft a [Floral Shirt](/wiki/Floral_Shirt "Floral Shirt") in the *Reign of Giants* DLC. It was later changed to require [Cactus Flowers](/wiki/Cactus_Flower "Cactus Flower") instead.
  + However, in *Shipwrecked* worlds, Petals are used once again to replace Cactus Flowers, as they cannot be easily obtained.
* Succulents were added in the *[Against the Grain](/wiki/A_New_Reign#February_2nd,_2017_-_Against_the_Grain "A New Reign")* update.
