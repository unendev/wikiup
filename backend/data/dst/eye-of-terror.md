The **Eye of Terror** is a boss exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in the *[An Eye for An Eye](/wiki/An_Eye_for_An_Eye "An Eye for An Eye")* update.

It is spawned at the beginning of the [night](/wiki/Day-Night_Cycle#Night "Day-Night Cycle") while an active [Terrarium](/wiki/Terrarium "Terrarium") exists in the world, targeting a player and spawning near them regardless of the location of the Terrarium. If a player fails to kill the Eye of Terror during the night that it is spawned, it can be summoned again the next night with the same amount of HP remaining if the Terrarium is activated again. For each full night spent without reactivating the Terrarium, the Eye of Terror heals itself for 250 HP.

Upon death, the Eye of Terror drops 2-4 [Monster Meat](/wiki/Monster_Meat "Monster Meat"), 3-5 [Milky Whites](/wiki/Milky_Whites "Milky Whites"), an [Eye Mask](/wiki/Eye_Mask "Eye Mask"), and a [Sketch](/wiki/Sketch "Sketch") for the [Eye of Terror Chess Piece](/wiki/Chess_Pieces#Eye_of_Terror_Figure "Chess Pieces").

### Brain Behavior[]

Upon spawning, the Eye of Terror charges towards the player, dealing area of effect damage, then spawns one Egg of Terror which will hatch into a [Suspicious Peeper](/wiki/Suspicious_Peeper "Suspicious Peeper"). The Eye of Terror will repeat this pattern of attack until its HP drops below 3250, at which point it reveals its mouth and enters its second phase.

During its second phase, the Eye of Terror attacks in a pattern of charging 3-5 times consecutively, then spawning two Eggs of Terror at once instead of the previous one. The Eye of Terror also gains a slam attack that triggers when the player comes too close, creating a small sinkhole in the ground that lasts for 20 seconds, slowing down players who walk on it.

### Spear Strategy[]

The Suspicious Peepers spawned by the Eye of Terror take some time before they hatch, but killing them should always be a priority before they hatch, as they can quickly overwhelm the player.Winona's spotlight can be extremely usefull since its light tracks the player.

## Twins of Terror[]

The **Twins of Terror**, or **The Twins**, are two entities, **Retinazor** and **Spazmatism**, who spawn instead of the Eye of Terror if the Terrarium is fueled with one [Nightmare Fuel](/wiki/Nightmare_Fuel "Nightmare Fuel") before activating it. They have nearly identical attacks to the Eye of Terror but have 10,000 HP each, and both entities enter their second phase when they lose 7000 HP in total.

Unlike the Eye of Terror, the Twins do not regenerate HP if a night is skipped without spawning them. Once they have been summoned at least once, if a single night is spent without summoning them again, the Terrarium immediately enters its dormant state, and cannot be activated for another 15 days.

**Retinazor** retains the same attack patterns as the Eye of Terror, but prioritizes spawning Suspicious Peepers over charging. Its charges are slower than that of Spazmatism but its charging distance is longer. It starts with 10,000 health and it will enter its second phase when The Twins' combined health drops below 7,000 or immediately when Spazmatism is killed. In its second phase, Retinazor moves faster, gains the same sinkhole attack as the Eye of Terror, charges more often, and spawns even more Suspicious Peepers.

Upon death, Retinazor drops one [Yellow Gem](/wiki/Yellow_Gem "Yellow Gem"), 3-5 [Gears](/wiki/Gears "Gears"), 1-2 [Frazzled Wires](/wiki/Frazzled_Wires "Frazzled Wires"), and 2-3 [Electrical Doodads](/wiki/Electrical_Doodad "Electrical Doodad"). If Spazmatism has already been defeated first, Retinazor will also drop a [Shield of Terror](/wiki/Shield_of_Terror "Shield of Terror") and a [Sketch](/wiki/Sketch "Sketch") for the [Twins of Terror Chess Piece](/wiki/Chess_Pieces#Twins_of_Terror_Figure "Chess Pieces").

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* The Eye of Terror and Twins of Terror were added as a crossover event with Re-Logic's *Terraria* in the *[An Eye for An Eye](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together/Version_History#November_18,_2021_-_An_Eye_for_An_Eye "Don't Starve Together/Version History")* update.
* The Eye of Terror is referred to as the Eye of Cthulhu in *Terraria*, while the Twins of Terror in *Terraria* are just referred to as The Twins. In *Terraria*, Retinazor is spelled "Retinazer", and Suspicious Peepers are referred to as Servants of Cthulhu. The Twins are based off of the Eye of Cthulhu, with the aforementioned bosses being a mechanical and more difficult version of the Eye of Cthulhu.
* The Twins of Terror have different attacks and appearances in *Terraria*.
  + Retinazor fires lasers whilst retaining distance from the player, while Spazmatism prioritizes on charging towards the player, occasionally spewing green, cursed flames. In *Terraria*, they never summon Suspicious Peepers (Servants of Cthulhu).
  + The Twins in *Terraria* appear differently, however. Both Retinazor and Spazmatism appear similarly to the Eye of Cthulhu, with the only difference being the color of their pupils. Retinazor's pupil is red, while Spazmatism's pupil is green.
    - On their second phase, Retinazor reveals a laser on its pupil, while Spazmatism reveals its fangs, similar to Don't Starve Together's Spazmatism.
    - The Twins in *Terraria* are always connected by a long tendril, unless separated from each other far enough. This is, however, absent in *Don't Starve Together*.
    - In *Terraria*, Servants of Cthulhu (Suspicious Peepers) are only spawned by the Eye of Cthulhu (Eye of Terror) but not by the Twins (Twins of Terror).
* In *DST*, both the Eye of Terror and the Twins of Terror share the same soundtrack, which is an orchestral version of the *Terraria* soundtrack, "*Boss 1*".
  + The Twins in *Terraria*, however, has a different soundtrack ("*Boss 2*").
* The Eye of Terror and Twins of Terror's summoning quote is from *Terraria*, which is the exact message when they are about to spawn naturally.
* Wigfrid´s quote for the Eye of Terror may be a reference to an ancient Norse myth, referencing the fact that the Eye of Terror itself might be one of Odin´s very eyes.
* The Eye of Terror could also reference a [Warp Storm](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Eye_of_Terror) in the Warhammer 40K Franchise.
