**Green Caps** are [Vegetable](/wiki/Vegetable "Vegetable") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Items](/wiki/Items "Items") that can be harvested on [Green Mushrooms](/wiki/Green_Mushroom "Green Mushroom") or Green [Mushtrees](/wiki/Mushtree "Mushtree"). A raw Green Cap will take away a hefty 50 Sanity when eaten, while also restoring 12.5 Hunger. It has no effect on health. Green Caps can also be used as [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") filler with a 0.5 vegetable value.

A **Cooked Green Cap** is a Vegetable Food Item made by [Cooking](/wiki/Cooking "Cooking") a Green Cap on a fire ([Campfire](/wiki/Campfire "Campfire"), [Fire Pit](/wiki/Fire_Pit "Fire Pit"), [Dwarf Star](/wiki/Dwarf_Star "Dwarf Star"), etc.). Unlike its raw counterpart, the Cooked Green Cap will instead restore 15 Sanity and take away 1 Health when eaten, not affecting hunger at all.

## Downloadable Content[]

In the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Shipwrecked "Shipwrecked")* DLC, Green Caps can be obtained by using the [Slot Machine](/wiki/Slot_Machine "Slot Machine").

In *[Hamlet](/wiki/Hamlet "Hamlet")* Green Caps can be bought for 2 [Oincs](/wiki/Oinc "Oinc") at the [Curly Tails Mud Spa](/wiki/Curly_Tails_Mud_Spa "Curly Tails Mud Spa") in the [Pig City](/wiki/Pig_City "Pig City").

## Don't Starve Together Don't Starve Together[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, the [Toadstool](/wiki/Toadstool "Toadstool") has a chance to drop Green Cap upon death. Green Caps are required to craft [Dusky Lures](/wiki/Lures "Lures") and the [Green Funcap](/wiki/Green_Funcap "Green Funcap"). A [Mushroom Planter](/wiki/Mushroom_Planter "Mushroom Planter") can be used to grow Green Mushrooms by planting a Green Cap or Green [Mushroom Spores](/wiki/Mushroom_Spore "Mushroom Spore") in it.

## Tips[]

* Raw Green Caps can be eaten to decrease Sanity (to hunt [Shadow Creatures](/wiki/Shadow_Creature "Shadow Creature") or [Beardlings](/wiki/Beardling "Beardling"), for example).
* Having an equal positive effect to the Raw Blue Cap's negative, the Cooked Green Cap may be eaten in conjunction with a Raw Blue Cap for a resulting +19 Health, +12.5 Hunger, and 0 Sanity.

## Trivia[]

* Some real-life mushrooms, such as [psilocybin mushrooms](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Psilocybin_mushroom "wikipedia:Psilocybin mushroom"), have psycoactive properties and can cause visual and auditory hallucinations similar to those seen with low Sanity.
