**Stuffed Fish Heads** is a [Meat](/wiki/Meats "Meats") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in the *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")* updates. It can be cooked in the [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") by adding 1 [Barnacles](/wiki/Barnacles "Barnacles") and at least 1 other [Fishes](/wiki/Fishes "Fishes") with a total value of at least 1, filler can be anything. It takes 40 seconds and 3 [Days](/wiki/Day-Night_Cycle "Day-Night Cycle") to [Spoil](/wiki/Spoil "Spoil").

When consumed, it restores 20 Health and 75 Hunger. Unlike most other Crock Pot dishes, this one does not increase sanity, however, it restores more [Health](/wiki/Health "Health") and [Hunger](/wiki/Hunger "Hunger") than most of it's ingredients.

Adding any [Vegetables](/wiki/Vegetables "Vegetables") may result in [Barnacle Pita](/wiki/Barnacle_Pita "Barnacle Pita").
