**Feijoada** (pronounced /fejʒuˈadɐ/ or "feyzhu-A-da") is a [Meat](/wiki/Meat "Meat") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC. It is cooked in the [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") by using 3 [Bean Bugs](/wiki/Bean_Bugs "Bean Bugs") and a [Meat](/wiki/Meats "Meats") item. Feijoada does not require Meat if at least one of the Bean Bugs has been cooked (see Bugs section below). It takes 70 seconds to cook and 8 [Days](/wiki/Day-Night_Cycle "Day-Night Cycle") to [spoil](/wiki/Spoil "Spoil").

When consumed, it restores 20 Health, 75 Hunger and 15 Sanity.
