**Jungles** are a [Rainforest](/wiki/Rainforest "Rainforest") biome introduced in the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Shipwrecked "Shipwrecked")* DLC. They are characterized by their reddish-green [Jungle Turf](/wiki/Jungle_Turf "Jungle Turf"), various [Jungle Trees](/wiki/Jungle_Tree "Jungle Tree"), [Bamboo Patches](/wiki/Bamboo_Patch "Bamboo Patch"), [Viney Bushes](/wiki/Viney_Bush "Viney Bush"), [Flowers](/wiki/Flower "Flower"), and the occasional [Gold Vein Boulder](/wiki/Boulder#Gold_Vein "Boulder"). While [Mushrooms](/wiki/Mushrooms "Mushrooms") and Leafy [Berry Bushes](/wiki/Berry_Bush "Berry Bush") also regularly grow here, a [Regular Jungle Tree](/wiki/Tree/Regular_Jungle_Tree "Tree/Regular Jungle Tree") or a normal [Boulder](/wiki/Boulder "Boulder") may only be found rarely.

Jungles are somewhat dangerous areas during evening and night, due to the presence of [Spiders](/wiki/Spider "Spider") and [Snakes](/wiki/Snake "Snake"), which spawn from [Spider Dens](/wiki/Spider_Den "Spider Den") and [Viney Bushes](/wiki/Viney_Bush "Viney Bush"), respectively. Also, [Prime Apes](/wiki/Prime_Ape "Prime Ape") spawn from their [Prime Ape Huts](/wiki/Prime_Ape_Hut "Prime Ape Hut") in this biome. [Parrot Pirates](/wiki/Parrot_Pirate "Parrot Pirate") and regular [Parrots](/wiki/Birds "Birds") are birds typically found in Jungles. They often intersect with [Beaches](/wiki/Beach "Beach"), and sometimes [Marshs](/wiki/Marsh "Marsh"), [Meadows](/wiki/Meadow "Meadow"), [Mangrove](/wiki/Mangrove "Mangrove"), or [Volcanic](/wiki/Volcanic "Volcanic") biomes.
