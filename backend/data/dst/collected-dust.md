The **Collected Dust** is a [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")*. It can be crafted with 1 [Salt Crystals](/wiki/Salt_Crystals "Salt Crystals"), 2 [Rocks](/wiki/Rocks "Rocks") and 1 [Nitre](/wiki/Nitre "Nitre"). To craft it, a player must unlock the [Blueprint](/wiki/Blueprint "Blueprint") from a red [Fountain of Knowledge](/wiki/Fountain_of_Knowledge "Fountain of Knowledge")'s [Distilled Knowledge](/wiki/Distilled_Knowledge "Distilled Knowledge"). Additionally, each [Ancient Kiln](/wiki/Ancient_Kiln "Ancient Kiln") found in the [Ancient Archive](/wiki/Ancient_Archive "Ancient Archive") will spawn with a single Collected Dust.

It is used to cook [Amberosia](/wiki/Amberosia "Amberosia").

## PlaceholderTrivia[]

* The Collected Dust was introduced in the *[Forgotten Knowledge](/wiki/Return_of_Them#Forgotten_Knowledge "Return of Them")* update.
* Wendy's quote is likely a reference to the famous quote from the bible, referencing death and rebirth.
* Wortox' quote is a reference to [Disney's Peter Pan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Peter_Pan)'s famous quote "All it takes is faith, trust and a little bit of pixie dust."
* Woodie's quote references Maple Sugar, a common Canadian treat.
