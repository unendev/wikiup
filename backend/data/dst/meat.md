**Meat** is a [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [item](/wiki/Item "Item") dropped by [numerous fleshy creatures](#Drop_Table) ([Pigs](/wiki/Pig "Pig"), [Beefalo](/wiki/Beefalo "Beefalo"), [Deerclops](/wiki/Deerclops "Deerclops") etc.) It can be used to make the [Ham Bat](/wiki/Ham_Bat "Ham Bat"), or as an ingredient in multiple [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") [Recipes](/wiki/Crock_Pot#Crock_Pot_Dishes "Crock Pot"). It also can be dried on a [Drying Rack](/wiki/Drying_Rack "Drying Rack") for 2 days to make [Jerky](/wiki/Jerky "Jerky"). Its less-filling alternative is a [morsel](/wiki/Morsel "Morsel"), which only provides 12.5 [hunger](/wiki/Hunger "Hunger").

*See [general meat uses](/wiki/Meats#Usage "Meats").*

**Cooked Meat** is Meat that has been [cooked](/wiki/Cooking "Cooking") on a [Campfire](/wiki/Campfire "Campfire"), [Fire Pit](/wiki/Fire_Pit "Fire Pit"), or [Star](/wiki/Star_Caller%27s_Staff "Star Caller's Staff"). It can also be used in multiple Crock Pot [recipes](/wiki/Crock_Pot_Recipes "Crock Pot Recipes"). [Mobs](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs") that ordinarily drop Meat and die from being set on [fire](/wiki/Fire "Fire") will drop Cooked Meat instead. Cooked Meat is required to make [Meat Effigies](/wiki/Meat_Effigies "Meat Effigies").
