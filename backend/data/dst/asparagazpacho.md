**Asparagazpacho** is a [Warly](/wiki/Warly "Warly")-specific [food](/wiki/Food "Food") item cooked in the [Portable Crock Pot](/wiki/Portable_Crock_Pot "Portable Crock Pot"), exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*. It requires two [Asparagus](/wiki/Asparagus "Asparagus") and two [Ice](/wiki/Ice "Ice") or [Ice Bream](/wiki/Ice_Bream "Ice Bream") to cook.

When consumed, it restores 25 Hunger, 3 Health and 10 Sanity, and decreases the [Character's](/wiki/Character "Character") body temperature by up to 20° below ambient for 5 minutes.

Asparagazpacho is the only food item which can provide [Overheating](/wiki/Overheating "Overheating") protection comparable to 240 insulation (starting at 35°).
