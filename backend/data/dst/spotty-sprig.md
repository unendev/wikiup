The **Spotty Sprig** is an item exclusive to [The Gorge](/wiki/The_Gorge "The Gorge") event in *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*. It can be obtained by picking a [Spotty Shrub](/wiki/Spotty_Shrub "Spotty Shrub") in the Sugarwood Memorial Park area for one Sprig per Shrub. 3 Spotty Sprigs are used to make **Spot Spice** which can be ground up at the [Mealing Stone](/wiki/Mealing_Stone "Mealing Stone"). Spot Spice can be used in a variety of [Cooking Recipes](/wiki/The_Gorge_Recipes "The Gorge Recipes") exclusive to The Gorge.
