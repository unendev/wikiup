**Hard Shell Tacos** are a [Vegetable](/wiki/Vegetable "Vegetable") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC. They can be cooked in a [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") by combining exactly two [Weevole Carapaces](/wiki/Weevole_Carapace "Weevole Carapace") and one [Vegetable](/wiki/Vegetable "Vegetable"). They take 20 seconds too cook and 15 [Days](/wiki/Day-Night_Cycle "Day-Night Cycle") to [spoil](/wiki/Spoil "Spoil").

When consumed, they restore 20 Health, 37.5 Hunger and 5 Sanity.

Hard Shell Tacos are counted as a vegetable dish, therefore [Wigfrid](/wiki/Wigfrid "Wigfrid") cannot eat them.
