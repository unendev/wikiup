**Driftwood Pieces** are [Items](/wiki/Items "Items") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")*.

They can be found floating in the [Ocean](/wiki/Ocean "Ocean") where they spawn [regularly](/wiki/Ocean_Debris "Ocean Debris"). They are common in the [Rocky Beach](/wiki/Rocky_Beach "Rocky Beach") [Biome](/wiki/Biomes "Biomes") on the [Lunar Island](/wiki/Lunar_Island "Lunar Island"). Driftwood Pieces can also be obtained by chopping [Driftwood](/wiki/Driftwood "Driftwood") and [Great Tree Trunk](/wiki/Great_Tree_Trunk "Great Tree Trunk") roots.

Driftwood Pieces are required to craft [Driftwood Oars](/wiki/Driftwood_Oar "Driftwood Oar"). They can also be used to repair [Boats](/wiki/Boat_Kit "Boat Kit") and as [Fuel](/wiki/Fuel "Fuel") for [Fire](/wiki/Fire "Fire").
