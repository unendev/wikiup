**Snakes** are hostile [Mobs](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs") found in the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC. They may drop [Monster Meat](/wiki/Monster_Meat "Monster Meat"), [Snakeskin](/wiki/Snakeskin "Snakeskin"), or [Snake Oil](/wiki/Snake_Oil "Snake Oil"). There are two different types of Snakes, distinguished by the color of their skin.

Regular Snakes spawn from chopping down a [Jungle Tree](/wiki/Tree/Jungle "Tree/Jungle"), or from [Hacking](/wiki/Machete "Machete") or being in the proximity of Snake Den [Viney Bushes](/wiki/Viney_Bush "Viney Bush"). Snake-infested Viney Bushes will make hissing sounds and periodically shake, allowing them to be distinguished from normal Viney Bushes. Snakes may hide in [Berry Bushes](/wiki/Berry_Bush "Berry Bush") as well. Spending [Dubloons](/wiki/Dubloons "Dubloons") at the [Slot Machine](/wiki/Slot_Machine "Slot Machine") or digging up a [X Marks the Spot](/wiki/X_Marks_the_Spot "X Marks the Spot") treasure chest with a [Shovel](/wiki/Shovel "Shovel") has a chance to spawn three Snakes. Additionally, digging up a [Sandy Pile](/wiki/Sandy_Pile "Sandy Pile") has a chance to spawn a Snake.

The **Poison Snake** is a more dangerous type of Snake. If a Poison Snake attacks the player, they will be afflicted with [Poison](/wiki/Poison "Poison"). Once a player is poisoned, they will continue to be so unless cured with [Anti Venom](/wiki/Anti_Venom "Anti Venom") or a [Venom Gland](/wiki/Venom_Gland "Venom Gland"). The player will lose roughly three hit points every 1.5 seconds. When killed, in addition to dropping Monster Meat, Snakeskin, and Snake Oil, they have a chance to drop a Venom Gland. Spending Dubloons at the Slot Machine has a chance to spawn two Poison Snakes.

## Behavior[]

Snakes are nocturnal creatures, and unless they are provoked by chopping down a Jungle Tree, or walking by or hacking a Viney Bush, they will not be active until [Dusk](/wiki/Dusk "Dusk") and throughout the [Night](/wiki/Night "Night"). When the next day begins, the Snakes that spawned from a Viney Bush will return there. If any Snakes are attacked, all other Snakes in the vicinity will join in the battle to attack their aggressor. If the player runs far enough away, the Snakes will give up, and fall asleep until another creature or the player walks near. Snakes will actively pick fights with most creatures, hostile or not.

Normal Snakes will sometimes try to eat [Meat](/wiki/Meats "Meats") and [Eggs](/wiki/Eggs "Eggs") that are left on the ground. Poison Snakes can smell Meat left on the ground from a far distance.

## Fighting Strategy[]

* Both types of Snakes may be kited by dodging their attack and then attacking them 3 times which kills them before they can hit the player, as long as a weapon at least as strong as a [Spear](/wiki/Spear "Spear") is used.
* Wearing a [Seashell Suit](/wiki/Seashell_Suit "Seashell Suit") will prevent the player from getting poisoned.
* Snakes can be hit once before they attack and still miss their target. This is very useful for dealing with groups of Snakes.
* Snakes are very aggressive and attack most other Mobs, including Spiders. Players can farm Snakeskin and Venom Glands by placing a [Spider Den](/wiki/Spider_Den "Spider Den") near a Snake-infested Viney Bush.
* Snakes can be trapped with a [Trap](/wiki/Trap "Trap").

## Trivia[]

* Red Snakes resemble either [Mud Snakes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mud_snake "wikipedia:Mud snake") or [Milk Snakes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Milk_snake "wikipedia:Milk snake"), while the Poison variant resembles [Banded Krait Snakes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Banded_krait "wikipedia:Banded krait") or [Mangrove Snakes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Boiga_dendrophila "wikipedia:Boiga dendrophila"), both of which are venomous.
