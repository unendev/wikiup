The **Warren Wreath** is a wearable [item](/wiki/Item "Item") for the head slot, exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in the *[From Beyond](/wiki/From_Beyond "From Beyond")* updates. It can only be crafted when standing close to the [Benevolent Rabbit King](/wiki/Benevolent_Rabbit_King "Benevolent Rabbit King") and requires 6 [Cut Grass](/wiki/Cut_Grass "Cut Grass") and a [Rabbit](/wiki/Rabbit "Rabbit") to craft.

## Usage[]

Similar to a [Garland](/wiki/Garland "Garland"), wearing a Warren Wreath will provide 1.33 Sanity/min. It will perish after 5 days when the Rabbit dies, turning into a [Morsel](/wiki/Morsel "Morsel"). Before it perishes, it can be refreshed to max by feeding it a [vegetable](/wiki/Vegetable "Vegetable") or [fruit](/wiki/Fruit "Fruit") (similar to any creature in the inventory). If the player's [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity") is low, the Rabbit will turn into a [Beardling](/wiki/Beardling "Beardling"). However, this has no effect.

While wearing a Warren Wreath, Rabbits will not run away, and can be safely picked up and added to the inventory.

## Tips[]

* The Warren Wreath is easy to maintain by simply feeding it [Petals](/wiki/Petals "Petals") or [Foliage](/wiki/Foliage "Foliage") every 5 days.

## Trivia[]

* The Warren Wreath was added in the *[Depths of Duplicity](/wiki/Depths_of_Duplicity "Depths of Duplicity")* update.
* When on the ground, the Rabbit in the Warren Wreath can be put to sleep. However, this does not have any purpose.
* Also when on the ground, the Rabbit will move around and look around if approached.
