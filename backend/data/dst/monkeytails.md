**Monkeytails** are harvestable [Plants](/wiki/Plants "Plants") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[The Curse of Moon Quay](/wiki/The_Curse_of_Moon_Quay "The Curse of Moon Quay")* update. They can be found growing natively on [Moon Quay](/wiki/Moon_Quay "Moon Quay"). When harvested they produce 1 [Cut Reeds](/wiki/Cut_Reeds "Cut Reeds"). The plant can be harvested again after three days.

Unlike [Reeds](/wiki/Reeds "Reeds"), they can be dug up with a shovel and transplanted to another place. The transplanted bush can be harvested 5-7 times before it must be [fertilized](/wiki/Fertilizer "Fertilizer") to grow again. There is a small chance to receive new Monkeytails in their item form from the [Unnatural Portal](/wiki/Unnatural_Portal "Unnatural Portal").

Monkeytails can also be used as [fuel](/wiki/Fuel "Fuel").

### Insight Skill Crafting[]

[Wormwood](/wiki/Wormwood "Wormwood") can craft Monkeytails with 1 [Cave Banana](/wiki/Cave_Banana "Cave Banana"), 4 [Cut Reeds](/wiki/Cut_Reeds "Cut Reeds"), and 15 Health after learning the [Monkeytail Crafting](/wiki/Monkeytail_Crafting "Monkeytail Crafting") skill.
