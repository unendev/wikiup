**Asparagus** is a [Vegetable](/wiki/Vegetable "Vegetable") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") introduced in the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC. It can both be found growing naturally in the world and be obtained through [Farming](/wiki/Farm "Farm"). Asparagus restores 12.5 [Hunger](/wiki/Hunger "Hunger") when eaten raw, compared to 25 [Hunger](/wiki/Hunger "Hunger") eaten cooked, which makes it a much better item cooked than raw (as both restore 3 health anyway). The only disadvantage is that the spoilage time is cut in half after being cooked, which makes it advisable to cook asparagus when around halfway through its perishing time to preserve the beneficial properties of cooked asparagus for as long as possible.

## Don't Starve Together icon Don't Starve Together[]

Asparagus is also present in *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")* where it can only be obtained through [Farming](/wiki/Farm "Farm"). Planting a Tubular Seed will produce Asparagus. [Warly](/wiki/Warly "Warly") requires Asparagus to cook [Asparagazpacho](/wiki/Asparagazpacho "Asparagazpacho") in his [Portable Crock Pot](/wiki/Portable_Crock_Pot "Portable Crock Pot").
