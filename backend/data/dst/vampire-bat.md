The **Vampire Bat** is a [Mob](/wiki/Mob "Mob") exclusive to the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC. Vampire Bats are essentially the *Hamlet* version of [Hounds](/wiki/Hound "Hound"): They will attack the player periodically and will come in waves where their numbers will increase the more days the player has lived. They have 130 [health](/wiki/Health "Health") and can be kited in the same way as Hounds. When killed, they have a chance to drop [Monster Meat](/wiki/Monster_Meat "Monster Meat"), a [Pig Skin?](/wiki/Pig_Skin#Downloadable_Content "Pig Skin") and a [Batilisk Wing?](/wiki/Batilisk_Wing "Batilisk Wing").

Apart from their periodic attacks, they can also be found sleeping in their den, a [Cave Cleft](/wiki/Cave_Cleft "Cave Cleft"). The Vampire Bat will seemingly stay asleep until something violent wakes it up, as a player's attack or an earthquake.

## Prototype Tips[]

* If the player enters a building when a Vampire Bat attack is about to happen, the Vampire Bats won't spawn until the player exits the room.
* If the player waits in the building long enough the attack will be dismissed completely
* An easy way to deal with Vampire Bats in the early days is to simply lead them to [Royal Guards](/wiki/Royal_Guard "Royal Guard"). However, caution must be taken to ensure that the Royal Guards don't burn down the environment, as they wield [Torch](/wiki/Torch "Torch") at [Dusk](/wiki/Dusk "Dusk") and [Night](/wiki/Night "Night").
  + As a bonus, the player can steal from nearby farms while the Royal Guards are busy fending off the Vampire Bats.

* Take caution in [Humid Season](/wiki/Humid_Season "Humid Season"), as the fog greatly reduces the player's visibility, making hitting hard.
