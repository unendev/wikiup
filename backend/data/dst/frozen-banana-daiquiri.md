**Frozen Banana Daiquiri** is a [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") recipe exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")* introduced in the *[An Eye for An Eye](/wiki/An_Eye_for_An_Eye "An Eye for An Eye")* update. It is made from 1 [Banana](/wiki/Cave_Banana "Cave Banana"), 1 [Ice](/wiki/Ice "Ice"), and 2 filler.

Using [Twigs](/wiki/Twigs "Twigs") as filler will result in a [Banana Pop](/wiki/Banana_Pop "Banana Pop"). Adding any additional Bananas may result in [Banana Shake](/wiki/Banana_Shake "Banana Shake"). Adding more than 1 [Durian](/wiki/Durian "Durian") will result in [Monster Lasagna](/wiki/Monster_Lasagna "Monster Lasagna").

This item is classified under "Goodies," meaning that [Wigfrid](/wiki/Wigfrid "Wigfrid") is able to consume it. However, a [Beefalo](/wiki/Beefalo "Beefalo") will not.
