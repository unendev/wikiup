The **Crabby Hermit**, or **Pearl**, is a [Mob](/wiki/Mob "Mob") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")*. She can be found on the [Hermit Island](/wiki/Hermit_Island "Hermit Island").

Becoming friends with the Crabby Hermit is the only way to acquire [Pearl's Pearl](/wiki/Pearl%27s_Pearl "Pearl's Pearl") which is a critical part of the long chain of events necessary to summon the [Celestial Champion](/wiki/Celestial_Champion "Celestial Champion"). Players help the Crabby Hermit in various ways to increase friendship. Each time her friendship level increases, she drops Bundles of Thanks (see below) or offers increased access to the [Bottle Exchange Filter](/wiki/Bottle_Exchange_Filter "Bottle Exchange Filter"). She is immune to all damage.

## Behavior[]

The Crabby Hermit goes out of the [Hermit Home](/wiki/Hermit_Home "Hermit Home") during the day. She roams her island fishing, harvesting, and offering crabby comments. As friendship increases, her tone softens. In worlds where daytime is turned off, she will roam her island regardless of the time of day.

## Hermit Drying Rack Friendship[]

Completing various acts of service for the Crabby Hermit makes her more friendly. Friendship level 1 grants access to the [Bottle Exchange Filter](/wiki/Bottle_Exchange_Filter "Bottle Exchange Filter") when a player clicks on the Crabby Hermit. New crafting recipes are unlocked at friendship levels 3, 6, 8, and 9. She drops a [Bundle of Thanks](#Bundle_of_Thanks) at other levels. She also drops a Bundle of Thanks when certain tasks are repeated, though repeating a task does not advance her friendship level. When all three of the [Hermit Home](/wiki/Hermit_Home "Hermit Home") upgrades are completed, plus any 7 of the other tasks below, she will award the player with [Pearl's Pearl](/wiki/Pearl%27s_Pearl "Pearl's Pearl").

Some tasks can be repeated for extra Bundles of Thanks (and duplicate adverts), but friendship points are only granted the first time a task is completed. Friendship points remain even if players take back their gifts after the point is earned.

Here are all the ways to earn friendship points:

* Tasks completed on (or around) [Hermit Island](/wiki/Hermit_Island "Hermit Island"):
  + Upgrading the [Hermit Home](/wiki/Hermit_Home "Hermit Home"). Three upgrades are available for a total of 3 points.
  + [Drying](/wiki/Drying_Rack "Drying Rack") 6 [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") items simultaneously. The [Bull Kelp Stalks](/wiki/Bull_Kelp "Bull Kelp") on her island can be dried into [Kelp Fronds](/wiki/Kelp_Fronds "Kelp Fronds") for an easy first point.
  + Planting 10 [Flowers](/wiki/Flower "Flower"). Once this task is completed, Pearl's Bundles of Thanks will include honey.
  + Planting *and Fertilizing* 8 [Berry Bushes](/wiki/Berry_Bush "Berry Bush") or [Juicy Berry Bushes](/wiki/Juicy_Berry_Bush "Juicy Berry Bush").
  + Removing all [Underwater Salvageables](/wiki/Underwater_Salvageable "Underwater Salvageable") around the Island.
  + Killing the [Meat Bulb (Lureplant)](/wiki/Meat_Bulb_(Lureplant) "Meat Bulb (Lureplant)") that appears at the beginning of spring. A Lureplant planted on Hermit Island by the player cannot be killed for a friendship point.
  + Building a [Wooden Chair](/wiki/Wooden_Chair "Wooden Chair") (when Pearl sits on it). Pearl gives a blueprint for this chair after a [Replica Relic Chair](/wiki/Relic "Relic") is built on the island. The Wooden Chair can then be crafted at a [Sawhorse](/wiki/Sawhorse "Sawhorse").
* Gifts given to Pearl:
  + A [Pretty Parasol](/wiki/Pretty_Parasol "Pretty Parasol"), [Umbrella](/wiki/Umbrella "Umbrella") or [Eyebrella](/wiki/Eyebrella "Eyebrella"), but only when it's [Raining](/wiki/Rain "Rain").
  + An [insulated chest-slot item](/wiki/Freezing#Insulated_Rate "Freezing") when [Snow](/wiki/Snow "Snow") is falling such as the [Breezy Vest](/wiki/Breezy_Vest "Breezy Vest"), [Puffy Vest](/wiki/Puffy_Vest "Puffy Vest"), [Dapper Vest](/wiki/Dapper_Vest "Dapper Vest"), or [Rain Coat](/wiki/Rain_Coat "Rain Coat"). Head slot clothing doesn't count.
  + A [Flower Salad](/wiki/Flower_Salad "Flower Salad").
  + A heavy [Fallounder](/wiki/Fallounder "Fallounder"). Pearl awards the player an [Advert](/wiki/Advert "Advert") for the [Stupefying Lure](/wiki/Stupefying_Lure "Stupefying Lure") in return.
  + A heavy [Bloomfin Tuna](/wiki/Bloomfin_Tuna "Bloomfin Tuna"). Pearl awards an advert for the [Rainy Day Lure](/wiki/Rainy_Day_Lure "Rainy Day Lure").
  + A heavy [Scorching Sunfish](/wiki/Scorching_Sunfish "Scorching Sunfish"). Pearl awards an advert for the [Heavy Weighted Lure](/wiki/Heavy_Weighted_Lure "Heavy Weighted Lure").
  + A heavy [Ice Bream](/wiki/Ice_Bream "Ice Bream"). Pearl awards an advert for the [Snow Day Lure](/wiki/Snow_Day_Lure "Snow Day Lure").
  + 5 heavy [Ocean Fish](/wiki/Ocean_Fishes "Ocean Fishes"), excluding the four seasonal fish.

An Ocean Fish is heavy if it's in the upper 70% of its [Weight](/wiki/Weight "Weight") range. Heavy fishes produce an extra examination quote when weighed with a [Pocket Scale](/wiki/Pocket_Scale "Pocket Scale"). The player can also offer all fish to Pearl and she will rudely return any that are underweight. A [Heavy Weighted Lure](/wiki/Lures "Lures") will only attract the heavy fishes within a shoal, saving the player time catching and weighing all of the fish. A [Tin Fishing Bin](/wiki/Tin_Fishin%27_Bin "Tin Fishin' Bin") will keep all Ocean fishes fresh until they can be shown to Pearl.

To finish Pearl's quests and obtain the maximum level of friendship the player will need to bring many of the following resources:

* 10 [Cookie Cutter Shells](/wiki/Cookie_Cutter_Shell "Cookie Cutter Shell")
* 12 [Boards](/wiki/Boards "Boards") (10 for the home upgrade, 2 for the [Pinchin' Winch](/wiki/Pinchin%27_Winch "Pinchin' Winch"))
* Any boat and an oar
* 1 [Firefly](/wiki/Fireflies "Fireflies")
* 10 [Marbles](/wiki/Marble "Marble")
* 6 [Cut Stone](/wiki/Cut_Stone "Cut Stone") (5 for the home upgrade, 1 for the [Pinchin' Winch](/wiki/Pinchin%27_Winch "Pinchin' Winch"))
* 3 [Light bulbs](/wiki/Light_Bulb "Light Bulb")
* 10 [Moon Rocks](/wiki/Moon_Rock "Moon Rock")
* 7 [Ropes](/wiki/Rope "Rope") (5 for the home upgrade, 2 for the [Pinchin' Winch](/wiki/Pinchin%27_Winch "Pinchin' Winch"))
* 5 [Carpeted Flooring](/wiki/Carpeted_Flooring "Carpeted Flooring")
* 1 [Empty Bottle](/wiki/Empty_Bottle#Don.27t_Starve_Together "Empty Bottle")
* 10 [Butterflies](/wiki/Butterfly "Butterfly") (or 1 butterfly and a [Bug Net](/wiki/Bug_Net "Bug Net") with at least 9 uses left to catch more butterflies on the island once you plant the flower).
* 8 [Berry Bushes](/wiki/Berry_Bush "Berry Bush")
* 8 Fertilizer
* 1 rain protection item while it's raining (tip: the player can pick the flowers they planted to craft a Pretty Parasol as soon as it starts to rain)
* 1 body insulation item while it's snowing
* Any weapon or tool to destroy the Lureplant
* 6 meats (Or kelps from the island)
* A [Flower Salad](/wiki/Flower_Salad "Flower Salad")

The color of the Crabby Hermit's speech goes from light pink at level 1, to magenta at level 4 and light purple at level 8. Players begin to see her as **Pearl** and her Hermit Home as *Pearl's Place* at level 8. As she gets friendlier, she becomes more likely to dance, particularly when a player performs the dance emote.

A **Bundle of Thanks** is given by the Crabby Hermit to reward tasks.

Bundle of Thanks contains 3 random Shell Bells (Soprano Shell Bell40%, Alto Shell Bell40%, Baritone Shell Bell20%), except at level 2 where there aren't any. It also contains 1 [Honey](/wiki/Honey "Honey") if the Flower task has been completed.

At level 2, 4, 5 and 7 the Bundle of Thanks also contains one random [Lure](/wiki/Lures "Lures") from the following: Rainy Day Lure, Snow Day Lure, Stupefying Lure and Heavy Weighted Lure, with a 25% chance each.

The **Shell Bell Bundle** is an [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") that can be obtained in exchange for 1 [Empty Bottle](/wiki/Empty_Bottle#Don't_Starve_Together "Empty Bottle") in the [Bottle Exchange Filter](/wiki/Bottle_Exchange_Filter "Bottle Exchange Filter") of the Crabby Hermit.

A Shell Bell Bundle contains 8 random [Shell Bells](/wiki/Shell_Bells "Shell Bells") with a 40% chance for Soprano Shell Bells, a 40% chance for Alto Shell Bells and a 20% chance for Baritone Shell Bells.

## Trivia[]

* The Crabby Hermit and the Bundle of Thanks were introduced in the *[She Sells Sea Shells](/wiki/Return_of_Them#She_Sells_Sea_Shells "Return of Them")* update.
* Based on the gifts she gives the player (along with her appearance, and the update that added her), she appears to be based on [Mary Anning](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mary_Anning "wikipedia:Mary Anning"), a fossil collector, palaeontologist, and dealer who inspired the popular tongue twister "She sells seashells".
* The name "Crabby Hermit" could be a play on Hermit Crab
