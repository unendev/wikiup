The **Naked Mole Bat** is a [mob](/wiki/Mob "Mob") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*. They spawn when a player approaches one of the large lights shining from above into the [Lunar Grotto](/wiki/Lunar_Grotto "Lunar Grotto") biome. They are aggressive towards the player. When killed, they will respawn from the same light after 4 [days](/wiki/Day "Day").

They attack all other small creatures. The only [monsters](/wiki/Monster "Monster") they don't attack naturally are [Batilisks](/wiki/Batilisk "Batilisk") and other Naked Mole Bats. When attacking, they bite their target with their sharp teeth. Before attacking, they sometimes screech.

When finding [food](/wiki/Food "Food"), they move towards the food item and eat it with their nose by sniffing it. They are omnivore and eat both [meats](/wiki/Meats "Meats") and [vegetables](/wiki/Vegetables "Vegetables")/[fruits](/wiki/Fruits "Fruits").

Upon death, they drop either 1 [Monster Meat](/wiki/Monster_Meat "Monster Meat") (66%) or 1 [Naked Nostrils](/wiki/Naked_Nostrils "Naked Nostrils") (33%), and possibly [Guano](/wiki/Guano "Guano") (15%).

After some time they will go to sleep and create a [Naked Mole Bat Burrow](/wiki/Naked_Mole_Bat_Burrow "Naked Mole Bat Burrow").

## Spear Hunting[]

* When in combat, Naked Mole Bats have a chance to summon another Mole Bat from the ceiling by screaming upwards if there isn't another mole bat near.
* [Bunnymen](/wiki/Bunnymen "Bunnymen") and [Pigs](/wiki/Pigs "Pigs") attack them on sight as they are considered Monsters. Pigs only leave their [house](/wiki/Pig_House "Pig House")s underground when exposed to light during the day, so it's advised to build [Rabbit Hutches](/wiki/Rabbit_Hutch "Rabbit Hutch") near Cave Lights, where they spawn.
* [Batilisks](/wiki/Batilisk "Batilisk") will aid Naked Mole Bats in battle. If a Naked Mole Bat is attacked, nearby Mole Bats and Batilisks will join the fight. When a Batilisk is attacked, nearby Mole Bats will join the fight as well.
* They take 2 shots from an [Ice Staff](/wiki/Ice_Staff "Ice Staff") to be frozen and 3 [Sleep Darts](/wiki/Sleep_Dart "Sleep Dart") to be put to [sleep](/wiki/Sleeping "Sleeping").

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* The Naked Mole Bat was introduced in the *[Forgotten Knowledge](/wiki/Return_of_Them#Forgotten_Knowledge "Return of Them")* update.
* Its name is a pun of the name for [Naked Mole Rats](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Naked_Mole_Rat "wikipedia:Naked Mole Rat").
