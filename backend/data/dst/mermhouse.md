A **Mermhouse** is a naturally occurring [Structure](/wiki/Structure "Structure") found in a [Marsh](/wiki/Marsh "Marsh") biome. Its main purpose is spawning and housing [Merms](/wiki/Merm "Merm"). It has an appearance similar to that of a [Pig House](/wiki/Pig_House "Pig House"), but in a dilapidated state. It cannot be crafted by the player, making it [non-renewable](/wiki/Category:Non-Renewable "Category:Non-Renewable"). Mermhouses can hold up to four Merms and spawn a Merm every four days if any Merms are killed. Normally, only one Merm per Mermhouse is outside during the day. If that Merm gets into combat or if the house is struck with a [Hammer](/wiki/Hammer "Hammer"), the rest of the Merms will come out and help it fight. Also, when [Dusk](/wiki/Dusk "Dusk") comes, all Merms exit their house to wander around in the vicinity.

Deconstructing a Mermhouse with a Hammer yields 1-2 [Rocks](/wiki/Rocks "Rocks"), 1 [Board](/wiki/Board "Board"), and 1 [Fish](/wiki/Fish "Fish").

## Gift Icon Downloadable Content[]

In the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Shipwrecked "Shipwrecked")* DLC, Mermhouses are replaced by **Merm Huts** in [Tidal Marshes](/wiki/Marsh "Marsh"). Merm Huts have a more tropical and better maintained appearance compared to Rundown Houses. When destroyed, instead of Fish, they drop [Tropical Fish](/wiki/Tropical_Fish "Tropical Fish"). Otherwise, their behavior is identical.

## Don't Starve Together icon Don't Starve Together[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, Mermhouses are called **Leaky Shacks**.

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* Before the release of *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")*, Mermhouses were called Rundown Houses.
