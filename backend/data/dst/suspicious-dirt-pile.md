**Suspicious Dirt Piles** are naturally spawning objects. They can be found randomly in the world and will change locations if players are more than 200 metre away. Once examined, the Suspicious Dirt Pile will be uncovered by the [Character](/wiki/Character "Character") and replaced by an Animal Track pointing in the approximate direction of travel. Another pile of dirt will then spawn 40 metres away in that approximate direction. There is a ±30° difference in the actual direction compared to the direction shown by animal track.

Upon uncovering the final footprint (there can be between 6 and 12 per hunt), the character will alert the player that the creature is close. A [Koalefant](/wiki/Koalefant "Koalefant") or an [Ewecus](/wiki/Ewecus "Ewecus") will be spawned rather than another track. The chance for spawning an Ewecus starts at 5% on [Day](/wiki/Day "Day") 1 and increases gradually to 33% on Day 100. The mob will remain in the world until it is killed, regardless of which [Seasons](/wiki/Seasons "Seasons") pass.

The track will disappear after 37.5 seconds, terminating the hunt. The track will disappear after 22.5 seconds if there is rain or when the world wetness is more than 15. Player has to wait 5 seconds after their track has washed away before a new set tracks will spawn for the player.

There is a cooldown of 432-720 seconds after completing a hunt

## https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/dont-starve-game/images/f/f8/Reign of Giants icon.png/revision/latest?cb=20140408110712 Reign of Giants[]

In the *[Reign of Giants](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Reign_of_Giants "Don't Starve: Reign of Giants")* DLC, Suspicious Dirt Piles have a 50% chance to lead to a [Varg](/wiki/Varg "Varg") instead of an Ewecus. As such, on day 100 there will be a 16.5% chance of spawning an Ewecus and another 16.5% of spawning a Varg.

## Don't Starve Together Don't Starve Together[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, tracking Suspicious Dirt Piles in the [Oasis](/wiki/Oasis "Oasis") [Desert](/wiki/Desert "Desert") during a [Spring](/wiki/Spring "Spring") [Rain](/wiki/Rain "Rain") will lead to a [Volt Goat](/wiki/Volt_Goat "Volt Goat") instead of a Koalefant (retaining the chances for an Ewecus or a Varg).

### Suspicious Dirt Pile (with Tooth)[]

Sometimes, tracking Suspicious Dirt Piles can lead to two different ones, one of them having a [Hound's Tooth](/wiki/Hound%27s_Tooth "Hound's Tooth") stuck in it. Tracking the Pile with the Hound's Tooth will replace the normal, elephant tracks with clawed tracks. Following the clawed tracks will eventually lead to a Koalefant Carcass surrounded by a Varg and some [Hounds](/wiki/Hound "Hound") or to an Ewecus. Tracking the other Pile will eventually lead to a Koalefant.

### Event[]

During the [Year of the Varg](/wiki/Year_of_the_Varg "Year of the Varg") [event](/wiki/Category:Events "Category:Events") in *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, if a trail of Suspicious Dirt Piles is investigated while an active [Varg Shrine](/wiki/Year_of_the_Varg#Varg_Shrine "Year of the Varg") is in the world, it will always lead to the event-exclusive [Set Piece](/wiki/Set_Piece "Set Piece") consisting of a [Clay Varg](/wiki/Year_of_the_Varg#Clay_Varg "Year of the Varg") and twelve [Clay Hounds](/wiki/Year_of_the_Varg#Clay_Hound "Year of the Varg").
