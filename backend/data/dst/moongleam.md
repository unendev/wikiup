A **Moongleam** is an item exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")*. They will randomly form and float around inside [Moonstorms](/wiki/Moonstorm "Moonstorm") in a similar manner to [Spores](/wiki/Mushroom_Spore "Mushroom Spore") and can similarly be caught with a [Bug Net](/wiki/Bug_Net "Bug Net").

Moongleams will crackle every 5-15 seconds with electricity, dealing 10 damage to players, mobs and structures with health such as walls in 4 metres around them. [Astroggles](/wiki/Astroggles "Astroggles") in the radius, whether equipped, in inventory or on the floor, are recharged by 10% with each crackle.

When there are more than 10 Moongleams on the floor within a radius of 20 metres, they will be deleted within 30 seconds.

It takes 15 Moongleams in total to complete the [Lunar Siphonator](/wiki/Lunar_Siphonator "Lunar Siphonator") and summon the [Celestial Champion](/wiki/Celestial_Champion "Celestial Champion"). Five Moongleams are needed to build the first stage of the [Lunar Siphonator](/wiki/Lunar_Siphonator "Lunar Siphonator"), and an additional ten are required to complete the second stage.

Moongleams can be used to force the [Unnatural Portal](/wiki/Unnatural_Portal "Unnatural Portal") to generate items.
