The **Lavae Tooth** is an [item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*. Once a [Lavae Egg](/wiki/Lavae_Egg "Lavae Egg") hatches, it will yield a Lavae Tooth and an [Extra-Adorable Lavae](/wiki/Extra-Adorable_Lavae "Extra-Adorable Lavae"). The Extra-Adorable Lavae will follow the Lavae Tooth that spawned from the same egg, similar to [Chester](/wiki/Chester "Chester") and his [Eye Bone](/wiki/Eye_Bone "Eye Bone").

Dropping the Tooth on the ground or leaving it in a stationary container will cause the Extra-Adorable Lavae to stay by that location. If the Extra-Adorable Lavae dies, the Lavae Tooth will turn to [Ashes](/wiki/Ashes "Ashes").

## Prototype Tips[]

* Lavae Eggs can be obtained by killing the [Dragonfly](/wiki/Dragonfly "Dragonfly"). They will hatch after leaving them near a constant heat source for 2 [days](/wiki/Day "Day").
* If [Krampus](/wiki/Krampus "Krampus") steals this item, the Extra-Adorable Lavae will start to follow Krampus.
