**Moon Moth Wings** are a [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")*. They are dropped by [Moon Moths](/wiki/Moon_Moth "Moon Moth") and take 6 [Days](/wiki/Day "Day") to spoil.

## Prototype Tips[]

* As it is much easier to obtain [Butterfly Wings](/wiki/Butterfly_Wings "Butterfly Wings"), it is recommended to eat Moon Moth Wings raw rather than cooking them.
  + But Moon Moth Wings can be used in the [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") to substitute Butterfly Wings in the recipe for [Butter Muffins](/wiki/Butter_Muffin "Butter Muffin") for improved healing at the cost of sanity.
