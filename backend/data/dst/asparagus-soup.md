**Asparagus Soup** is a [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") dish introduced in the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC. It requires at least one raw or cooked [Asparagus](/wiki/Asparagus "Asparagus"), and at least another 0.5 [Vegetables](/wiki/Vegetables "Vegetables"). Asparagus Soup takes 10 seconds to cook and restores 20 Health, 18.75 Hunger and 5 Sanity when eaten.

## Don't Starve Together Don't Starve Together[]

Asparagus Soup is also included in *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*. However, it requires more [Vegetables](/wiki/Vegetables "Vegetables") (1.5). Filler cannot be [Meats](/wiki/Meats "Meats") or [Inedible](/wiki/Inedible "Inedible"). Using [Ice](/wiki/Ice "Ice") will result in [Vegetable Stinger](/wiki/Spicy_Vegetable_Stinger "Spicy Vegetable Stinger").
