**Barnacles** are a [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Items "Items") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in the *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")* updates. When cooked, it produces **Cooked Barnacles**. Barnacles have a 0.25 value of meat and fish.

They can be harvested from [Sea Weed](/wiki/Sea_Weed "Sea Weed") directly or with a [Razor](/wiki/Razor "Razor"). They also drop from [Crab King](/wiki/Crab_King "Crab King") and [Rockjaws](/wiki/Rockjaw "Rockjaw").
