**Clippings** are [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Items](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC. They are acquired by trimming [Hedges](/wiki/Hedge "Hedge") in the Pig City. Clippings can be gathered by using the [Razor](/wiki/Razor "Razor") or [Shears](/wiki/Shears "Shears"). The Razor yields one Clipping while the Shears yield two, but the Razor has infinite durability while the Shears only have twenty uses.

As with other Foods, Pigs will eat Clippings if left on the ground, producing [Manure](/wiki/Manure "Manure") similar to [Vegetables](/wiki/Vegetable "Vegetable") and other plant based edibles.

## Icon Tools Usage[]

Clippings can be sold to the [Shopkeep](/wiki/Shopkeep "Shopkeep") for 1 [Oinc](/wiki/Oinc "Oinc") each. There is no limit to how many the player can sell per day. They can also be used to craft several variations of [Hedges](/wiki/Hedge "Hedge") using the [Key to the City](/wiki/Key_to_the_City "Key to the City").

## Prototype Tips[]

* Clippings can be eaten to gain a small amount of [Hunger](/wiki/Hunger "Hunger"). However they never spoil, so they can make for a good emergency source of food. A full stack of Clippings will take some time to eat but will restore 40 Health and 188 Hunger.
* Clippings are good to sell for lots of quick Oincs, but if one need manure for farms, fuel, and fertilization, one can drop some Clippings on the floor, Pigs will eat them and produce Manure which one can then pick up for one Oinc each.
