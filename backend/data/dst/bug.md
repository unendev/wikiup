**Bug** is a [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") category added in the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC. There are currently only 2 items in this category: [Bean Bugs](/wiki/Bean_Bugs "Bean Bugs") and [Gummy Slugs](/wiki/Gummy_Slug "Gummy Slug").

Both of these items can only be obtained by flipping [Stone Slabs](/wiki/Stone_Slab "Stone Slab"). after 8 in-game days the [Stone Slab](/wiki/Stone_Slab "Stone Slab")s will regenerate their loot, making these items renewable

Each of these items has one [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") recipe associated with it, however, they both require numerical amounts of one of the specific items, not a decimal amount of bugs. Due to this, the category is never used for determining the produced crockpot dish.
