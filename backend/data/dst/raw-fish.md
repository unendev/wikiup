**Raw Fish** is a [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") found in the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Shipwrecked "Shipwrecked")* DLC and *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*. Both [White Whales](/wiki/White_Whale "White Whale") and [Blue Whales](/wiki/Blue_Whale "Blue Whale") drop 4 Raw Fish upon death. Also, the [Tiger Shark](/wiki/Tiger_Shark "Tiger Shark") drops 8 Raw Fish when killed.

It can be used in multiple [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") recipes, giving both a [Fish Value](/wiki/Crock_Pot#Fish/Seafood_values "Crock Pot") and a half [Meat Value](/wiki/Crock_Pot#Meat_value "Crock Pot"). It is especially useful for the [Surf 'n' Turf](/wiki/Surf_%27n%27_Turf "Surf 'n' Turf") recipe since it complements both of its requirements, thus the remaining ingredients can be filled flexibly with whatever is more abundant. Raw Fish can also be dried on a [Drying Rack](/wiki/Drying_Rack "Drying Rack") to acquire [Jerky](/wiki/Jerky "Jerky").

**Fish Steak** is Raw Fish, a [Dogfish](/wiki/Dogfish "Dogfish"), or a [Swordfish](/wiki/Swordfish "Swordfish") that has been [cooked](/wiki/Cooking "Cooking") on a [Campfire](/wiki/Campfire "Campfire"), [Fire Pit](/wiki/Fire_Pit "Fire Pit"), [Chiminea](/wiki/Chiminea "Chiminea"), or [Star](/wiki/Star_Caller%27s_Staff "Star Caller's Staff"). Additionally, ["Ballphin Free" Tuna](/wiki/%22Ballphin_Free%22_Tuna "\"Ballphin Free\" Tuna") drops a Fish Steak when opened. It can also be used in multiple Crock Pot recipes.

## Don't Starve Together icon Don't Starve Together[]

Raw Fish and Fish Steak were added to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")* in the *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")* beta branch. Any large [Ocean Fish](/wiki/Ocean_Fish "Ocean Fish") variant will drop Raw Fish. Additionally, [Gnarwails](/wiki/Gnarwail "Gnarwail") and [Rockjaws](/wiki/Rockjaw "Rockjaw") drop 4 Raw Fish when killed.

## Prototype Tips[]

* Because of the fast spoilage time of Raw Fish and the fact it takes only a 1 [Day](/wiki/Day "Day") to become Jerky, it is highly recommended to dry it on a Drying Rack if the player does not intend to consume it early.

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* Raw Fish was introduced to *Don't Starve Together* in the *[Hook, Line, and Inker](/wiki/Return_of_Them#Version_History "Return of Them")* update.
* Despite the name and appearance of Raw Fish in-game, a high majority of the mobs that drop the item are mammalian creatures; [Whales](/wiki/Whale "Whale") and [Gnarwails](/wiki/Gnarwail "Gnarwail") are actually marine mammals and the Tiger Shark is mainly mammalian despite also having shark traits. The only purely fish creatures that drop Raw Fish are the [Ocean Fish](/wiki/Ocean_Fish "Ocean Fish").
