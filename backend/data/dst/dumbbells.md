*Exclusive to:* Don't Starve Together

**Dumbbells** are items crafted by [Wolfgang](/wiki/Wolfgang "Wolfgang") and can only be held by him. They can be used as weapons or can be lifted to fill up Wolfgang's [Mightiness Meter](/wiki/Wolfgang#Don't_Starve_Together "Wolfgang") each time he lifts them, depending on his current strength. Wolfgang does 1 lift per second. Attacking using a Dumbbell will have the effects as lifting it 0.5 times, and Mighty Wolfgang can throw the Dumbbells, causing damage to targets in a small radius. Melee attacking costs 2 uses of the Dumbbell, and throwing cost 10 uses. While holding out any Dumbbell and running, Mightiness drain will be paused, making managing your Might much easier.

## Prototype Tips[]

* As the Dumbbell is very inefficient, it could be used to keep Wolfgang's Mightiness from dropping until a better source of Mightiness is acquired.
* Due to the Golden Dumbbell's overall cheapness, it can easily replace the Dumbbell in the early-game. However, as the Golden Dumbbell only has higher lifting efficiency compared to a Dumbbell when Wolfgang is Wimpy (5 instead of 3 Mightiness per lift only in that form), this upgrade may be unnecessary if the player never goes Wimpy.
* The Marbell increases mightiness at quite a quick rate. If you can find marble it's a viable alternative to the Mighty Gym that doesn't require gems. Full 100 mightiness can be reached in around 30 seconds starting at 0.
* Dumbbells can make for very efficient crowd-clearing weapons when thrown, albeit at the cost of durability. This can be effective against hordes of [Splumonkeys](/wiki/Splumonkey "Splumonkey") in the [Ruins](/wiki/Ruins "Ruins") during the [Nightmare Cycle](/wiki/Nightmare_Cycle "Nightmare Cycle").
* Wolfgang players can equip multiple Icebells and continually throw them at [Crab King](/wiki/Crab_King "Crab King"), permanently stunning him and easily cheesing him this way with enough time and dumbbells.

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* The Dumbbell's crafting description "Sticks and stones make muscles honed." is a reference to the children's rhyme "[Sticks and Stones](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sticks_and_Stones "wikipedia:Sticks and Stones")". The rhyme is used as a defense against name-calling and verbal bullying, intended to increase resiliency, avoid physical retaliation and to remain calm and good-living. The full rhyme is usually a variant of: "Sticks and stones may break my bones, but words shall never hurt me."
* Despite being heavy weights, they don't sink when dropped on water.
  + However, it makes sense for the Gembell as [Gems](/wiki/Gems "Gems") don't sink in water.
* Dumbbells were heavily changed in the [March 2022 QoL update](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together/Version_History#March_24,_2022_-_March_2022_QoL_Update "Don't Starve Together/Version History"):
  + The Marbell was introduced.
  + The Cut Stone requirement was removed, and the amount of Twigs was reduced from two to only one.
