**Houndius Shootius** is a craftable [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") found in the [Ancient Tab](/wiki/Ancient_Tab "Ancient Tab"). It requires 1 [Guardian's Horn](/wiki/Guardian%27s_Horn "Guardian's Horn"), 1 [Deerclops Eyeball](/wiki/Deerclops_Eyeball "Deerclops Eyeball"), and 5 [Thulecite](/wiki/Thulecite "Thulecite") to craft, and can only be crafted near an [Ancient Pseudoscience Station](/wiki/Ancient_Pseudoscience_Station "Ancient Pseudoscience Station"). Like all items from the Ancient Tab, Houndius Shootius cannot be prototyped and [Characters](/wiki/Characters "Characters") always need to be near an Ancient Pseudoscience Station to craft them again.

Once placed, the Houndius Shootius becomes a permanent [Structure](/wiki/Structure "Structure") that will act much like an immobile [Mob](/wiki/Mob "Mob"). It will automatically attack Mobs targeting the Character or any Mob that the player attempts to attack, except for [Shadow Creatures](/wiki/Shadow_Creatures "Shadow Creatures"). The Houndius Shootius briefly emits some light when charging a shot.

It has 1,000 [Health](/wiki/Health "Health") and its projectile deals 65 damage per hit. When damaged, it will regenerate 12 Health per second and can also be repaired using non-edible [healing Items](/wiki/Category:Healing "Category:Healing"). Its crafting materials cannot be recovered using a [Hammer](/wiki/Hammer "Hammer") or [Deconstruction Staff](/wiki/Deconstruction_Staff "Deconstruction Staff") so it can only be "killed" in battle, which will not yield any drops (except in [Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together") where you can get 100% of the drops from the staff, and it drops 2 Thulecite and 3-4 Thulecite fragments when it dies). Care must be taken in deciding where to place one because the crafting materials cannot be recovered from a placed turret in any way. Each turret will drain 6.25 Sanity per minute when the Character is close to them.

In RoG, giving [Seed of Ruins](/wiki/Seed_of_Ruin "Seed of Ruin") to the Cave entrance will automatically regenerate both the cave and the ruins, making it possible to craft infinite amount of Houndius Shootius without going through a [Teleportato](/wiki/Teleportato "Teleportato").

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, it is not limited since the Ruins can be regenerated by killing the Ancient Fuelweaver allowing 1 Houndius Shootius per regeneration.

## Tips[]

* Three Houndius Shootius can kill a [Deerclops](/wiki/Deerclops "Deerclops") or a [Moose/Goose](/wiki/Moose/Goose "Moose/Goose") with minimal help from the player. If the Deerclops cycles between them rather than focusing on one, it is possible that none will be destroyed.
* Despite its name, it can be useful for much more than just Hound attacks.
* If a player attacks a turret, other turrets will begin to attack it. Turrets will continue to fire at each other until one of them is destroyed or distracted.
* The Houndius Shootius can be used in conjunction with a [Bat Bat](/wiki/Bat_Bat "Bat Bat") to heal quickly; its high Health and passive regeneration makes it ideal to attack and heal (at the cost of [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity")).
* The Houndius Shootius is an excellent item to use to defend the player against [Hounds](/wiki/Hounds "Hounds") over [day](/wiki/Day "Day") 100. However the player could be swarmed before the Houndius Shootius kills the hounds so making 2 will be sufficient enough.
* Players can use multiple Houndius Shootius to farm different Mobs penned inside [Walls](/wiki/Walls "Walls"). Good examples of this are Bunnymen, and [Vargs](/wiki/Varg "Varg") (from the *[Reign of Giants](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Reign_of_Giants "Don't Starve: Reign of Giants")* DLC).
* A Houndius Shootius can be used to kill [Birds](/wiki/Birds "Birds") cost free. Paired with a [Feather Hat](/wiki/Feather_Hat "Feather Hat") players can easily farm [Morsels](/wiki/Morsels "Morsels") and [Feathers](/wiki/Feathers "Feathers").
* [Bee Boxes](/wiki/Bee_Box "Bee Box") should be placed a good distance away from the Houndius Shootius, as the angry [Bees](/wiki/Bee "Bee") that come out of a harvested Bee Box will be attacked and killed, slowing [Honey](/wiki/Honey "Honey") gathering.
* Players can use the [Hostile Flare](/wiki/Hostile_Flare "Hostile Flare") to fight Deerclops more than once per [Winter](/wiki/Seasons/Winter "Seasons/Winter") season, with the goal to acquire more than one Deerclops Eyeball.

## Trivia[]

* The Houndius Shootius was added in *[A Moderately Friendly Update](/wiki/Version_History#September_10.2C_2013_-_A_Moderately_Friendly_Update "Version History")*.
* The Houndius Shootius bears a resemblance to an [Obelisk of Light](https://cnc.fandom.com/wiki/Obelisk_of_Light_(Tiberium_Wars) "w:c:cnc:Obelisk of Light (Tiberium Wars)") from *Command & Conquer*.
* The Houndius Shootius was originally called "Minotaur Clopper". This was changed to "Mino-Clopper", and finally its current name, **Houndius Shootius**, alluding to its role against Hound attacks.
* The maximum amount of Houndius Shootius one can legitimately possess in a single world without the Seed of Ruin is 32. When traveling through the Teleportato, a player can take 29 of them (15 in the inventory, 14 in a [Krampus Sack](/wiki/Krampus_Sack "Krampus Sack")) and 3 can be made in the new world. If you use Bundling Wrap and have a new world, the maximum is 119.
  + This means if you are dedicating a Megabase or so in *[Don't Starve](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve "Don't Starve")* or *[Don't Starve: Reign of Giants](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Reign_of_Giants "Don't Starve: Reign of Giants")*, you would have to use the [Teleportato](/wiki/Teleportato "Teleportato") 13 times.
  + In the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC, this number has no limit since players can keep escaping Sandbox Mode worlds, which have been linked to a *Shipwrecked* one, infinitely, without affecting the *Shipwrecked* world. Players can thus store an infinite amount of Houndius Shootius in the *Shipwrecked* world.
  + The same goes for the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Hamlet "Hamlet")* DLC as well, using the [Skyworthy](/wiki/Skyworthy "Skyworthy").[Verification Needed]
* Judging from several character quotes, the Houndius Shootius appears to be sentient.

## Bugs[]

* If a target is killed while a Houndius Shootius is charging its shot, it will continue to charge the shot (emitting light) until a new target is available.
* Hitting the Houndius Shootius with an [Ice Staff](/wiki/Ice_Staff "Ice Staff"), [Fire Dart](/wiki/Blow_Dart#Fire_Dart "Blow Dart"), [Fire Staff](/wiki/Fire_Staff "Fire Staff"), or [Weather Pain](/wiki/Weather_Pain "Weather Pain") will cause it to attack the player.
  + This does not work in *Don't Starve Together*, as the Houndius Shootius will lose aggro immediately after having been damaged by a player. However, a player can still begin attacking the Houndius Shootius until it's destroyed with any weapon after triggering momentary aggro with the [Weather Pain](/wiki/Weather_Pain "Weather Pain") for example.
