The **Teletransport Station** is a [structure](/wiki/Structure "Structure") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in the *[Skill Spotlight Updates](/wiki/Skill_Spotlight_Update "Skill Spotlight Update")*. It can only be crafted by [Winona](/wiki/Winona/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Winona/Don't Starve Together") with 6 [Trusty Tapes](/wiki/Trusty_Tape "Trusty Tape"), 6 [Electrical Doodads](/wiki/Electrical_Doodad "Electrical Doodad"), 3 [Boards](/wiki/Boards "Boards"), and 1 [Gizmo](/wiki/Gizmo "Gizmo") after learning the [Founder's Keepers II](/wiki/Founder%27s_Keepers_II "Founder's Keepers II") skill and scanning the corresponding [Unstable Transmission](/wiki/Unstable_Transmission "Unstable Transmission") with a [Calibrated Perceiver](/wiki/Calibrated_Perceiver "Calibrated Perceiver").

## Usage[]

The Teletransport Station is crafted as an [item](/wiki/Item "Item") that can only be placed by Winona within the range of [Winona's Generator](/wiki/Winona%27s_Generator "Winona's Generator") or [Winona's G.E.M.erator](/wiki/Winona%27s_G.E.M.erator "Winona's G.E.M.erator") to be powered. Winona can then use a [Portasol](/wiki/Portasol "Portasol") to teleport to the nearest powered Teletransport Station.

After Winona has learned the [Portability](/wiki/Portability "Portability") skill, she can dismantle the Teletransport Station to its item form. Otherwise she or other [characters](/wiki/Characters "Characters") have to use a [Hammer](/wiki/Hammer "Hammer") to get rid of it.

After Winona has learned the [Energy-Saver](/wiki/Energy-Saver "Energy-Saver") skill, a placed Teletransport Station will no longer consume energy from generators while it is not in use.
