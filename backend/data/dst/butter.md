## Placeholder Trivia[]

* The fact that it comes from Butterflies, as stated by [Maxwell](/wiki/Maxwell "Maxwell"), is a pun on the word "Butterfly".
  + Butter's striped appearance, and the fact that it comes from butterflies may also be a reference to the browser-based MMO *[Glitch](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Glitch_(video_game) "wikipedia:Glitch (video game)")*, that ran from 2009 to 2012.
* Butter gave 80 [research points](/wiki/Research_points_list "Research points list") when used on a [Science Machine](/wiki/Science_Machine "Science Machine") in the early beta of *Don't Starve*.
* Wilson's quote "I can't believe it's butter!" is a reference to the margarine brand "I Can't Believe It's Not Butter!"
