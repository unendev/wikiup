The **Royal Rabbit Enforcers** are hostile [mobs](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in the *[From Beyond](/wiki/From_Beyond "From Beyond")* updates. They are a variant of [Bunnymen](/wiki/Bunnyman "Bunnyman") summoned by the [Wrathful Rabbit King](/wiki/Wrathful_Rabbit_King "Wrathful Rabbit King"). If there are less than 3 Royal Rabbit Enforcers within a time period of 60 seconds, the King will summons more Enforcers. When the Wrathful Rabbit King is put to sleep, the Royal Rabbit Enforcers will lose aggro. After the King dies, they leave.

During [Acid Rain](/wiki/Acid_Rain "Acid Rain"), the attack damage and movement speed of the Royal Rabbit Enforcers is reduced by 15%, while the damage taken is increased by 25%.
