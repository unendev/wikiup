The **Carpentry Filter** is a filter in the crafting menu of *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*. It is accessible by standing near an [Sawhorse](/wiki/Sawhorse "Sawhorse").
