The **Loot Stash** is a naturally-spawning object exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[A New Reign](/wiki/A_New_Reign "A New Reign")*. It can usually be found in the [Deciduous Forest](/wiki/Deciduous_Forest "Deciduous Forest") or in the [Mosaic](/wiki/Mosaic "Mosaic") Biome. A Loot Stash will spawn 1-3 days after the beginning of every [Winter](/wiki/Winter "Winter") or spawn on day 1 during the *[Winter's Feast](/wiki/Winter%27s_Feast "Winter's Feast")* event.

The spawn location of the Loot Stash changes with each passing Winter, it will despawn 1-3 days after the beginning of [Spring](/wiki/Spring "Spring") and will respawn at the start of the next winter. Attempting to open the Loot Stash with a [Deer Antler](/wiki/Deer_Antler "Deer Antler") will drop [Bone Shards](/wiki/Bone_Shards "Bone Shards") and summon [Klaus](/wiki/Klaus "Klaus"). Using the [Stag Antler](/wiki/Stag_Antler "Stag Antler") on the Loot Stash will properly unlock it, causing it to drop 4 [Bundled Supplies](/wiki/Bundled_Supplies "Bundled Supplies") (6 during [Winter's Feast](/wiki/Winter%27s_Feast "Winter's Feast") event).

## Prototype Tips[]

* The loot content is decided upon creation of the Loot Stash, meaning that if the player is fast enough, they can re-roll the contents of the stash by rolling back the world.
* Due to long nights, it's rewarding to catch [Fireflies](/wiki/Fireflies "Fireflies") during the search of the loot stash, as walking around the deciduous biome will reveal the stash on the map even if the player doesn't directly see it.

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* The Loot Stash was introduced in the *[Winter's Feast](/wiki/A_New_Reign#December_8th,_2016_-_Winter's_Feast_Sneak_Preview "A New Reign")* update, alongside the event of the same name.
* The Loot Stash's chain's are golden during the Winter's Feast event.
* The following Bosses have exclusive drops unobtainable through the Loot Stash: Misery Toadstool (rare [Blueprint](/wiki/Blueprint "Blueprint") for the [Napsack](/wiki/Napsack "Napsack")), Ancient Guardian ([Guardian's Horn](/wiki/Guardian%27s_Horn "Guardian's Horn")), Ancient Fuelweaver ([Shadow Thurible](/wiki/Shadow_Thurible "Shadow Thurible"), [Bone Helm](/wiki/Bone_Helm "Bone Helm"), & [Bone Armor](/wiki/Bone_Armor "Bone Armor")), and the Celestial Champion ([Enlightened Crown](/wiki/Enlightened_Crown "Enlightened Crown")).

## Mosquito Bugs[]

* The Loot Stash will despawn after the end of the Winter's Feast event.
  + If it despawns in the winter, it will not spawn again until the next winter.
