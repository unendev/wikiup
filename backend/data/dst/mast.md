The **Mast** is a craftable [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, and introduced in *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")*. It is found in the [Seafaring Filter](/wiki/Seafaring_Filter "Seafaring Filter"), requires 3 [Boards](/wiki/Boards "Boards"), 3 [Ropes](/wiki/Rope "Rope") and 8 [Silk](/wiki/Silk "Silk") to craft, and a [Think Tank](/wiki/Think_Tank "Think Tank") to prototype.

The Mast item can be deployed on a [Boat](/wiki/Boat_Kit "Boat Kit"). The Mast's sail can then be lowered to allow the players to sail the Boat. A [Steering Wheel](/wiki/Steering_Wheel_Kit "Steering Wheel Kit") can be used to direct the Mast's rotation.

Masts can be upgraded with a [Deck Illuminator](/wiki/Deck_Illuminator "Deck Illuminator") to emit light, or with a [Lightning Conductor](/wiki/Lightning_Conductor "Lightning Conductor") to protect from [Lightning](/wiki/Lightning "Lightning").

To raise the sail efficiently, the player should hover their mouse over the Mast with an open sail and wait for the text to say "Heave!". Clicking on the Mast at this time will have the character raise the Mast halfway. The text will change to "Ho!", and once the player clicks the Mast again, the sail will be closed completely. If the player clicks the Mast before or after these text prompts, the character will struggle and it will take longer to close the sail. This applies to the Winged Sail as well.

The **Winged Sail** is a craftable [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*. It is found in the Seafaring filter, and requires 3 [Driftwood Piece](/wiki/Driftwood_Piece "Driftwood Piece"), 3 Ropes and 4 [Feathery Canvas](/wiki/Feathery_Canvas "Feathery Canvas") to craft. It is prototyped at the [Think Tank](/wiki/Think_Tank "Think Tank").

The Winged Sail item can be deployed on a Boat. Its sail can then be raised to allow players to sail the Boat. Winged Sails take more time to be deployed than normal Masts, but they can be retracted much faster, allowing the boat to stop more easily. Winged Sails apply a stronger force than normal Masts, but turn at the same speed.

Winged Sails can be upgraded with a Deck Illuminator or a Lightning Conductor.

## Gears Physics[]

An open Mast increases the maximum velocity of a Boat by 2.5, and an open Winged Sail increases the speed by 4. Each other successive mast is 30% less effective than the previous (Winged Sail effects apply first). The final maximum speed is bounded between 1 (without mast) and 10. The following table gives some examples:

Note that the base speed is 0, which is raised to 1 by the bound.
