**Iron Ore** is a resource exclusive to the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC. It can be found lying around in the [Painted](/wiki/Painted "Painted") [Biome](/wiki/Biomes "Biomes") and in [Thunderbirds](/wiki/Thunderbird "Thunderbird")' [Nests](/wiki/Thundernest "Thundernest"). It can also be obtained from mining [Iron Hulks](/wiki/Iron_Hulk "Iron Hulk") and [Gnat Mounds](/wiki/Gnat_Mound "Gnat Mound") which makes it renewable. Another way to obtain iron ore is through [trawling](/wiki/Trawl_Net "Trawl Net") in Hamlet [Lilly Ponds](/wiki/Lily_Pond "Lily Pond") (3.92% chance).

Iron Ore is required for several [Crafting](/wiki/Crafting "Crafting") recipes, including those found in the [Treasure Hunting Tab](/wiki/Treasure_Hunting_Tab "Treasure Hunting Tab").
