The **Anenemy** is a naturally-spawning object exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")*. It is found on the [Rocky Beach](/wiki/Rocky_Beach "Rocky Beach") and [Lunar Archipelago](/wiki/Lunar_Archipelago "Lunar Archipelago") biomes of the [Lunar Island](/wiki/Lunar_Island "Lunar Island").

If a player or [mob](/wiki/Mob "Mob") walks over an Anenemy, it will trigger and deal 60 damage to anything on top of it and close itself for around 1 minute. The Anenemy has unlimited durability, but unlike the [Tooth Trap](/wiki/Tooth_Trap "Tooth Trap") the player cannot reset it.

Closed Anenemies can be dug up with a [Shovel](/wiki/Shovel "Shovel"), giving an **Anenemy Trap**, which can be picked up and replanted elsewhere, spawning a closed Anenemy at the chosen spot.

Anenemies cannot die or get destroyed. However, they can be stolen by [Krampus](/wiki/Krampus "Krampus") when they are not planted. Anenemies are not renewable.

## Tips[]

* The Anenemy can not be set on fire so it is a great way to kill [Fire Hounds](/wiki/Fire_Hounds "Fire Hounds").
* Anenemies are excellent at mass producing [Spider](/wiki/Spider "Spider") drops. Placing multiple Anenemies around a [Spider Den](/wiki/Spider_Den "Spider Den") will kill multiple spiders. Provided the player is capable of extracting the drops from the Anenemies, one can amass silk with little work.
  + The farm's effectiveness is increased by reducing the are on which Spiders wander by placing the den on a coast or by surrounding it with End Tables and using some food as bait that will lead the spiders in the anenemies' way.
  + The best way of collecting the loot for most characters is using a [Lazy Forager](/wiki/The_Lazy_Forager "The Lazy Forager") and walking near the Anenemies. Players should consider the range of Lazy Forager when setting up Anenemies if they choose this method of extraction.
  + [Maxwell](/wiki/Maxwell/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Maxwell/Don't Starve Together")'s Shadow Servant allows for easy access for the loot as the shadow's item picking range is roughly the size of a grown-up Spider Den's range, provided the Spider Den is placed on a coast.
  + Charged [WX-78](/wiki/WX-78 "WX-78") with a [Walking Cane](/wiki/Walking_Cane "Walking Cane") will be able to activate all the Anenemies without risking any damage by running over them.
* Anenemies are a good way of producing other loot from mobs like [McTusk](/wiki/MacTusk_N%27_Son "MacTusk N' Son"), [Bunnymen](/wiki/Bunnyman "Bunnyman"), [Spluonkeys](/wiki/Splumonkey "Splumonkey"), [Hounds](/wiki/Hound "Hound") (Using [Vargs](/wiki/Varg "Varg") or [Varglets](/wiki/Varglet "Varglet")) [Pigs](/wiki/Pig "Pig"), [Rabbits](/wiki/Rabbit "Rabbit") and others, but are less effective and require setting up proper [Walls](/wiki/Wall "Wall") and using food as bait, due to their higher hp and lower spawn rates.

## Trivia[]

* The Anenemy was introduced in the *[Turn of Tides](/wiki/Return_of_Them#Turn_of_Tides "Return of Them")* update.
* "Anenemy" is a pun on sea "[anemone](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sea_anemone "wikipedia:Sea anemone")" and "an enemy".
* A dug out Anenemy left on the ground appears to be breathing.
