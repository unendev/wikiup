The **Hot Dragon Chili Salad** is a *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")* and [Warly](/wiki/Warly "Warly") specific [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [item](/wiki/Item "Item") cooked in the [Portable Crock Pot](/wiki/Portable_Crock_Pot "Portable Crock Pot") by combining one [Dragon Fruit](/wiki/Dragon_Fruit "Dragon Fruit"), one [Pepper](/wiki/Pepper "Pepper"), and two fillers.

Using two [Twigs](/wiki/Twigs "Twigs") as filler will result in [Dragon Pie](/wiki/Dragon_Pie "Dragon Pie") while using meat will result in [Meatballs](/wiki/Meatballs "Meatballs"). Attempting to cook a Hot Dragon Chili Salad in a regular [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") will result in Dragon Pie.

Consuming a Hot Dragon Chili Salad keeps player temperature up to 40° above ambient temperature for 5 minutes. However, it does not make player immune to being frozen over by [Deerclops](/wiki/Deerclops "Deerclops"), [Blue Hounds](/wiki/Blue_Hound "Blue Hound") or [Ice Staffs](/wiki/Ice_Staff "Ice Staff").
