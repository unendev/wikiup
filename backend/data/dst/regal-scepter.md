The **Regal Scepter** is an [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") introduced in the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC. It is found in the [Pinacle](/wiki/Pinacle "Pinacle") [Biome](/wiki/Biome "Biome") and can be given to the [Pig Queen](/wiki/Pig_Queen "Pig Queen") in exchange for a [Royal Gallery Key](/wiki/Royal_Gallery_Key "Royal Gallery Key"). It can also be used as an infinitely durable weapon, though it only does 10 damage per hit.
