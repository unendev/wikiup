The **Crashed Balloon** is a set of naturally spawning objects in the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC. They mark the player's spawn point and are meant to infer the player has crashed their hot air balloon on their starting island.

There are six different pieces: A **Deflated Balloon**, a **Wicker Basket**, two **Mongrammed Luggages**, **Useless Flags**, and a **Bag of Sand**. The debris can be destroyed with a [Hammer](/wiki/Hammer "Hammer") to yield [Cloth](/wiki/Cloth "Cloth"), [Boards](/wiki/Boards "Boards"), a [Hardened Rubber Bung](/wiki/Trinkets#Hardened_Rubber_Bung "Trinkets"), a [Razor](/wiki/Razor "Razor"), a [Rope](/wiki/Rope "Rope"), and [Sand](/wiki/Sand "Sand"), respectively.

## Prototype Tips[]

* The Wicker Basket has its own icon on the player's [Map](/wiki/Map "Map").
* The piece of Cloth can be used to craft a [Pith Hat](/wiki/Pith_Hat "Pith Hat") for [Humid Season](/wiki/Seasons/Humid "Seasons/Humid") or a [Gas Mask](/wiki/Gas_Mask "Gas Mask") to explore the [Gas Rainforest](/wiki/Gas_Rainforest "Gas Rainforest").

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* The Deflated Balloon and Wicker Basket share their textures with the [Deflated Balloon](/wiki/Deflated_Balloon "Deflated Balloon") and [Wicker Basket](/wiki/Wicker_Basket "Wicker Basket") found in *Hamlet*-compatible worlds.
* The Crashed Balloon is functionally similar to the [Debris](/wiki/Debris "Debris") from the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC.
