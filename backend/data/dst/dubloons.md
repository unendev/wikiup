**Dubloons** are an [Item](/wiki/Items "Items") in the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC. They are randomly left behind by [Parrot Pirates](/wiki/Parrot_Pirate "Parrot Pirate") (similarly to [Seeds](/wiki/Seeds "Seeds") left by normal [Birds](/wiki/Birds "Birds")) or obtained from giving [Trinkets](/wiki/Trinkets "Trinkets") or [Fishes](/wiki/Fishes "Fishes") to [Yaarctopus](/wiki/Yaarctopus "Yaarctopus"). The loot from the [X Marks the Spot](/wiki/X_Marks_the_Spot "X Marks the Spot") treasure chests also have a chance to include Dubloons. Using a [Trawl Net](/wiki/Trawl_Net "Trawl Net") may yield Dubloons in all [Seasons](/wiki/Season "Season"), however in [Mild Season](/wiki/Mild_Season "Mild Season") and [Monsoon Season](/wiki/Monsoon_Season "Monsoon Season") players will need to use the Trawl Net on Medium and Deep [Ocean](/wiki/Ocean "Ocean") to have a chance at Dubloons. The [Booty Bag](/wiki/Booty_Bag "Booty Bag") will periodically drop Dubloons while equipped and the [Slot Machine](/wiki/Slot_Machine "Slot Machine") has a chance to drop them.

## Icon Tools Usage[]

Dubloons can be spent at the [Slot Machine](/wiki/Slot_Machine "Slot Machine") for a chance on a variety of rewards. Additionally, they can be refined into [Gold Nuggets](/wiki/Gold_Nugget "Gold Nugget"), or used by [Woodlegs](/wiki/Woodlegs "Woodlegs") to craft his character specific items.
