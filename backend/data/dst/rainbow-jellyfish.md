**Rainbow Jellyfish** are passive [Mobs](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs") found in the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC. Similar to regular [Jellyfish](/wiki/Jellyfish "Jellyfish"), they float around in the [Ocean](/wiki/Ocean "Ocean"), sometimes in groups. They will not attack the player or flee if they are approached. They will not shock the player on contact as regular Jellyfish do. Once killed, their carcass will float in the water until picked up.

Rainbow Jellyfish can also be caught alive using a [Net](/wiki/Net "Net") or a [Trawl Net](/wiki/Trawl_Net "Trawl Net"). As inventory items, they can be murdered by right-clicking them to produce [Dead Rainbow Jellyfish](/wiki/Dead_Rainbow_Jellyfish "Dead Rainbow Jellyfish"). A Rainbow Jellyfish placed on the ground will immediately turn into a Dead Rainbow Jellyfish. They will have a short animation where they twitch twice before settling.

Rainbow Jellyfish can be cooked to make [Cooked Rainbow Jellyfish](/wiki/Cooked_Rainbow_Jellyfish "Cooked Rainbow Jellyfish"). They can be eaten to glow rainbow colours in order to keep Charlie away, lasting for about 3 segments. The glow becomes weaker as time passes, until fading completely.

Rainbow Jellyfish take 2 blasts from the [Ice Staff](/wiki/Ice_Staff "Ice Staff") to be frozen and 1 [Sleep Dart](/wiki/Sleep_Dart "Sleep Dart") to be put to sleep.

They are considered innocent creatures, and killing one adds 1 point to the player's [naughtiness](/wiki/Krampus#Naughtiness "Krampus") level, which can cause [Krampus](/wiki/Krampus "Krampus") to appear.

## Volcano Migration[]

During [New Moon](/wiki/Moon_Cycle "Moon Cycle") nights, Rainbow Jellyfish migrations take place. They will spawn in large groups at the [Volcano](/wiki/Volcano "Volcano") and migrate towards the middle of the Map. After the migration takes place, Rainbow Jellyfish will return to the [Volcano](/wiki/Volcano "Volcano").[[1]](#cite_note-1)

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* Rainbow Jellyfish were added to the *Shipwrecked* DLC more than a year after its official completion in the *[Home Sea Home](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked#November_21,_2017_-_Home_Sea_Home_Update "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* update, after being tested in a beta branch for several months.

## Mosquito Bugs[]

* If a Rainbow Jellyfish is captured and released to enclosed water, it will despawn with untimely manners, such as leaving off screen or world-hopping with [Seaworthy](/wiki/Seaworthy "Seaworthy") or [Skyworthy](/wiki/Skyworthy "Skyworthy").
