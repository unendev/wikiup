**Guano** is an item dropped by [Batilisks](/wiki/Batilisk "Batilisk") similar to [Manure](/wiki/Manure "Manure"), colored grayish-white. It can occasionally be found where Batilisks are commonly found, such as near unplugged [Sinkholes](/wiki/Sinkhole "Sinkhole") and on [Stalagmite Terrain](/wiki/Stalagmite_Terrain "Stalagmite Terrain").

## Usage[]

Guano can be used to [Fertilize](/wiki/Fertilizers "Fertilizers") the vegetables produced in a [Farm](/wiki/Farm "Farm") and helps them grow faster, unless it is [Winter](/wiki/Winter "Winter"). It is 1.5× more effective than Manure, although it cannot be used to craft [Farm](/wiki/Farm "Farm") plots. Guano can also be used to fertilize [Grass Tufts](/wiki/Grass_Tuft "Grass Tuft") or [Berry Bushes](/wiki/Berry_Bush "Berry Bush") after they have been dug up with a [Shovel](/wiki/Shovel "Shovel") and replanted, although 1 [Rot](/wiki/Rot "Rot") is just as effective for this.

Guano creates an aesthetic gas cloud when used as a [fuel source](/wiki/Fire#Fuel "Fire").

### Don't Starve TogetherHamlet Don't Starve Together & Hamlet[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")* and the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Hamlet "Hamlet")* DLC, [Wormwood](/wiki/Wormwood "Wormwood") can use Guano to restore [Health](/wiki/Health "Health") (3 Health per use in *DST*, 2 Health per use in *Hamlet*).

In *DST*, Guano can also be used on [Farm Soil Turf](/wiki/Farm_Soil_Turf "Farm Soil Turf") to add [Nutrients](/wiki/Nutrient "Nutrient") (+16 Manure). There is also a 33% Chance for Guano to drop when feeding [Seeds](/wiki/Seeds "Seeds") to a [Bird](/wiki/Bird "Bird") in a [Birdcage](/wiki/Birdcage "Birdcage"). It will, however, appear much smaller than the Guano dropped by Batilisks.
