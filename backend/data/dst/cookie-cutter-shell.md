The **Cookie Cutter Shell** is an [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")*. It is dropped by the [Cookie Cutter](/wiki/Cookie_Cutter "Cookie Cutter"), which can be found near [Salt Formations](/wiki/Salt_Formation "Salt Formation") in the Ocean.

Cookie Cutter Shells are required to craft the [Cookie Cutter Cap](/wiki/Cookie_Cutter_Cap "Cookie Cutter Cap"), and 10 of them are needed to repair the [Hermit Home](/wiki/Hermit_Home "Hermit Home").

They are also required for [The Rainy Day Lure](/wiki/Lures "Lures"), [The Snow Day Lure](/wiki/Lures "Lures"), [The Stupefying Lure](/wiki/Lures "Lures") and [The Heavy Weighted Lure](/wiki/Lures "Lures").
