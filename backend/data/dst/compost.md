**Compost** is an [item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")*. It is produced by a [Composting Bin](/wiki/Composting_Bin "Composting Bin").

## Usage[]

Compost can be used on [Farm Soil Turf](/wiki/Farm_Soil_Turf "Farm Soil Turf") to add [Nutrients](/wiki/Nutrient "Nutrient") to it (+24 Compost).

[Wormwood](/wiki/Wormwood "Wormwood") can use Compost to restore 1 Health/sec over 8 seconds for a total of 8 Health.

## Trivia[]

* Compost was added in the *[Reap What You Sow](/wiki/Reap_What_You_Sow "Reap What You Sow")* update.
