The **Plain Omelette** is a [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*. It is cooked in a [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") by adding at least 3.0 [Eggs](/wiki/Eggs "Eggs"), which can be fulfilled by 3 small [Eggs](/wiki/Egg "Egg") or a [Tallbird Egg](/wiki/Tallbird_Egg "Tallbird Egg"). It takes 10 seconds to cook and 10 [Days](/wiki/Day-Night_Cycle "Day-Night Cycle") to [Spoil](/wiki/Spoil "Spoil").

When consumed, it restores 3 Health, 50 Hunger and 5 Sanity.

Using more than 0.5 [Vegetables](/wiki/Vegetable "Vegetable") as filler will result in [Breakfast Skillet](/wiki/Breakfast_Skillet "Breakfast Skillet"). Combining different fillers with a Tallbird Egg can result in other dishes.
