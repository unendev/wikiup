**Fish Tacos** is a [Meat](/wiki/Meats "Meats") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [item](/wiki/Item "Item") made in the [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") by combining 1 [Corn](/wiki/Corn "Corn") and 1 [Fishes](/wiki/Fishes "Fishes"). It takes 10 seconds to cook and 6 [Days](/wiki/Day-Night_Cycle "Day-Night Cycle") to [Spoil](/wiki/Spoil "Spoil").

When consumed, it restores 20 Health, 37.5 Hunger and 5 Sanity.

Adding exactly 1 [Twig](/wiki/Twigs "Twigs") may result in [Fishsticks](/wiki/Fishsticks "Fishsticks"), while adding 2 Twigs will result in Fish Tacos. Adding a [Mandrake](/wiki/Mandrake "Mandrake") may result in [Mandrake Soup](/wiki/Mandrake_Soup "Mandrake Soup").

## Tips[]

* Fish Tacos restores the same amount of [Hunger](/wiki/Hunger "Hunger") as Fishsticks, but only half as much [Health](/wiki/Health "Health"). This dish is guaranteed not to gain any hunger over its components, and is more expensive than [Fishsticks](/wiki/Fishsticks "Fishsticks") with only half the health value. It might be preferable to completely avoid this dish.

## Trivia[]

* Since Corn is required specifically, one can assume that Fish Tacos have [corn tortillas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Corn_tortilla "wikipedia:Corn tortilla").
