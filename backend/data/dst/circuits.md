*Exclusive to:* Don't Starve Together

**Circuits** are [WX-78](/wiki/WX-78 "WX-78")-exclusive [items](/wiki/Items "Items") craftable in the [Survivor Items Filter](/wiki/Survivor_Items_Filter "Survivor Items Filter") in *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*. See [this page](/wiki/WX-78#Don't_Starve_Together "WX-78") for the targets that need to be scanned with a [Bio Scanalyzer](/wiki/Bio_Scanalyzer "Bio Scanalyzer") to unlock the blueprints.

The **Electrification Circuit** is an item that can only be crafted by [WX-78](/wiki/WX-78 "WX-78"), exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*. It can be crafted after scanning a [Volt Goat](/wiki/Volt_Goat "Volt Goat"). Plugging in an Electrification Circuit uses two slots.

With an Electrification Circuit equipped, WX-78 will do damage to an attacker when WX-78 is hit, subject to a cooldown of 0.3 seconds. The damage has a base value of 20, but is electric, dealing more damage if the target does not have immunity to electricity (1.5×, or 30 damage, to dry mobs; 2.5×, or 50 damage, to wet mobs). This counterattack will also charge an attacking Volt Goat.

Additionally, WX-78 gains immunity to electricity with this circuit equipped.
