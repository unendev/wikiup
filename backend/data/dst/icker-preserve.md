The **Icker Preserve Kit** is an [item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in the *[From Beyond](/wiki/From_Beyond "From Beyond")* updates. It can only be crafted at a [Shadowcraft Plinth](/wiki/Shadowcraft_Plinth "Shadowcraft Plinth"), requiring 3 [Icker Jars](/wiki/Icker_Jar "Icker Jar") and 5 [Dreadstone](/wiki/Dreadstone "Dreadstone"). Crafting it will return the [Empty Bottles](/wiki/Empty_Bottle "Empty Bottle") used for the Icker Jars.

## Usage[]

The Icker Preserve Kit can be placed to build the **Icker Preserve** to store one stack of [food](/wiki/Food "Food") items in it. The food will not spoil as long as it remains in the Icker Preserve. The stored food cannot be eaten by [mobs](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs") or [bosses](/wiki/Bosses "Bosses").

Storing [Light Bulbs](/wiki/Light_Bulb "Light Bulb") or [Glow Berries](/wiki/Glow_Berry "Glow Berry") in the Icker Preserve will make it glow in a small radius. [Scorching Sunfishes](/wiki/Scorching_Sunfish "Scorching Sunfish") will create a heat source and can set nearby items on [fire](/wiki/Fire "Fire"), [Ice Breams](/wiki/Ice_Bream "Ice Bream") will create a cold source.
