A **Doydoy** is a [passive](/wiki/Passive_Mobs "Passive Mobs") [Mob](/wiki/Mob "Mob") in *[Don't Starve: Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")*. It appears as a large blue and green colored bird. It drops [Meat](/wiki/Meat "Meat"), [Drumsticks](/wiki/Drumstick "Drumstick"), and [Doydoy Feathers](/wiki/Doydoy_Feathers "Doydoy Feathers") when killed.

World generation places two Doydoys in the world, usually on separate islands. These islands are usually mostly jungle, but contain a rounded beach section with some withered [Grass Tufts](/wiki/Grass_Tuft "Grass Tuft") and [Saplings](/wiki/Sapling "Sapling"). In order to produce a Doydoy flock, they must be picked up and brought to the same location. It is not recommended to kill them before they have been bred, as doing so will prevent obtaining more than 4 Doydoy Feathers, and limit access to other resources they drop.

Baby Doydoys dropped in the [Ocean](/wiki/Ocean "Ocean") will sink and die. Adult and teen Doydoys will float when dropped over water.

## Behavior[]

Doydoys sleep at night, like [Beefalo](/wiki/Beefalo "Beefalo"). When asleep, they can be picked up. While in the inventory, they need to be fed periodically to prevent them from starving to death. They can be fed with anything except Doydoy Eggs. While in the inventory they can be murdered by right-clicking. When not in the inventory, they do not need to be fed, and they do not starve.

It also has a tendency to eat nearly everything; it will eat food from [Farms](/wiki/Farm "Farm") and [Berry Bushes](/wiki/Berry_Bush "Berry Bush"), out of [Crock Pots](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot"), from the ground. It even eats [Gems](/wiki/Gems "Gems") and [Rocks](/wiki/Rocks "Rocks") whilst the only edible items they do not eat are their own eggs. It also has a mating call, allowing it to mate with other Doydoys to produce a [Doydoy Nest](/wiki/Doydoy_Nest "Doydoy Nest"). Looting a Doydoy Nest gives a [Doydoy Egg](/wiki/Doydoy_Egg "Doydoy Egg") which can be cooked. Hatched Doydoys have an age cycle, similar to [Tallbirds](/wiki/Tallbirds "Tallbirds"), including a baby stage and a teen stage. When attacked, it does not attempt to flee from the player, instead acting as if the player does not exist at all.

[Killer Bees](/wiki/Killer_Bee "Killer Bee") and [Spiders](/wiki/Spider "Spider") ignore Doydoys completely even when entering a [Spider Den](/wiki/Spider_Den "Spider Den")'s sticky webbing. However, [Snakes](/wiki/Snake "Snake"), [Dragoons](/wiki/Dragoon "Dragoon") and [Crocodogs](/wiki/Crocodog "Crocodog") do attack Doydoys. To maximize the chance of keeping Doydoys alive, they should be kept away from these Mobs. Doydoys take 1 blast from the [Ice Staff](/wiki/Ice_Staff "Ice Staff") to be frozen and 1 [Sleep Dart](/wiki/Sleep_Dart "Sleep Dart") to be put to sleep.

## Doydoy Egg Breeding[]

Every 2–3 days, the world attempts to pair two Doydoys, causing them to mate. If no Doydoys are close to each other, or there are already 20 Doydoys in the world, then no mating occurs.

Mating involves a ten-second dance, in which the male will start to scream, alerting nearby players, after which the [Nest](/wiki/Doydoy_Nest "Doydoy Nest") is made. If left alone, the egg hatches from the nest after two days, producing a Baby Doydoy, which only eats seeds. After two days, the Baby Doydoy will grow into a Teen Doydoy, which also eats vegetables. After only one day as a teen, the Doydoy will grow to an adult and be able to eat anything.

When two Doydoys mate, there is a 20% chance for a single [Doydoy Feather](/wiki/Doydoy_Feather "Doydoy Feather") to drop.

## Krampus Naughtiness[]

The amount of [Naughtiness](/wiki/Naughtiness "Naughtiness") given by killing a Doydoy varies greatly depending on how many Doydoys remain. Note that nests with eggs in them count towards the Doydoy population.

| Doydoys remaining | Naughtiness |

| 1-2 | 50 |
| 3-4 | 10 |
| 5-10 | 4 |
| 11-20 | 1 |

## Tips[]

* Breeding Doydoys is strongly advantageous to the player's survival as they supply mixed [Meat](/wiki/Meat "Meat") items and Doydoy Feathers which are used to craft the [Feather Lite Sail](/wiki/Feather_Lite_Sail "Feather Lite Sail") and [Tropical Fan](/wiki/Tropical_Fan "Tropical Fan"). The feathers are also a significant offering to the [Volcano Altar of Snackrifice](/wiki/Volcano_Altar_of_Snackrifice "Volcano Altar of Snackrifice").
* When a Doydoy is first found, it would be prudent to scout the island for potential threats if the player intends to leave it there as they are known to wander about and are easily killed. Notable threats are [Viney Bushes](/wiki/Viney_Bush "Viney Bush") that spawn snakes, [Krissures](/wiki/Krissure "Krissure") and [Tallbirds](/wiki/Tallbird "Tallbird"), mating [Water Beefalo](/wiki/Water_Beefalo "Water Beefalo"), [Flups](/wiki/Flup "Flup"), [Poisonous Holes](/wiki/Poisonous_Hole "Poisonous Hole"), [Merms](/wiki/Merm "Merm"), and [Wildbores](/wiki/Wildbore "Wildbore")/[Werepigs](/wiki/Werepig "Werepig").
* Player-made flooring such as [Wooden Flooring](/wiki/Wooden_Flooring "Wooden Flooring") can be used to protect penned Doydoys from [Lureplants](/wiki/Lureplants "Lureplants") and keep puddles from spawning during Monsoon Season as Poison Mosquitoes will attack Doydoys and deal AoE damage if given the chance.
* Hammering a [Doydoy Nest](/wiki/Doydoy_Nest "Doydoy Nest") gives the player 2 [Twigs](/wiki/Twigs "Twigs"), 2 [Manure](/wiki/Manure "Manure"), 1 [Cut Grass](/wiki/Cut_Grass "Cut Grass") and 1 Doydoy Feather. This provides the player with a source of feathers without needing to kill the birds.
* Caution should be exercised when dropping items around Doydoys as they will eat nearly anything edible. The only exceptions are [Doydoy Eggs](/wiki/Doydoy_Egg "Doydoy Egg"), Fried Doydoy Eggs, and rotten foods ([Rot](/wiki/Rot "Rot"), [Rotten Eggs](/wiki/Rotten_Egg "Rotten Egg"), and [Wet Goop](/wiki/Wet_Goop "Wet Goop")). This means that they will eat rare foods such as [Mandrakes](/wiki/Mandrake "Mandrake"), [Eyes of the Tiger Shark](/wiki/Eye_of_the_Tiger_Shark "Eye of the Tiger Shark"), and even [Guardian Horns](/wiki/Guardian%27s_Horn "Guardian's Horn").
* A pair of Doydoys can be taken into the volcano and killed, causing your naughtiness to rise instantly to 50 per kill. The game thinks that there are only two Doydoys alive and thus spawns the Krampus despite there being Doydoys in the mainland. This can be used to farm [Krampus](/wiki/Krampus "Krampus") easily.
* Doydoys placed inside a [Bundling Wrap](/wiki/Bundling_Wrap "Bundling Wrap") are not counted towards the total population. By keeping all but two Doydoys wrapped and then killing them as needed, players can summon Krampus easily without the need to travel to the volcano.
* Doydoys can be killed by repeatedly feeding them raw [Monster Meat](/wiki/Monster_Meat "Monster Meat") or [Red Caps](/wiki/Red_Cap "Red Cap") without raising the player's naughtiness level. Punching them with your bare fist several times will reduce the amount needed.
* When mousing over Doydoys, "attack" is replaced with "examine". The only way to attack and kill a Doydoy is to force-attack (default CTRL + F on PC) This was likely done to prevent players from accidentally killing Doydoys when they are nearing extinction.

## Trivia[]

* Based on their name, appearance and passive nature, the Doydoy is the *Don't Starve* version of the extinct flightless bird known as the [Dodo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dodo "wikipedia:Dodo").
  + Real life dodos also lived in islands known as [Mauritius](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mauritius "wikipedia:Mauritius"), they were hunted down by humans until they became extinct in the 1600s.
  + Its colors also somewhat resemble the [Nicobar pigeon](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nicobar_pigeon "wikipedia:Nicobar pigeon"), which is the dodo's closest living relative.

## Bugs[]

* When Doydoys are enclosed in a small pen, with one of the sides facing the ocean, it is possible for a Doydoy to be pushed out and onto the shore after the mating process, making it walk on water.
* After the *[Quality of Life](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve/Version_History "Don't Starve/Version History")* update, Doydoys won't eat [Cut Grass](/wiki/Cut_Grass "Cut Grass") and [Twigs](/wiki/Twigs "Twigs").
