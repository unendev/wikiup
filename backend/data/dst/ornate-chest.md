An **Ornate Chest** is a [Structure](/wiki/Category:Structures "Category:Structures") found in the [Labyrinth](/wiki/Labyrinth "Labyrinth") [biome](/wiki/Biomes "Biomes") of the [Ruins](/wiki/Ruins "Ruins"). Ornate Chests are usually surrounded by [Dangling Depth Dweller](/wiki/Dangling_Depth_Dweller "Dangling Depth Dweller") webbing.

Ornate Chests do not drop anything when destroyed.

### Shipwrecked Shipwrecked[]

In the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Shipwrecked "Shipwrecked")* DLC, Ornate Chests have a chance to randomly be dug in [X Marks the Spot](/wiki/X_Marks_the_Spot "X Marks the Spot") locations, with no items in it.

Opening an Ornate Chest has a 66% chance to trigger a random effect out of five possibilities. The type of trap is determined randomly, but changes each time the game is reloaded. Each Ornate Chest can only trigger a trap once. After the Ruins are reset, all Ornate Chests will again have a 66% chance to trigger a trap:

Ornate Chest traps

| **Trap** | **Effect** | **Amount** | **Notes** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Sanity Meter Sanity Trap | Raises or lowers the player's [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity") | -20 to +20 |  |
| Health Meter Health Trap | Raises the player's [Health](/wiki/Health "Health") | 0 to +20 | Cannot kill the player. Code comment reads "Changes health (For the better! We don't want the player to die from this)" |
| Hunger Meter Hunger Trap | Raises or lowers the player's [Hunger](/wiki/Hunger "Hunger") | -20 to +20 |  |
| Terrorbeak Monster Trap | Spawns Dangling Depth Dweller or Terrorbeak | 1-3 | Nightmarebeaks do not spawn from this trap in Don't Starve Together |
| Backpack Inventory Trap | Raises or lowers the [durability](/wiki/Durability "Durability") or [spoilage](/wiki/Spoilage "Spoilage") of a random item in the player's [Inventory](/wiki/Inventory "Inventory") | -20% to +20% | This may lead to unorthodox percentages on tools or armor. |

## Trivia[]

* The name "pandoraschest" is a reference to the Greek myth of [Pandora's box](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pandora%27s_box "wikipedia:Pandora's box"), a box that contained all the evils in the world. The fact that the chest can spawn Terrorbeaks and Dangling Depth Dwellers when opened is likely a reference to this.
* In DST, the Large Ornate Chest's drops were improved in the [March 2022 QoL update](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together/Version_History#March_24,_2022_-_March_2022_QoL_Update "Don't Starve Together/Version History").
