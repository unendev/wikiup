**Bioluminescence** is a passive immobile [Mob](/wiki/Mob "Mob") introduced in the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC. It is found in [Deep Oceans](/wiki/Ocean#Shipwrecked "Ocean") in groups of 12. It can be considered the aquatic version of [Fireflies](/wiki/Fireflies "Fireflies"), and emits light during the [night](/wiki/Night "Night"). It will not appear until dusk arrives, and will stay until dawn. It does however still persist during a [Full Moon](/wiki/Full_Moon "Full Moon"). Once found, it can be caught with [Trawl Net](/wiki/Trawl_Net "Trawl Net") or [Net](/wiki/Net "Net") while it is visible. Unlike Fireflies, it won't disappear when player character approaches, so they can be used as a permanent protection against [Charlie](/wiki/Charlie "Charlie").

Bioluminescence can be used to craft [Bottle Lanterns](/wiki/Bottle_Lantern "Bottle Lantern"), [Buoys](/wiki/Buoy "Buoy"), and can be used to refuel Bottle Lanterns, [Miner Hats](/wiki/Miner_Hat "Miner Hat") as well as [Boat Lanterns](/wiki/Boat_Lantern "Boat Lantern") (restores 38.5% of its capacity). It does not change location so once the map is generated, its location can be marked on the [map](/wiki/Map "Map") by placing any items that have a map icon, such as [Backpack](/wiki/Backpack "Backpack"), [Log Raft](/wiki/Log_Raft "Log Raft"), [Raft](/wiki/Raft "Raft") or placing a Buoy nearby.

When dropped on land, Bioluminescence is destroyed, but it can be placed in the water safely.

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* [Bioluminescence](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bioluminescence "wikipedia:Bioluminescence") typically refers to the production of light by living organisms. Given the appearance and habitat of the Bioluminescence in-game, it appears to be modelled after [Dinoflagellates](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dinoflagellate "wikipedia:Dinoflagellate"), which are bioluminescent Plankton that can cause water to glow in a phenomenon known as [Red Tide](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Red_Tide "wikipedia:Red Tide").
* Before Bioluminescence was made obtainable through trading with the Yarrctopus, it could only be made renewable with a [Construction Amulet](/wiki/Construction_Amulet "Construction Amulet") and a [Deconstruction Staff](/wiki/Deconstruction_Staff "Deconstruction Staff") by first using the amulet to craft a Bottle Lantern (or Buoy), and then using the deconstruction staff to get the full materials back. This must be done on water to ensure the bioluminescence isn't destroyed when dropped. With this method, any amount of bioluminescence can be doubled with the right amount of [Green Gems](/wiki/Green_Gem "Green Gem").
