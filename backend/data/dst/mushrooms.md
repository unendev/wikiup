**Mushrooms** are naturally generated objects.

There are three types of Mushrooms; Red, Green, and Blue, which can be harvested to receive a [Red Cap](/wiki/Red_Cap "Red Cap"), [Green Cap](/wiki/Green_Cap "Green Cap"), and [Blue Cap](/wiki/Blue_Cap "Blue Cap"). They can be used as [Vegetable](/wiki/Vegetable "Vegetable") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") or filler in [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") recipes, or given to a [Pig](/wiki/Pig "Pig") to produce [Manure](/wiki/Manure "Manure"). Each mushroom has different effects on [Health](/wiki/Health "Health"), [Hunger](/wiki/Hunger "Hunger"), and [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity"), therefore, a player needs to know the advantages and disadvantages of their effects and think hard before consuming them. However, when combined effectively, mushrooms are a very synergistic food.

| Mushroom Effects | Health | Hunger | Sanity |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Red Cap Raw Red Cap | **-20** | **12.5** | 0 |
| Cooked Red Cap Cooked Red Cap | **1** | 0 | **-10** |
| Green Cap Raw Green Cap | 0 | **12.5** | **-50** |
| Cooked Green Cap Cooked Green Cap | **-1** | 0 | **15** |
| Blue Cap Raw Blue Cap | **20** | **12.5** | **-15** |
| Cooked Blue Cap Cooked Blue Cap | **-3** | 0 | **10** |

Red Mushrooms are typically found in [Grasslands](/wiki/Grasslands "Grasslands") or [Forests](/wiki/Forest "Forest") and can only be picked during the day. Red Mushrooms have the worst effects of all the mushrooms, both raw and cooked. Green Mushrooms are typically found in Forests and [Marshes](/wiki/Marsh "Marsh"), and can only be picked at Dusk. Blue Mushrooms are typically found in Grasslands and Marshes and can only be picked at Night.

Each mushroom will re-grow after 5 to 10.5 minutes of [Rain](/wiki/Rain "Rain"). They can also be dug up at any time using a [Shovel](/wiki/Shovel "Shovel") to obtain two Caps, but the mushroom spawn will be destroyed. Mushrooms do not grow during [Winter](/wiki/Seasons/Winter "Seasons/Winter").

## Downloadable Content[]

In all [DLCs](/wiki/DLC "DLC"), Mushrooms will turn into [Mushtrees](/wiki/Mushtree "Mushtree") during a [Full Moon](/wiki/Moon_Cycle "Moon Cycle"). After chopping down these Mushtrees, their stumps will turn into a harvested Mushroom spawn point.

In *[Reign of Giants](/wiki/Reign_of_Giants "Reign of Giants")*, all three types of mushrooms can be found within the [Deciduous Forest](/wiki/Deciduous_Forest "Deciduous Forest").

In *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Shipwrecked "Shipwrecked")*, all three types of mushrooms can be found within the [Jungle](/wiki/Jungle "Jungle"). Also, Green Mushrooms can be found within the [Tidal Marsh](/wiki/Tidal_Marsh "Tidal Marsh").

In *[Hamlet](/wiki/Hamlet "Hamlet")*, all three types of mushrooms can be found within the [Gas Rainforest](/wiki/Gas_Rainforest "Gas Rainforest").

## Don't Starve Together Don't Starve Together[]

The [Mushroom Planter](/wiki/Mushroom_Planter "Mushroom Planter") can be used to grow Mushrooms of all types by planting Caps or [Mushroom Spores](/wiki/Mushroom_Spore "Mushroom Spore") on them. Mushrooms will not turn into Mushtrees during a Full Moon. Raw Green Mushrooms are good for depleting sanity or for lowering enlightenment, which can help the player avoid [Gestalt](/wiki/Gestalt "Gestalt") attacks.

## Tips[]

* Raw Blue Mushrooms are good for regenerating health and hunger, and cooked green caps make up for the lost sanity. So, raw Blue Mushrooms and cooked Green Mushrooms are a full diet for characters that can eat vegetarian meals.
* Red Mushrooms are good for giving to [Pigs](/wiki/Pig "Pig"), luring [Gobblers](/wiki/Gobblers "Gobblers"), and using as [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") filler.
* If a ghost player haunts a mushroom in *Don't Starve Together*, it is possible to change the type of mushroom that it is, such as from red to blue.

## Trivia[]

* Mushrooms were added in *[A Little Rain Must Fall](/wiki/Version_History#A_Little_Rain_Must_Fall "Version History")* update.
* Rings of Mushrooms can be found in some worlds as [Set Pieces](/wiki/Set_Piece "Set Piece"). These resemble [Fairy Rings](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fairy_ring "wikipedia:Fairy ring"), which are naturally occurring rings of mushrooms that are thought to have supernatural origins in various mythologies.
* Although they are considered [vegetables](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vegetable "wikipedia:Vegetable") in *Don't Starve*, [mushrooms](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mushroom "wikipedia:Mushroom") are body parts of [fungi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fungi "wikipedia:Fungi"), and are technically not plants. However, they act more like vegetables than anything else during digestion.

## Bugs[]

* If a mushroom is picked as it sinks into the ground, the player will receive a mushroom but the hole the mushroom left would look like the mushroom is still there and sleeping. The player will still view the mushroom as if it were picked already.
* Sometimes, Mushtrees will not transform back into normal mushrooms when the day starts.
