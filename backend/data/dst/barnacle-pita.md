**Barnacle Pita** is a [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") recipe exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")*. It is made of 1 [Barnacles](/wiki/Barnacles "Barnacles") or 1 [Cooked Barnacles](/wiki/Cooked_Barnacles "Cooked Barnacles") and at least 0.5 [Vegetables](/wiki/Vegetables "Vegetables"), fillers can be anything. It takes 40 seconds to cook.

When consumed, it restores 20 Health, 37.5 Hunger and 5 Sanity, and takes 15 [Days](/wiki/Day-Night_Cycle "Day-Night Cycle") to [Spoil](/wiki/Spoil "Spoil").

Adding a total value of 1 or more [Fishes](/wiki/Fishes "Fishes") may result in [Stuffed Fish Heads](/wiki/Stuffed_Fish_Heads "Stuffed Fish Heads").
