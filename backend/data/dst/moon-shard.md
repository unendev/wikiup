**Moon Shards** are a resource exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")*.

There are various ways to obtain Moon Shards. They can be mined from [Moon Glass](/wiki/Moon_Glass "Moon Glass") boulders and [Glassed Hot Springs](/wiki/Hot_Spring "Hot Spring") in the [Lunar Island](/wiki/Lunar_Island "Lunar Island") [Biome](/wiki/Biomes "Biomes") and can be found naturally on the ground of the [Lunar Mine](/wiki/Lunar_Mine "Lunar Mine") [Biome](/wiki/Biomes "Biomes"), and also can be mined from [Vitreoasis](/wiki/Vitreoasis "Vitreoasis") in the [Lunar Grotto](/wiki/Lunar_Grotto "Lunar Grotto"). [Infused Moon Shard](/wiki/Infused_Moon_Shard "Infused Moon Shard") would turn into Moon Shard as it spoiled. [Lunar Wobster](/wiki/Lunar_Wobster "Lunar Wobster") drops one Moon Shard when killed, and [Celestial Champion](/wiki/Celestial_Champion "Celestial Champion") will drop a lot of shards in the fight and upon its death. Glass-overed [Moon Dials](/wiki/Moon_Dial "Moon Dial") will also drop a shard as Celestial Champion dies and the [Moonstorm](/wiki/Moonstorm "Moonstorm") subsequently ceases.

Hot Springs that have [Bath Bombs](/wiki/Bath_Bomb "Bath Bomb") thrown in will become glassed on a [Full Moon](/wiki/Moon_Cycle "Moon Cycle") and can be mined for Moon Shards, mined Vitreoasis also have a chance to reappear on a Full Moon.

Moon Shards can be used to craft items from the [Celestial Tab](/wiki/Celestial_Tab "Celestial Tab") via the [Celestial Altar](/wiki/Celestial_Altar "Celestial Altar"). They can also be used on a [Potter's Wheel](/wiki/Potter%27s_Wheel "Potter's Wheel") to produce green [Sculptures](/wiki/Chess_Pieces "Chess Pieces").
