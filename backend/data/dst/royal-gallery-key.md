The **Royal Gallery Key** is an [item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC.

It is given to players from the [Pig Queen](/wiki/Pig_Queen "Pig Queen") in exchange for the [Regal Scepter](/wiki/Regal_Scepter "Regal Scepter"), [Royal Crown](/wiki/Royal_Crown "Royal Crown"), [The Blue Sow](/wiki/Lost_Relics#The_Blue_Sow "Lost Relics") or [The Jeweled Truffle](/wiki/Lost_Relics#The_Jeweled_Truffle "Lost Relics"). It is used to unlock one of the items in the [Royal Gallery](/wiki/Royal_Gallery_Exhibit "Royal Gallery Exhibit").
