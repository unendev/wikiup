The **Portasol** is an [item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in the *[Skill Spotlight Updates](/wiki/Skill_Spotlight_Update "Skill Spotlight Update")*. It can only be crafted by [Winona](/wiki/Winona/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Winona/Don't Starve Together") with 4 [Trusty Tapes](/wiki/Trusty_Tape "Trusty Tape"), 4 [Electrical Doodads](/wiki/Electrical_Doodad "Electrical Doodad"), 4 [Twigs](/wiki/Twigs "Twigs"), and 1 [Gizmo](/wiki/Gizmo "Gizmo") after learning the [Founder's Keepers II](/wiki/Founder%27s_Keepers_II "Founder's Keepers II") skill and scanning the corresponding [Unstable Transmission](/wiki/Unstable_Transmission "Unstable Transmission") with a [Calibrated Perceiver](/wiki/Calibrated_Perceiver "Calibrated Perceiver").

## Usage[]

Winona can use the Portasol to teleport to the nearest powered [Teletransport Station](/wiki/Teletransport_Station "Teletransport Station"). When using it while standing within 2[m](/wiki/Distance "Distance") of the center of Teletransport Station, approximately the circle pad, she will teleport to the next powered Teletransport Station. It is not possible to teleport between the surface and the [Caves](/wiki/Caves "Caves").

Items, that can possibly be picked up, within 1.84m of Winona will also get teleported. The items include dropped items, heavy object like [Lunar Altars](/wiki/Lunar_Altars "Lunar Altars"), mobs that can possibly be put into inventory, like [Spiders](/wiki/Spiders "Spiders") and [Moleworm](/wiki/Moleworm "Moleworm").

She can also teleport while riding a [Beefalo](/wiki/Beefalo "Beefalo").

Every use reduces the durability by 6.7%, so the Portasol can be used up to 15 times before its durability reaches 0%. It can be recharged by dropping it in the range of [Winona's Generator](/wiki/Winona%27s_Generator "Winona's Generator") or [Winona's G.E.M.erator](/wiki/Winona%27s_G.E.M.erator "Winona's G.E.M.erator"). When dropped in range, the bulb at the top of the Portasol will light up and a cable becomes visible on the side of the Portasol leading into the ground. A full recharge takes 8 minutes (or 4 minutes after learning the [Quick Charge](/wiki/Quick_Charge "Quick Charge") skill). When fully charged, the light of the Portasol will turn off.

The Portasol can also be equipped by any [Character](/wiki/Character "Character") to gain 35% [Wetness](/wiki/Wetness "Wetness") protection and protection from [Lightning](/wiki/Lightning "Lightning"). In this regard the Portasol has a durability of 15 [days](/wiki/Days "Days").

## Trivia[]
