The **Queen of Moon Quay** is an [NPC](/wiki/NPC "NPC") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")* introduced in the *[The Curse of Moon Quay](/wiki/The_Curse_of_Moon_Quay "The Curse of Moon Quay")* update. She lives on the [Moon Quay](/wiki/Moon_Quay "Moon Quay") along with [Powder Monkeys](/wiki/Powder_Monkey "Powder Monkey"), who reside in [Monkey Huts](/wiki/Monkey_Hut "Monkey Hut"). She is a source of shade and 20% rain protection (like a tree). The [Monkey Bars](/wiki/Monkey_Bar "Monkey Bar") surrounding her provide constant light.

Unlike the [Pig King](/wiki/Pig_King "Pig King"), who is only asleep during the [Night](/wiki/Day-Night_Cycle#Night "Day-Night Cycle") and won't wake up until [Daytime](/wiki/Day-Night_Cycle#Day "Day-Night Cycle"), the Queen of Moon Quay is constantly asleep until approached by a survivor or a Powder Monkey with a [Banana](/wiki/Banana "Banana"), causing her to wake up. If all survivors are gone she will go back to sleep.

## Banana Exchange[]

The Queen's primary purpose is to rid the player of [Accursed Trinkets](/wiki/Accursed_Trinket "Accursed Trinket") in exchange for [Bananas](/wiki/Banana "Banana") and [Cooked Bananas](/wiki/Banana#Cooked_Banana "Banana"). If the player has any Accursed Trinkets in their inventory and a Banana is given to the Queen, she will remove 4 Trinkets from that player's inventory. This is the only way to get rid of Accursed Trinkets, besides dying.

If the player does not have any Accursed Trinkets on them and gives a Banana to the Queen, she will instead drop a random [Rare Blueprint](/wiki/Blueprint#Rare_Blueprint "Blueprint") from a small selection consisting of the [Cannon Kit](/wiki/Cannon_Kit "Cannon Kit"), [Cannonball](/wiki/Cannonball "Cannonball"), [Dock Kit](/wiki/Dock_Kit "Dock Kit"), [Dock Piling Kit](/wiki/Dock_Piling_Kit "Dock Piling Kit") and [Moon Quay Beach Turf](/wiki/Moon_Quay_Beach_Turf "Moon Quay Beach Turf"). She will give one of each for the first five Bananas and a random Blueprint for every Banana afterwards.

She can also be presented with other non-Banana [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") Items and [Trinkets](/wiki/Trinkets "Trinkets"), which she promptly rejects.

## Right of Passage[]

Every time an exchange is made with the Queen she will activate the Right of Passage for 4 minutes. While the Right of Passage is active, Powder Monkeys on the Moon Quay become neutral to all players and will not attack players unless provoked.

The Right of Passage cannot be stacked and will always be reset to 4 minutes.

## Prototype Tips[]

* The origin of Bananas used for trade is irrelevant; Bananas can be picked from the Banana Bushes around the Moon Quay, looted from Powder Monkeys or plundered from the [Ruins](/wiki/Ruins "Ruins") and the Queen will accept them regardless.
* While the player must receive 10 Accursed Trinkets to transform into [Wonkey](/wiki/Wonkey "Wonkey"), they will not turn back to normal until all Accursed Trinkets are removed from their inventory.

## Trivia[]

* Due to fact that the Queen of Moon Quay is an orangutan, and is the queen and leader of the monkeys on the island, she appears to be based on the Disney Jungle Book character [King Louie](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/King_Louie), who is portrayed as an orangutan.
