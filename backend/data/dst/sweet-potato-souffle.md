**Sweet Potato Souffle** is a [Warly](/wiki/Warly "Warly")-specific [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [item](/wiki/Item "Item") cooked in the [Portable Crock Pot](/wiki/Portable_Crock_Pot "Portable Crock Pot") by adding 2 [Raw Sweet Potatoes](/wiki/Sweet_Potato "Sweet Potato") and 2.0 units of [Eggs](/wiki/Eggs "Eggs"). It takes 40 seconds to cook. Using Cooked Sweet Potatoes will result in [Ratatouille](/wiki/Ratatouille "Ratatouille") instead.

It's *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")* counterpart is [Puffed Potato Soufflé](/wiki/Puffed_Potato_Souffl%C3%A9 "Puffed Potato Soufflé"), which functions exactly the same, but is made with [Potato](/wiki/Potato "Potato") instead.

## Prerequisites[]

* Requires: Sweet Potato
* Requires: Eggs
