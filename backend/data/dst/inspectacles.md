The **Inspectacles** are a head-slot [item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in the *[Skill Spotlight Updates](/wiki/Skill_Spotlight_Update "Skill Spotlight Update")*. They can only be crafted by [Winona](/wiki/Winona/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Winona/Don't Starve Together") with 1 [Fashion Goggles](/wiki/Fashion_Goggles "Fashion Goggles") and 2 [Electrical Doodads](/wiki/Electrical_Doodad "Electrical Doodad") after learning the [Founder's Keepers I](/wiki/Founder%27s_Keepers_I "Founder's Keepers I") skill.

## Usage[]

Only Winona can wear the Inspectacles. They can be used to find [Cacheboxes](/wiki/Cachebox "Cachebox"). When a Cachebox spawns, the light bulb on the Inspectacles will begin to light constantly. When wearing, the Inspectacles will change their appearance and blue waves will appear periodically at the border of the overlay pointing to the direction the cachebox can be found.

After learning the [Founder's Keepers II](/wiki/Founder%27s_Keepers_II "Founder's Keepers II") skill, there is a 25% chance to find an [Advanced Cachebox](/wiki/Advanced_Cachebox "Advanced Cachebox") instead of a normal Cachebox.
