*What will be needed:*

* Any [Weapon](/wiki/Weapon "Weapon") ([Spear](/wiki/Spear "Spear") or [Tentacle Spike](/wiki/Tentacle_Spike "Tentacle Spike") is best)
* [Log Suit](/wiki/Log_Suit "Log Suit") or [Football Helmet](/wiki/Football_Helmet "Football Helmet") (optional but recommended)
* [Bird traps](/wiki/Bird_trap "Bird trap")
* [Spider Eggs](/wiki/Spider_Eggs "Spider Eggs")
* [Honey](/wiki/Honey "Honey") or [Bee Boxes](/wiki/Bee_Box "Bee Box") (optional)

*Steps:*

1. Locate a [Pig Village](/wiki/Pig_Village "Pig Village") with a [Pig King](/wiki/Pig_King "Pig King").
2. Plant Spider Eggs near Pig Village.
3. Startle [Spiders](/wiki/Spiders "Spiders"), and let the [Pigs](/wiki/Pigs "Pigs") kill them. Loot [Meat](/wiki/Meat "Meat"), [Pig Skin](/wiki/Pig_Skin "Pig Skin"), [Monster Meat](/wiki/Monster_Meat "Monster Meat"), [Silk](/wiki/Silk "Silk") and [Spider Glands](/wiki/Spider_Gland "Spider Gland") as quickly as possible.
4. Give the collected Meat to the Pig King to receive [Gold Nuggets](/wiki/Gold "Gold").
5. Feed the collected Monster Meat to the Pigs. They will turn into [Werepigs](/wiki/Werepigs "Werepigs") and fight the Pigs.
6. Collect the Meat and give it to the Pig King.
7. Use the collected [Silk](/wiki/Silk "Silk") to craft [Bird Traps](/wiki/Bird_Traps "Bird Traps") and hunt [Birds](/wiki/Birds "Birds").
8. Give the [Morsels](/wiki/Morsel "Morsel") to the Pig King, and collect Gold Nuggets.

*Notes:*

* If the player has a Honey Farm, one may take the [Honey](/wiki/Honey "Honey") and [Monster Meat](/wiki/Monster_Meat "Monster Meat"), and cook them both in the [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") to make [Honey Nuggets](/wiki/Honey_Nuggets "Honey Nuggets").
* Silk is required for many useful [Survival](/wiki/Survival "Survival") [Items](/wiki/Items "Items"); the player will be able to get the Silk from Spiders killed.
* As of the [Reap What You Sow DST update](https://forums.kleientertainment.com/forums/topic/124931-reap-what-you-sow-update-now-available/): Caged Birds will now eat raw monster meat

## Alternative Method[]

Build a [birdcage](/wiki/Birdcage "Birdcage"), feed the [bird](/wiki/Birds "Birds") [Monster Meat](/wiki/Monster_Meat "Monster Meat"). This will yield one [Egg](/wiki/Egg "Egg"). The Egg can be given the Pig King in exchange for one [Gold Nugget](/wiki/Gold_Nugget "Gold Nugget"). If the [Monster Meat](/wiki/Monster_Meat "Monster Meat") from [Hounds](/wiki/Hounds "Hounds") isn't enough, additional [Monster Meat](/wiki/Monster_Meat "Monster Meat") can be obtained in various ways. One of the easiest ways is roaming the [Marshes](/wiki/Marsh "Marsh") and cleaning up after battles between [Tentacles](/wiki/Tentacle "Tentacle"), [Merms](/wiki/Merm "Merm") and [Spiders](/wiki/Spider "Spider") (this can easily be combined with collecting [Reeds](/wiki/Reeds "Reeds") and [Tentacle Spikes](/wiki/Tentacle_Spike "Tentacle Spike")).

## Cave Fishing Method[]

In the caves you can fish for [eels](/wiki/Eel "Eel"), which are valued at 5 gold from Pig King. Once you have gathered the desired amount of eels you may use [Bundling Wrap](/wiki/Bundling_Wrap "Bundling Wrap") to prevent them from spoiling, and return to the surface.

## Cave/Ruins Clockwork Method[]

[Broken Clockworks](/wiki/Broken_Clockworks "Broken Clockworks") are scattered heavily throughout the ruins and caves underground when hit with a [hammer](/wiki/Hammer "Hammer") they have a chance to drop [Melty Marbles](/wiki/Trinkets "Trinkets") (x4) and [Frazzled Wires](/wiki/Trinkets "Trinkets") (x5). These trinkets can be carried in stacks of 20 and traded to the pig king for gold.
