The **Doors Tab** is a crafting category exclusive to the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC. It is found under the [Renovate Tab](/wiki/Renovate_Tab "Renovate Tab"). [House Expansion Permit](/wiki/House_Expansion_Permit "House Expansion Permit") and [Demolition Permit](/wiki/Demolition_Permit "Demolition Permit") can be used on doors.
