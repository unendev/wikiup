**Gunpowder** is a [Science](/wiki/Science "Science") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") and explosive [Weapon](/wiki/Weapon "Weapon"). It requires 1 [Rotten Egg](/wiki/Rotten_Egg "Rotten Egg"), 1 [Charcoal](/wiki/Charcoal "Charcoal"), and 1 [Nitre](/wiki/Nitre "Nitre") to craft and an [Alchemy Engine](/wiki/Alchemy_Engine "Alchemy Engine") to prototype. Rotten [Eggs](/wiki/Egg "Egg") are produced by waiting for Eggs to rot, and can usually be found in [Pengull](/wiki/Pengull "Pengull") nests sometime after [Winter](/wiki/Winter "Winter") ends. [Charcoal](/wiki/Charcoal "Charcoal") can be obtained by burning trees. [Nitre](/wiki/Nitre "Nitre") is dropped from mining [boulders](/wiki/Boulders "Boulders"), or by digging up [Moleworm](/wiki/Moleworm "Moleworm") burrows in the Reign of Giants DLC.

When placed and lit on [fire](/wiki/Category:Fire_Starter "Category:Fire Starter"), Gunpowder will explode after 3–6 seconds, destroying objects and dealing massive damage within 3[m](/wiki/Distance "Distance"). It deals 200 damage per charge and can be exploded in stacks, which is more than enough to instantly kill an unarmored character at full health (with the exception of [Wigfrid](/wiki/Wigfrid "Wigfrid"), an upgraded [WX-78](/wiki/WX-78 "WX-78") or (DS only) [Wolfgang](/wiki/Wolfgang "Wolfgang")). This damage is not affected by a Character's damage multipliers.

Any loot dropped by [Mobs](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs") killed with Gunpowder will turn to [Ashes](/wiki/Ashes "Ashes") or the cooked version of their respective [Meat](/wiki/Meats "Meats") ([Cooked Morsel](/wiki/Cooked_Morsel "Cooked Morsel"), [Fried Egg](/wiki/Fried_Egg "Fried Egg"), etc.), *except* for loot dropped from [Dragonfly](/wiki/Dragonfly "Dragonfly") in the *[Reign of Giants](/wiki/Reign_of_Giants "Reign of Giants")* DLC and *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*.

Flammable items such as [Cut Grass](/wiki/Cut_Grass "Cut Grass") left on the ground can act like a fuse to chain explosions from one clump of gunpowder to the next over an arbitrarily large area (unless it's raining).

## Gift Icon Downloadable Content[]

In the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Shipwrecked "Shipwrecked")* DLC, Gunpowder can be used to craft [Coconades](/wiki/Coconade "Coconade") and [Boat Cannons](/wiki/Boat_Cannon "Boat Cannon"). It can also be used to destroy [Obsidian Boulders](/wiki/Obsidian_Boulder "Obsidian Boulder"). Gunpowder sinks in the water, meaning it cannot be used to fight the [Sealnado](/wiki/Sealnado "Sealnado"), [Tiger Shark](/wiki/Tiger_Shark "Tiger Shark"), or [Quacken](/wiki/Quacken "Quacken") in the water.

In the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Hamlet "Hamlet")* DLC, Gunpowder can be used to load the [Blunderbuss](/wiki/Blunderbuss "Blunderbuss"), a ranged weapon dealing 200 damage. It can also be used to destroy [Suspicious Cracks](/wiki/Ancient_Pig_Ruins "Ancient Pig Ruins") in the [Ancient Pig Ruins](/wiki/Ancient_Pig_Ruins "Ancient Pig Ruins").

## Don't Starve Together Don't Starve Together[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, Giants and bosses from *[A New Reign](/wiki/A_New_Reign "A New Reign")* gain a resistance to explosive damage when it's received within a short time span. This resistance will fade completely if no other explosive damage is done within 10 seconds. Gunpowder sinks in the water, meaning it cannot be used to fight [Malbatross](/wiki/Malbatross "Malbatross") and [Crab King](/wiki/Crab_King "Crab King") without sacrificing a boat, unless an ice flingomatic is used.

**Monsters with resistance**:

* [Ancient Fuelweaver](/wiki/Ancient_Fuelweaver "Ancient Fuelweaver")
* [Bearger](/wiki/Bearger "Bearger")
* [Bee Queen](/wiki/Bee_Queen "Bee Queen")
* [Deerclops](/wiki/Deerclops "Deerclops")
* [Dragonfly](/wiki/Dragonfly "Dragonfly")
* [Klaus](/wiki/Klaus "Klaus")
* [Moose/goose](/wiki/Moose/Goose "Moose/Goose")
* [Shadow Chess Pieces](/wiki/Shadow_Pieces "Shadow Pieces")
* [Toadstool](/wiki/Toadstool "Toadstool")

**Formula**:

* explosions add **(explosion damage / 8000) \* 100%** explosive resistance
  + each Gunpowder adds **2.5%** and **0.2 seconds** of decay time
  + each Slurtle Slime adds **0.625%** and **0.05 seconds** of decay time
* resistance remains at full strength for **2 seconds**
* resistance then decays by **12.5% per second**
* maximum time for resistance to decay to 0% is therefore **10 seconds.**

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* Gunpowder was added in *[A Little Rain Must Fall](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve/Version_History#A_Little_Rain_Must_Fall "Don't Starve/Version History")* update.
* Gunpowder explosions can cause structures to drop their resource, but not if they are on fire.
* Gunpowder explosions have the same lighting effect as a [Lightning](/wiki/Lightning "Lightning") strike.
* In earlier versions of the game, Gunpowder explosions in [Caves](/wiki/Caves "Caves") could cause [Earthquakes](/wiki/Earthquake "Earthquake").
* Real-life black powder is made by combining charcoal (carbon), nitre (potassium nitrate), and sulfur which is mistakenly believed to smell like rotten eggs (an average egg contains about 0.2 milligrams of sulfur).
* Realistically, a pile of gunpowder would only burn, not explode. It needs to be contained (like inside a [coconut](/wiki/Coconade "Coconade")) to allow pressure to build.
* Gunpowder can be set off by [Moleworms](/wiki/Moleworms "Moleworms").
