**Pipton, the Cognizant Bog Merm** is a [Mob](/wiki/Mob "Mob") exclusive to the [The Gorge](/wiki/The_Gorge "The Gorge") event in *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*. He can be found in the Sugarwood Memorial park area, selling various advanced items. Due to his location, Pipton is only accessible after purchasing the Iron Key from [Sammy](/wiki/Sammy "Sammy"), making him more oriented towards the late game.
