**Doohickeys** are [items](/wiki/Items "Items") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in the *[Skill Spotlight Updates](/wiki/Skill_Spotlight_Update "Skill Spotlight Update")*. They can only be obtained by [Winona](/wiki/Winona/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Winona/Don't Starve Together") after learning the [Founder's Keepers I](/wiki/Founder%27s_Keepers_I "Founder's Keepers I") skill. 1-2 Doohickeys drop from a [Cachebox](/wiki/Cachebox "Cachebox"), which can be found by wearing [Inspectacles](/wiki/Inspectacles "Inspectacles"). When dropped on the ground, Doohickeys will disappear after a few seconds. Other [characters](/wiki/Characters "Characters") cannot see or interact with them and they cannot be placed into containers such as [Backpacks](/wiki/Backpack "Backpack") or [Chests](/wiki/Chest "Chest").

Doohickeys are used to craft a [W.I.N.bot](/wiki/W.I.N.bot "W.I.N.bot").
