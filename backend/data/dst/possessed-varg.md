**Possessed Varg** is a boss monster exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in the *[From Beyond](/wiki/From_Beyond "From Beyond")* updates.
It will spawn from a [Varg](/wiki/Varg "Varg") or [Gingerbread Varg](/wiki/Winter%27s_Feast "Winter's Feast") corpse when a fully formed [lunar rift](/wiki/Rift_Cycle "Rift Cycle") is active in the world. A [Brightshade Gestalt](/wiki/Brightshade_Gestalt "Brightshade Gestalt") spawned by said rift will target the corpse and reanimate it. This reduces the number of [Deadly Brightshades](/wiki/Deadly_Brightshade "Deadly Brightshade") spawned from that rift. Players can prevent this by [burning](/wiki/Fire "Fire") the Varg's corpse.

After defeating it, [Wagstaff](/wiki/Wagstaff "Wagstaff") will appear in his [Grainy Transmission](/wiki/Grainy_Transmission "Grainy Transmission") form, saying some words and disappearing. This is part of the quest for receiving a [Spark Ark](/wiki/Spark_Ark "Spark Ark"). If all three mutated bosses have been defeated once before and the previous defeated mutant is not a Posessed Varg, Wagstaff will give the player a Spark Ark.

## Behavior[]

### General[]

Possessed Vargs are aggressive towards any creature, with the exception of other [Lunar Aligned creatures](/wiki/Lunar_Aligned "Lunar Aligned").

### Attacks[]

The Posessed Varg will use the same attacks as a normal Varg, with the addition of a fire breath attack. The fire breath attack will cover everything in front of the Posessed Varg in white flames that do not set other objects on fire and cool the player down instead of burning them.

A Posessed Varg’s spawned [Hounds](/wiki/Hound "Hound") will turn into [Horror Hounds](/wiki/Horror_Hound "Horror Hound") on death.

## Strategy[]

The Posessed Varg can be stunned for a short period of time if it takes enough [Planar Damage](/wiki/Combat "Combat"). During this time, it is best to use quick, high-damage weapons against it such as [Brightshade Bombs](/wiki/Brightshade_Bomb "Brightshade Bomb"). It is best to use Planar Damage weapons against it, as the Possessed Varg has Planar Damage Protection.

The Hounds it spawns have to be carefully dealt with, as the fact that they turn into Horror Hounds can quickly overwhelm players. If possible, one or two players should exclusively target the Hounds and Horror Hounds, while the rest should target the Possessed Varg.

## Trivia[]

* The crystals growing out of the Possessed Varg's mouth may be a reference to the fact that Vargs drop [Hound's Teeth](/wiki/Hound%27s_Tooth "Hound's Tooth") on death and therefore their corpses no longer have teeth.
* Possessed Varg was introduced in the *[Host of Horrors](/wiki/From_Beyond#Host_of_Horrors "From Beyond")* update.
  + It is the only one amongst the three lunar-mutated bosses introduced whose predecessor is not internally considered a boss, since the Varg lacks the "Epic" tag.
