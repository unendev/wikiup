**Lichen** is a [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") item in *[Don't Starve](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve "Don't Starve")*. It is classified as a [Vegetable](/wiki/Vegetable "Vegetable"), and can only be obtained in the [Ruins](/wiki/Ruins "Ruins") by harvesting [Cave Lichen](/wiki/Cave_Lichen "Cave Lichen"). Lichen cannot be cooked at a [Fire Pit](/wiki/Fire_Pit "Fire Pit"), [Campfire](/wiki/Campfire "Campfire"), or [Star Caller's Staff](/wiki/Star_Caller%27s_Staff "Star Caller's Staff"), but it can be used as an ingredient in a [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot"). Lichen regrows after 5 days.

Lichen can be eaten raw and restores 3 Health and 12.5 Hunger, at the cost of 5 Sanity. The Sanity penalty can be avoided by using it in a Crock Pot instead. The [Unagi](/wiki/Unagi "Unagi") recipe for the Crock Pot specifically requires Lichen, however, Lichen can also be used as a filler. Lichen spoils very quickly and becomes [Rot](/wiki/Rot "Rot") 2 days after being harvested.

As of the *[Eye of the Storm](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")* beta, Lichen can be fed to [Beefalo](/wiki/Beefalo "Beefalo") and [Pigs](/wiki/Pig "Pig").
