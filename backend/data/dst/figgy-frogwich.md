**Figgy Frogwich** is a [Meat](/wiki/Meat "Meat") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")* and introduced in the *[Waterlogged](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together/Version_History#Waterlogged_Beta_.28since_July_26.2C_2021.29 "Don't Starve Together/Version History")* update. It is cooked in the [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") by combining 1 [Fig](/wiki/Fig "Fig") and 1 [Frog Leg](/wiki/Frog_Legs "Frog Legs"). It takes 20 seconds to cook and 15 [Days](/wiki/Day-Night_Cycle "Day-Night Cycle") to [Spoil](/wiki/Spoil "Spoil").

When consumed, it restores 20 Health, 25 Hunger and 5 Sanity, and increases the player's perceived temperature by 1 degree per second for 15 seconds.

Adding a [Vegetable](/wiki/Vegetables "Vegetables") has a 50% chance to result in a [Froggle Bunwich](/wiki/Froggle_Bunwich "Froggle Bunwich"). Adding [Honey](/wiki/Honey "Honey") results in [Honey Nuggets](/wiki/Honey_Nuggets "Honey Nuggets"). Using only one [Twig](/wiki/Twigs "Twigs") will result in Kabobs. Adding one Twig and any piece of Meat (besides a [Barnacle](/wiki/Barnacles "Barnacles")) will result in [Figkabab](/wiki/Figkabab "Figkabab"). Using any [Egg](/wiki/Eggs "Eggs") and a vegetable will result in [Pierogi](/wiki/Pierogi "Pierogi"). Using a Barnacle and a vegetable will result in [Barnacle Pita](/wiki/Barnacle_Pita "Barnacle Pita").
