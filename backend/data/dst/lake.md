The **Lake** is a feature exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[A New Reign](/wiki/A_New_Reign "A New Reign")*. It is a large body of water that spawns in its own [Desert](/wiki/Desert "Desert") [Set Piece](/wiki/Set_Piece#Oasis "Set Piece") called the Oasis.

During any [Seasons](/wiki/Season "Season") other than [Summer](/wiki/Summer "Summer"), the Lake will be dry and unusable. Players can walk over it during this time.

During Summer, the Lake will fill up with water. By using a [Fishing Rod](/wiki/Fishing_Rod "Fishing Rod"), [Freshwater Fish](/wiki/Freshwater_Fish "Freshwater Fish") and [Crumpled Packages](/wiki/Crumpled_Package "Crumpled Package") can be caught at any time of [Day](/wiki/Day "Day"). There are 15 Fish or Packages, and one is refilled every 90 seconds. The chance of getting a Fish is 40%, and getting a Package is 60%. Fishing takes 4 seconds, with 2.4 seconds added for every fish missing from the Lake.

Three [Fireflies](/wiki/Fireflies "Fireflies") naturally spawn around the center of the Lake when it fills every Summer, illuminating it at night. They are not catchable while the Lake is full of water.

## Prototype Tips[]

* Making a small outpost near the Oasis can be beneficial as players may need to stay at the Lake for long periods of time to fish out the various rare [Blueprints](/wiki/Blueprints "Blueprints") only obtainable through Crumpled Packages.
* The Oasis is also a viable place to build a Summer base, as plants and structures will not smolder and cause [Wildfires](/wiki/Summer#Wildfires "Summer") throughout the entire surrounding Desert biome. This also gives players easy access to [Cactus Flowers](/wiki/Cactus_Flower "Cactus Flower"), [Volt Goats](/wiki/Volt_Goat "Volt Goat") and the [Antlion](/wiki/Antlion "Antlion"), as long as they have obtained [Desert Goggles](/wiki/Desert_Goggles "Desert Goggles") to counter the constant [Sandstorm](/wiki/Sandstorm "Sandstorm").
* The Lake may dry up after filling on the first day of Summer if it [Rains](/wiki/Rain "Rain"). Once rain stops falling, this unexpected change is reversed, and more [Succulents](/wiki/Petals#Succulent "Petals") can be gathered.
* The Lake will not fill up when something that isn't Fireflies is right on top of it, such as a player or an item that wasn't properly pushed away.

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* The Lake was added in the *[Against The Grain](/wiki/A_New_Reign#February_2nd.2C_2017_-_Against_the_Grain "A New Reign")* update.
* Items dropped on the dried Lake will be pushed off to the edge so that they may still be picked up after it fills with water.
* The Lake is renewable source of [Fireflies](/wiki/Fireflies "Fireflies") such as surrounding area of the [Great Tree Trunks](/wiki/Great_Tree_Trunk "Great Tree Trunk").
* If the player begins fishing in the lake with zero fishes in it, the character will simply hold the rod indefinitely until the player cancels the action, even if the count of fishes becomes non-zero after the line has been cast.

## Mosquito Bugs[]

* When the Lake fills up with water, some entities standing on it will persist doing so until they move away.
