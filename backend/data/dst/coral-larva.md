The **Coral Larva** is an [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") introduced in the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC. It can be obtained by [Hammering](/wiki/Hammer "Hammer") [Coral Reefs](/wiki/Coral_Reef "Coral Reef") and can be used to craft a [Coral Nubbin](/wiki/Coral_Nubbin "Coral Nubbin").
