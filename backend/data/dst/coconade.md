The **Coconade** is a throwable [Weapon](/wiki/Weapon "Weapon") introduced in the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC. It requires 1 [Coconut](/wiki/Coconut "Coconut"), 1 [Gunpowder](/wiki/Gunpowder "Gunpowder") and 1 [Rope](/wiki/Rope "Rope") to craft and a [Science Machine](/wiki/Science_Machine "Science Machine") to prototype. A Coconade can be used as a ranged explosive weapon or it can be used as a placed explosive like Gunpowder. Coconades are possible rewards from [X Marks the Spot](/wiki/X_Marks_the_Spot "X Marks the Spot") treasure [Chests](/wiki/Chest "Chest"), and can also be found in [Steamer Trunks](/wiki/Steamer_Trunk "Steamer Trunk").

When one explodes, a Coconade deals 250 damage to any [Mobs](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs") and destroys [Structures](/wiki/Structures "Structures") within a 1.5 [Tile](/wiki/Tile "Tile") radius.

Note that since mobs killed by either type of Coconade are considered to have been on fire when they died, any flammable items they drop will turn into [Ashes](/wiki/Ashes "Ashes").

The **Obsidian Coconade** is a stronger and more expensive version of the Coconade exclusive to the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")* DLC. It requires 3 Coconades, 3 [Obsidian](/wiki/Obsidian "Obsidian"), and a [Dragoon Heart](/wiki/Dragoon_Heart "Dragoon Heart") to craft, and may also be found in [X Marks the Spot](/wiki/X_Marks_the_Spot "X Marks the Spot") treasure Chests. Like all items in the [Volcanic Tab](/wiki/Volcanic_Tab "Volcanic Tab"), it cannot be prototyped and the player must be standing near an [Obsidian Workbench](/wiki/Obsidian_Workbench "Obsidian Workbench") or be wearing a [Brain of Thought](/wiki/Brain_of_Thought "Brain of Thought") to craft it again.

When it explodes, an Obsidian Coconade deals 340 damage and destroy structures within a 2.25 [Tile](/wiki/Tile "Tile") radius.

## Icon Tools Usage[]

To use a Coconade or Obsidian Coconade, the player must first light it either by right-clicking a [Torch](/wiki/Torch "Torch") on the Coconade in their [Inventory](/wiki/Inventory "Inventory"), or by placing it on the ground then lighting it. The lit Coconade in the inventory can be thrown by first equipping it, then right-clicking the target. If the player does not throw a Coconade that was lit in the inventory after 5 seconds, it will explode, dealing its usual 250 or 340 damage to the player.

## Gift Icon Downloadable Content[]

In the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Hamlet "Don't Starve: Hamlet")* DLC, regular Coconades can be bought for 20 [Oincs](/wiki/Oinc "Oinc") at [The Boar's Tusk Weapon Shop](/wiki/The_Boar%27s_Tusk_Weapon_Shop "The Boar's Tusk Weapon Shop") in the [Pig City](/wiki/Pig_City "Pig City"). Coconades can be used to destroy [Suspicious Cracks](/wiki/Ancient_Pig_Ruins "Ancient Pig Ruins") in the [Ancient Pig Ruins](/wiki/Ancient_Pig_Ruins "Ancient Pig Ruins"), and are capable of instantly killing the [Pugalisk](/wiki/Pugalisk "Pugalisk") boss if aimed well.

## Prototype Tips[]

 File:How to Throw a Coconade 

How to Throw a Coconade

 

* Effects of worn [Armor](/wiki/Armor "Armor") apply to a Coconade exploding inside the inventory meaning it can be used as a last resort if the player is being overwhelmed by hostile mobs.
* Coconades can one-shot the [Pugalisk](/wiki/Pugalisk "Pugalisk") with the correct placement.
* When used in conjunction with a [Pan Flute](/wiki/Pan_Flute "Pan Flute"), a single Coconade can wipe out an entire wave of [Crocodogs](/wiki/Crocodog "Crocodog"). This is especially useful when sailing in a damaged boat far from land or friendly [Ballphins](/wiki/Bottlenose_Ballphin "Bottlenose Ballphin") (often when exiting the Volcano after days inside).
* Coconades are much more effective when lit in the inventory then thrown due to the ability to target the explosion. Lighting up for explosion placed on ground, multiple [Gunpowder](/wiki/Gunpowder "Gunpowder") will do more damage.
* Inexperienced players should take great care while using the Obsidian Coconade, as the explosion radius may take many players by surprise, and kill or hurt them if they are not out of range.
* Using a [Deconstruction Staff](/wiki/Deconstruction_Staff "Deconstruction Staff") on a single Obsidian Coconade yields 2 Coconades, 2 Obsidian, and 1 Dragoon Heart. This can be done to farm hearts (in the same manner that [Scaled Flooring](/wiki/Scaled_Flooring "Scaled Flooring") can be used to farm scales).
* [Krampus](/wiki/Krampus "Krampus") can still steal Coconades when their fuse is lit, and they will explode in his inventory, this can kill Krampus instantly.

## Mosquito Bugs[]

* If the player is on a boat when the game is loaded from a save, Coconades that were in their inventory can become bugged and cannot be lit. Dropping them on land and picking them back up will restore normal function.
