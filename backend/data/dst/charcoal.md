**Charcoal** is an [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") which is obtained by burning down a [Tree](/wiki/Tree "Tree") or by killing [Krampus](/wiki/Krampus "Krampus"). Charcoal burns at the same rate as [Logs](/wiki/Log "Log") when used in a [Campfire](/wiki/Campfire "Campfire") or Fire Pit; however, it can stack up to 40 rather than 20. Though, it is usually less than ideal as fuel, because a Tree only produces 1 Charcoal versus multiple Logs.

Charcoal can be used to construct a [Drying Rack](/wiki/Drying_Rack "Drying Rack") or a [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot"), and is also used to craft [Fire Darts](/wiki/Fire_Dart "Fire Dart"), [Boomerangs](/wiki/Boomerang "Boomerang"), and [Gunpowder](/wiki/Gunpowder "Gunpowder").

Charcoal will be dropped by [Campfires](/wiki/Campfire "Campfire"), [Fire Pits](/wiki/Campfire#Fire_Pit "Campfire"), [Chiminea](/wiki/Chiminea "Chiminea"), [Buoyant Chiminea](/wiki/Buoyant_Chiminea "Buoyant Chiminea"), and [Obsidian Fire Pit](/wiki/Obsidian_Fire_Pit "Obsidian Fire Pit") when the fire goes out, if it was fueled to the max stage beforehand.

## Don't Starve Together icon Don't Starve Together[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, Charcoal is also dropped by [Klaus](/wiki/Klaus "Klaus") when it is killed the second time, and can be found in [Bundled Wraps](/wiki/Bundling_Wrap "Bundling Wrap") dropped by the [Loot Stash](/wiki/Loot_Stash "Loot Stash").

## Gathering[]

Charcoal is made by setting a tree on fire using a [Torch](/wiki/Torch "Torch") (or any other source of [Fire](/wiki/Fire "Fire")) and then chopping the tree down once it has finished burning. Except for the recently planted trees (sapling size), each burned tree yields 1 Charcoal when chopped regardless of how big it is. If a tree stump is burned, it will only yield [Ashes](/wiki/Ashes "Ashes"). Sapling-sized trees will also only yield Ashes.

For efficiency, multiple trees can be planted close together, given time to grow into a small tree, and then ignited. The fire will jump from each tree in close enough proximity, but to prevent the unwanted spread of fire, care should be taken to clear away any other flammable objects beforehand. After the fire dies out, the entire grove can be harvested.

Extra [Pine Cones](/wiki/Pine_Cone "Pine Cone") will sometimes drop from burned trees, but, unlike chopping down larger trees, they are not guaranteed. Burned trees require only one axe blow to chop down.

In the [Adventure Mode](/wiki/Adventure_Mode "Adventure Mode") world "King of Winter", Charcoal can be collected on the first day due to the burnt trees at the spawn point.
