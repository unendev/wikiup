**Crop Seeds** are a specialized version of [Seeds](/wiki/Seeds "Seeds"), used primarily for [Farming](/wiki/Farming "Farming"). When planted in a [Basic](/wiki/Basic_Farm "Basic Farm")/[Improved Farm](/wiki/Improved_Farm "Improved Farm"), they produce the crop they are named after (for example, Carrot Seeds produce [Carrots](/wiki/Carrot "Carrot")).

Crop Seeds can be obtained by feeding a [Bird](/wiki/Birds "Birds") imprisoned in a [Birdcage](/wiki/Birdcage "Birdcage") any raw Crop from Farm Plots (except [Berries](/wiki/Berries "Berries") and [Mandrake](/wiki/Mandrake_(item) "Mandrake (item)"), which do not have Seeds). The Bird will drop 1–2 Crop Seeds per Crop fed, plus a 50% chance for 1 regular Seeds. Birds will not eat Crop Seeds.

When eaten, Crop Seeds provide 0.5 [Health](/wiki/Health "Health") and 9.375 [Hunger](/wiki/Hunger "Hunger"). Like regular Seeds, Crop Seeds spoil in 40 days, and they turn into [Toasted Seeds](/wiki/Toasted_Seeds "Toasted Seeds") when cooked.

## https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/dont-starve-game/images/d/d8/Gift Icon.png/revision/latest?cb=20151222151842 Downloadable Content[]

In the *[Reign of Giants](/wiki/Reign_of_Giants "Reign of Giants")* DLC, friendly [Catcoons](/wiki/Catcoon "Catcoon") may puke Crop Seeds.

In the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Hamlet "Hamlet")* DLC, Crop Seeds can be purchased from [Miss Sow's Floral Arrangements](/wiki/Pig_Shops#Miss_Sow.27s_Floral_Arrangements "Pig Shops").

## DST icon Don't Starve Together[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, Crop seeds have different visuals but they have the same functionality. Initially crop seeds have an assumed name related to their shape, once the player have accumulated enough knowledge by researching [Farm Plant](/wiki/Farm_Plant "Farm Plant") with a [Gardeneer Hat](/wiki/Gardeneer_Hat "Gardeneer Hat") their name change to the regular name.

They are obtained by harvesting [Farm Plants](/wiki/Farm_Plant "Farm Plant"), by hammering [Giant Vegetables](/wiki/Giant_Vegetables "Giant Vegetables") or by feeding a [Vegetable](/wiki/Vegetable "Vegetable") to a [Birdcage](/wiki/Birdcage "Birdcage"). The Birdcage drops only 1 seeds. The [Lord of the Fruit Flies](/wiki/Lord_of_the_Fruit_Flies "Lord of the Fruit Flies") drops 4 to 8 plant seeds upon death.

There is a 33% chance that [Carrats](/wiki/Carrat "Carrat") will drop [Carrot](/wiki/Carrot "Carrot") Seeds upon death.
[Durian](/wiki/Durian "Durian"), [Eggplant](/wiki/Eggplant "Eggplant"), [Pumpkin](/wiki/Pumpkin "Pumpkin"), [Onion](/wiki/Onion "Onion"), [Garlic](/wiki/Garlic "Garlic") and [Pepper](/wiki/Pepper "Pepper") seeds can be obtained by trading with a [King of the Merms](/wiki/King_of_the_Merms "King of the Merms").

The [Seed Pack-It](/wiki/Seed_Pack-It "Seed Pack-It") will slow spoilage of any contained seeds by 50%.

During the [Midsummer Cawnival](/wiki/Midsummer_Cawnival "Midsummer Cawnival"), [Seed Clusters](/wiki/Midsummer_Cawnival/Prize_Booth#Seed_Clusters "Midsummer Cawnival/Prize Booth") can be crafted at the [Prize Booth](/wiki/Midsummer_Cawnival/Prize_Booth "Midsummer Cawnival/Prize Booth") at a cost of 12 [Prize Tickets](/wiki/Midsummer_Cawnival/Prize_Booth#Prize_Tickets "Midsummer Cawnival/Prize Booth"), yielding 4 Clustered Seeds (Corn Seeds) when opened.
