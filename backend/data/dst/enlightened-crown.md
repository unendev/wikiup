The **Enlightened Crown** is an item exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")*. It is dropped by the [Celestial Champion](/wiki/Celestial_Champion "Celestial Champion"). If a [Deconstruction Staff](/wiki/Deconstruction_Staff "Deconstruction Staff") is used to break this item, it will drop five [Enlightened Shards](/wiki/Enlightened_Shard "Enlightened Shard").

It restores 10 Sanity per minute while worn. When worn by a player with [Sanity](/wiki/Sanity "Sanity") above 85%, it provides constant light. It will also produce a Minor Gestalt with each hit that will attack the target for 42.5 damage, at the cost of one Sanity per hit. Players wearing it will not be attacked by naturally spawned [Gestalts](/wiki/Gestalt "Gestalt").

The Enlightened Crown has five inventory slots that will each accept one of any color of [Mushroom Spore](/wiki/Mushroom_Spore "Mushroom Spore"). The color of the spores inside the crown influences the color of light emitted by the crown while worn, similar to a [Glowcap](/wiki/Mushroom_Lights "Mushroom Lights"). Any spores inside the crown will not be affected by spoilage. Any spores inside a crown will be dropped on the ground when deconstructed.
