**Sunken Chests** are naturally spawning objects exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in the *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")* updates.

A Sunken Chest appears in the [Ocean](/wiki/Ocean "Ocean") in the form of [Underwater Salvageable](/wiki/Underwater_Salvageable "Underwater Salvageable") after the player read a [Message in a Bottle](/wiki/Message_in_a_Bottle "Message in a Bottle"). It can be retrieved from water with a [Pinchin' Winch](/wiki/Pinchin%27_Winch "Pinchin' Winch") and have to be [hammered](/wiki/Hammer "Hammer") to drop its contents plus 3 [Broken Shells](/wiki/Broken_Shell "Broken Shell"). A Sunken Chest has 9 slots, so it can drop up to 9 different kind of items.

Using a [Deconstruction Staff](/wiki/Deconstruction_Staff "Deconstruction Staff") on a Sunken Chest will drop its loot and 5 [Broken Shells](/wiki/Broken_Shell "Broken Shell").

Below is a table of the possible Sunken Chest loots (only one of the rows will drop from a chest).

| 30% | Malbatross Feather×4-10 Boat Patch×4-8 Azure Quill Float×2-5 Dusky Spoon×1-4 Heavy Weighted Lure×0-2 Lost Scrapbook Page×0-1 Recipe Card Sea Fishing Rod  1 of (Archaic Boat Kit50%, Steering Wheel Kit10%, Mast10%, Blueprint(for Tin Fishin' Bin)10%, Boat Kit10%, Anchor Kit10%) |
| 20% | Cut Stone×3-6 Gold Nugget×3-6 Moon Shard×3-6 Moon Rock×3-6 Lost Scrapbook Page×0-1 Opulent Pickaxe  1 of (Purple Gem50%, Yellow Gem20%, Orange Gem20%, Green Gem10%) |
| 10% | Papyrus×4-8 Spoiled Fish (DST)×3-5 Feather Pencil×2-4 Lost Scrapbook Page×0-3 Recipe Card Thermal Stone Stage 3 Walking Cane Gnarwail Horn  1 of (Fashion Goggles75%, Compass25%) |
| 10% | Thulecite×4-8 Gears×1-2 Lost Scrapbook Page×0-1 PickSlashAxe Lantern Thulecite Suit  1 of (Yellow Gem50%, Orange Gem50%)  1 of (Purple Gem90%, Green Gem10%) |

Besides the loots above, if there is a slot left, there is a chance it will contain a [Surprising Seed](/wiki/Surprising_Seed "Surprising Seed"). The chance is 10% on day 1 and slowly increases up to 60% on day 150.

There is an additional 2% chance of a random trinket among:   
Gord's Knot ,Gnome ,Tiny Rocketship ,Frazzled Wires ,Ball and Cup ,Hardened Rubber Bung ,Mismatched Buttons ,Bent Spork ,Frayed Yarn and Wire Hanger.

## Trivia[]

* The Sunken Chest was introduced in the *[She Sells Sea Shells](/wiki/Return_of_Them#She_Sells_Sea_Shells "Return of Them")* update.
* If the chest will drop a [Thermal Stone](/wiki/Thermal_Stone "Thermal Stone"), it will be always fully heated in [Winter](/wiki/Seasons/Winter "Seasons/Winter") or fully cooled in [Summer](/wiki/Seasons/Summer "Seasons/Summer").
