The **Wood Wall** is the third strongest of the four [Walls](/wiki/Wall "Wall"), a craftable [Item](/wiki/Item "Item"), and [Structure](/wiki/Structure "Structure") that prevents the player's and [Mobs](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs")' movement. It can be crafted with 2 [Boards](/wiki/Boards "Boards") and 1 [Rope](/wiki/Rope "Rope"), yielding 8 Wood Walls, and requires a [Science Machine](/wiki/Science_Machine "Science Machine") to prototype.

Once built, it can be repaired or upgraded to a second tier with another piece of Wood Wall or enough [Twigs](/wiki/Twigs "Twigs"), [Logs](/wiki/Logs "Logs"), or Boards. It can be attacked and destroyed, leaving a destroyed Wood Wall. Using a [Hammer](/wiki/Hammer "Hammer") on the wall will yield one Log from tier one and two Logs from tier three. It has a maximum of 200 health and begins with 100 health when first built before being upgraded.

### Downloadable Content[]

In *[Reign of Giants](/wiki/Reign_of_Giants "Reign of Giants")* or *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, as Wood Walls are highly flammable, measures should be taken when fighting [Red Hounds](/wiki/Red_Hound "Red Hound"), or in the [Summer](/wiki/Summer "Summer") regarding [Wildfires](/wiki/Wildfires "Wildfires").

### Shipwrecked Shipwrecked[]

In the *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Shipwrecked "Shipwrecked")* DLC, [Strong Winds](/wiki/Strong_Winds "Strong Winds") can erode and destroy Wood Walls.
