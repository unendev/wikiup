Beginning a new game puts the player in Survival Mode

 

**Survival Mode** (previously called **Sandbox Mode**) is the default game mode in *Don't Starve*. Players enter Survival Mode when starting a new world. Each new world is randomly generated, and can optionally be [customized](/wiki/World_Customization "World Customization") before the world is created, specifying various options like frequency of [Hound](/wiki/Hound "Hound") attacks or the occurrence of [Seasons](/wiki/Seasons "Seasons"). When the player enters the world, their character is greeted by [Maxwell](/wiki/Maxwell_(NPC) "Maxwell (NPC)"), telling them to find some food before night comes.

There is no preset goal in this mode, aside from survival. Players can choose to gather [Things](/wiki/Things "Things") randomly placed around the world, and build the [portal](/wiki/Teleportato "Teleportato") to another survival world, keeping their knowledge and items that they can carry on them, but sacrificing the existing world. In order to enter [Adventure Mode](/wiki/Adventure_Mode "Adventure Mode"), the player must find [Maxwell's Door](/wiki/Maxwell%27s_Door "Maxwell's Door"), which is located in a random location in each world, though usually quite far from where the player first spawned.
