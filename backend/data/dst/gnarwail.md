The **Gnarwail** is a neutral [Mob](/wiki/Mob "Mob") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")*.

Every time a fish school emerges in the [Ocean](/wiki/Ocean "Ocean") (only Rough or Swell) near player, there is a 7.5% chance that Gnarwail will spawn. When killed, the Gnarwail drops four [Raw Fish](/wiki/Raw_Fish "Raw Fish") and one [Gnarwail Horn](/wiki/Gnarwail_Horn "Gnarwail Horn") if its horn is not destroyed.

Gnarwails are neutral toward players. They are sometimes seen noding.

They regularly chase other [Ocean Fishes](/wiki/Ocean_Fishes "Ocean Fishes") and eat their meat.

When attacked, they will drill [Boats](/wiki/Boat_Kit "Boat Kit") with their Horn, damaging them and creating a leak. When drilling, the Horn can be attacked and destroyed without killing the Gnarwail, dropping one [Gnarwail Horn](/wiki/Gnarwail_Horn "Gnarwail Horn"). If a Gnarwail doesn't have a horn, it will attack by jumping into their targets, creating [Waves](/wiki/Waves "Waves") in the ocean. They attack anything that isn't located on a boat with their jump attack only.

Gnarwails can be befriended by giving them [Meats](/wiki/Meats "Meats"). They follow for 25 Hunger per day for up to 2.5 days. Follower Gnarwails will regularly toss [Items](/wiki/Item "Item") floating in the [Ocean](/wiki/Ocean "Ocean") towards the player.

When not hunting ocean fish, Gnarwails attack Monsters that come too close, including [Cookie Cutters](/wiki/Cookie_Cutter "Cookie Cutter"), [Malbatrosses](/wiki/Malbatross "Malbatross"), [Skittersquids](/wiki/Skittersquid "Skittersquid"), and [Rockjaws](/wiki/Rockjaw "Rockjaw") with their jump attack.

During [night](/wiki/Day-Night_Cycle "Day-Night Cycle"), they sleep unless provoked or already in combat.

Gnarwails will despawn if the player travels far away enough such that they are unloaded from the player's vicinity.

## Placeholder Trivia[]

* Gnarwails were introduced in the *[Hook, Line, and Inker](/wiki/Return_of_Them#Hook,_Line,_and_Inker "Return of Them")* update.
* When a Gnarwail does its jump attack, an E-Guitar can be heard.
* Even though they are neutral [animals](/wiki/Category:Animals "Category:Animals"), they are considered hostile mobs in the game files and attacked by Malbatrosses.
* Its name may be a pun on Narwal and Gnarly, given it's rock guitar aesthetic, in addition to wail, as in the wail of a guitar.
* As stated by [Wickerbottom](/wiki/Wickerbottom "Wickerbottom"), the Gnarwail is a variation of *[Monodon monoceros](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Narwhal "wikipedia:Narwhal")*, which is the common narwhal in real life.
* Like the [Whales](/wiki/Whale "Whale") in *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve:_Shipwrecked "Don't Starve: Shipwrecked")*, Gnarwails drop Raw Fish, despite themselves being marine mammals.
