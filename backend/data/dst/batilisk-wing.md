A **Batilisk Wing** is a [Meat](/wiki/Meats "Meats") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") dropped by [Batilisks](/wiki/Batilisk "Batilisk") when killed. It can be [cooked](/wiki/Cooked "Cooked") to make a Cooked Batilisk Wing or dried on a [Drying Rack](/wiki/Drying_Rack "Drying Rack") for 2 days to give [Small Jerky](/wiki/Small_Jerky "Small Jerky"). It can be traded to the [Pig King](/wiki/Pig_King "Pig King") for a [Gold Nugget](/wiki/Gold_Nugget "Gold Nugget"). It is also the essential item in crafting the [Bat Bat](/wiki/Bat_Bat "Bat Bat").

They can also be used as a [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") ingredient with a 0.5 [Meats](/wiki/Meats "Meats") value.

**Cooked Batilisk Wing** is made by cooking a Batilisk Wing over a [Campfire](/wiki/Campfire "Campfire"), [Fire Pit](/wiki/Fire_Pit "Fire Pit") or [Dwarf Star](/wiki/Star_Caller%27s_Staff "Star Caller's Staff"). It cannot be used in the Crock Pot. However, it can be given to [Pig King](/wiki/Pig_King "Pig King") for a [Gold Nugget](/wiki/Gold_Nugget "Gold Nugget"). When cooked, the amount of [health](/wiki/Health "Health") and [hunger](/wiki/Hunger "Hunger") that it restores slightly increases, the sanity penalty is eliminated, and it spoils more slowly. It provides the same amount of health as small jerky and fills more hunger making it slightly better if sanity is not needed at the moment plus being quicker to make than small jerky.

## Gift Icon Downloadable Content[]

In the *[Hamlet](/wiki/Hamlet "Hamlet")* DLC, [Vampire Bats](/wiki/Vampire_Bat "Vampire Bat") will drop an item called **Batilisk Wing?** (question mark included). It looks identical to the regular Batilisk Wing. Both types of Batilisk Wings will combine together in one stack. The Batilisk Wing?'s spawn code is `"vampire_bat_wing"`.

[Pogs](/wiki/Pog "Pog") and [Snaptooths](/wiki/Snaptooth "Snaptooth") will eat Batilisk Wings left on the ground.

## Don't Starve Together Don't Starve Together[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")* Batilisk Wings have a 25% chance to drop from Batilisks.
