**Salsa Fresca** is a [Vegetable](/wiki/Vegetable "Vegetable") [Food](/wiki/Food "Food") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*. It is cooked in the [Crock Pot](/wiki/Crock_Pot "Crock Pot") by combining a [Toma Root](/wiki/Toma_Root "Toma Root"), an [Onion](/wiki/Onion "Onion") and two fillers. Fillers cannot be [Meats](/wiki/Meats "Meats"), [Inedibles](/wiki/Inedibles "Inedibles"), or [Eggs](/wiki/Eggs "Eggs"). Salsa Fresca takes 10 seconds to cook and 15 [Days](/wiki/Day-Night_Cycle "Day-Night Cycle") to [Spoil](/wiki/Spoil "Spoil").

When consumed, it restores 3 Health, 25 Hunger and 33 Sanity.

## Tips[]

* If there is a [Warly](/wiki/Warly "Warly") player in the server, it is highly recommended to use the Toma Root and Onion, along side a single fish, to make [Moqueca](/wiki/Moqueca "Moqueca") instead, as it provides 4.5 times more hunger and 20 times more health, while restoring the same amount of sanity.
