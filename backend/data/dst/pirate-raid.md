**Pirate Raids** are events exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, introduced in *[The Curse of Moon Quay](/wiki/The_Curse_of_Moon_Quay "The Curse of Moon Quay")* update. They occur periodically when a player is on a [Boat](/wiki/Boat_Kit "Boat Kit") within 200 tiles of [Queen of Moon Quay](/wiki/Queen_of_Moon_Quay "Queen of Moon Quay").

Pirate Raids increase in frequency when there are more players. If there are more players within 75 tiles of the Queen of Moon Quay, not counting an average age of players being higher on a server, then the maximum spawn rate will be capped at 81 days. With only a single player within 75 tiles of Queen of Moon Quay, and an average age of 81 days, the pirate raid has 20% chance to spawn every 360 seconds.

The raid involves a [Pirate Sloop](/wiki/Pirate_Sloop "Pirate Sloop"), with 2 to 5 [Powder Monkeys](/wiki/Powder_Monkey "Powder Monkey") and a [Prime Mate](/wiki/Prime_Mate "Prime Mate") quickly approaching the player's boat. The number of Powder Monkeys increases with the number of days in the world, with the maximum number of monkeys capped at 61 days. Once close enough, the monkeys will jump on board to steal items from the player's boat, then they return to their boat and leave.

Monkeys will not attack the player during the raid unless attacked themselves. If any of the monkeys finds a [Banana](/wiki/Banana "Banana") as part of the raid, they will take it and immediately leave. Otherwise, they typically empty one chest and take one stack of items from it.

Fighting the monkeys is dangerous. An unprepared player can be easily overwhelmed and stun-locked by all the monkeys attacking. However defeating the [Prime Mate](/wiki/Prime_Mate "Prime Mate") is the only way to obtain the [Pirate Map](/wiki/Pirate_Map "Pirate Map"), which points out the location of a [Pirate Stash](/wiki/Pirate_Stash "Pirate Stash"), which holds any stolen items as well as potentially the rare blueprints for [Polly Roger's Hat](/wiki/Polly_Roger%27s_Hat "Polly Roger's Hat") and the [Pirate Banner](/wiki/Moon_Quay_Pirate_Banner "Moon Quay Pirate Banner"). Defeating the Prime Mate is also the only way to obtain the [Captain's Tricorn](/wiki/Captain%27s_Tricorn "Captain's Tricorn") and the [Battle Paddle](/wiki/Battle_Paddle "Battle Paddle"), the former of which is required to craft Polly Roger's Hat.

If the player is able to successfully hit an oncoming Pirate Sloop with a [Cannon](/wiki/Cannon "Cannon"), the raiders will leave.

All [Powder Monkeys](/wiki/Powder_Monkey "Powder Monkey") on the [Sloop](/wiki/Pirate_Sloop "Pirate Sloop") are equipped with a [Pirate's Bandana](/wiki/Pirate%27s_Bandana "Pirate's Bandana") and a [Cutless](/wiki/Cutless "Cutless").
