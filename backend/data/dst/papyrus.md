**Papyrus**, also known as **Paper**, is a [Refined](/wiki/Refined "Refined") [Item](/wiki/Item "Item") crafted with 4 [Cut Reeds](/wiki/Cut_Reeds "Cut Reeds"). A [Science Machine](/wiki/Science_Machine "Science Machine") is required to unlock the recipe. Papyrus can be used to make [Honey Poultice](/wiki/Honey_Poultice "Honey Poultice") for healing as well as a [Birdcage](/wiki/Birdcage "Birdcage"), [Night Armor](/wiki/Night_Armor "Night Armor"), and [Compass](/wiki/Compass "Compass"). [Wickerbottom](/wiki/Wickerbottom "Wickerbottom") can use it to craft [Books](/wiki/Books "Books") and starts out with two Papyrus. Papyrus can be used to fuel [Campfires](/wiki/Campfire "Campfire"), though it has a low burn time.

## Downloadable Content[]

In the *[Reign of Giants](/wiki/Reign_of_Giants "Reign of Giants")* and *[Shipwrecked](/wiki/Shipwrecked "Shipwrecked")* DLCs, Papyrus is one of the ingredients to craft a [Floral Shirt](/wiki/Floral_Shirt "Floral Shirt"). [Webber](/wiki/Webber "Webber") can use Papyrus to craft [Spider Eggs](/wiki/Spider_Eggs "Spider Eggs").

## Don't Starve Together Don't Starve Together[]

In *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")*, Papyrus can be obtained by erasing [Blueprints](/wiki/Blueprint "Blueprint"), [Tackle Adverts](/wiki/Advert "Advert"), [Sketches](/wiki/Sketch "Sketch"), [Costume Patterns](/wiki/Year_of_the_Beefalo#Costume_Patterns "Year of the Beefalo"), and [Recipe Cards](/wiki/Recipe_Card "Recipe Card") using [Cartographer's Desk](/wiki/Cartographer%27s_Desk "Cartographer's Desk").

## Trivia[]

* In the old research system, the [research cost](/wiki/Research_points_list "Research points list") of Papyrus was 30.
* Despite being made out of four [Cut Reeds](/wiki/Cut_Reeds "Cut Reeds"), Papyrus has the same burn time as one.
* Papyrus is a thin paper-like material made from the pith of Cyperus Papyrus, a species of reed native to Africa, also called the paper reed.
