The **Mush Gnome** is a [Mob](/wiki/Mobs "Mobs") exclusive to *[Don't Starve Together](/wiki/Don%27t_Starve_Together "Don't Starve Together")* and introduced in *[Return of Them](/wiki/Return_of_Them "Return of Them")*. They can be found in the [Lunar Grotto](/wiki/Lunar_Grotto "Lunar Grotto"), and are passive towards the player. They will occasionally spawn [Lunar Spores](/wiki/Lunar_Spore "Lunar Spore") when walking around. When attacked, they spin and spawn multiple explosive Lunar Spores around them. They are immune to the damage caused by the Lunar Spores. When involved in combat, they avoid most mobs and walk away from them.

Upon death, they drop 1 to 2 [Living Logs](/wiki/Living_Log "Living Log") (with a 50% chance for the second one), 3 to 5 Lunar Spores, and 2 [Moon Shrooms](/wiki/Moon_Shroom "Moon Shroom"). They reappear 1 [day](/wiki/Day "Day") after being killed.

They take 3 [Sleep Darts](/wiki/Blow_Dart#Sleep_Dart "Blow Dart") to be put to sleep and 2 blasts from an [Ice Staff](/wiki/Ice_Staff "Ice Staff") to be frozen.

When on the surface, Mush Gnomes will sleep at night. Outside of the [Lunar Grotto](/wiki/Lunar_Grotto "Lunar Grotto"), they also have an [insanity aura](/wiki/Sanity_Loss "Sanity Loss").

## Combat[]

* A [Fire Staff](/wiki/Fire_Staff "Fire Staff") may be a good choice of weapon, as setting a Mush Gnome on fire will cause the explosive lunar spores it produces to be burnt. However, one should keep in mind that making it die to fire damage will cause it to only drop [Ash](/wiki/Ash "Ash"), destroying its original drops.

## Tips[]

* Mush Gnomes can be a good way to farm Living Logs.

## Trivia[]

* The Mush Gnome was introduced in the *[Forgotten Knowledge](/wiki/Return_of_Them#Forgotten_Knowledge "Return of Them")* update.
* They are considered [Monsters](/wiki/Monster "Monster") and are auto-attacked by [Pigs](/wiki/Pig "Pig"), [Bunnymen](/wiki/Bunnyman "Bunnyman"), or other monster-adverse creatures.
* They glow similar to [Slurpers](/wiki/Slurper "Slurper"), enough to protect the player from [Charlie](/wiki/Charlie "Charlie"). The Lunar Spores may be a problem when using them as [light sources](/wiki/Light_sources "Light sources").
